# The Best Eyeliner Shade for Your Eye Color ...

Some shades of eyeliner work better on blue eyes than brown eyes and vice versa. That's why _Allure_ has some information for you. According to their site, here's the best eyeliner color to fit your eye color:

## 1. Blue Eyes

![hair,human hair color,face,eyebrow,blond,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fx/x5/dqgwp_hair_human-hair-color_face_eyebrow_blond_420x560.jpg)

"Contrasting shades bring out the eye color, so copper and gold make blue eyes appear even bluer."

## 2. Green Eyes

![eyebrow,face,hair,cheek,nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ys/zt/nxyf6_eyebrow_face_hair_cheek_nose_420x560.jpg)

"Red complements green on the color wheel, so pick shades with reddish undertones."

## 3. Brown Eyes

![face,hair,facial expression,eyebrow,person,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/da/iu/ciwfj_face_hair_facial-expression_eyebrow_person_420x560.jpg)

"You can't go wrong with earthy browns, but "amber and midnight-blue tones emphasize the light flecks in brown eyes."
