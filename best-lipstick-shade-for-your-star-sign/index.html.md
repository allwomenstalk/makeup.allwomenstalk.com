# Best Lipstick Shade for Your Star Sign ...

I can't remember the rule... is it "taupe for Taurus" or "aubergine for Aquarius" or something else altogether? If you're wondering what your best lipstick shade is, according to your star sign, here's a list with some very good examples of the right hue for you.

If you're an Aries, you're bold and you don't mind taking a few risks, so why not try something striking, like this gorgeous lavender lippie?

Bite Beauty Amuse Bouche Lipstick in Persimmon

## 6. Virgo

![Allure, cosmetics, lipstick, lip, organ,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s8/wd/o1abzwuq57e5b0e274f49587794649_600x591.jpg)

Yves Saint Laurent Volupté Tint-In-Oil in I Rose You
Price: $32 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Aside from her soft, shimmery pink lipstick, it's her sweetness and softness. Choose a pale, barely-there pink, one with a little shine to it, and you'll be as gorgeous on the outside as you are on the inside.

## 7. Libra

![lip, cosmetics, lip gloss, eye, Stay,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hs/k8/qggtuvxd57e5b0e6b7f68977827286_600x584.jpg)

stila Stay All Day Liquid Lipstick in Dolce
Price: $24 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 8. Scorpio

![pink, product, magenta, cosmetics, lip gloss,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/id/iy/jbtvxl4957e5b0fcdd40e855697214_600x584.jpg)

PAT McGRATH LABS Lust 004 Lipstick in Venom 2
Price: $25 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
There's no star sign as bold and brave as a Scorpio, so why not strut you stuff in a color as vibrant as you? Go for deep, daring shades, like purples and reds in the darker end of the spectrum.

## 9. Sagittarius

![lipstick, cosmetics, eye, BURBERRY, BURBERRY,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tf/vz/mckggkd857e5b10642415861172111_600x582.jpg)

Burberry Kisses Lipstick in Nude Cashmere
Price: $33 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
In a word, a Sagittarius girl is down-to-earth, relate-able, and fun. Her best hues? Rich reds and gorgeous browns, but not necessarily nudes.

## 10. Capricorn

![lipstick, cosmetics, lip, eye, HelA,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nd/sf/eobexovt57e5b0eeaa014336979790_600x582.jpg)

ILIA Lipstick in Pink Kashmir
Price: $26 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Pale pinks are on order for a Capricorn, since they're typically calm, gentle, patient, practical, and relaxed. There are so many pinks to choose from, but I especially love this one.

## 11. Aquarius

![lipstick, cosmetics, product, lip, eye,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n8/th/xferurgp57e5b0fbacc7a606797293_600x582.jpg)

Marc Jacobs Le Marc Lip Crème Lipstick in Willful
Price: $30 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
You're fun, bold, adventurous and spontaneous, so you, Aquarius, can wear almost anything, but why not express yourself with something bright, like this vibrant orchid?

## 12. Pisces

![cosmetics, lip gloss, lip, Mar,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qw/th/e6gdc0qc57e5b107258b6682802451_600x583.jpg)

Kat Von D Everlasting Liquid Lipstick in Backstage Bambi
Price: $20 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Go bold and bright, Pisces! You're an artist, creative and bold, so go for something fun, and change your shades as often as you like, maybe more than once a day.
