# 7 Best Beauty Products for Applying Makeup on the Go ...

Are you one of the many people who apply makeup on the go? Although it’s not the most ideal situation, there are times in our lives when doing makeup on the run is inevitable. Sometimes we just didn’t have enough time to finish our face or maybe you have long days which require a lot of touch ups while you’re out and about. Either way, I found the seven perfect beauty products for applying makeup on the go. They’re easy-to-use, compact and totally totable!

## 1. Laneige BB Cushion

![Laneige BB Cushion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rd/kf/azf9n86h_410x391.jpg)

I’m still slightly obsessed with BB creams and this one is spill-proof which makes it ideal for makeup on the go. Just apply the applicator to the sponge soaked in BB cream and pat on your face for a more even skin-tone and sun protection, as well as cooling and hydrating benefits. There’s never been an easier way to get your BB fix without the mess! Available at Target for $34.

## 2. Bite Beauty Luminous Crème Lipstick Duo

![Bite Beauty Luminous Crème Lipstick Duo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q1/eh/kqyqts1i_450x448.jpg)

Lipstick is one of the easiest products to apply on the go in my opinion. If you have a few seconds of time and a reflective surface, you can instantly brighten up your face and look more put together. Bite Beauty’s travel size lipstick duo has vibrant, contrasting colors on each end and each lipstick is said to have potent anti-aging benefits. Available at Sephora for $12.

## 3. Maybelline Face Studio Master Glaze Glisten Blush Stick

![Maybelline Face Studio Master Glaze Glisten Blush Stick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vo/q7/m4hwze8r_410x400.jpg)

Any time you’re applying makeup on the go, you’re going to want to use beauty products that don’t require extra tools or applicators. Maybelline’s Master Glaze Glisten Blush Stick is an instant pick-me-up and all you have to do is blend it in with your clean fingers. Other reasons why you’ll fall hard for this blush are that it contains nourishing shea butter and shimmer pearls for a sheer wash of color that looks dewy and fresh. Available at Target for $7.99.

## 4. Urban Decay B6 Vitamin-Infused Complexion Prep Spray

![Urban Decay B6 Vitamin-Infused Complexion Prep Spray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jo/ma/u7562daz_450x448.jpg)

B Vitamins are known for its energizing properties which makes this vitamin infused spray perfect for mornings you’re running late or when your skin is in need of a little wake-up call. Spritz this versatile, travel-sized spray morning, noon or night to prep your skin for products, hydrate and refresh. It works on all skin types and helps minimize pores, reduce redness and absorb oil. Available at Sephora for $15.

## 5. L'Oreal True Match Crayon Concealer

![L'Oreal True Match Crayon Concealer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d1/ur/nruwnk95_410x393.jpg)

No matter how much of a rush I’m in, I try not to skip concealer unless it’s one of those rare days I can go without it. L’Oreal’s True Match Crayon Concealer comes in an easy-to-carry and use crayon form so you can draw it on where you need extra coverage, blend and go. The pointy tip allows for precise application and the high liquid content helps it glide onto skin for easily blending. Available at Target for $7.99.

## 6. Too Faced 3 - Way Lash Lining Tool

![Too Faced 3 - Way Lash Lining Tool](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lg/s7/sdg3tvh5_450x448.jpg)

This eyeliner/lash lining tool might look a little intimidating to use when you’re on the go, but it makes emphasizing the lashes, tight lining and lining eyes a breeze. The innovative triple-tip allows you to dot the lash line to accentuate eyes, get super close to the lash line to tightline or you can use it to draw on a cat eye. Having options are always a good thing. Available at Sephora for $22.

## 7. CoverGirl LashBlast Volume Blasting Mascara

![CoverGirl LashBlast Volume Blasting Mascara](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rw/s8/p8f2np33_330x527.jpg)

I don’t know about you, but no matter how much makeup I’m wearing, I feel naked without mascara. Mascara is one of those beauty products that you don’t want to apply in a rush, but for those days that you don’t have a ton of time on your hands, a couple of swipes of this volumizing mascara should do the trick. If the wand is too big for your liking, try L’Oreal Voluminous or Telescopic mascara. Available at Walmart for $6.94.

Applying makeup on the go can be fairly quick and easy with the right tools and lots of practice under your belt. These beauty products can help make your morning beauty routine on the go a little bit easier and without having to lug around a ton of products! Do you have any favorite beauty products for makeup on the go?
