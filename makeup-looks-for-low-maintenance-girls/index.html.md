# 10 Fab Makeup Looks for All the Low Maintenance Girls ...

You don't have to spend hours in front of the mirror applying your makeup. You don't have to cover your face in a million different products, either. You can throw on a little bit of lipstick and eyeliner and call it a day. Want proof? Here are a few amazing makeup looks for low maintenance women:

## 1. Makeup for the Average Girl

This is the most realistic, easy to follow tutorial that you'll ever see. It doesn't require you to do all that much work, which means you can honestly do it in under five minutes. That makes it perfect for women who are in school or have a job with early hours.

## 2. Realistic Drugstore Makeup Routing

You don't have to splurge on expensive products in order to look gorgeous. You can pick up some cheap products from the drugstore and still look glamorous. If you watch this video, then you'll learn a quick and easy way to do your makeup for school, for work, or for a trip to the grocery store.

## 3. Fresh and Clean Makeup Routine

This makeup look won't take you long. After you apply your foundation, you should pat some blush on your cheeks, fill in your eyebrows, and start working on your eye makeup. For that, all you have to do is add some shadow, apply some liner, and curl your lashes. Of course, it never hurts to add a little bit of mascara! You could even add fake lashes if you want your eyes to appear wider.

## 4. Sweet and Simple Makeup Look

You don't actually need to bronze or contour your face in order to look amazing, and the woman in this video proves that. Instead, you can skip right to applying your blush, filling in your brows, and putting on some eyeshadow. Even if you don't want to follow her routine, you'll probably want to pick up the lipstick she used, because it makes her lips look amazing!

## 5. Everyday Makeup Anyone Can do

Even though it takes the woman in the video a little bit of time to finish this makeup look, the end result will look simple and sweet. After she applies her foundation, concealer, and bronzer, all she really does is apply a little bit of mascara and puts on some gorgeous pink lipstick. It's a look that any woman could pull off.

## 6. Megan Fox Inspired Makeup

Even though Megan Fox is considered one of the sexiest women in the world, it isn't all that hard to do your makeup like she does. All it takes is a little bit of blush, brown eyeshadow, and brown eyeliner. Top it all off with some rose pink liner and lip gloss, and you'll look as amazing as a celebrity!

## 7. Everyday Makeup Routine

This routine might look a little intense, but it's not as bad as it seems. Once you get the hang of it, it shouldn't take you all that long to do in the morning. Even if you don't follow the woman's exact routine, the video is still worth watching, because she gives you some pretty helpful eyeliner tips and product reviews.

## 8. Fresh Five Minute Face

Here's a fast and easy makeup look that only takes 5 minutes from start to finish. It's definitely ideal for summer, but will also work year round. If you want to look your best without investing a ton of time, you are going to love this tutorial.

## 9. Low Maintenance Chic

This is a Bare Minerals tutorial so you know it's going to be great. Not only are you going to have a fuss free look, but it's super fast and easy to get it done. Bare Minerals knows their stuff so you can count on this tutorial to help you perfect your low maintenance makeup routine in no time at all.

## 10. Sweatpants of Makeup

I love the name of this one! If you're more of a sweatpants kind of girl, this makeup look is perfect for you. It's comfortable and easy to throw on, but you'll still look your fabulous self, no matter where you're going. You're going to turn heads with this one, even if you are wearing your sweats.

You don't have to cover your face in makeup in order to look beautiful. How much makeup do you usually apply before you leave the house?
