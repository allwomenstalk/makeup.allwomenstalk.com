The Perfect Concealer for Hiding Every Flaw on Your Skin ...

NARS Radiant Creamy Concealer • YSL TOUCHE ÉCLAT - Radiant Touch • Urban Decay Naked Skin Weightless Complete Coverage Concealer • MAKE up for EVER Full Cover Concealer • Tarte Maracuja Creaseless Concealer • More ...

By Jennifer

I'm not even sure the Pope himself could absolve me of my various skin sins, but these concealers sure can! Whatever your own skin sin, there's a concealer that can help... after all, that's why God created them, right?

1. NARS Radiant Creamy Concealer

Price: $29.00 at sephora.com
In ten natural-looking shades in full-size containers, and two in the handy travel-size, there's no reason for anyone else to know you partied too hard last night and paid for it in under-eye circles.

2. YSL TOUCHE ÉCLAT - Radiant Touch

Price: $42.00 at sephora.com
This little pen contains a miracle - one of ten shades of concealer that, once applied, makes skin radiant and the very opposite of tired-looking.

3. Urban Decay Naked Skin Weightless Complete Coverage Concealer

Price: $28.00 at sephora.com
The wonder of this high-coverage concealer is how natural it looks. Bravo!

4. MAKE up for EVER Full Cover Concealer

Price: $32.00 at sephora.com
A dozen different shades, one simple mission: to make your skin as flawless as possible without divine intervention.

5. Tarte Maracuja Creaseless Concealer

Price: $25.00 at sephora.com
With 14 skin tone-matching shades, this concealer could be the answer to your fervent prayers.

6. Kat Von D Lock-It Concealer

Price: $25.00 at sephora.com
So maybe that tequila-fueled late-night visit to the tattoo parlor wasn't your best idea. No worries. Use this highly-pigmented, water-proof concealer to hide it... til you can either fall in love with it, or have it removed.

7. Benefit Cosmetics Boi-ing Industrial-Strength Full Coverage Concealer

Price: $20.00 at sephora.com
My only complaint with this concealer? It only comes in 5 shades, so you might not find the color you need.

8. Laura Mercier Secret Camouflage

Price: $35.00 at sephora.com
This two-hue palette, available in 8 color combinations, tells me that Laura Mercier's been there, committing her own skin sins, and wanting to camouflage them.

9. SEPHORA COLLECTION Bright Future Gel Serum Concealer

Price: $14.00 at sephora.com
This applicator lets you apply concealer only where you need it, with complete precision, and with 20 shades, there's bound to be one to match your complexion.

10. Amazing Cosmetics Amazing Concealer

Price: $42.00 at sephora.com
A tiny dot of this ultra-awesome concealer can hide a multitude of sins, including blemishes, sun spots, dark circles, and more.

11. CLINIQUE beyond Perfecting Foundation + Concealer

Price: $27.00 at sephora.com
With 21 shades to choose from, you'd think at least two of them would be made for women of color, but sadly, there's only one. If you have a lighter complexion, though, chances are, one of the shades will be a perfect match.

12. Lancôme MAQUICOMPLET - Complete Coverage Concealer

Price: $31.00 at sephora.com
I love this concealer because it's lighter than a feather, but it provides full, natural-looking coverage.

13. Givenchy Mister Light Instant Light Corrective Pen

Price: $36.00 at sephora.com
This little pen really is a magic marker, only better, because rather than making you slightly dippy when you're coloring, it hides every blemish.

14. COVER FX Cream Concealer

Price: $27.00 at sephora.com
Again, this concealer comes in a range of colors, but only for light complexions. Come on, Cover FX, add some shades for darker skin! Because your concealer is too fab to be limited to only half the population.

15. Guerlain Multi-perfecting Concealer

Price: $40.00 at sephora.com
A hydrating concealer that blurs imperfections for a perfected complexion. comes in 5 shades.

16. Benefit Cosmetics Fake-up Undereye Hydrating Concealer

Price: $24.00 at sephora.com
Oh, girl. Those dark under-eye circles have made every step this day a mini walk of shame... we all know you were up way too late, binge-watching OITNB. It's okay, though. This concealer soothes, hydrates, and conceals. Try it.

17. Hourglass Hidden Corrective Concealer

Price: $34.00 at sephora.com
Hide all the sins! And still look totally natural and even - gasp! - saintly in your luminous skin.

18. Josie Maran Argan Creamy Concealer Crayon

Price: $20.00 at sephora.com
Get the dewy finish of your favorite argan oil with a little long-lasting concealer coverage.

19. Bareminerals Well-Rested Face & Eye Brightener

Price: $22.00 at sephora.com
Look well-rested even when you're exhausted from all your good deeds.
