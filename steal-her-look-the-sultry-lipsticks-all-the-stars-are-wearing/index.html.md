# Steal Her Look: the Sultry Lipsticks All the Stars Are Wearing ...

A good lip color can take any boring outfit and turn it into something chic! That's why it's important for every girl to have a signature color. Experiment with different shades until you find your favorite and then, well, wear it everywhere you go! Here's some inspiration from some of the most stylish celebs - whose look would you steal?

## 1. Kate Bosworth

![Kate Bosworth](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/av/py/gfh6w5oy_600x969.jpg)

Turn heads with NARS' Velvet Lip Pencil ([narscosmetics.com](http://www.narscosmetics.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)), $25, and Tarte Lipsurgence Lip Tint ([tartecosmetics.com](http://tartecosmetics.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)) in Lust, $24.

## 2. Emma Stone

![Emma Stone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qw/bu/igx7d0li_600x969.jpg)

Want Emma's look? Get Revlon's ColorBurst Lacquer Balm ([ulta.com](http://www.ulta.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)) in Whimsical for $6!

## 3. Jessica Alba

![Jessica Alba](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zb/c4/pjoko365_600x969.jpg)

Get Jessica's subtle look with Avon Ultra Color Absolute Lipstick ([avon.com](https://www.avon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)) in pink renewal for $9.

## 4. Rose Byrne

![Rose Byrne](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w3/ac/st2rg8ut_600x969.jpg)

Rock Tom Ford ([tomford.com](https://www.tomford.com/beauty/lips/)) in Bruised Plum for Rose's look! It's $50.

## 5. Allison Williams

![Allison Williams](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pt/sv/fh2lfh1n_600x969.jpg)

Make a statement in Sephora Collection Cream Lip Stain ([sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)) in Strawberry Kissed for only $13.

## 6. Emmy Rossum

![Emmy Rossum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nr/ad/ddttrsm2_600x969.jpg)

Soft pink lips never looked so good! Get this look with Givenchy's Le Rouge ([sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)) at $36.

## 7. Nina Dobrev

_ For Nina's look, we have Jouer Hydrating Lipstick (jouercosmetics.com_) to thank! It's $22.

## 8. Jaime King

![Jaime King](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iv/dq/s2wr0lqm_600x969.jpg)

This look is a bit more than meets the eyes. It was achieved by using two shades of Burt's Bees Lip Crayons ([burtsbees.com](http://www.burtsbees.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com))! Those shades are Sedona Sands and Hawaiian Smolder, $9 each.

## 9. Tia Mowry

![Tia Mowry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sn/ue/bgvc8d7k_600x969.jpg)

Tia's coral lip is everything! She achieved this look with Stila Stay All Day Liquid Lipstick in Carina ([sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)) at only $22.

## 10. Claire Danes

![Claire Danes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hv/hi/xtygiphf_587x882.jpg)

Homeland's Claire Danes nailed the [sultry look](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/amazing-sultry-makeup-tips/) by wearing Chanel Rouge Allure lip color in Flamboyant ([chanel.com](http://www.chanel.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)) and Chanel Rouge Allure Extrait de Gloss in Pirat ([chanel.com](http://www.chanel.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)), for $32.
