# 7 Lush Pink Lippies for Day or Night ...

Pink lipstick is completely on-trend right now, and if you want to try the trend but aren’t sure which pink lippies are the prettiest, I can help! I’ve scoured Sephora and come up with this lovely list, my picks for the top 7 lush pink lipsticks for day or night… and all for summer!

## 1. Clinique Chubby Stick Moisturizing Lip Colour Balm

![Clinique Chubby Stick Moisturizing Lip Colour Balm](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/04/1_clinique-chubby-stick-moisturizing-lip-colour-balm_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $16.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
It’s so chubby and cute…and this shade is such a lush pink! Of all 8 hues of this lippie, the Woppin Watermelon is my favorite. It’s the perfect pink lipstick for summer, what with “watermelon” in the name and all.

## 2. Yves Saint Laurent ROUGE VOLUPTÉ Silky Sensual Radiant Lipstick SPF 15

![Yves Saint Laurent ROUGE VOLUPTÉ Silky Sensual Radiant Lipstick SPF 15](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/04/2_yves-saint-laurent-rouge-volupt-silky-sensual-radiant-lipstick-spf-15_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $34.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Protect your pretty pucker with this lush lipstick, available in 12 shades. My favorite? Lingerie Pink, so pale and pretty, perfect for sealing a love note or just catching his eye on date night. Then there’s the slightly darker Luscious Pink… also very pretty!

## 3. Guerlain Shine Automatique Hydrating Lip Shine

![Guerlain Shine Automatique Hydrating Lip Shine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/04/3_guerlain-shine-automatique-hydrating-lip-shine_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $35.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
So much shimmer and shine! This pink lipstick does more than just provide a blush of color… it also keeps your lips hydrated and happy! The prettiest pink shade here is the Jardin de Bagatelle. Gorgeous!

## 4. Make up for Ever Rouge Artist Natural

![Make up for Ever Rouge Artist Natural](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/04/4_make-up-for-ever-rouge-artist-natural_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $19.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
18 gorgeous colors, and more than half of them are pink lipsticks! There are so many I love, it’s hard to chose a favorite… but if I had to pick one, I’d choose Copper Pink… or maybe Iridescent Blue Pink… that’s right! It’s pink with a hint of blue. Sexy!

## 5. Smashbox Be Legendary Lipstick

![Smashbox Be Legendary Lipstick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/04/5_smashbox-be-legendary-lipstick_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $19.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Why, yes, actually, I would love to be legendary! And it’s easy with this pink lipstick. There are more than two dozen shades to choose from, in matte or cream, and I like the Bubblegum Pink best… it really is the perfect bubblegum pink color!

## 6. Dior Rouge Dior Lipstick

![Dior Rouge Dior Lipstick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/04/6_dior-rouge-dior-lipstick_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $32.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Dior is classic, and they always have the prettiest pink lippies! This summer is no different… check out Andalouse and Tourbillon, both so lush and sweet, they look almost edible! They’re both long-wearing and provide loads of hydration. Hooray for happy, hydrated lips!

## 7. Kat Von D Painted Love Lipstick

_ **Price:** $19.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This line was designed by reality TV bad girl Kat Von D, and it includes her signature smoldering red, but it also includes a fun, fabulous neon pink called Backstage Bambi. See? Even bad girls can rock pink lipstick!

With so many lush pink lipsticks to choose from, how will you ever pick just one? Or even two? Which of these pink lippies do you lust after, or have you found another one you love more? Do tell!
