# 7 Bold and Bright Beauty Products to Try Now ...

If you’re a fan of wearing bold and bright beauty products, you’re going to fall in love with the products I picked out! As it gets closer to summer, you’ll likely be seeing more bright, cheery colors and less pastels and neutrals. This is great news for those of us who love to rock a bold lip or vibrant colors on the eyes. Scroll down to check out my picks for the best bright beauty products to try out now!

## 1. L'Oreal Colour Riche Eye Shadow Quads in Neon Skirt

![L'Oreal Colour Riche Eye Shadow Quads in Neon Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/05/1_loreal-colour-riche-eye-shadow-quads-in-neon-skirt_224x224.jpg)

When you’re talking about bright beauty products, I don’t think it gets better than this eye shadow quad! OMG, look at the gorgeous colors! I can only imagine how pretty this would look on various eye colors and what a great way to show off your wild side! If you’re raring to get your summer started, why not kick things off by rocking these hot hues?!

## 2. Obsessive Compulsive Cosmetics Lip Tar in Beta

![Obsessive Compulsive Cosmetics Lip Tar in Beta](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/05/2_obsessive-compulsive-cosmetics-lip-tar-in-beta_250x250.jpg)

If you’ve never seen a swatch or tried out one of OCC’s lip tars, you’re in for a big shock! All of their lip tars are unbelievably pigmented, long-lasting and moisturizing. OCC is the epitome of bold, bright makeup, and their lip tar in Beta is extremely intense and perfect for those days you really want to stand out from the crowd!

## 3. Chanel Le Vernis in Mimosa

![Chanel Le Vernis in Mimosa](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/05/3_chanel-le-vernis-in-mimosa_512x512.jpg)

When I think of summer beauty products, I think Chanel totally nailed it with their nail polish! Mimosa is a brilliant yellow that’s sure to brighten up your nails and your day. If you’re not keen on wearing bright eye shadow or lip colors, painting your nails a bold color is the next best way to go! Even if the skies are still gray where you live, you can easily make like you’re surrounded by the sun with this fun polish!

## 4. NARS Blush in Gaiety

![NARS Blush in Gaiety](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/05/4_nars-blush-in-gaiety_328x264.jpg)

Another fabulous option in bright makeup is this upbeat and lively bright pink blush! Gaiety is a fun way to add some warmth and color to your cheeks without resorting to traditional pink and peach colors. This perky hue looks great on a range of skin tones and although it appears really bright, it can easily be worn toned down to work or school!

## 5. COVERGIRL Blast Flipstick Lip Color in Flashy

![COVERGIRL Blast Flipstick Lip Color in Flashy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/05/5_covergirl-blast-flipstick-lip-color-in-flashy_410x410.jpg)

If you’re the type who likes to have options when it comes to lip colors, Covergirl’s flipstick lipsticks offer you two contrasting yet complementary colors in one product! Flashy has a flattering bright violet and shimmering pink color that you can wear on their own or layered. Either way, your lips are sure to take center stage!

## 6. Nails Inc. Crystaltastic Nails in Multi-Colored Pastel

![Nails Inc. Crystaltastic Nails in Multi-Colored Pastel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/05/6_nails-inc-crystaltastic-nails-in-multi-colored-pastel_250x250.jpg)

Think press-on nails died in the '80s? Well, think again! Nails Inc. created a line of fun and flirty press-on nails that are perfect for those who are nail art challenged or when you’re in need of perfectly manicured nails in seconds! Summer beauty doesn’t end with makeup, dress up your nails with these cheerful colors to match your cheerful mood!

## 7. Sultra Color Hair Mascara in Capri Coral

![Sultra Color Hair Mascara in Capri Coral](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/05/7_sultra-color-hair-mascara-in-capri-coral_250x250.jpg)

Mascara for your hair?! You read that right! If you admire candy-colored hair but don’t want to make a long-term commitment, colored hair mascara is for you! With Sutra's hair mascara you can easily add touches of coral, blue, pink and purple to any hair color, type or length. The color can last up to two days and washes out easily with shampoo!

I hope these bright beauty products have got you in the mood to add some color to your beauty routine! It’s always fun to add some color to your everyday look and these products make it easy to make a lasting impression with big, bold colors! What’s your favorite way to wear bright makeup?
