# You Have to Try These Gorgeous Smoky Eye Looks ...

Smoky eye looks can come in many shades but ultimately each speak something different about the person wearing it.

These ideas will give you a gorgeous smoky eye for all eyes on you! If you're a risk-taker with your makeup, try the brighter shades like magenta and red - get creative!

## 1. Gold

![Gold](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j9/ip/brijalz35701b0a227392097613597_485x343.jpg)

A gold smoky eye gives the perfect amount of shimmer and brightness to any makeup look. Apply your main gold shade from the inner to middle part of your eyelid and a dark gold to the outer corner, blending outwards and upwards slightly. A gold smoky eye looks best with eye colours such as green, brown and blue.

## 2. Blue

![Blue](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fn/l6/oxm6x5uy5701b12d56cae067991277_578x500.jpg)

A blue smoky eye gives a vibrant, bold effect to make your makeup look more creative and unique. Apply your main blue shade on the main part of your eyelid and a dark, navy blue for the outer corner. A blue smoky eye is best suited eye colours such as blue and green.

## 3. Silver

![Silver](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/al/v3/qs25acs95701b15e8934d780175070_517x386.jpg)

A silver smoky eye is a bright choice which is bound to make your eyes sparkle and pop! Use a dark silver or even coal black for the outer corner and blend it into your main silver shade. A silver smoky eye is best suited for eye colours such as blue, green and hazel.

## 4. Black

![Black](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o0/bq/rt5rizmz5701b0c309aab344195200_600x502.jpg)

A black smoky eye is perfect for being dramatic and bold, best suited for a night time look. It's a great way to vamp up your style and give it that little bit extra. A black smoky eye is best suited for eye colours such as hazel and brown.

## 5. Magenta

![Magenta](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f2/g0/x19qxoz55701b0d78a0a5921382448_600x610.jpg)

A magenta smoky eye will add a touch of femininity and playfulness to your makeup. Finish the look by using a subtle red shade to be applied just underneath the brow bone. A magenta smoky eye is best suited for eye colours such as
brown and hazel.

## 6. Bronze / Brown

![Bronze / Brown](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k9/ch/o4urnwke5701b1109038b646522918_512x343.jpg)

A bronze or brown smoky eye will always be a classic look,
giving your makeup a touch of sophistication and glamour. Apply your darkest shade in the outer corner of your eyelid and underneath, on the lower lash line. A bronze or brown smoky eye is best suited for eye colours such as blue, green, brown and hazel.

## 7. Green

![Green](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n1/e5/nvpn8a3b5701b1fa1aa09762406304_600x516.jpg)

A green smoky eye is a bold and eye-catching choice. It will show that you're not afraid to try new things with your makeup and have fun while experimenting. A green smoky eye is best suited for eye colours such as brown, blue and hazel.

## 8. Red

![Red](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pc/nd/rt7p6quc5701b0fad8ade760460812_600x444.jpg)

A red smoky eye gives a look of seduction and mystery. Give it a little bit extra by adding red glitter over the eyelid, as your final step. A red smoky eye is best suited for eye colours such as brown and hazel.
