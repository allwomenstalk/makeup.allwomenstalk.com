# 8 Mascara Tips for Short Eyelashes ...

**Mascara tips** have become something of an obsession for me over the years. Growing up with _short eyelashes_, I've spent what feels like eons tinkering with different types of **mascara**, brushes, and techniques. I felt like a classic fairy tale character, only instead of Princess and the Pea, it was Princess and the Mascara Wand. In the pursuit of that elusive "wow" factor for my lashes, I've amassed a treasure trove of insider knowledge that I'm excited to share with all you fabulous people out there struggling with the same dilemma.

As we glide into 2024, mascara formulations have evolved practically to the level of space technology (no kidding!). Now, you can find mascaras with _fiber extensions_, _curl enhancers_, and _lash conditioners_, making it easier than ever to achieve the fluttery, voluminous lashes of our dreams. In this article, you’ll discover not just any run-of-the-mill mascara tips, but the crème de la crème specifically tailored for our gorgeous yet challenging short eyelashes.

First off, let’s talk about [curling techniques](#1). Curling your eyelashes is an absolute game-changer, especially if your lashes are short. Think of it like giving your lashes a little pep talk to stand up and be noticed. Curl them before you slap on any mascara because one wrong move afterward and you could end up pulling out precious lashes. Trust me, I've learned the hard way.

Next, on the topic of [primers](#2). Holy smokes, where was eyelash primer all my life? Using an eyelash primer before applying mascara ensures that every microscopic lash gets coated, separated, and primed for volume. It’s like laying down a solid foundation before building a skyscraper. And let’s not forget the magic of [wiggling techniques](#5)-the ol' wiggle and sweep technique can make your lashes look lengthier and fuller, making each application more efficient.

Don't worry if some of these tips sound like rocket science. With a bit of practice, you'll become a mascara maestro in no time. We’ll also dive into [choosing the right formula](#7), because not all mascaras are created equal. If you have sensitive eyes (been there, done that), you’ll want to pay close attention to hypoallergenic and natural ingredient formulations.

And hey, let’s not forget about the impact of [mascara removal](#6)! Using the right remover can save your lashes from shedding and breaking, keeping them in tip-top shape for your next glam session.

By the end of this read, you'll be armed with the best mascara tips that 2024 has to offer, ready to take on the world with your dazzling lashes!

## 1. Use an Eyelash Curler

First and foremost girls, you've got to use that eyelash curler! I know, I know, I hate using it too, but this mascara tip for short eyelashes works! Eyelash curlers actually will curl your eyelashes upward and make them appear longer. The trick is to use the eyelash curler before you use your mascara. Remember that!

## 2. Lengthening Mascara

Of course, the top mascara tip for short eyelashes is using lengthening mascara, but what brand really does it? Maybelline girls! I can't tell you enough how much Stiletto has helped my lashes look beautiful and stand out. The brush alone is absolutely wonderful and gets every lash coated perfectly!

## 3. Double-up on Mascara

When you find that ideal mascara, ladies, it's time to double-up! Basically, you're going to place one coat on and before your first coat dries, you'll go back over it and coat it again. You want to make sure that you do it before it dries, otherwise you'll end up with spider mascara and that won't lengthen anything!

## 4. Volumizing Mascara

Have you ever heard of volumizing mascara? Well girls, this is absolutely the key to lengthening your short eyelashes! This mascara tip for short eyelashes is all about lengthening and this mascara will keep your eyelashes curled and looking beautiful!

## 5. Brush Your Eyelashes

Okay, so I know it sounds a little weird, but if you use an eyebrow brush to comb on through your lashes before you put on your mascara, you'll see how much of a difference it makes! Not only will you get every single lash, but your lashes will look that much better!

## 6. Curved Mascara Brushes

Ladies, the mascara brush that you have inside your mascara absolutely matters! You want something that is going to be flexible and not pull your eyelashes down or coat them too heavy. This mascara tip for short eyelashes can actually help every girl!

## 7. Falsies

If you don't think that your eyelashes are that long and there is no amount of mascara that can help, why not switch on over to falsies? False eyelashes are super easy to put on once you get the hang of it and I swear, they look real!

## 8. No Dense Brushes

Finally ladies, remember when I said that the mascara brush matters? Well, a dense brush can actually coat your lashes too much and weigh them down. You want a brush that is curved, flexible and going to be really loose! Try it!

There you have it girls! My top 8 mascara tips for short eyelashes that will absolutely help lengthen! What are some of your favorite mascara tips for short eyelashes? Any to share?
