# 7 Pastel Eyeshadows to Wear This Spring ...

This spring is the perfect time to wear pastel eyeshadows. Winter is all about the dark and dramatic colors, while spring is all about bright and pastel shades. Pastel eyeshadows will help you channel all of the wonderful things about springtime. I think every makeup bag should contain at least a few pastel eyeshadows for spring and these are my top picks.

## 1. Dior – Couture Colour Eyeshadow Palette in Pastel Fontanges

![Dior – Couture Colour Eyeshadow Palette in Pastel Fontanges](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c9/w7/w9/uh1qndd3_449x383.jpg)

In this Dior palette, the pastel eyeshadows were inspired by the French Trianon Palace and gardens. This palette was designed to celebrate femininity and elegance. The palette includes five colors: a baby blue, a blush pink, a light yellow, brown, and silver. This palette is a limited edition set for the spring 2014 season. You can find this palette through [dior.com](https://goo.gl/sUxeNw).

## 2. Make up for Ever – Aqua Cream in Pastel Blue

![Make up for Ever – Aqua Cream in Pastel Blue](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k4/n2/d2/mrekzxl5_449x438.jpg)

I am obsessed with Make Up For Ever’s Aqua Creams and for spring, I love the pastel blue shade. Although it is a cream shadow, this product will not crease or smudge once dry. The Aqua Cream shadows are loaded with pigment and mother of pearl particles to give the shadow lots of color and a little shimmer. Aqua Cream eyeshadows are great because they are easy to blend, long-lasting, and waterproof. Visit [makeupforever.com](http://www.makeupforever.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) to get the pastel blue Aqua Cream.

## 3. Lime Crime – Fantasy Palette in Palette D’Antoinette

![Lime Crime – Fantasy Palette in Palette D’Antoinette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cq/ht/tz/uodwum7e_474x247.jpg)

Lime Crime’s Palette D’Antoinette is, of course, inspired by Marie Antoinette. There are five pastel colors in the palette: silver, pink, purple, green, and orange. The colors work flawlessly together and each shade is designed to work as a blush as well as an eyeshadow. The five colors come encased in a Unicorn tin that I can’t get enough of! These shadows are soft, blendable, and full of pigment guaranteeing easy application. You can get yours at [limecrime.com](http://limecrime.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 4. Givenchy – Le Prisme Yeux Quatour Eyeshadow in Pastel

_ I love the Givenchy eyeshadow quads. Each of them contain shadows with different textures and purposes to help you achieve the perfect makeup application. Each set includes a shadow, liner, and unifier that have a satiny texture and a highlighter that has a shinier appearance. You can use each color in the palette separately, but it looks amazing when you use all four at a time. I was thrilled when they released the pastel collection for spring. I believe this is one shadow collection no makeup bag should be without this spring. You can get yours at barneys.com_.

## 5. Sugarpill – Pressed Eyeshadow in Mochi

![Sugarpill – Pressed Eyeshadow in Mochi](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ga/di/qh/gcnhri15_480x480.jpg)

I don’t think I’ll ever be over the mint green trend. Not only is it a wonderful color for fashion, it also makes a gorgeous eyeshadow color. Sugarpill Cosmetics has a matte mint seafoam green shadow called Mochi. This color looks great on everyone and won’t break the bank. You can add pops of this color to a smokey eye or apply all over for a beautiful spring statement. Get Mochi at [sugarpillshop.com](https://goo.gl/7eJcRn).

## 6. MAC Cosmetics – Mineralize Eye Shadow X4 in a Party of Pastels

![MAC Cosmetics – Mineralize Eye Shadow X4 in a Party of Pastels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sq/s5/ek/marrqtau_599x571.jpg)

MAC’s Party of Pastels Palette features four pastel shades that complement each other for the perfect smokey eye. Each shade contains MAC’s unique Mineral-Rich Yeast Extract (a blend of 77 minerals) that nourish the skin. The texture is silky and the shades are designed to go on softly so they can be layered. You can get this collection at [maccosmetics.com](https://goo.gl/6XHmEe).

## 7. NARS – Eyeshadow Duo in Elsa

![NARS – Eyeshadow Duo in Elsa](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u9/kz/ok/y2vz07qw_524x351.jpg)

Elsa is a NARS eyeshadow duo that includes a lilac shade and a frosty blue. These two pastels make a gorgeous combo on your eyes. These colors go on lightly, but if you apply them with a damp brush, you will get more intensity out of the colors. You can use these colors for shading, highlighting, and lining making them incredibly versatile. You can get Elsa at [narscosmetics.com](https://www.narscosmetics.com/USA/duo-eyeshadow/999NAC0000078.html).

These are some of my favorite pastel eyeshadows for the season. Pastel eyeshadows work great in spring, but you can also wear them throughout the summer. Would you wear pastel eyeshadows? Give us your opinion in the comments section.
