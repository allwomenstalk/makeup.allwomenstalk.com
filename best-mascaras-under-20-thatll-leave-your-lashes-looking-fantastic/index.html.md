# 11 Best Mascaras under 20 Thatll Leave Your Lashes Looking Fantastic ...

After watching a good chick-flick, you might find yourself in a middle of a crying sesh, which isn’t such a bad thing if you’ve found the best mascaras under $20 that can hold up against smudge and racoon eyes! Your eyes are usually the first part of you that people pay attention to, so it’s important to highlight one of your best features ladies! Whether your goal is to lengthen, thicken, or waterproof your lush lashes, these best mascaras under $20 are definitely the way to go for a bang for your buck!

Maybelline’s Lash Stiletto has been on numerous lists of best mascaras, simply because it really is that good. Lash Stiletto boasts a lot of great features like a brush that helps to strengthen and condition, while also ensuring long, luxurious, defined and clump free lashes! Plus, with a price point at $8.99, you can’t go wrong. It’s definitely one of my top choices of best mascaras under $20 out there!

![Covergirl Lashblast Volume Mascara](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/Iy/FD/OocHSlSa6Pjx4i7CfL9OborS_319x392.jpg)

Their LashBlast Mascara is one of the best ones I’ve used--ever, and it's only $8.99 in most drugstores! The brush helps it glide over the lashes easily, leaving no way for clumps, it applies pretty evenly and rarely smudges. Plus, it doesn’t flake either. Sounds like a winner to me!

I think it’s safe to say that we ladies all want lashes with some volume to give that extra oomph to our look. Well, you’ll be making a good choice if you choose this lovely mascara! Its unique formula helps amp up 5x normal lash thickness while leaving behind all the unneeded side effects of a lesser mascara (no smudges, clumps or flakiness here). Say hello to a low, low price tag ($7.99) and goodbye to raccoon eyes.

## 4. MAC Opulash

![MAC Opulash](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f3/zR/Kp90s8GPRwcv0LYf6bIZKMeg_599x905.jpg)

Use this one if you’re turning up the drama for a night out on the town. And, you won't have to worry about it wearing off, smudging or clumping (or about paying more than $16.99) either!

Best of all, it's priced at a very reasonable $9.99 retail.

It has a thick brush that doesn’t clump too!

![Avon WASH-off WATERPROOF Mascara](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wE/zz/nMvToiijjhumoTGvDFMLnTRn_599x617.jpg)

Plus, it’ll stick with you through wet times like chick-flicks, weddings, funerals and breakups, without the ever-infamous raccoon eyes. You'll also be pocketbook-savvy, spending only $6.99.

## 10. Tarte Lights, Camera, Lashes! Mascara

![Tarte Lights, Camera, Lashes! Mascara](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/UH/Vv/yfrPX8J6awHyrQrVag5Lah7S_599x586.jpg)

Not to mention all of the other added benefits like it being Paraben, Phthlate and Petroleum free among others. This is hands down one of the best mascaras I’ve used in the $20 region - it's only $19.00.
