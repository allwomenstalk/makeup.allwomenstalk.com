# Top 10 Fab Affordable Makeup Brands ...

Learning to pick out fantastic affordable [makeup brands](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/worst-makeup-brands-out-there/) is hard right? I mean, it’s hard to know what is quality and what is crap. Well girls, I’ve got the low-down on the top affordable makeup brands that not only work, but they are just as high quality as the expensive stuff! So, are you ready to save a bit of money and dig through the top 10 affordable makeup brands out there?

## 1. Lumiere Cosmetics

Now, this brand can be found in Macy's -- and your first thought is probably: why is that expensive brand on here? Well girls, it's actually pretty inexpensive, but all I get is their mineral foundation. Lumiere Cosmetics is great for their foundation. It covers, it's light, it's airy and it's so awesome! Give it a try!

## 2. Revlon

Revlon is another affordable makeup brand that has not only been around forever, but it’s also incredible! Their lipstick alone is absolutely awesome to try and I love their mascara. What’s your favorite Revlon product?

## 3. Maybelline

Now ladies, Maybelline is hands down, my favorite affordable makeup brand for mascara. Stiletto is one of my favorite mascaras and I have to say, the brand is awesome. Have you tried the Dream Matte Mousse? What about their eyeliner? Give Maybelline a try ladies and I promise, you’ll forget about those expensive brands!

## 4. L'Oreal

Now, I love L’Oreal for their hair color typically, but their lipgloss? It’s amazing! This affordable makeup brand is not only one that stands out above the rest, but it’s also got a great reputation! So ladies, what’s in your makeup bag that’s L’Oreal?

## 5. Avon

So I've got to say, Avon is one of my favorite affordable makeup brands. Not only do they have a ton of different options, but all of the makeup is super affordable! If you haven't tried Avon, you've got to give it a try. They have everything from great powder all the way to fantastic eyeliner.

## 6. Physicians Formula

Physicians Formula is actually known for their organic packaging and their unique and different palettes, but I have to say, the blush is my favorite. The way that they have a ton of different pinks all swirled together really makes this one of my favorite affordable makeup brands!

## 7. IQ Natural

One of my go-to brands for any eyeshadow palettes is IQ Natural. They have a ton of different eyeshadow palettes at around $10.00 - $15.00 -- how awesome is that? Also, the eyeshadow is super good. It goes on smooth, is absolutely fresh and lasts for a really long time! Totally one of my favs!

## 8. Almay

Almay is one of those brands that you probably don't exactly think about right? But it is also one of my favorite brands! Not only does the foundation work amazing, but it's absolutely one of the best brands for eyeshadow! If you haven't tried Almay, you've got to give it a try!

## 9. Milani

Milani is one of those brands that you might have to order online, but honestly, it’s so good! Milani makeup has some of the best colored eyeshadows and definitely some of the best lipstick. Give it a try girls!

## 10. Rimmel London Mascara

Finally, we get to the very last affordable makeup brands that I am in love with: Rimmel London! This makeup is not only long-lasting, but it’s also cool! I love the mascara, love the lipstick and love the powder. I'm not a fan of their foundation, but I do love their mascara.

There you have it ladies, my top 10 affordable makeup brands that I’ve tried and loved! What are some of your favorite affordable makeup brands? Any not listed?
