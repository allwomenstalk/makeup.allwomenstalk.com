# 8 Great Gold Beauty Products to Try out ...

Do you want to get the gilded look with gold beauty products? Gold makeup can appear to be intimidating at first but they’re actually quite easy to wear and I think you’ll love it! Whether you’re a beauty risk taker or you like to play it safe, gold beauty products can add a touch royalty and glamour to your makeup!

## 1. China Glaze Angel Wings Nail Polish

![China Glaze Angel Wings Nail Polish](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/12/1_china-glaze-angel-wings-nail-polish_450x450.jpg)

Gold nails might seem tacky to some, but they’re actually really elegant and understated. Gold beauty products are metallic and very shiny but it’s the perfect touch to add some unexpected color and shimmer to your nails. Whether you’re wearing it to work or school or for a special event, gold nails are a perfect way to add a touch of opulence!

## 2. Make up for Ever Metal Powder

![Make up for Ever Metal Powder](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/12/2_make-up-for-ever-metal-powder_250x250.jpg)

Make Up For Ever’s Metal powder is a gilty pleasure! You can use this pigmented and long-lasting powder which comes in gold, olive, tan and copper shimmers to add some sparkle and shine to your eyes. This gold powder was used on Cinna on Hunger Games so if you liked the look on him you’ll probably love it on you!

## 3. Urban Decay 24/7 Waterproof Liquid Liner

![Urban Decay 24/7 Waterproof Liquid Liner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/12/3_urban-decay-24-7-waterproof-liquid-liner_250x250.jpg)

If you’re looking for an easy way to make your eyes glimmer, try Urban Decay’s highly rated 24/7 eye liner pencil. This liner is unbelievably pigmented, sparkly and looks gorgeous on just about anyone! Use this liner in conjunction with your standard liner to add some glitter or on its own!

## 4. Yves Saint Laurent Eyeliner Effet Faux Cils Longwear Cream Eyeliner

![Yves Saint Laurent Eyeliner Effet Faux Cils Longwear Cream Eyeliner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/12/4_yves-saint-laurent-eyeliner-effet-faux-cils-longwear-cream-eyeliner_336x240.jpg)

Cream eye liner is a great alternative to its liquid or pencil equivalents as it offers long-lasting color and can be much easier to use. YSL’s cream eyeliner in Gold Star gets rave reviews for providing all-day coverage and a rush of gold to give eyes brilliant color and shimmer!

## 5. Victoria’s Secret Gold Fragrant Shimmer Powder

![Victoria’s Secret Gold Fragrant Shimmer Powder](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/12/5_victorias-secret-gold-fragrant-shimmer-powder_600x496.jpg)

A flirty and fun gold beauty product to try out is fragranced shimmer powder. Dust this soft shimmery powder all over your body to give yourself a subtle glow and wrap yourself in a fruity and floral fragrance. Use this when you’re going out for the evening or layer with a complementary scent to envelop yourself in the fragrance all day.

## 6. PRAI 24K Gold Wrinkle Repair Crème

![PRAI 24K Gold Wrinkle Repair Crème](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/12/6_prai-24k-gold-wrinkle-repair-cr-me_423x346.jpg)

Gold beauty products don’t all have to be all about gold makeup, our skin wants the royal treatment too! Prai’s 24-karat wrinkle repair crème is infused with 24-karat gold and other precious metals which is said to minimize the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles and improve your complexion.

## 7. Oribe Gold Pomade

![Oribe Gold Pomade](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/12/7_oribe-gold-pomade_195x169.jpg)

Let’s not leave our tresses out of this gold rush! We can really get glowing by using Oribe’s glitzy gold pomade. Coat a few strands to give wet or dry hair a light hold and a festive and glittery look. Use the pomade sparingly during the day for a subtle sheen or use it all over your hair at night to make strands stand out!

## 8. Too Faced Absolutely Invisible Candlelight Powder

Powder your nose with luminescent gold powder and feel like a golden goddess. Too Faced’s candlelight powder is ideal as a final finish to setting makeup and giving your skin a subtle yet sexy glow. Packed with antioxidants, gold and natural extracts, this translucent powder gives skin anti-aging and calming benefits!

Are you ready to try some gold beauty products now?! There are lots of different ways to incorporate the golden hue into your beauty routine so it’s easy to get a dose of glam anytime you want! Gold beauty products can warm up your complexion, give your skin an anti-aging boost or make our toes twinkle! Do you own any gold beauty products?
