# 10 Natural Nude Lippies to Have You Looking Sexy This Spring ...

The natural, nude lip that’s been so popular the last few years isn’t going anywhere. In fact, it may be more popular than ever. It goes beautifully with the fresh, dewey makeup looks of spring. These 10 lippies of both high and low end prices will look sexy this spring.

## 1. MOdels Own Lipstick in Honeylove

![lipstick, cosmetics, lip, eye, models,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ii/l1/uqhp5xys58c9662ca162a291551905_750x750.jpg)

$46 at [amazon.com](https://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This fabulous lippie is on the top of my Models Own wish list. It’s so lovely and so wonderfully, well, nude! Described as a “light beige toned with rose”, it’s a beautiful lipstick you need in your nude collection. It’s a matte formula so you may want to pair it with a lip gloss to get the shine you want. I can’t wait to add it to my lipstick rotation!

## 2. Rimmel Lasting Finish Lipstick in Airy Fairy

![Rimmel Lasting Finish Lipstick in Airy Fairy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kw/xc/qbiufyad58d6317039388701516359_750x750.jpg)

$6.16 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I love this lipstick so much I buy it over and over and that’s saying something. Very few lipsticks get repurchased by me once, let alone multiple times. This’s a cool toned nude that’s a slight bit on the pink side. It looks lovely when you’re wearing a smoky eye or even going for eye makeup in silvers or blues. And while you can get it for $6.16 on Amazon, you can usually pick one up for around $5 if you’re close to a Target.

## 3. Tarte Amazonian Butter Lipstick in Angelic Nude

![Tarte Amazonian Butter Lipstick in Angelic Nude](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e1/b3/irormdmz56ef291f0b31e187687922_750x750.jpg)

$17 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you love the feel of a lip butter then this’s the [nude lipstick](https://allwomenstalk.com/hi/5705cc040c4fe7be538b45c3/) for you. It’s creamy, soft and applies like a dream. If you want your nude to be a good mixture of nude and pink then this one’s for you. It has nude feel but isn’t completely colorless. And I have to mention that Tarte’s Butter Lipsticks are packaged beautifully!

## 4. Maybelline Color Sensational Lipstick in Daringly Nude

![Maybelline Color Sensational Lipstick in Daringly Nude](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lc/fz/abvu2exi58d631857b957811603403_750x750.jpg)

$5.59 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This’s a nice nude lipstick option. It’s a matte lipstick so it’s definitely going to step back and allow your eye makeup to have center stage. That’s the reason most women choose to wear a nude lipstick, isn’t it? To show off your eyes and eye makeup? If you don’t love the matte formula then you can always add gloss.

## 5. Charlotte Tilbury K.I.S.S.I.N.G. Lipstick in Nude Kate

![Charlotte Tilbury K.I.S.S.I.N.G. Lipstick in Nude Kate](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xv/t3/e2ljc6q256ef29d492ee8113467100_750x750.jpg)

$32 at [charlottetilbury.com](http://www.charlottetilbury.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you’re in the mood to treat yourself then go for this nude lipstick. Charlotte Tilbury has quickly skyrocked to the top when it comes to luxury makeup brands. Even the packaging screams luxury. This lipstick is truly queen when it comes to a nude lipstick and makes you want to buy it, even though it’s pricey. If what you want is a true nude with the perfect amount of shine then this’s the one you’ve been searching for.

## 6. Rimmel Moisture Renew Lipstick in to Nude or Not to Nude

![Rimmel Moisture Renew Lipstick in to Nude or Not to Nude](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ox/w3/bcbx374p56ef2a77330db783136608_750x750.jpg)

$4.16 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Let’s just say it. Rimmel is kicking it when it comes to drugstore nudes. This nude has got a “YLBB” (your lips but better) quality. It’s nude but it can also come off as a little plumy like your real lips. It’s one you need to try on to decide if it’s for you or not.

## 7. Too Faced La Crème Dolor Drenched Lip Cream in Naked Dolly

![Too Faced La Crème Dolor Drenched Lip Cream in Naked Dolly](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/in/ge/wmda847q56ef291d44cc1192281604_750x750.jpg)

$22 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I love this nude. Not only is packaged luxuriously in a gold tube, it looks stunning on. It glides on easily and has staying power. It’s described as being a “satin pink” but I always feel it has more of a peachy finish. The look of the finish could depend on each individual’s skin tone, too.

## 8. Mineral Fusion Nude Lipstick

![pink, lipstick, product, cosmetics, lip,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yc/qu/xcejcdnr58c966a692b28548381280_750x750.jpg)

$15 at [amazon.com](https://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This luxurious nude lipstick slides on smooth and has hours of staying power. It's also formulated to resist smudges and feathering so it will always look fantastic. The lipstick is also infused with vitamins and minerals that keep your lips looking and feeling great. What more could a girl ask for?

## 9. Nyx Matte Nude Lipstick

![NYX Cosmetics, product, skin, cosmetics, lip,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q3/dl/wca0qmci58c9672fcf108339991629_750x750.jpg)

$6 at [amazon.com](https://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
When you need an affordable, but fabulous nude lipstick, this is the one for you. It has a highly pigmented formula so you get a great looking color when it's on your lips. The matte finish is ideal for day and night so you can wear this color to work and out on the town. Perfect!

## 10. L'oreal Fairest Nude

![lipstick, product, cosmetics, eye, bottle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cu/vm/n4izmeu658c967b5df9cc945917751_750x750.jpg)

$6 at [amazon.com](https://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This one looks kind of dark, but when you put it on, it has just the right nude shade for any occasion. The lipstick contains vitamins and minerals that promise to give you the best looking lips in town. You know you can trust L'Oreal to give you perfect lip products and this is one of them.

These’re 10 lippies that’ll look sexy in spring. Which ones do you want to try? What’s your favorite nude lipstick?
