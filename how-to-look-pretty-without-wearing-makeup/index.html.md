# Secrets to Unlocking Your Natural Beauty and Going Makeup Free!

Some of us hate to leave the house without throwing on some mascara, filling in our eyebrows, and covering up our blemishes. However, makeup isn't what makes you pretty. You're already pretty without it! If you don't believe it, here are a few ways to look and feel pretty without wearing makeup:

## 1. Sunscreen and Face Masks

The woman in this tutorial stresses the importance of buying moisturizer that contains sunscreen. Even if it's the middle of Winter and you don't even intend on stepping outside, it's important to use it to protect your skin. Secondly, if you're feeling too insecure about your blemishes to walk around without makeup, you should create a face mask out of baking soda and lemon juice. It should wipe those pimples right off of your face! The last, and most important thing that she mentions, is that you need to be drinking as much water as you can to keep your skin hydrated. You can even drink some lemon water if you like the taste!

## 2. Rosy Cheeks and Closed Pores

When you wash your face, make sure to use cold water, because it will close your pores and make you look refreshed. You can also pinch your cheeks and bite down on your lips a bit instead of using blush and lipstick, because it'll make your face look rosy.

## 3. Smooth Lips and Bright Faces

If you want your lips to look clean and smooth, you should exfoliate them with your toothbrush while you're brushing your teeth. Once you're finished with your oral care, you should slap your face as gently as you can in order to get your blood flowing, which will bring color into your skin.

## 4. Eye Cream and Eating Habits

It's important to moisturize your skin every morning and every night. Of course, you should also use an eye cream under your eyes in order to prevent them from appearing red or baggy. Even more importantly, you should change your eating habits if you're used to eating unhealthy foods. Like the woman in the video says, "If you improve your body from the inside, it'll look better on the outside."

## 5. Hydrated and Bright Eyed

Without makeup, you can look dull and tired. However, there's a fix that doesn't involve any beauty products. All you have to do is place two household spoons over your eyes in order to reduce puffiness. Of course, you can also place some lotion all over your face in order to give your skin a hydrated look.

## 6. Toner and Lemon Juice

This video lists all of the different products that you should be using on your skin if you aren't a fan of makeup. It stresses the importance of using a toner, moisturizer, sunscreen, face wash, and even lemon juice.

## 7. Inner Beauty

This might not be an actual beauty tutorial, but it's important for you to watch. It aims to boost your self-esteem by reminding you that your beauty comes from within. It doesn't matter what you look like on the outside, because the inside is what really matters.

See? You don't actually need lipstick and eyeshadow in order to look your best! You're beautiful just the way you are. What do you do to make yourself feel pretty that doesn't involve any makeup products?
