By Rinki

Are you afraid to try foundation because of sensitive and dry skin? Don’t be! The keyword for your skin type is HYDRATION! Most foundations on dry and sensitive skin appear to clog the skin therefore, it’s good to find foundation that allows your skin to breath and provides hydration. Here are few foundations that I strongly recommend.

1. MAC Studio Sculpt SPF 15 Foundation

This gel based foundation provides light to full coverage. Its creamy texture hydrates your skin and eliminates any dullness. It helps to cover blemishes, dark circles, and scars. It feels fresh and lasts throughout the day. For application, it’s important to start with a small amount as it spreads easily on the skin. Two small squeezes out of the tube will give you moderate to full coverage!

Price: $34.00 at shop.nordstrom.com

2. Clinique Super Balanced Makeup

It’s very important that dry and sensitive skin always has a good amount of moisturizer. This foundation is great for this purpose as it works as a moisturizer as well. It provides moderate coverage leaving your skin with a matte and smooth finish. If you prefer using your hands to apply foundation, this is great start!

Price: $25.00 at shop.nordstrom.com

3. Make up Forever- HD Foundation

This foundation can be used for dry to oily skin. As it is HD foundation, it gives a soft focus on the skin and is great for pictures! Most of the foundation only covers dark circles and scars however, this is also helpful for hyperpigmentation. This formulation provides a flawless finish and gives full coverage on the skin.
P.S if you want to look like a celebrity, this is the foundation to use!

4. L’Oreal True Match Super Blendable Foundation

This liquid foundation provides a range of colors to find the correct match for your skin tone. It feels non-greasy and gives a natural look on the skin. Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best foundation for dry and sensitive skin?
Oh, let me tell you! For dry and sensitive skin, you want something that's hydrating and gentle. Brands like NARS and Clinique have some amazing options. Look for foundations with hyaluronic acid or glycerin; they work wonders!
Can I use the same foundation for textured skin?
Absolutely! Foundations for dry and sensitive skin often work great for textured skin too. They usually offer smooth coverage and don't cling to dry patches. Just make sure to exfoliate and moisturize well beforehand.
How do I choose a makeup brand for my dry, sensitive skin?
Go for brands that focus on skincare. Brands like BareMinerals, IT Cosmetics, and Wet n Wild are known for their skin-friendly formulas. Checking ingredient lists helps too; avoid alcohol and fragrance.
What type of foundation is best for mature skin that's dry and sensitive?
Great question! For mature skin, creamy or liquid foundations are usually the way to go. They provide good hydration and minimize the appearance of fine lines. Try something from L'Oreal Paris or Estée Lauder.
Is liquid foundation better for dry sensitive skin?
In most cases, yes! Liquid foundations generally offer more hydration compared to powders. They’re less likely to emphasize dry spots and give a dewy, fresh finish – perfect for dry and sensitive skin types!
