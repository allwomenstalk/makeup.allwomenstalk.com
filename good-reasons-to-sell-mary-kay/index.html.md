# 7 Good Reasons to Sell Mary Kay ...

There are tons of reasons to sell Mary Kay and to get into business for yourself. I gotta say ladies, even though I don't sell Mary Kay, with my list of the top 7 reasons to sell Mary Kay, I might get into the business! The benefits of selling Mary Kay are incredible! Everything from the hours all the way to the motivation and the people that you meet? It's all fantastic! So ladies, take a look at my top reasons to sell this incredible product!

## 1. Great Company

Mary Kay is one of those companies that is known for their reputation. It is known for being a fantastic company to work for. They back all of their consultants and truly, any questions you have, they are more than willing to help you find the answer! So ladies, do you see why this is one of the top reasons to sell Mary Kay?

## 2. You Are Your Own Boss

I actually love this reason to sell Mary Kay – how many other jobs do you get to be your own boss? This is a huge, huge benefit and the best part? You have excellent work and home life balance, because you make your own hours!

## 3. Self Motivating

I know that I always love to feel really motivated, as if I am working toward something. One of the reasons to sell Mary Kay is because you are motivating yourself. Not only are your trying to make your goals (which we will talk about below), but you are trying to be the best and working toward something great. Try it ladies, it's great!

## 4. Sharpens Sales Skills

So, I actually work for a major hotel chain in sales and I gotta say, whenever I speak to someone from Mary Kay to book their meeting space, they are saleswomen! Mary Kay as a company really sharpens any and all of the sales skills that you have! It let's you really become the saleswoman that we all have inside of us!

## 5. Discounted Products

Now, I am not 100% sure about the discounts, as I do not sell Mary Kay, but from what I have heard, there is a huge discount on some great Mary Kay products if you are a consultant. Really ladies, try it – even if it's just for the discount!

## 6. Goal Motivation

Remember how I said one of the reasons to sell Mary Kay is because of goal motivation? Well, you've seen those pink Mary Kay cars around right? What an incentive program!

## 7. The People

Finally ladies, one of the major reasons to sell Mary Kay is because of the people. Not only the people that you are helping, but also the people that you meet. You'll feel more outgoing, you'll get a great customer base and the other consultants that you meet? They could be friends for life!

Mary Kay is one of those companies that is never going to go away. It's a company that offers up a ton of different reasons to sell Mary Kay that are all great! So ladies, what are some of the reasons to sell Mary Kay that you can think of? Anyone sell it now and looking to leave some tips and tricks?
