# Best Lipstick Shades for Girls Who Rock the Smoky Eye Look ...

When you walk around with [smokey eyes](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/different-ways-to-create-gorgeous-smokey-eyes/), you want everyone's focus to be on your eye makeup. That's why you need to stick to wearing subtle shades of blush and lipstick. If you don't, then no one is going to know where to look on your face, because there will be too much going on at once. With that idea in mind, here are the best [lipstick shades](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/gorgeous-lipstick-shades-for-pale-skin/) to go with smokey eyes:

## 1. Pink

Pale pink works wonders when you're rocking a smokey eye, especially if you used a lot of grey eyeshadows. Pink and grey look great together. It's a combination that will really bring out your best features.

## 2. Peach

Pale peach lipstick will soften up any face. However, it looks the best when you're a woman with a warm skin tone. If you don't have a lipstick of this shade in your collection yet, then you should purchase it right away, because it works well for any occasion--even when you're not wearing smokey eyes.

## 3. Plum

You have to be careful when choosing a plum colored lipstick to go with your smokey eyes. You don't want to wear anything too dark, because it will create a look that is way too drastic for most occasions. Stick to light plum shades, unless you're trying to stand out from the crowd.

## 4. Red

Even though you should stay away from most bold colors when you're rocking a smokey eye, there is one exception. If you want to create a vintage look, then you can try wearing a deep red on your lips. Instead of taking away the attention from your smokey eyes like most bold colors would, the red can actually enhance the look, making you seem put-together.

## 5. Any Color That Matches Your Lip Color

You don't have to wear anything on your lips when you're rocking smokey eyes. However, if you desperately want to put on lipstick in order to fix your cupid's bow or to even out your skin tone, you can wear a shade that matches your lip color. That way, you can fix all of your "flaws" while looking natural.

## 6. Just Clear Gloss

You don't actually have to put on lipstick in order to complete your look. You can just grab a clear lip gloss in order to make your lips look shinier. That way, they won't be distracting, but they will look delicious. Plus, if you have a boyfriend, you won't have to worry about your makeup leaving a stain on his cheeks.

## 7. Black

You should only use this color if you're 100% sure that you can pull it off. Some women will have to wait until Halloween in order to make this style seem appropriate. However, if you wear a dark outfit that is meant to seem mysterious, then you might be able to get away with wearing black lipstick with your black smokey eyes. Beware: it could make you look gothic.

You don't want your smokey eyes to go to waste, and that's exactly what will happen if you wear a lipstick shade that is too distracting. That's why you should stick to the ones on this list the next time that you make your eyes as pretty as can be. What color lipstick do you usually wear with smokey eyes?
