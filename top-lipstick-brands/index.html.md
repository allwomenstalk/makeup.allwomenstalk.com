# Top 10 Lipstick Brands ...

**Lipstick brands** are super important whenever you are choosing a great lipstick. The brand can actually make or break the formula of the lipstick. Don't worry though, I've got the top 10 lipstick brands out there! Now, these lipstick brands are in no order at all, I just love 'em all!

## 1. Revlon

Revlon is one of my favorite **lipstick brands** of all time! It is a brand that has been around forever and I can honestly say, in my makeup bag right now, half of 'em are Revlon. The color choices are amazing and this is lipstick that truly won't rub off!

## 2. The Body Shop

Now, you probably wouldn't think of The Body Shop as the first place to go for lipstick right? Well, The Body Shop has some of the best lipstick out there! The best part is that it is all natural and although it might be on the expensive side, it's so worth it, I promise!

## 3. Dior

Christian Dior has the best shoes, the best wedding dresses and definitely the best clothes right? I gotta say, Dior as a **lipstick brand** is also the best. The lipstick feels great, the color choices are off the charts and the quality is so perfect. Expensive, but definitely worth the splurge!

## 4. Max Factor

I actually hadn't tried Max Factor products, any of 'em until about a year ago and within that year, a have gotten quite a few of the lipsticks and they are beautiful. They last a really long time, totally have all of the colors that I want and quality is unmatched. Definitely worth the investment!

## 5. Chanel

So my BFF actually works for Chanel in Nordstorm, so even though I get a discount, I think I would still buy Chanel lipstick even without the discount! This is one of the **lipstick brands** that goes on so easily, the colors are so beautiful and come on, you can't match the quality of Chanel right? Try Chanel lipsticks, you'll fall in love!

## 6. Lancôme

Oh, how do I love my Lancome. Not only has this **lipstick brand** become one of my favorite, but it's definitely a brand that has been around forever, which means that they have perfected the formula of lipstick!

## 7. L'Oreal Paris

L'Oreal Paris is for sure one of the top **lipstick brands** in the world. Not only does this brand feature some of the hottest colors out there, but the choices are amazing! You can get stain, gloss, shiny, plumpers – it's all there and it's all affordable! What more could you want out of a lipstick brand?

## 8. Maybelline

I actually love Maybelline mascaras. I think that Maybelline has perfected mascara in all of it's forms and now that I've tried their lipstick? I'm never going back! Maybelline has transformed lipstick into a necessity and a need more than just a want and a desire. What is your favorite Maybelline product?

## 9. Rimmel

I love anything British and Rimmel London is definitely a brand that knows what they are doing in the lipstick department. Whether you are looking for a super lip plumper or a lipstick that is going to be truly red. This is the brand for you!

## 10. Yves Saint Laurent

Finally, we slip back into luxury with YSL. This brand goes on so beautifully, lasts forever and the lipstick colors are incredible. The only problem? It's expensive, but I promise ladies, if you are going to splurge, this is the brand to splurge on!

Lipstick is an important part of makeup isn't it? It's typically the last thing that you put on and one of the most noticeable things. Now, these are my **top 10 lipstick brands**, what lipstick brands do you ladies love? Did I miss any?
