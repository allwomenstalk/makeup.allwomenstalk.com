## The Magic Behind Scarlet Moon

Scarlet Moon isn’t just another fragrance; it’s an experience. From the very first spritz, you're enveloped in a world of warmth and intrigue. The top notes of Blood Orange offer a refreshing burst that’s both sweet and tart, awakening the senses in an instant. As it melds with the heart notes of Violet Leaf, a soft, green aroma emerges-elevating the overall scent profile and adding a touch of earthiness. Finally, the base note of Japanese Hinoki introduces a woody depth, grounding the fragrance with its calming, resinous character.

**The Composition:** What sets Scarlet Moon apart is the masterful_ blending of its components. Each note is carefully selected to create a balanced tapestry of aromas. The Blood Orange is vibrant yet subtle, never overpowering the other accents. Violet Leaf adds a luxurious, leafy tone that perfectly complements the citrus, while Hinoki wood infuses a sense of calm reflection with its rich, balsamic quality. Together, they form an olfactory symphony that’s both invigorating and soothing.
