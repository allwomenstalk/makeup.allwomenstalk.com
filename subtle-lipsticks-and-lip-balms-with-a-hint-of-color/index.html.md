# 7 Subtle Lipsticks and Lip Balms with a Hint of Color ...

There are days when you just want a hint of color on your lips. Maybe you’re doing a 5-minute face and don’t want to go overboard with the lipstick. You might just want to grab something with a hint of color since you’re super casual in joggers and a tee. For those days, these are 7 subtle lipsticks and lip balms with only a hint of color.

## 1. Maybelline Baby Lips

![Maybelline Baby Lips](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ll/kf/juprqzc455f38a7372003698288058_600x400.jpg)

All of the products listed here offer sheer coverage, including this one. Maybelline Baby Lips is a fabulous lippie that’s gained a cult following. This shouldn’t be surprising with the quality of this product paired with the adorable packaging. They have a great variety of color options and at around $3 a pop, they’re very affordable. My personal favorite shade is Cherry Me.

## 2. Dior Addict Lip Glow

![Dior Addict Lip Glow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p3/g3/k56whprq55f38a27ec1b3598919509_600x400.jpg)

At $33, this product is a bit of a splurge. But we all deserve a splurge now and then! This amazing lippy works with your unique body chemistry to give you a one of a kind lip color that’s all your own. Choose a base color of pink or coral and watch it evolve before your eyes. Not only will it create a beautiful sheer lip color but it’s fun, too!

## 3. Almay Smart Shade Butter Kiss Lipstick

![Almay Smart Shade Butter Kiss Lipstick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gq/j4/c6uatywx55f38a8e5cf7b160072258_600x338.jpg)

I just purchased this lip product in pink for light skin tones. It’s a moist lip product which isn’t surprising since it’s a form of lip butter. I’ve found that lip products that have ‘butter’ in the name are generally a creamy texture and feel lovely on your lips. It’s nice that they’ve done a lot of the work for you and have recommendations in different shades for your specific skin tone. It’s a unique approach that’s easy for buyers.

## 4. Bite Beauty Lush Lip Tint

![Bite Beauty Lush Lip Tint](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t4/gj/ecmn4zqp55f38abf06eef510929634_600x450.jpg)

Bite Beauty is where it’s at, ladies. This brand is climbing in popularity with no sign of stopping soon. This lip product is organic, moisturizing and beautiful on your lips. As with most of these sheer lip products, it’s buildable for more color. With 8 different shades to choose from, there’s something for everyone.

## 5. L’Oreal Color Rich Balm

![L’Oreal Color Rich Balm](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mo/ie/mv0rkdh855f38ad666c64945492956_600x450.jpg)

I’ve heard many great things about this product, which is why it’s on my makeup wish list. Everyone keeps a makeup wish list, right? Anyways, if you do keep one then this is one to add. It promises 8 hours of hydration for your lips while giving you the perfect amount of sheer lip color. The Rose Elixir shade looks universally flattering.

## 6. Fresh Sugar Lip Treatment Sunscreen

![Fresh Sugar Lip Treatment Sunscreen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/an/b1/k0l57c2u55f3913f1488e447890775_600x418.jpg)

This lip treatment is full of natural ingredients that are good for your lips. Black currant oil, grape seed and a collection of vitamins make this lip product healthy for your lips. It offers sun protection while delivering the right amount of color for a light makeup look. At 22.50, it’s an affordable treat. Also worth noting is the fact that it’s a bestselling and highly rated product.

## 7. Maybelline Color Whisper

![Maybelline Color Whisper](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/au/qg/g1l5sxju55f39166c00a2649255298_600x435.jpg)

With 20 amazing shades, it’s no wonder this product is popular. You can go subtle or bold depending on what color you choose. It’s a little different than the other formulations listed in that it’s a gel color. It still offers your lips the moisture they’re craving, though. This product feels great on the lips and can be purchased for around $5.

These are 7 beautiful lippies to choose from when you only want a hint of color. What’s your go-to lip product when you don’t want to wear a full on lipstick? I can’t wait to learn about your favs!
