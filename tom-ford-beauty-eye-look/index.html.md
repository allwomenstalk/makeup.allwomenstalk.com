## Perfecting the Dimensional Eye Look

Creating the perfect eye makeup isn’t just about the products – it’s about the art and technique behind it. Here’s a step-by-step guide to using the TOM FORD Gel Eyeliner and Extreme Mascara together for a look that will turn heads.

**Step 1: Prime Your Eyes**
Begin with a good eye primer to ensure a smooth surface for your liner and shadow. This not only aids in application but extends the wear of your makeup.

**Step 2: Line with Precision**
Using the Gel Eyeliner, draw a line as close to the lashes as possible. Start from the inner corner and work your way outward. For more drama, extend the line beyond the corner of your eye and flick upwards for that iconic cat-eye effect.

**Step 3: Add Dimension**
Line the waterline (inner rim of the eyelids) for an intensified look. This step isn’t mandatory, but it adds a sultry depth that’s perfect for evening wear.

**Step 4: Mascara Magic**
Apply the Extreme Mascara by starting at the base of your lashes and wiggling upwards. This technique ensures even coverage and builds volume. Apply additional coats to match the intensity desired.

**Step 5: Blend and Balance**
Balance the boldness of the top lash line by lightly lining the lower lashes if desired, and finish the look with a touch of highlighter on the brow bone and inner corner of the eyes for that pop of brightness.
