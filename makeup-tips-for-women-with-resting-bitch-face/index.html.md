# 10 Makeup Tips for Girls Suffering from Resting Bitch Face ...

Don't you hate when people walk up to you and ask you what's wrong, even though you're in a perfectly good mood? It's frustrating, but it's what's always happens when you have resting bitch face. People just assume that you're miserable, because of the way that you look when you don't think anyone else is paying attention. Well, you don't have to deal with those pesky comments forever. Here are a few makeup tips for women with resting bitch face:

## 1. Add White Shadow to the Inner Corners of Your Eyes

![Add White Shadow to the Inner Corners of Your Eyes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z6/o4/ypgl1qqb5723c46943a44089655502_600x600.jpg)

If you look tired and miserable, then it'll make your resting bitch face worse. That's why you should add a dab of white eyeshadow to the inner corners of your eyes as soon as you finish the rest of your eye makeup. That little bit of shadow will create the illusion that your eyes are wider and you're more awake. That means you'll look friendlier than usual.

## 2. Wear Bright Lipstick Instead of Dark Lipstick

![Wear Bright Lipstick Instead of Dark Lipstick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/it/un/e3rik25w5723c489663a0914169281_600x600.jpg)

If you wear dark colored lips, you'll look like a badass. However, you might look like you don't want to be bothered. That's why you should try wearing bright lipstick and pairing it with a colorful outfit. It'll make you look bubbly and ready for a conversation, even when you're not smiling and are just staring straight into the distance.

## 3. Don’t Create Eyebrows That Are Too Sharp

![Don’t Create Eyebrows That Are Too Sharp](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x6/pz/cu9jrqly5723c4f627c7d566492261_600x600.jpg)

Eyebrows are important in this day and age. However, you don't want to make them look too fierce. If they're super sharp and pointed, then you'll look a little too intense. But if you keep them rounded, it'll make your face look softer. That means people will be more willing to approach you.

## 4. Highlight Your Face in the Right Places

![Highlight Your Face in the Right Places](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s3/cz/uazut1g35723c52ef10d6842523758_600x600.jpg)

Highlighting your face is a lot simpler than contouring your face. All you have to do is make an upside down triangle underneath your eyes, make a line down your nose, and dab a little bit on your chin. When you're finished, your face will look brighter in the spots that you applied the highlighter. It'll put a little life into your look.

## 5. Add Color to Your Cheeks

![Add Color to Your Cheeks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rq/ky/vngwilj65723c5a027848494998821_600x600.jpg)

You don't want your face to look too pale. In order to fix it, all you have to do is add a little bit of color to your cheeks. Either grab some light pink blush or use your lipstick as blush, so that it matches your lips. Just make sure that the color is light and subtle. You don't want anything too dark and dramatic.

## 6. Cover up the Bags under Your Eyes

![Cover up the Bags under Your Eyes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sg/kg/s75iuqad5723c6339749e926970811_600x600.jpg)

Adding white to your inner corners might not be enough. You should also remember to cover up the bags underneath your eyes. That way, you won't look like you've had a rough night and hate the world.

## 7. Use Light Eye Makeup

![Use Light Eye Makeup](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mh/cs/mj8wlixn5723c67eee322374311167_600x683.jpg)

Smokey eyes are sexy, but you don't have to cover your lids with dark makeup every time you leave the house. If you're just going to the store or to school, then you should try using light eye makeup. Try different shades of pink or even light brown.

## 8. Don't Wing Your Liner

![eyebrow, face, cheek, hair, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ik/jq/dxhfh67e58e0f6a198a46447229772_600x600.jpg)

A fabulous trick for preventing, or at least mellowing your resting bitch face, is to stop your liner at the outter corner of your eye. This prevents an overdone look that makes your eyes look fierce. Using a lighter shade of liner also helps because it's not as harsh. Opt for a brown instead of black or even add a pop of color.

## 9. Use False Lashes

![hair, face, black hair, person, blue,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jp/q7/ifxpy95u58e0f6e42188a388123005_600x600.jpg)

Other girls who suffer from RBF swear by false lashes. They make your eyes appear wider and larger, making you look friendlier. I guess I've never really paid attention to lashes that much but I suppose I see how wider doll-like eyes could make you appear more approachable. :)

## 10. Use Shimmer

![hair, eyebrow, face, cheek, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mv/kw/rpge0j9v58e0f70c6a211646389254_600x750.jpg)

Whether it's a shimmery shadow, gloss, or even applying some shimmery bronzer, adding shimmer is another fan favorite of RBF sufferers. It's add an angelic glow to your face without being overbearing like you're trying to hard.

If you follow these makeup tips, then your face will look a little more open and welcoming, even when you're not smiling. Do you suffer from resting bitch face?
