# 7 Solutions to Your Pesky Makeup Problems ...

Don't you hate when your makeup looks like a mess? Well, that only happens when you apply it incorrectly. To be safe, follow these tips from [Beauty and Tips](http://www.beautyandtips.com/makeup/10-makeup-tips-and-tricks/) to make sure you never make mistakes with your makeup again:

## 1. How to Stop Your Eyeshadow from Creasing

![How to Stop Your Eyeshadow from Creasing](https://img.allw.mn/content/bp/ce/khdp1lxv562f76356df62307092304_500x283.gif)

It's important to apply a base before using your eyeshadow. You can use either primer, foundation, or concealer. It doesn't make much of a difference.

## 2. How to Stop Getting Lipstick on Your Teeth

![How to Stop Getting Lipstick on Your Teeth](https://img.allw.mn/content/hk/qm/gok0iahx562f7634c2ddd728716952_500x213.gif)

After you apply your lipstick, place a finger in your mouth and wrap your lips around it. Then you should slowly pull your finger out, and all of the excess product will come off with it.

## 3. How to Make Makeup Brushes Last Longer

![How to Make Makeup Brushes Last Longer](https://img.allw.mn/content/tz/in/bkc0znsi562f7636a7b7e598139076_500x281.gif)

You shouldn't be buying new brushes every week. If yours get ruined quickly, you need to clean them more often to get rid of build-up.

## 4. How to Remove Flecks of Eyeshadow

![How to Remove Flecks of Eyeshadow](https://img.allw.mn/content/t8/r4/kxlkoq9s562f7635557e4291912549_500x265.gif)

When eyeshadow falls onto your cheeks, wiping it away can rub off all of your foundation. That's why you should use a small piece of tape instead.

## 5. How to Stop Lipstick Feathering

![How to Stop Lipstick Feathering](https://img.allw.mn/content/ec/yl/dbnbnc0f562f7636390ed933946761_462x260.gif)

Just like when you're applying your eyeshadow, you need a base before you apply your lipstick. If you use a lip primer, it'll make your color last longer and stop that pesky feathering.

## 6. How to Stop Foundation from Looking Cakey

![How to Stop Foundation from Looking Cakey](https://img.allw.mn/content/q9/i1/ooecewqy54d3a800e7fa3068722653_500x279.gif)

When you apply your foundation, start at the center of your face and then move outward. That way, you won't end up with too much extra product all in the middle.

## 7. How to Make Brows Look Natural

![How to Make Brows Look Natural](https://img.allw.mn/content/ge/gl/m2d62zsx562f7635b242d060614298_360x202.gif)

Don't go too heavy with your brow pencil. Use short, quick strokes with your pencil, and make sure they're facing the same way as your hairs are growing.
