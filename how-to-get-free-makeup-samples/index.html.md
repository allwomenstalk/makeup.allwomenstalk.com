# Great Ways to Get Free Makeup Samples for Girls Wanting to Try Something New ...

Wondering **how to get free makeup samples**? If there’s one thing that all women across the world can agree on, it’s that makeup is way more expensive than we want it to be! No matter what kind of budget you are operating on, there are always those one or two items that you wish you could push the boat out for but can’t because you know you have other financial commitments to consider. What if, though, I was to tell you that you could find the money for those extra special items thanks to picking up other makeup for nothing at all? Sounds perfect, right! Here is exactly **how to get free makeup samples**.

## 1. Mail Requests

The mail is one of the best answers for **how to get free makeup samples**. If you have the time to do a little research, there are plenty of organizations and companies that will send out free makeup samples to people who request them. It isn’t a tip that is going to work 100% of the time, but the more requests you send to the widest group possible, the more chance you are going to have of getting some positive replies. As far as I’m concerned, any amount of free makeup is a win!

## 2. Store Samples

If you have the confidence to walk into a department store and inquire about their free samples, you may well be surprised at the amount of great stuff that you can walk away with. Don’t let them know that I told you, but Sephora is particularly well known for being kind to people who request free makeup samples. Go ahead, try your luck!

## 3. Test Groups

There are a number of different product test groups that you can sign up for in your spare time, and as a member, you will be treated to lots of different free samples that aren’t only limited to makeup, but obviously include a lot of beauty products! The L’Oreal Consumer Participation Panel is one that has long been renowned as a great test group to join, but there are also plenty of others on the internet to get involved with.

## 4. Facebook Giveaways

Facebook is a great place for scoping out free samples. Lots of companies that have a big presence on the platform will do multiple giveaways throughout the year, as many as a few a week if you really pay attention. It’s an easy way for them to get likes and shares, and it can end up with you having a basket full of goodies for free!
