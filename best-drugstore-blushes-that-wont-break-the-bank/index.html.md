# 7 Best Drugstore Blushes That Wont Break the Bank ...

It’s not always easy to find the best drugstore blushes that don’t cost too much. In fact, it can often be a lot of trial and error to find the best drugstore blushes. However, it doesn’t need to be, thanks to this list! The next time you’re at the drugstore, you’ll laugh in the face of the overwhelming makeup section, because you’ll be armed with a list of the best drugstore blushes!

## 1. Maybelline Dream Bouncy Blush

![Maybelline Dream Bouncy Blush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p8/zk/iw/a59w2dph_399x319.jpg)

**Buy here:** [maybelline.com](https://www.maybelline.com/face-makeup/blush-and-bronzer/fit-me-blush)

Maybelline’s Dream Bouncy Blush is something so different from any other blush on the market that it’s a must-try, even if it’s just once. Plus, with a seven-dollar price tag, it’s not exactly breaking the bank. Beyond the price, it also comes in ten shades, meaning that there’s something to match every skin tone as well as every mood! It’s so unique and versatile that I personally think it’s one of the best drugstore blushes you can try out!

## 2. E.L.F Blushes

![E.L.F Blushes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/om/qy/kt/ol9d3xub_409x386.jpg)

**Buy here:** [eyeslipsface.com](https://goo.gl/0Bc8F0)

If you love NARS blushes but don’t love the price tag, E.L.F.’s blushes are a perfect fit for you! E.L.F. is known for their NARS blush dupes, meaning that you can get the look for a fraction of the cost. This is especially great because NARS is known for their beautiful, unique blushes, making it hard to find cheaper look-a-likes. Thanks E.L.F!

## 3. Physicians Formula Happy Booster

![Physicians Formula Happy Booster](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s9/ne/s6/hv0wkhkp_300x300.jpg)

**Buy here:** [physiciansformula.com](https://www.physiciansformula.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

One of the most unique blushes on the market, and amazingly a drugstore blush at that, is Physicians Formula’s Happy Booster blush. It’s a gorgeous mix of iridescent shades that comes infused with natural ingredients that create the effect of boosting your endorphins and making you feel happier. If the shades alone don’t sell you, the “happy boosting properties” will and make this a must-try.

## 4. Maybelline Coral Burst Blush

![Maybelline Coral Burst Blush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uw/vq/yc/v31fifae_549x551.jpg)

**Buy here:** [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Maybelline really know what they’re doing when it comes to blushes, and that’s especially apparent in their Coral Burst blush. Good coral blushes aren’t exactly the easiest to find in the beauty section of your local drugstore, and Maybelline makes that search worlds easier with their Coral Burst blush. If you’re looking to try something different with a coral blush, Maybelline’s Coral Burst is the best way to go.

## 5. L’Oreal True Match Blush

_ **Buy here:** lorealparisusa.com_

If you’re not one for bright pinks and warm corals, L’Oreal’s True Match blush is the right pick for you. There are twelve hues to choose from, and they’re all natural shades that go well with any skin tone. They’re not the craziest colors, but they’re perfect for your go-to everyday makeup routine!

## 6. Maybelline Dream Mousse Blush

_ **Buy here:** maybelline.com_

Maybelline’s Dream Mousse Blush is very similar to their Dream Bouncy Blush line, but these have a different texture to them. They’re great for blending quickly and on the go, making them perfect for the days that you’re running late! Some people are reporting that this blush is no longer sold in stores, but it should be sold in most drugstores still, and if it’s not in yours, a few Google searches can fix your problem!

## 7. NYX Blush in Pinched

![NYX Blush in Pinched](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yv/vg/fl/yw1ojxep_300x300.jpg)

**Buy here:** [ulta.com](http://www.ulta.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Every blush that NYX makes is beautiful, yet there’s something very unique and different about their powder blush Pinched. It’s a dazzling blend of lighter corals and pretty pinks, with a little bit of shimmer added to the mix. It reminds me of a shade you’d see in a MAC blush collection, making it even sweeter when you realize it’s only five dollars.
