This tinted moisturizer does it all! It's available in 10 shades and includes a hint of moisturizer and SPF protection.

For those seeking a natural, dewy finish that enhances the skin's beauty, the BareMinerals COMPLEXION RESCUE is a must-try. Not only does it provide a sheer to medium coverage, but it also quenches dry skin with its marine botanicals and hydrating ingredients.
