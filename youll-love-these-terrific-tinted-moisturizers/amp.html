20 Tinted Moisturizers to Absolutely Fall in Love with ...

BareMinerals COMPLEXION RESCUE Tinted Hydrating Gel Cream • Laura Mercier Tinted Moisturizer Broad Spectrum SPF 20 • Tarte BB Tinted Treatment 12-Hour Primer • NARS Pure Radiant Tinted Moisturizer • Smashbox Camera Ready BB Cream SPF 35 • More ...

By Jennifer

If you suffer from dry skin, some foundations will only cause more dryness and irritation. That's why I love tinted moisturizer - you get a little coverage and color, and your skin stays hydrated and happy. Here are a few of my favorite tinted moisturizers... will one of them be your new best friend?

1. BareMinerals COMPLEXION RESCUE Tinted Hydrating Gel Cream

Price: $29.50 at sephora.com
This tinted moisturizer does it all! It's available in 10 shades and includes a hint of moisturizer and SPF protection.

2. Laura Mercier Tinted Moisturizer Broad Spectrum SPF 20

Price: $44 at sephora.com
This oil-free tinted moisturizer won't clog your pores, and it comes in 15 hues. It also contains a broad spectrum SPF 20 UVB/UVA sunscreen... sweet!

3. Tarte BB Tinted Treatment 12-Hour Primer

Price: $36 at sephora.com
Once again, this is a multi-tasking magic cream! It also contains broad-spectrum SPF protection, and the color is designed to last up to 12 hours.

4. NARS Pure Radiant Tinted Moisturizer

Price: $44 at sephora.com
Want your tinted moisturizer with a little sun protection and a soft glow? Pick your shade from more than a dozen colors, and give it a go.

5. Smashbox Camera Ready BB Cream SPF 35

Price: $39 at sephora.com
Always be camera-ready with this BB cream plus sunscreen, available in 8 shades.

6. Dr. Jart+ Premium Beauty Balm SPF 45

Price: $39 at sephora.com
It's a sunscreen, a tinted moisturizer, and an anti-aging balm... amazing!

7. Bobbi Brown BB Cream SPF 35

Price: $46 at sephora.com
It's surprising how well this BB cream suits so many complexions - there are only 7 shades to choose from, after all, but they all blend beautifully!

8. CLINIQUE Moisture Surge CC Cream

Price: $38 at sephora.com
This oil-free cream hides minor skin sins while providing SPF protection and hydration.

9. Perricone MD No Foundation Foundation Serum

Price: $60 at sephora.com
If you're planing to add more makeup after your tinted moisturizer, use this one - it's got foundation built in, so your makeup will last all day.

10. Diorskin Nude BB Creme

Price: $45 at sephora.com
Get a soft, natural glow with this light-weight tinted moisturizer, available in 4 natural-looking shades.

11. Boscia B.B. Cream

Price: $38 at sephora.com
Get your hint of coverage, your hydration, and your broad-spectrum sun protection, too.

12. Supergoop! CC Cream Daily Correct

Price: $32 at sephora.com
This moisturizer comes in three colors, and it helps protect your skin from the sun's damaging rays with SPF 35.

13. Giorgio Armani Luminessence CC Color Control Bright Moisturizer

Price: $55 at sephora.com
This ultra light-weight moisturizer is so natural-looking, you might forget you're wearing it, except for how flawless you look.

14. Erborian BB Crème

Price: $39 at sephora.com
This CC cream contains ginseng to help moisturize and even out skin tone, as well as SPF 20 to protect your skin from sun damage.

15. Guerlain Super Aqua-Serum BB Hydra+

Price: $79 at sephora.com
Looking for a tinted moisturizer that's ultra-luxe? This is the serum for you, then.

16. Stila Stay All Day 10-in-One HD Illuminating Beauty Balm

Price: $38 at sephora.com
Ten in one? Yes ma'am! This magical little cream provides coverage, hydration, SPF protection, and so much more, all in a one-hue-fits-all shade.

17. REN Satin Perfection BB Cream

Price: $38 at sephora.com
This BB cream is so versatile! Sure, it hydrates, and protects your skin from the sun, but it also provides an even finish. Hooray!
