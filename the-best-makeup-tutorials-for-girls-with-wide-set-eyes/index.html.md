# The Best Makeup Tutorials for Girls with Wide Set Eyes to Flip for ...

Wide-set eyes can be beautiful, but enhancing and showing them off is much easier with certain makeup tricks. Whether you love or hate your eyes, learning some new ways to make them look their best is something you’ll never regret. That’s why you need to watch each of these cool tutorials. In no time at all, you’ll have all the tips and tricks you need to have the best looking pair of peepers in town.

## 1. For Beginners

Getting started on a new makeup technique can be a bit difficult at first, but watching someone else do it first can go a long way toward helping you master it too. This easy to follow video is perfect if you are just getting started with makeup on wide set eyes, but it’s also great if you just want to take your look to a new level. Either way, this is one you’ll love.

## 2. Bring Them Together

When you have wide-set eyes, you want them to look closer together, right? This video will teach you how to do just that simply by changing the way you apply your eye shadow. The girl teaching you how to do this is a celebrity makeup artist so you know you can count on her to show you exactly what to do.

## 3. Tips and Tricks

Having several tips and tricks in your arsenal is a great way to create different looks based on where you’re going. This video will give you some great ideas so you can enhance your wide-set eyes and make them look just the way you want them to. What you’ll learn by watching this tutorial will totally change the way you do your eyes.

## 4. Summer Look

If you want to make your eyes appear a bit closer together, but don’t want to go over the top with the makeup, this is the video for you. It will teach you how to create a subtle eye makeup look that leaves you looking gorgeous for the summer, but without being so obvious that people know right away you look way different without the cosmetics.

## 5. Master the Bronze Look

Bronze eye makeup is having a definite moment right now and you can easily rock the trend on wide-set eyes. Watch this video to learn just where to distribute the color to give you the look of the moment, while also helping counteract wide-set eyes. You’re going to start turning some heads after you watch this tutorial. Yay!

## 6. Lining Wide-Set Eyes

One of the ways you can make your wide-set eyes look perfect is learning how to apply liner. Putting on eyeliner can be a hard task to get done just right, but this video will help you learn just how to do it. Follow the easy steps and you’ll have the best looking eyeliner you’ve ever seen. People are going to be so jealous!

## 7. Make Your Eyes Look Bigger

Part of the problem with wide set eyes is that they can look smaller so when you apply your eye makeup, you want to help your eyes look closer together and bigger at the same time. This video will help you do just that with easy to follow directions that take you from drab to fab in no time flat.
