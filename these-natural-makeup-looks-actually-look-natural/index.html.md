# Natural Makeup Looks That Are Actually Natural ...

There's a difference between wearing no makeup and wearing natural makeup, of course, but finding the balance is a delicate art. The natural makeup look isn't for everyone, and that's cool – but if you want a natural look that actually looks natural, unlike the faux natural looks you so often see in pictures, I think these gorgeous Instagrammers can offer up a few lovely examples.

## 1. A Hint of Red Lip

![clothing, dress, gown, sleeve, hairstyle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tj/c4/bzulx2ki58c95a2b178f2822279286_600x600.jpg)

This is the perfect gloss of color. Look how the flush of red on the lips brightens everything else.

## 2. A Blush of Pink

![hair, human hair color, face, eyebrow, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ip/bz/vbhcys5t58c9598332dcf511195375_600x749.jpg)

A pinky-brown or pinky-beige or pinky-peach shade works every time.

## 3. Light on the Eye Shadow

![hair, black hair, clothing, eyebrow, brown hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gc/vh/yp1y7djp58c957cb66d80664831636_600x600.jpg)

Just a brush of subtle color, really.

## 4. Almost No Makeup

![hair, face, eyebrow, nose, hairstyle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zo/j9/qgue41aq58c957713f494556471602_600x749.jpg)

Keyword almost. Light coverage and mascara can make a lot of difference.

## 5. Just the Lashes

![hair, face, nose, black hair, eyebrow,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rt/ow/pvsxqh5h58c956e50ab05582786516_600x749.jpg)

You can also go with just one bold statement – like your eyelashes.

## 6. Use Your Glasses

![eyewear, glasses, hair, vision care, face,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l3/gm/vukw34bl58c95a88a43ea657461617_600x611.jpg)

Larger frames can hide all kinds of things. Focus on your lips, your brows, and your hair. By the way, I am experiencing hair envy.

## 7. Some Subtle Color Blending

![hair, face, black hair, clothing, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ic/tb/ai31kh6058c9580f95826966858879_600x600.jpg)

Keep it light and blend it well. It's that simple.

## 8. Find the Light

![hair, human action, clothing, person, photography,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tz/b4/o4uhlemf58c95b8eeaf42668550898_600x729.jpg)

Dewy skin, glossy lips, and lightly flushed cheeks – perfect!

## 9. Focus on Your Eyes

![hair, face, black hair, eyebrow, clothing,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kw/nc/s737tu7o58c95b26bfe99981787177_600x749.jpg)

Make your eyes and/or eyebrows the focal point, then keep everything else soft and natural.

## 10. Go for the Gloss

![hair, face, person, woman, black hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wa/eh/rjvv5pyy58c95a4708dde224795973_600x749.jpg)

Oh, this is so gorgeous. Add a little liner on your lower lids and you're good to go.

## 11. Step out with Long but Natural-looking Lashes

![face, eyebrow, cheek, nose, beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lq/nf/u2aysolp58c95ac14f3ae735865911_600x749.jpg)

Here's a different take on the eyelash tip. Wow, those lashes ...

## 12. Embrace Your Glow

![hair, black hair, face, eyebrow, person,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rw/m4/w2ng40d458c95907f1c2c024200333_600x600.jpg)

Everything about this is fabulous. She is serving and making it look effortless.

## 13. Try a Little Light Contouring

![hair, face, facial expression, person, eyebrow,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e0/ci/xrn96noo58c9594b80a14845037327_600x600.jpg)

Honestly, if you really do keep it light, you can pull off a natural look. Focus on your cheekbones, just like this beauty.

## 14. Take Inspiration from Another Era

![hair, human hair color, face, black hair, eyebrow,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ge/w3/csyegvi358c95c04384b7322890876_600x600.jpg)

## 15. Be a Little Bold with Your Lipstick

![hair, human hair color, face, eyebrow, blond,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ve/cx/qxuqiz5z58c95840bb7e1459837728_600x749.jpg)

This peachy shade is so … ripe!

## 16. Create a Bit of a Blush

![hair, face, clothing, black hair, photography,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gg/d3/pdx591sy58c959ce8512c692767845_600x749.jpg)

A light brush of blush on your cheeks will brighten your eyes and give your face definition.

## 17. Just a Little Liner

![hair, human hair color, black hair, brown, face,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s6/i7/v63qsb4458c95a74bb5dc193723796_600x749.jpg)

Well, and some light coverage, too, of course.

How do you define your own natural look? I stick with blush, mascara, and a light dusting of powder foundation, myself. Okay, and the eyebrows. Always with the eyebrows.
