# 7 Tips for Wearing Coloured Mascara ...

**How to wear coloured mascara:** this knowledge can instantly add a ‘wow’ factor to your look. Despite having a reputation as one of those tacky makeup fads of the 80s and 90s, coloured mascara has been popping up on the runways in recent seasons. It can be a fun way to incorporate colour into your makeup routine. Coloured mascara can be tricky to master so I’m all for the idea that the more tips on how to wear coloured mascaras, the better. Check out the following wearing coloured mascara how-to.

## 1. Assess Your Eye Colour

One of the key tips in pulling off the coloured mascara look is to choose a shade that complements your eye colour. Generally speaking, purple shades suit brown and green eyes, blue and yellow-based shades look great with blue eyes, and green shades work well with hazel eyes. This is definitely one of those things you have to consider when **it comes to how to wear coloured mascara** successfully.

## 2. Choose Complementary Colours

Avoid matching your coloured mascara to your eyeshadow. This can make your eyes look too busy. Instead, choose complementary colours as they will make your eyes pop a bit more. For example, if you’re wearing peachy pink shades on your eyes go for a mascara in a green hue. Consulting a colour wheel may prove helpful!

## 3. Ditch the Eyeliner

Well, you don’t have to ditch your eyeliner completely for this wearing coloured mascara how-to. The general idea is to keep a natural looking lash line as possible, to really make the coloured mascara pop. A boldly lined eye will only compete with the coloured mascara. If you do want to wear eyeliner, apply a thin line as close to the lash line as possible.

## 4. Layer It on

The whole point of coloured mascara is, well, colour! Don’t be afraid to layer on the colour. When wearing coloured mascara, your eyes will be the focal point, so you really want the colour to show.

## 5. Colour at the Tips

Take a subtler approach by applying coloured mascara to the tips of your eyelashes. This wearing mascara how-to is perfect for those who are still not ready for the full impact of coloured lashes. Apply a black or dark brown mascara to the length of your lashes, wait for it to dry, and then swipe a coloured mascara to the tips.

## 6. Half Half

For a look that’s a little bit unexpected, try applying coloured mascara to your bottom eyelashes only. Apply your usual black or dark brown mascara to your top lashes and swipe on some colour on your lower lashes

## 7. Keep It Simple

Coloured mascara can be quite a dramatic look, so try and keep the rest of your makeup as natural and fresh-looking as possible. When looking at **how to wear coloured mascara, a** neutral eye, natural flush and glossy lip is all that’s needed for the rest of your face.

Wearing coloured mascara can be a great alternative to wearing coloured eyeshadow. Hopefully this **wearing coloured mascara how-to will inspire** you to experiment with this trend. Do you have any tips to add when it comes to how to wear coloured mascara? Or is this a makeup trend that you’ll be staying well away from?
