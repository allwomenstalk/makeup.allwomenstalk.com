# 7 Things Every Makeup Addict Understands ...

Some of us are obsessed with makeup. We just can't help it. But that's okay, because _Cambio_ knows what the life of a makeup addict is like:

## 1. You'll Spend Your Last Dollar on a New Makeup Release

![You'll Spend Your Last Dollar on a New Makeup Release](https://img.allw.mn/content/w5/vs/eeqerp2e5671de269a327855105726_500x209.gif)

You can't be the only person around without the new lip kit. It'll drive you crazy.

## 2. There's No Such Thing as Last Minute Plans

![There's No Such Thing as Last Minute Plans](https://img.allw.mn/content/p8/oi/skz86fz45671de26d18b1909418007_479x227.gif)

You need plenty of time to do your makeup before you leave the house.

## 3. You'll Extend Your Day if Your Makeup is Beat

![You'll Extend Your Day if Your Makeup is Beat](https://img.allw.mn/content/yn/z9/h002y0pd5671de2720eca307359554_500x279.gif)

If your makeup looks amazing, you won't want to go to sleep. You'll want to go out and show off your fabulous look.

## 4. After Shopping for New Products, It'll Take Days to Get All Those Swatches off Your Hand

![After Shopping for New Products, It'll Take Days to Get All Those Swatches off Your Hand](https://img.allw.mn/content/rj/ba/lewq1d885671de769ba6a860792743_320x180.gif)

You don't mind it though, because you have to try them all out to see what they look like.

## 5. That Soul Crushing Feeling when Your Eyeliner Doesn't Match up

![That Soul Crushing Feeling when Your Eyeliner Doesn't Match up](https://img.allw.mn/content/tf/n1/h19gq6gq5671de27cd26b915265708_504x284.gif)

Even though you're obsessed with makeup, it doesn't mean you can do it perfectly. It's impossible to make both eyes match.

## 6. When Your Favorite Product Breaks

![When Your Favorite Product Breaks](https://img.allw.mn/content/gq/gx/o1fsr1885671de2868858221897557_500x237.gif)

If the tip of your lipstick breaks, you'll feel like you lost a best friend.

## 7. Having to Take Your Makeup off at Night

![Having to Take Your Makeup off at Night](https://img.allw.mn/content/ws/oc/moz8ussg5671de2885325603732714_500x286.gif)

You have so many products on your face that it takes a while to wipe them all away.

Are you a makeup addict?
