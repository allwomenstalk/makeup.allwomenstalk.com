# Top 9 Urban Decay Products I Love ...

Urban Decay is one of those brands that just clicks with me, which is why I came up with a list of the top Urban Decay products that look amazing on! Do you have like Urban Decay? Do you have a top Urban Decay products list in your head? Who knows, maybe our lists will match! So ladies, without any further comments, let's talk about my top Urban Decay products list below!

I am a huge fan of any and all top Urban Decay products that have a pop-up story with them. That's exactly what this playful and cute eyeshadow palette has with it! When you open it up, it has the glittering and shimmering lights of New York City along with 16 different and versatile eyeshadows! This is definitely a product that you should check out!

![Urban Decay Naked Palette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/05/2_urban-decay-naked-palette_250x190.jpg)

**Price:** $40.99 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
One of the top Urban Decay products that is very well-known is the naked palette of eyeshadows. I've seen this palette featured all over the place and it's actually an incredibly well put together palette of eyeshadow colors. It's got everything from taupe all the way to some deeper grays!

## 3. Urban Decay Eyeshadow Primer Potion

![Urban Decay Eyeshadow Primer Potion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/05/3_urban-decay-eyeshadow-primer-potion_250x250.jpg)

**Price:** $17.00 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Have you been looking for a way for your eyeshadow and liner not to move at all during the day? Well, this eyeshadow primer is it! It's an incredible mix and it really works! It prevents eyeshadow creasing, blends flawlessly and will ensure that your eyeshadow color stays fully in place! It's by far one of the top Urban Decay products that I use all of the time!

![Urban Decay Ammo Eye Palette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/05/4_urban-decay-ammo-eye-palette_250x250.jpg)

As you can probably tell, Urban Decay as a brand is very well-known for their eyeshadow palettes. They seem to put together not just the best colors, but also the most incredible palettes of colors. Take this one, it has everything from deep forest green all the way to sea blue! It's quite pretty!

![Urban Decay Wicked Lipstick Limited Edition](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/05/5_urban-decay-wicked-lipstick-limited-edition_250x190.jpg)

**Price:** $17.99 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 6. Urban Decay Mariposa Palette

![Urban Decay Mariposa Palette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/05/6_urban-decay-mariposa-palette_250x250.jpg)

**Price:** $39.00 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Are you looking to brighten up your eyes this spring? Are you dreaming about colors of bright blue and vibrant pink? Well, this incredible eyeshadow palette has them all!

## 7. Urban Decay De-Slick Mattifying Powder

![Urban Decay De-Slick Mattifying Powder](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/05/7_urban-decay-de-slick-mattifying-powder_250x209.jpg)

**Price:** $79.50 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Another top Urban Decay product that I love is all about stopping the shine that constantly seems to pop up in the middle of the day. This pressed-powder keeps all of the oil at bay and looks great over any and all foundation! The reason why it keeps oil away? It's super lightweight and it's got some oil-absorbing rice powder as an ingredient in it! How clever!

## 8. Urban Decay De-Slick in a Tube Mattifying Gel

![Urban Decay De-Slick in a Tube Mattifying Gel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/05/8_urban-decay-de-slick-in-a-tube-mattifying-gel_250x250.jpg)

**Price:** $59.50 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you are looking for something that works when you aren't wearing makeup, why not use a lightweight gel? This incredible gel will keep all of the oil away from your face and it also helps to tighten up your pores. That means your entire face will look more youthful and will glow!

## 9. Urban Decay All Nighter Long-Lasting Makeup Setting Spray

![Urban Decay All Nighter Long-Lasting Makeup Setting Spray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/05/9_urban-decay-all-nighter-long-lasting-makeup-setting-spray_250x250.jpg)

**Price:** $12.00 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Finally, the last top Urban Decay product that I love is a finishing spray. Let's say that you want to dance the night away, but constantly worry about your makeup setting. After your look is on, just a few spritzes of this and you'll be all set for a night of dancing – and you won't have to worry about your makeup!

Urban Decay is one of those brands that goes deeper than just makeup. These top Urban Decay products were designed to not only make your makeup stay put, but the eyeshadow palettes are irresistible! So ladies, now it's time for you to share your top Urban Decay products list!
