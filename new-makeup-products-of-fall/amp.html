10 New Makeup Products of Fall 2011 ...

Charlotte Ronson All Eye Need Eye Shadow Palette • Tarte Lip Service LipSurgence Collection • Lancome French Touch Absolu • Bobbi Brown Skin Foundation Mineral Makeup • Tarte SmolderEYES Amazonian Clay Waterproof Liner Collection • More ...

By Lyndsie

New Makeup Products are my favorite thing. I love trying something new, especially when it's seasonal – and since fall in my favorite season, you'd be correct in assuming that I'm currently spending way too much money on new makeup products. But that's okay, what the Better Half doesn't know won't hurt 'er, right? Seriously, there are tons of gorgeous new makeup looks this season, so if you're ready to clean out your current kit and find something brand new, check out these new makeup products for fall 2011!

1. Charlotte Ronson All Eye Need Eye Shadow Palette

Price: $22.00 at sephora.com
This is one of the must-have new makeup products for fall because of the color base. All those neutral browns, tans, bronzes, and creams lend themselves well to the fall trend of a nude, natural look. The idea is to get a little color without going overboard, and this is perfect!

2. Tarte Lip Service LipSurgence Collection

Price: $29.00 at sephora.com
Not only is this awesome because it's a $120 value for a tiny fraction of the full price, but the colors are to die for. More than that, they again follow one of the prevailing trends for the season. Pink lips of all shades are all the rage, and with this set, you'll get every luscious shade you could want.

3. Lancome French Touch Absolu

Price: $35.00 at sephora.com
Among the new makeup products you buy for fall, you have to have red lipsticks. Specifically, wine red shades are making their way from the runway to the streets. With Lancome's lipstick, you might pay a little more, but you're getting a high quality, longer lasting color.

4. Bobbi Brown Skin Foundation Mineral Makeup

Price: $38.00 at sephora.com
To keep up with the previously mentioned natural look, you have to have a good foundation. This powder foundation also has SPF 15, and that's important even in the cooler autumn climates. Perfect for the natural look, this covers blemishes without being too heavy.

5. Tarte SmolderEYES Amazonian Clay Waterproof Liner Collection

Price: $39.00 at sephora.com
Think that's a lot for eyeliner? Not so! You get six shades with this collection and, best of all, they come in bright colors. That's a must for fall's new makeup products, because colorful eye makeup is surging in popularity.

6. Sephora Collection Color Flip

Price: $15.00 at sephora.com
This palette is a must have this season due to all the colors it contains. Remember, bright eye makeup is coming in big this season. Don't be afraid of pinks, blues, greens, and even oranges.

7. Dior Backstage Brow Design Brow Shaping Stencils Kit

Price: $40.00 at sephora.com
Since bold, dramatic eyebrows are making a comeback, this stenciling kit by Dior is definitely one of the necessary new makeup products this season. A bit expensive, sure, but with everything you get, it's well worth the price. No matter what kind of arch you like, no matter what color your brows, the kit has absolutely everything you need!

8. Urban Decay Mariposa Palette

Price: $39.00 at sephora.com
Urban Decay is known for bold color choices and dramatic palettes. This one, with shades of pink, brown, purple, peacock blue, and more, will take care of all your color needs this season. Don't be afraid to go bold!

9. Dior DiorShow Waterproof Liner

Price: $29.00 at sephora.com
Dark, dramatic, winged, Egyptian, or smudged, bold eyeliner is another popular look this season. Whatever other new makeup products you buy, make sure you get a good eyeliner! Liquid or pencil, you need something that will help you get the dramatic effect on your pretty peepers!

10. Lancome Hypnose Doll Lashes Mascara

Price: $25.00 at sephora.com
Last but most certainly not least, lush, long, curling lashes are in this autumn. If you don't want to start wearing falsies, then get Lancome's new mascara. It's guaranteed to plump your lashes so much they look false!

I hope y'all love these new makeup products as much as I do. Of course, buying new makeup products is always a risky business. Make sure you're choosing something that you know will look good with your skin tone and coloring. However, if you want to get a little bold, don't be afraid to try one of the funkier products listed here either. Remember, variety is the spice of life! What are some new makeup products you want to try this fall?
