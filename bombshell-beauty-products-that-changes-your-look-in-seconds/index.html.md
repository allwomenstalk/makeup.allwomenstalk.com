# 7 Bombshell Beauty Products That Changes Your Look in Seconds ...

If you’re ready to spice up your makeup for the summer or for a special occasion, there are some genius beauty products that can help you get the job done beautifully. Sometimes all it takes is one beauty product to instantly wake up your look. The problem is finding those one or two products that are going to take your look to the next level in just a few steps. I understand this common beauty issue very well so I’m going to show you 7 excellent beauty products that are going to take your look from basic to bombshell in seconds!

## 1. Benefit They’re Real Push-up Liner

![Benefit They’re Real Push-up Liner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zt/o5/li8g1540_600x798.jpg)

This is one of the most highly anticipated beauty product releases to date and from what I hear, there’s good reason for all the hype. This innovative eyeliner has combined all the benefits of gel liner without all the muss and fuss of the pots and eyeliner brush. It’s super long-wearing, won’t dry out, the cutting-edge tip promises a simple, precise line and the soft tip makes it easy to get as close as humanly possible to the lash line, making lashes appear thicker and fuller. This eyeliner makes it easy and quick to create a range of looks in a snap. Available at Sephora for $24.

## 2. Make up for Ever Aqua Brow Kit

![Make up for Ever Aqua Brow Kit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nz/jv/uj03fr4c_450x448.jpg)

If your weekend plans include some time at the pool or beach, make sure you pick up this handy dandy brow kit. This three-piece kit includes all you need to create well-defined, natural-looking brows that won’t fade or budge in the water. MUFE’s Aqua Brow gel is highly rated for its ability to fill in, define and lengthen your brows. With this kit you can look like you were born with bold, beautiful brows. Available at Sephora for $38.

## 3. Laneige Complexion Correction BB Cushion

![Laneige Complexion Correction BB Cushion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ed/vv/haca57ug_410x391.jpg)

I love BB creams but hauling around a tube of liquid isn’t always convenient, especially when you’re traveling or you want to do touch-ups. Laneige created this genius BB cream that you can apply with a patented air cushion so there’s no mess and you don’t apply any more than you need. BB creams are awesome for evening out your skin tone, providing sun protection, reducing dark spots and minimizing shine. Available at Target for $34.

## 4. NYX Honey Dew Me up Primer

![NYX Honey Dew Me up Primer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ts/zy/lmx0fhg0_600x565.jpg)

Aside from beauty products, what goes underneath our makeup also makes a huge difference in how we look. NYX’s latest primer aptly called Honey Dew Me Up contains gold flecks, honey and collagen to counteract the appearance of redness and discoloration while helping skin look radiant and fresh all day. Use it alone for those no makeup days or under your favorite foundation. Available at NYXCosmetics.com for $17.

## 5. E.l.f. Studio Blush Palette

![E.l.f. Studio Blush Palette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ab/vx/i696iq2c_600x756.jpg)

If you own multiple shades of blush, investing in a blush palette is a much more economical and easier to carry around than toting around a bunch of blush singles. Apart from being convenient, having a blush palette makes it easy to create a custom color or use it contour and sculpt your cheeks. E.l.f’s Studio Palette is only $6 and you get four stunning shades of blush that comes in both light and dark palettes. Available at EyesLipsFace.com for $6.

## 6. Urban Decay Naked Skin Body Beauty Balm

![Urban Decay Naked Skin Body Beauty Balm](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zj/wr/ek5f0csj_450x448.jpg)

Summertime usually means pool parties, nights out, BBQs and lots of outdoor activities, so we have to make sure our skin is looking its best. Instead of slathering on just any old moisturizer, pick up some of Urban Decay’s Naked Skin Body Beauty Balm that’ll help blur imperfections, help firm up skin and leave a sexy, all-over glow. This non-greasy, lightweight formula will help ensure that your skin looks luminous and healthy no matter where you are. Available at Sephora for $32.

## 7. Sally Hansen Miracle Gel Nail Polish

![Sally Hansen Miracle Gel Nail Polish](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kq/nq/popy81tz_600x817.jpg)

Gel nail polishes are awesome because they look amazing and last so much longer than regular nail polish. The downside is that you need a lamp and polish removal can be kind of a pain. Sally Hansen has created somewhat of a miracle beauty product and made a gel polish that doesn’t require a lamp, comes off easy like regular nail polish and it’s a simple two-step process. And get this, there are 47 striking shades in this collection so you have plenty of colors to choose from. Top coat and gel polish available at Walgreens for $9.99 each.

Look like the bombshell you are with these kickass beauty products! These products will help you change your look in mere seconds plus, experimenting with new makeup and beauty products is always fun. Do you have any go-to products you use when you want to jazz up your makeup?
