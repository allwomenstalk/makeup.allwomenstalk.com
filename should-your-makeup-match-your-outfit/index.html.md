# Should Your Makeup Match Your Outfit?

Beauty is definitely in the eye of the beholder but while makeup trends have come and gone, matching your makeup to your outfit has always been a good idea. Whether it be with a subtle eye shadow or bold lip color, coordinating your look can earn you some serious style points. I mean, if you're wearing a classy and elegant outfit, your makeup should have the same feel to it, right? Take some inspiration from these gorgeous celebrities:

## 1. Elle Fanning

![Elle Fanning](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mg/m3/nckc7jn0_600x900.jpg)

Elle took her look to the next level by coordinating both her eyeshadow and lip stick to her floral gown.

## 2. Emma Stone

![Emma Stone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n9/vp/uw9typ5l_600x900.jpg)

Pretty in peach! Emma's monochromatic beauty look is everything.

## 3. Katy Perry

![Katy Perry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v3/l0/z0imntbu_600x900.jpg)

Katy matched her sparkling dress with some green and purple eyeshadow.

## 4. Selena Gomez

![Selena Gomez](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b7/j9/t6bcd4jz_600x900.jpg)

Selena went for a moody vibe with a oxblood red lip stain, matching her dark gown.

## 5. Emmy Rossum

![Emmy Rossum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v0/fm/qxx7bqlx_600x900.jpg)

Emmy sure knows how to bring attention to those big brown eyes!

## 6. Joan Smalls

![Joan Smalls](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rv/er/qe05je12_600x900.jpg)

When done right, you can steer an all-black look into the 'chic' territory and out of the 'goth' one.

## 7. Lupita Nyong'o

![Lupita Nyong'o](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/an/vy/theqp790_600x900.jpg)

Lupita knows how to make turquoise work hard for a great effect.

## 8. Taylor Swift

![Taylor Swift](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yc/vv/neimcsu0_600x850.jpg)

You can never go wrong with bold red lip to perfectly match a red dress..

## 9. Jennifer Lawrence

![Jennifer Lawrence](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/du/r3/qcmvkj8s_600x932.jpg)

J.Law was a golden babe at The Hunger Games movie premiere.
