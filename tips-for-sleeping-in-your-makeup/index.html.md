# Tips for Sleeping in Your Makeup ...

You shouldn't make a habit of sleeping in your makeup. However, Kim K does it all the time, and she knows if you're going to do it, you might as well do it right. So take some advice from [Marie Claire](https://www.marieclaire.com/beauty/news/a17150/tips-for-sleeping-in-your-makeup/) and do these things:

## 1. Set Your Makeup with Loose Powder

[https://media.giphy.com/media/gmHYv3SVxRdmM/giphy.gif](https://media.giphy.com/media/gmHYv3SVxRdmM/giphy.gif)
This way, your makeup will last longer.

## 2. Skip Your Normal Skincare Routine

[https://media.giphy.com/media/4ZcDctB90D16w/giphy.gif](https://media.giphy.com/media/4ZcDctB90D16w/giphy.gif)
Moisturizer, face oils, and eye cream will ruin your makeup, so don't use them if you plan on sleeping in your makeup.

## 3. Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

![Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase](https://img.allw.mn/content/xi/ti/igqylc745656182a70c91140852932_500x282.gif)

This way, you won't get as many breakouts.

## 4. Lay Mummy-style

![Lay Mummy-style](https://img.allw.mn/content/zb/ff/z01yqqnr5656182ac51d1838274217_500x229.gif)

Try to sleep on your back, so that your makeup doesn't rub off on your pillow and sheets.

## 5. Refresh Your Makeup in the AM

[https://media.giphy.com/media/i6hhAddlCU2C4/giphy.gif](https://media.giphy.com/media/i6hhAddlCU2C4/giphy.gif)
Spray some skin mist on your face and touchup the mascara that has faded away overnight.

## 6. Don't Wear the Same Face of Makeup for More than 18 Hours

[https://media.giphy.com/media/uMxlQ4Th8jYHu/giphy.gif](https://media.giphy.com/media/uMxlQ4Th8jYHu/giphy.gif)
Your skin needs to breathe to stay healthy, you know.

It's not healthy to sleep in your makeup. However, if you're going to do it every once in a while, make sure you follow these tips.
