# How do I Make My Makeup Last All Day ?

Are you tired of your makeup lasting no more than two hours on your skin? Do you ask yourself every day **how do I make my makeup last all day?**

The solution is very easy. Just follow these easy tips and you will not have to worry any more about the staying power of your makeup. No more moaning to your friends about **how to make my makeup last all day.**

## 1. Exfoliate Your Face

This will remove the dead skin from your face and help you avoid having cakey foundation. My favorite scrub is "Clear Skin Professional" from Avon as it cleans deeply and is affordable. One of the best answers for **how do I make my makeup last all day** is to exfoliate first.

## 2. Use a Moisturizer

Obviously for dry skin, moisturizer will keep your skin hydrated during the day and will not make your foundation crease. If you have oily or combination skin, try to find a moisturizer made for that skin type. Be sure to apply it about 30 minutes before your makeup so it can soak in.

## 3. Use a Primer

Primer is very important because it will help your foundation stick to your skin. It also creates a barrier so it will be harder for the foundation to sink inside your pores. Be sure to choose a primer that matches your skin type.

## 4. Foundation by Skin Type

Again, choose a foundation designed for your skin type. You also want one that contains sunscreen. If you have oily skin your foundation should have a matte finish. If you have dry skin, look for a foundation with hydrating ingredients.

## 5. Use a Translucent Powder

A translucent powder does not have any pigment, so you can apply it all over your foundation to set it in place. Be sure to give your powder about 2 minutes to set your foundation before you touch your face.

## 6. Use a Setting Spray

Why? Because setting spray will set the makeup and make it last all day long. After the setting spray, put on your highlighter and you're good to go.

If you follow these tips, I promise that your makeup will last all day long. Try it and let me know how it went in the comments.
