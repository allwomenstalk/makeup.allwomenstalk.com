# 9 Waterproof Eye Liners ...

A waterproof liner is an essential item to have in your makeup kit. Invest in a quality liner to (hopefully) avoid any instances of running and streaky makeup. A basic black or brown liner is a staple, while coloured liners and glitter formulas are great for creating fun party looks. The following are just a range of waterproof eye liners that you may want to try out.

## 1. Benefit BADgal Liner

![Benefit BADgal Liner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/im/kp/wovllcie54c927c23f36a217115722_250x246.jpg)

**Price:** $20.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This eye liner glides on smoothly and is designed not to smudge or smear. It has a waterproof formula and is long lasting. This liner has a pencil tip at one end and a blending tip on the other. The blending tip is great for achieving a smoky eye look.

## 2. Stila Stay All Day Waterproof Liquid Eye Liner

![Stila Stay All Day Waterproof Liquid Eye Liner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n0/h4/nm3nl2s254c927c2e3de5300442002_250x247.jpg)

**Price:** $20.00 at [sephora.com](https://goo.gl/HHtFhb)
This liquid eye liner is designed for precision. This eye liner pen has a marker-like tip which can deliver anything from thin precise lines to bold dramatic strokes. Like most waterproof eye liners, this one has a long-lasting formulation. This liner is available in black, indigo, and brown colours.

## 3. Smashbox ‘Limitless’ Liquid Liner Pen

![Smashbox ‘Limitless’ Liquid Liner Pen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2011/06/smashbox_326x500.jpg)

**Price:** $22.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Achieve a smooth and controlled application with this liquid liner pen. It has a long-wearing formula and comes in both black and brown colours. It has a precision-tip pen for easy application. Use this liquid liner to create the perfect winged liner look.

## 4. Barry M Intense Waterproof Eye Liner

![Barry M Intense Waterproof Eye Liner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ev/al/qjru7pzr54c927c3a199d934789834_235x243.jpg)

**Price:** $7.39 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/lNJoJh)
This ‘intense’ eye liner has a creamy formula. It comes in a standard black colour and has a waterproof and long-wearing formula. This eye liner pencil has a retractable tip which is quite clever. This eye liner would be a great item to have in your daily makeup bag.

## 5. Clinique Brush-on Cream Liner

![Clinique Brush-on Cream Liner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2011/06/clinique-liner_500x297.jpg)

**Price:** $15.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Unlike the other waterproof eye liners on this list, this one has a brush-on style cream formula. The liner comes in a tub with accompanying mini eye liner brush. This liner has a long wearing and waterproof formula. This liner is available in black, brown, and grey shades.

## 6. Topshop Semi Permanent Liner in Infinite

![Topshop Semi Permanent Liner in Infinite](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2011/06/liner_500x367.jpg)

**Price:** $16.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/vSuUnV)
This eye liner has a subtle metallic finish. It comes in a gunmetal grey colour and would be a great alternative to the more common matte blacks and browns. It comes with an ultra fine brush for precise application. To remove this liner, simply use waterproof eye makeup remover.

## 7. Dior Crayon Eyeliner

![Dior Crayon Eyeliner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k2/sm/s8rbzczw54c927c46137b592211715_250x247.jpg)

**Price:** $28.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This crayon eyeliner features a pencil tip at one end and blending tip at the other. The cream formula is designed to glide on effortlessly and to provide advanced matte coverage. Line your lashline with the crayon tip, and use the foam tip to blend. This eyeliner is available in a range of colours.

## 8. Sephora Collection Flashy Liner

![Sephora Collection Flashy Liner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c6/eq/t2ba5nht54c927c512174803015310_250x248.jpg)

**Price:** $4.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These Sephora Collection waterproof eye liners come in a range of bright colours. There’s a pink lavender colour and even a bright ‘leaf green’. They have a creamy formula and metallic finish. Add some playful colour to your makeup routine with these colourful liners.

## 9. Too Faced Liquif-Eye

![Too Faced Liquif-Eye](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kh/zr/rn40anr754c927c5bd3dd829270744_250x232.jpg)

**Price:** $18.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This clear liquid formulation has the potential to transform any eye shadow into a waterproof liquid liner. To use it, dip the brush tip into your desired eye shadow colour and apply as you would liquid liner. The resulting stroke of ‘liner’ is also designed to be waterproof and smudge proof. Just remember to clean the brush tip after each use.

These waterproof eye liners come in a range of formulas, but can all achieve similar effects. Simply choose the one that best suits you. Have you used any of these waterproof eye liners? Or are there any that you’d recommend?
