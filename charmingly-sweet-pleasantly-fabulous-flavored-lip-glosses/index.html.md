# 9 Charmingly Sweet & Pleasantly Fabulous Flavored Lip Glosses ...

Flavored Lip Glosses are something that every woman should have. I know that I have a ton of different flavored lip glosses that smell and taste amazing! When I was going through my flavored lip glosses, I realized that there are a ton of new scents and tastes out there that I haven't experienced yet. That's what inspired this post! Ladies, below, I'm going to detail out the top 9 sweet smelling, great tasting and awesome flavored lip glosses that I know you'll love!

## 1. Caramel Apple

_ **Price:** $10.00 at philosophy.com_
Caramel apple is totally one of my favorite scents and my favorite foods. When falls rolls in, immediately I hit the store for some caramel apples. This is one of my favorite flavored lip glosses because it tastes just like you've eaten a caramel apple – without the calories!

## 2. Cinnamon Buns

_ **Price:** $10.00 at philosophy.com_
If you are a huge cinnamon bun fan, this is totally the gloss for you! Not only does it leave your lips smelling and tasting like a fresh baked cinnamon bun, but it'll leave 'em silky and shiny too! I've totally tried this one and my lips were super shiny and totally tasty – definitely worth a try!

## 3. Candy Cane

_ **Price:** $10.00 at philosophy.com_
Although I absolutely love fall, it's my favorite time of year, I can't wait for winter to roll in with all of the holiday treats! Candy canes are actually one of my favorite tastes and this is one of the flavored lip glosses that gets the taste/scent right every single time! The best part about it? My lips are super shiny after I use it. It's high-gloss and has a sweet peppermint taste – what could be better?

## 4. Frosted Animal Cracker

_ **Price:** $10.00 at philosophy.com_
The people at Philosophy really stretch the walls of the box and really think outside of it when they are coming up with their flavor selections. I never would've thought to slip into some Frosted Animal Cracker flavored lip gloss, but I own this one and it's awesome! It tastes just like the cookies and leaves my lips totally hydrated – what more can I ask for?

## 5. Vanilla Birthday Cake

_ **Price:** $10.00 at philosophy.com_
For some reason, I think that food flavored lip glosses seem to be the best choice – probably because they taste so good! This is another one of my favorites. It tastes and smells JUST like fresh vanilla cake right from the oven. You'll be licking your lips all day with this lip gloss!

## 6. Sprinkled Funnel Cake

_ **Price:** $10.00 at philosophy.com_
Face it, funnel cakes are high in calories and filled with all kinds of fat – so why not skip the calories and go for the taste? This awesome tasting/smelling little concoction is just what I need to get me through the carnival food cravings I get! The added bonus is that you can even taste the sugary sprinkles too!

## 7. Raspberry Sorbet

_ **Price:** $10.00 at philosophy.com_
If you are feeling a little fruity and looking for flavored lip glosses that are fruit-filled, this is the one! It tastes exactly like you're eating raspberry sorbet without any of the calories. Dig in ladies!

## 8. Chewy Chocolate Lollipop

_ **Price:** $10.00 at philosophy.com_
If you're a chocolate lover – this is totally the lip gloss for you! It tastes just like you're chewing on a chewy chocolate lollipop and it's delicious. Seriously ladies, I brought this the other day and I haven't stopped putting it on my lips all day!

## 9. Candy Corn

_ **Price:** $10.00 at philosophy.com_
Whenever I see candy corn lining the shelves of my local Rite Aid, I totally know that fall is coming. Little did I know that the folks at Philosophy cosmetics were brewing up their own form of candy corn … in lip gloss! This tastes just like the sugary-sweet candy that makes my fall days awesome!

Finding the right flavored lip glosses is important – after all, who knows who you are gonna be kissing! These are a few of my favorite flavored lip glosses that I totally couldn't live without. What about you ladies? What lip gloss flavors do you absolutely love?
