# 9 Pretty Multipurpose Makeup Palettes to Try ...

There are lots of pretty makeup palettes to choose from but the best ones are the type that offer you a variety of makeup. One makeup palette can offer you everything from lip gloss, eye shadow and blush all in a pretty little package that you can tote anywhere you want! If you’re the type of gal who likes all of her makeup in a convenient package, try out one of these pretty makeup palettes on for size!

## 1. Too Faced Sweet Indulgence Palette

![Too Faced Sweet Indulgence Palette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/12/1_too-faced-sweet-indulgence-palette_248x200.jpg)

Too Faced is well known for their super pretty makeup palettes and I think they outdid themselves with this one! The Sweet Indulgence palette contains eye shadows, blush, bronzer, highlighter, a double-ended makeup brush and eye primer all in a cute tin! This might not be the type of thing you want to throw in your purse but it’s great to have when you’re traveling or applying makeup on-the-go!

## 2. Bare Minerals Fired up Set

![Bare Minerals Fired up Set](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/12/2_bare-minerals-fired-up-set_248x184.jpg)

Our next makeup palette is not only pretty to look at but pretty to wear, too! The Fired Up set contains eye shadow, blush, eye primer, waterproof eye liner, lip gloss, a blush brush, eye shadow applicator and comes in a dazzling gold snakeskin clutch! This palette is literally a one-stop shop for all things beauty so you can tuck it into your purse or use the mirrored lid to apply makeup while you’re out and about!

## 3. E.l.f. Studio Makeup Clutch Palette

![E.l.f. Studio Makeup Clutch Palette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/12/3_e-l-f-studio-makeup-clutch-palette_600x507.jpg)

e.l.f. is my go-to brand for affordable makeup and this multipurpose makeup palette makes doing makeup easy and economical! This “clutch” contains over 30 eye shadows, 6 lip shades, 2 blushes, a bronzer, shimmer, brow powder and cream and applicators all in a 6”x4” container! This would make a great gift for someone experimenting with color or for anyone who likes to keep their makeup handy for touch-ups!

## 4. The Balm Balmbini Vol. 2 Babies of the Balm Face Palette

![The Balm Balmbini Vol. 2 Babies of the Balm Face Palette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/12/4_the-balm-balmbini-vol-2-babies-of-the-balm-face-palette_250x250.jpg)

The Balm has tons of cheeky and fun beauty products and their makeup palettes offer us a chance to try out some of their best makeup! The Balmbini Vol.2 offers up 7 different products with 6 of them being multipurpose makeup! You’ve got your eyeshadow/liners, lip/cheek stains, highlighter/shadow and a lip gloss all in a compact little palette that you can take anywhere you please!

## 5. Stila Stylish in Seoul Travel Palette

![Stila Stylish in Seoul Travel Palette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/12/5_stila-stylish-in-seoul-travel-palette_250x250.jpg)

I’ve always been a big fan of Stila’s travel palettes because they’re compact and so convenient! You get five wet or dry eye shadows, lip gloss and their sensational convertible color all in a small yet stylish palette. Create your own look or follow the easy-to-follow face chart inside, your options are limitless with these blendable and complementary colors!

## 6. Smashbox Studio Pop Palette

![Smashbox Studio Pop Palette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/12/6_smashbox-studio-pop-palette_250x250.jpg)

What better kit to make eyes really pop than with Smashbox’s Studio Pop palette?! Create a custom look with 16 bold and beautiful shadows and 4 cream liners or use the get-the-look cards included in the kit to get some beauty inspiration. Either way, you’ll be rocking some sexy, smoldering eyes and the rest of your makeup will be a breeze!

## 7. Benefit She’s so…Jetset

![Benefit She’s so…Jetset](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/12/7_benefit-shes-so-jetset_248x224.jpg)

If you’re a jetsetter or a jetsetter in training, why not use multipurpose makeup that can keep up with your busy lifestyle?! Benefit’s She’s So…Jetset palette comes with eye shadows, face powder, primer, mascara, lip gloss and applicators. They’ve pretty much got all your bases covered so there’s no need to tote a big bag of makeup on your trips!

## 8. Pixi Early Bird Kit

![Pixi Early Bird Kit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/12/8_pixi-early-bird-kit_399x322.jpg)

This kit was designed for the night owls and late risers! Even if you don’t always have time for a full night’s rest, you can use makeup to look like early birds! If you frequently find yourself having rushed mornings, this palette is right up your alley! You get 3 blushes, 2 bronzers, 1 face glow, 4 eye base shades and 16 eye shadows to create a bright-eyed and rosy-cheeked look!

## 9. Urban Decay Fun Palette

![Urban Decay Fun Palette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/12/9_urban-decay-fun-palette_250x250.jpg)

Do you ever have those little beauty emergencies where you have to step out and get gorgeous fast? The Fun palette can help! This compact little kit comes with 6 eye shadows and a lip gloss to create playful and sultry eyes with a come-hither nude lip. UD also has Dangerous and Feminine palettes that you can use to play up your frisky or ladylike side!

Makeup palettes are a great way to try out different products or use to touch-up makeup on the go. There are tons of different makeup kits out there so there’s bound to be one that matches your makeup personality! Do you use makeup palettes? Which one is your favorite?
