# Sweat\-Proof Makeup Tutorials That Will See You through Summer ...

I love almost everything about summer, even the suffocating heat – but I hate what it does to my makeup. I have a corporate job, so looking perfect and polished every single day is basically part of my job description. On those days when the temperature reaches ninety degrees basically from the moment the sun comes up, I'm always left with a melting face by the time I walk from my car into the office. I know lots of you understand how real the struggle is, right? Then this is for you. These tutorials will help you out so much this summer because they're all about sweat-proof makeup!

## 1. All the Products You'll Need

Now, for starters, you need to know which products will really be sweat-proof. This tutorial has a great list to get you started. Mind you, there are plenty of other products out there that guard against sweat, but this is a fantastic place to get started! What do you think?

## 2. Smudge Proof

Smudges are a telltale sign that you're either sweating through your makeup, or you're about to. This video tells you everything you need to know about avoiding the dreaded smudge. Just a tip from me to you, waterproof mascara is a great place to start – you're way less likely to end up with raccoon eyes just because you're sweating.

## 3. Foundation Routine

Now, your foundation routine really is the foundation of a sweat-proof makeup technique. If your foundation is too heavy or greasy, it makes you sweat more. If you don't apply it properly, it almost guarantees you'll sweat through your makeup. So, it's time to build up a better makeup foundation … with your foundation!

## 4. Full-faced, Full Coverage

I know a lot of girls and women who avoid going out with a full face of makeup because the threat of sweating it all off is just too high. That sucks for women who enjoy full-face coverage every day. It also sucks for women who need a full-face look for a special occasion. Fortunately, this tutorial will show you how to get full coverage without all the fear.

## 5. Heat and Humidity Sweat Proof Makeup

The thing is, the heat is just one of the reasons why your makeup starts to melt and smudge. The humidity plays a huge factor, too. Damn that humidity! Ruining hair and makeup since the beginning of time! The good news is that now you have the know-how to wage war against it.

## 6. Heat Proof Makeup

Of course, on truly hot summer days, your makeup can start melting off as soon as you get into your car! On days when you're really battling the heat, let this video be your guide. It's true, you really can make your makeup heat proof.

## 7. Summer Makeup!

Last but most certainly not least, here's a great way to get a gorgeous summer makeup look that won't melt off your face! Say goodbye to smudges. No longer will you feel like your face is hidden beneath a hot, sticky mess of makeup!

Have any other tips for rocking your makeup even when it's hotter than blazes outside? Please share!
