# 19 Best and Cheapest Liquid Foundations for Fab Girls on a Budget ...

Don’t let drugstore liquid foundations scare you away-many drugstore formulas work just as well (if not better!) than department store brands. The key to finding the best liquid foundations is by matching the product to your face. The keyword here is face-not hands or arms. Your face tends to be lighter than the rest of your body, so a color that blends perfectly on the top of your hand is not necessarily the perfect shade for your face. You also want to be sure to test drive liquid foundations in natural lighting. The stark fluorescent lighting in stores isn’t conducive to perfect matching. Thankfully many stores are awesome about returning makeup products, so give it a shot in natural outdoor lighting and return if necessary. Stay tuned for this list of awesome drugstore liquid foundations:
