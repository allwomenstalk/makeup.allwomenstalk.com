# 7 Must Have Highlighters Every Girl Needs This Summer ...

Highlighters are all the trend right now and as summer is arriving, it is one of the staple makeup products for a gorgeous summer glam!

I definitely consider myself a highlighter hoarder, so I will be sharing with you my top 7 highlighters every girl needs this summer!

## 1. Becca Cosmetics X Jaclyn Hill Shimmering Skin Perfector in "Champagne Pop" - $38

![eyebrow, sun tanning, brown, powder, face powder,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i9/yg/qlgx3tfr577adafc58aab157656692_550x536.jpg)

[sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This is one of my all time favorite highlighters in existence for several reasons. First of all, it is a collaboration by Becca Cosmetics with my favorite beauty Youtuber, Jaclyn Hill! Secondly, it has become one of the top selling products in Sephora. I highly recommend this products for all you gals looking to glow up this summer!

## 2. Anastasia Beverly Hills Glow Kit in "Sun Dipped" - $40

![eyebrow, brown, eye, cheek, BRONZED,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z2/d7/tmj80edi577adaf1179f1820223111_500x400.jpg)

[sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This product is Anastasia Beverly Hill's third and most recent release of her glow kit line. It has four shades, so you can mix them up or use it to your preference, and you also get a great selection of undertones. This is a perfect kit for those of you looking for variety!

## 3. Hourglass Ambient Lighting Palette - $62

![cheek, baggage,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ta/nm/jaqivym0577adae7f06f1464728456_600x249.jpg)

[sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
The Ambient Lighting Palette has three gorgeous highlighting powders. The formula with this product is best described as lightweight, giving you that gorgeous glow from within. I recommend this for those of you who want a subtle, yet stunning glow that catches the light beautifully and do not want to look overdone.

## 4. ELF Baked Blush in "Pinktastic" - $3

![face powder, powder, eye, beauty, lip,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rc/vg/b0cafbc0577adadb520fb341062355_600x486.jpg)

[https://www.elfcosmetics.com/p/baked-blush](https://go.magik.ly:443/ml/oa4/)
This is the most affordable highlighter on the list. It is technically known as a baked blush, but it is so shimmery and light, that it is perfect for a glowy highlighter and/or blush topper. They do create baked highlighters now that I have not tried yet. This product is only $3 in which you just cannot go wrong, and will give you a gorgeous glow!

## 5. Benefit Cosmetics "Watts up!" Cream-to-Powder Highlighter - $30

![lip, skin, lotion, perfume, liquid,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pe/zs/c7ooabl9577adaca66f01212469201_600x564.jpg)

[sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This product is a cream formula, which is perfect for summery beach days where you want to wear minimal makeup and have a pretty glow catching the light. You can still wear this on a full glam look under a powder highlighter for an extra umph!

## 6. Benefit Cosmetics Liquid Face Highlighter in "High Beam" - $26

![distilled beverage, alcoholic beverage, gentleman, cosmetics, drink,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lt/st/etrk8ajs577adab90031a806984900_450x384.jpg)

[sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

I wanted to give you gals a variety of different highlighter formulas to choose from for the summer. This product by Benefit looks very similar to a nail polish in packaging, but what it is, is actually a highlighter in liquid form! You can apply this to the high points of your face on a summer day for a natural glow, blend it in, and you are good to go!

## 7. Laura Geller Baked Gelato Swirl Illuminator in "Gilded Honey" - $26

![cheek,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yw/fa/ovvmg6u1577ada9d0c146040102658_400x393.jpg)

[ulta.com](http://www.ulta.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This highlighter is a gorgeous true golden shade that is simply a must have for summer time! It is perfect for a soft every day summer look, or a night time glam look. A true golden highlighter looks amazing on tan to dark skin tones! Pop this stunning highlighter on the high points of your face and thank me later!

I hope you found this list of must have highlighters for the summer time useful. What are some of your favorite highlighters to rock this summer?
