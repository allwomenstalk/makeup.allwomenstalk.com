# 8 Gorgeous Sugarpill Eye Shadows ...

There are few things in life I love more than experimenting with eye shadows, especially the vivid, brilliant hues offered by Sugarpill. They’re vegan-friendly, sparkly, rich, and I adore the rainbow of hues to choose from! Here are 8 gorgeous Sugarpill eye shadows… take a peek!

## 1. Sugarpill Birthday Girl Eye Shadow

![Sugarpill Birthday Girl Eye Shadow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mw/j1/jmhugnhz54c9382171105796486245_270x270.jpg)

**Price:** $12.00 at [sugarpillshop.com](https://goo.gl/Y9p1qV)
Oh-so-pretty in pink! This sparkly pink is princess-approved, with a cotton-candy pink base and loads of fuchsia glitters. It’s darling, and ideal for all skin tones. Wouldn’t it be fun to layer this with a blue or orange color?

## 2. Sugarpill Darling Eye Shadow

![Sugarpill Darling Eye Shadow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mr/qz/s570p9i154c9382281fd1704602413_270x270.jpg)

**Price:** $12.00 at [sugarpillshop.com](https://goo.gl/qC9IbY)
Have you ever seen a more vivid turquoise shadow? It’s ultra-glimmery, with lots of sparkle and shine. It looks great with brown eyes or green, and is ideal for wearing alone, or to create special eye shadow effects.

## 3. Sugarpill Tipsy Eye Shadow

![Sugarpill Tipsy Eye Shadow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ub/mz/pam3op7z54c938239dee7773255569_270x270.jpg)

**Price:** $12.00 at [sugarpillshop.com](https://goo.gl/ThWD5X)
This shadow is the color of grass, so it reminds me of spring, which is a good thing! It looks gorgeous with green eyes or blue, and is ultra shimmery. And even the name sounds like fun, doesn’t it? Maybe it’s not grass green after all… maybe it’s the color of mint in a mint julep?

## 4. Sugarpill Paper Doll Eye Shadow

![Sugarpill Paper Doll Eye Shadow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s6/j1/opluqrcj54c93824a83e4000138447_270x270.jpg)

**Price:** $12.00 at [sugarpillshop.com](https://goo.gl/cAu0fq)
I have green eyes, so I learned years ago that purples make my eye color pop. If you have green eyes, too, you have to try this lavender shadow! It’s bright, vivid, and lush, with thousands of tiny star-shine twinkles inside.

## 5. Sugarpill Absinthe Eye Shadow

![Sugarpill Absinthe Eye Shadow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ay/gf/wthejere54c93825b5c94571392600_270x270.jpg)

**Price:** $12.00 at [sugarpillshop.com](https://goo.gl/l0Zczv)
Is it green-yellow, or yellow-green? I suppose it doesn’t matter… this shadow is gorgeous! It’s all springtime and flowers and love, a wicked yellow-green with loads of shimmer and shine. Wear it on a grey winter day to add your own cheer!

## 6. Sugarpill Goldilux Eye Shadow

![Sugarpill Goldilux Eye Shadow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p0/yd/v92nh0rk54c93826ca3a5018509342_270x270.jpg)

**Price:** $12.00 at [sugarpillshop.com](https://goo.gl/3kyh8J)
Line your eyes with lush black kohl, then apply this high-shimmer gold shadow generously… or use it sparingly as a bronzer or body dust. It’s brilliant, with so much shine, and it’s a true gold color, not too brassy or cold. It works beautifully for all skin tones, from peachy-cream to olive and African-American. I love it!

## 7. Sugarpill Magpie Eye Shadow

![Sugarpill Magpie Eye Shadow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e0/kr/jlzu9glt54c938281abc2696869546_270x270.jpg)

**Price:** $12.00 at [sugarpillshop.com](https://goo.gl/Uz6pQT)
The name might lead you to believe this shadow is black, but it’s not. It’s a rich deep blue, made with extra sparkle and a hint of turquoise thrown in for good measure. It looks stunning with brown eyes. Use it to create a smoky eye look with an edge. Gorgeous!

## 8. Sugarpill Tiara Eye Shadow

_ **Price:** $12.00 at [sugarpillshop.com](https://goo.gl/AFxm74)
This super-fine high-shine shadow is the color of the Ice Queen’s crown, snowy white but with endless glimmer. Use it to highlight a smoky eye look, or to brighten your eyes by itself. I’d layer this over a blue or green liner and add lush false lashes for a dramatic look… icy!

These are just a few of the gorgeous, ultra-shimmery eye shadows in the Sugarpill rainbow… I adore them all! Which of these colors do you like best, and how would you use it? Do tell!
