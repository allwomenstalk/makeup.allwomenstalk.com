# 7 Gorgeous Jewel Tone Lipsticks for Winter ...

Jewel Tone Lipsticks are a great way to make a winter fashion statement through your makeup. Deep jewel tone colors can be hard to find so I’ve made a list of my favorites for you! Pair these lipsticks with rosy cheeks and a sultry cat eye for the perfect winter look. Here are 7 gorgeous jewel tone lipsticks for winter.

## 1. NARS Semi Matte Scarlet Empress

![NARS Semi Matte Scarlet Empress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/12/1_nars-semi-matte-scarlet-empress_240x432.jpg)

[sephora.com](https://goo.gl/d0EbHU)

Empress is a creamy jewel tone lipstick that is enriched with Vitamin E for long lasting wearability. Its blue-red undertones make it an irresistible match for those who have cool skin tones. Its semi matte finish gives your lips just the right amount of shine and creates a dramatic full-bodied pout. It's one of my very favorite jewel tone lipsticks!

## 2. MAC Cyber

![MAC Cyber](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/12/2_mac-cyber_264x306.jpg)

[dillards.com](http://www.dillards.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Cyber is a gorgeous blackish-purple hue that is perfect for winter. Its satin finish goes on soft to create just the right amount of drama. This is a jewel tone lipstick anyone can pull off! Pair this shade with a little black dress and a low bun for a classic glamour look.

## 3. Manic Panic after Midnight

_ manicpanic.com_

Manic Panic is known for their over-the-top hair dyes, but did you know they make daring lipsticks as well? The lipsticks are the same shades as their hair dye, but are a less permanent way to rock their vivid colors. This metallic blue hue is sure to pump up any outfit. Accessorize this jewel tone lipstick with some sapphire blue heels and chunky jewelry.

## 4. Illamasqua Apocalips

![Illamasqua Apocalips](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/12/4_illamasqua-apocalips_300x300.jpg)

[illamasqua.com](https://www.illamasqua.com/shop/lips/lipstick/view-all.list)

Teal is a great color because it stays in style all year round. Illamasqua’s Apocalips is a great winter shade of teal. Use a lip primer and a couple of coats of this lipstick to really bring out its jewel tone. Apocalips looks great on tanned or olive skin and its matte finish insures it will last all night.

## 5. Lime Crime Serpentina

_ limecrimemakeup.com_

If you are feeling adventurous, this serpent-inspired lipstick is perfect for you! This deep emerald green color will look great on any skin tone and is a fun way to add a little pizzazz to your winter wardrobe. This jewel tone lipstick goes on smooth and opaque and comes in a sparkly unicorn-themed tube. Keep your wardrobe simple to really make your lips pop!

## 6. Makeup Forever Rouge Artist Intense in Pearly Dark Violet

![Makeup Forever Rouge Artist Intense in Pearly Dark Violet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/12/6_makeup-forever-rouge-artist-intense-in-pearly-dark-violet_208x448.jpg)

[sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

The Rouge Artist Intense collection is made with 50% more pigment than traditional lipstick. This insures a one-swipe application to get true-to-color results. Makeup Forever blends three different waxes in this luscious lipstick for soft, kissable lips. This is one jewel tone lipstick no winter makeup bag should be without!

## 7. Givenchy Rouge Interdit Satin Lipstick in Bright Cherry Red

![Givenchy Rouge Interdit Satin Lipstick in Bright Cherry Red](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/12/7_givenchy-rouge-interdit-satin-lipstick-in-bright-cherry-red_192x440.jpg)

[sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Not only will you look fabulous in Givenchy’s Bright Cherry Red lipstick, you will be doing your lips a favor by wearing this shade! This jewel tone lipstick is full of antioxidants that nourish and hydrate your lips. The light-reflective pigment in this ruby red shade will keep you looking radiant all night long. This lipstick is guaranteed to last as long as you can!

It doesn’t matter if you prefer emeralds, rubies, or sapphires, there is a lipstick shade to match! Adorn your lips with one of these 7 jewel tone lipsticks for an instant winter fashion statement. Which of these jewel tone lipsticks will you be picking up over the holiday season?
