# 10 Life\-Changing Makeup Tutorials for Gals with Glasses ...

You don't have to apply your makeup any differently when you have to wear your glasses for the day. However, there are some tricks to make your eyes look more flattering through your lenses. You don't want them to distort your appearance. So if you're not in the mood to pop in your contacts, here are some makeup tutorials for gals with glasses:

## 1. Cheekbones and Brows

The woman in this tutorial knows the importance of your eyebrows. When you have glasses on, it'll draw extra attention to your brows, which is why you should try to create an angled arch that'll appear more flattering than a rounded one. She also gives tips on how to avoid making your makeup smudge when you take your glasses on and off, so you don't have to worry about checking the mirror all day.

## 2. Neat and Natural

Here's a makeup routine that's impossible to mess up. It looks about as natural as you possibly could look while wearing eye shadow and liner. All it requires you to do is put on mascara and liner above your eyes without applying anything beneath your eyes. This tutorial will even show you a versatile pair of glasses that allow you to change up your look every single day.

## 3. Big and Glamorous

Don't let your glasses hold you back from trying something extreme. You don't have to keep your makeup subtle, all because you're wearing glasses. This tutorial will show you how to look fabulous beneath your frames. There's nothing wrong with creating a shimmery smokey eye and wearing a dark colored lipstick.

## 4. Fun and Flirty

This tutorial will show you how to create a flirty look that you can wear on a date or out to dinner with your friends. It will also give you tips on how to make your natural lashes stand out, since you should avoid wearing false lashes when wearing glasses (unless you want them to hit the lens whenever you blink).

## 5. Quick and Cute

This tutorial will give you tiny little tips that'll help glasses wearers everywhere, like avoiding cream eyeliners, because it's easy for them to smudge. It will also give you advice on how to open up your eyes, because your lenses can make your eyes look smaller than they actually are.

## 6. Nice and Neutral

If you're preparing yourself for a casual day, you should stick to wearing neutral eye shadows, because it will help you enhance your eyes without making them look too dramatic. You should also remember to draw a thicker line with your eyeliner if your frames are thick, and a thinner line if your frames are thin.

## 7. Beautiful and Blue

When you have your glasses on, your best bet is to stick to mineral foundation over liquid foundation. However, you can wear whatever color shadow that you'd like. If you want to make your eyes stand out, try wearing sky blue eyeshadow. According to the woman in this tutorial, those light colors can make your eyes appear much fresher than a black or [brown smokey eye](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/different-ways-to-create-gorgeous-smokey-eyes/) would.

## 8. Fresh Face

Natural, neutral colors create this bright eyed look. Winged eyeliner creates the finishing touch. You can wear this every day for a fresh look that's super simple and quick!

## 9. Neutral Vs Bold Makeup for Glasses Look Book

This tutorial shows that you should be comfortable and confident in your glasses! Switch up your frames and show them off like an accessory. Here are two different makeup looks with a neutral and bold lip. Pro tip: make your eyebrows extra bold and darker than normal.

## 10. GLAM for GLASSES

Your eyes will definitely pop and not be hidden behind your frames! This is a glam look for when you're wanting to spice up your every day makeup routine. Complete the look with false lashes!

These tips will help you remain fabulous, whether you're wearing your glasses or not. Do you wear a different style of makeup when you have your glasses on than you do when you have them off for the day?
