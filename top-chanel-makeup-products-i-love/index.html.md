# Top 10 Chanel Makeup Products I Love ...

With all of the different Chanel makeup products out there, how can you choose the right ones for you? If you're looking for all of the top Chanel makeup products but don't know where to start, don't worry! I've got your back, ladies! I've tried, tested and purchased each of the Chanel products below and I can tell you, they are stellar and pretty awesome! Take a look and explore!

## 1. Chanel Double Perfection Matte Powder Makeup

![Chanel Double Perfection Matte Powder Makeup](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/11/1_chanel-double-perfection-matte-powder-makeup_552x771.jpg)

One of the very first Chanel makeup products that I ever purchased was this awesome powder. Not only is it super light, but it also feels fantastic on, no matter what makeup you wear under or over it. It'll cover any imperfections and it truly leaves your skin looking completely and totally flawless!

## 2. Chanel Vitalumiere Aqua Skin Perfecting Makeup

![Chanel Vitalumiere Aqua Skin Perfecting Makeup](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/11/2_chanel-vitalumiere-aqua-skin-perfecting-makeup_600x920.jpg)

If you are looking for a Chanel makeup product that complements my first top product, this one is it! Not only is it incredible on, but it really does make your skin look perfect and flawless. It might be expensive (on Amazon, it's about $85.00), but it is so worth it! Give it a try, girls!

## 3. Chanel Les 4 Ombres Eye Makeup

![Chanel Les 4 Ombres Eye Makeup](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/11/3_chanel-les-4-ombres-eye-makeup_552x704.jpg)

Are you looking for the perfect eye shadow palette that will illuminate your eyes? Give this awesome eye shadow palette a try! It'll make doing smoky eyes super easy and the colors are beautiful! This top Chanel makeup product runs at about $61.00 on Amazon.com but it's definitely a great investment!

## 4. Chanel Joues Contraste Powder Blush

![Chanel Joues Contraste Powder Blush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/11/4_chanel-joues-contraste-powder-blush_600x920.jpg)

I always find it really hard to find a great blush that won't be smeary and will go on smooth, but this Chanel makeup product is ideal! Not only does it go on smooth, but it highlights my cheeks in just the right way! The color choices? They have a color for every skin tone!

## 5. Chanel Lift Lumiere Smoothing & Rejuvenating Eye Contour Concealer

![Chanel Lift Lumiere Smoothing & Rejuvenating Eye Contour Concealer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/11/5_chanel-lift-lumiere-smoothing-rejuvenating-eye-contour-concealer_600x920.jpg)

Finding a base for all of that fab makeup to go onto is never easy. I have used a lot of different primers, but this one is ideal! Not only does it help cover up under eye circles, but it reduces puffiness and it allows your eyes to feel bright and beautiful! Give this awesome top Chanel makeup product a try, girls!

## 6. Chanel Base Lumiere Illuminating Makeup Base

![Chanel Base Lumiere Illuminating Makeup Base](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/11/6_chanel-base-lumiere-illuminating-makeup-base_350x537.jpg)

Number 5 is all about the eyes, but this awesome makeup base is for your entire face! It'll not only make your foundation stick, but your powder will look amazing and your skin will look poreless and perfect. It's only about $55.00 on Amazon.com and it is so worth the price, girls!

## 7. Exclusive by Chanel Les 4 Ombres Eye Makeup - No. 14 Mystic Eyes

![Exclusive by Chanel Les 4 Ombres Eye Makeup - No. 14 Mystic Eyes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kr/py/koxe9pg854c93aabe5bc8003317165_350x317.jpg)

While the eye shadow palette that I named in #3 is perfect for smoky eyes, this eye shadow palette is ideal for the everyday. For me, I use the pink and the brown every single day! It's neutral and makes for some great eye makeup for the office! Only about $41.00 on Amazon.com!

## 8. Chanel Ecriture De Chanel Liquid Eyeliner

![Chanel Ecriture De Chanel Liquid Eyeliner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/11/8_chanel-ecriture-de-chanel-liquid-eyeliner_600x920.jpg)

As an eyeliner lover, I can tell you that it is extremely difficult to find a liquid eyeliner that is not only awesome, but that you want to use everyday! This top Chanel makeup product is not only my go-to eyeliner, but it's stays put! It's a water-based eyeliner that has a felt tip that is flexible and soft. It's such a great find!

## 9. Chanel Illusion D'Ombre Long Wear Eyeshadow

![Chanel Illusion D'Ombre Long Wear Eyeshadow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/11/9_chanel-illusion-dombre-long-wear-eyeshadow_552x432.jpg)

One of my biggest problems with a lot of eye shadows on the market today is that they don't stick on my eyes all day! This incredible Chanel product not only stays put, but it can last all day long! They also have a ton of different colors so you can mix and match everyday!

## 10. Chanel Pro Correcteur Palette Multi-Correcteurs

![Chanel Pro Correcteur Palette Multi-Correcteurs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/11/10_chanel-pro-correcteur-palette-multi-correcteurs_480x423.jpg)

Finally girls, the last top Chanel makeup product that we're going to talk about is this awesome concealer palette! Not only does it contain a bunch of color-correcting formulas, but this little palette will correct every imperfection and will leave your skin looking beautiful!

So girls, these are my top Chanel makeup products, what other Chanel products do you like? Any I haven't listed? Give your own top Chanel products below!
