# 7 Vegan Makeup Products ...

Vegan makeup products don’t contain any animal products and are not tested on animals. Even if you don’t have a vegan diet, you can help fight animal cruelty by choosing vegan makeup products to add to your makeup bag. The best part is with these amazing vegan makeup products you don’t have to compromise on quality!

## 1. Lime Crime Parfait Day Pastel Pink Nail Polish

![Lime Crime Parfait Day Pastel Pink Nail Polish](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/12/1_lime-crime-parfait-day-pastel-pink-nail-polish_176x220.jpg)

Vegan makeup products can be hard to find, especially vegan products that aren't meant for the face, so I was excited to find this vegan nail polish from Lime Crime! A majority of Lime Crime products are vegan, but their nail polishes are unbeatable. The pastel colors go on easily in just 1 coat, and I just love this pastel strawberry pink!

## 2. Tarte Lipsurgence Matte Lip Tint

![Tarte Lipsurgence Matte Lip Tint](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/12/2_tarte-lipsurgence-matte-lip-tint_158x633.jpg)

This Tarte jumbo lip pencil is one of my favorite products, and I was pleasantly surprised to learn that it was a vegan product when I began doing some research for this list. This is one matte lip color that does not feel thick or pasty on your lips. It hydrates while giving the lips a soft, matte finish. It comes in 6 pinky and plummy colors that are gorgeous on all skin tones!

## 3. Urban Decay Eyeshadows

![Urban Decay Eyeshadows](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/12/3_urban-decay-eyeshadows_236x236.jpg)

Not every single eye shadow from Urban Decay is vegan, but several of them are. They are noted on the website with a purple paw print next to them. Urban Decay is always working with their chemists to find the best vegan formulations and they are adding more and more vegan products all the time. One of my favorites from their collection is their eye shadow in Loaded-a deep, metallic emerald green.

## 4. Beauty without Cruelty Natural Liquid Vegan Make-up

![Beauty without Cruelty Natural Liquid Vegan Make-up](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/12/4_beauty-without-cruelty-natural-liquid-vegan-make-up_222x295.jpg)

Beauty Without Cruelty is a makeup line that strictly produces vegan and cruelty free products, hence their name! These liquid foundations provide color and coverage to even out your skin tone while protecting your skin with vitamin E and sunscreen. It has a lightweight feel on the skin and does not clog the pores.

## 5. Ecco Bella Lip Liner Pencils

![Ecco Bella Lip Liner Pencils](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/12/5_ecco-bella-lip-liner-pencils_600x599.jpg)

Ecco Bella’s vegan lip liner pencils create a great base for your favorite lipstick or lip gloss! Two of the 3 shades are vegan, Terracotta and Plum. The pencils contain natural vegetable waxes that create a soft, natural appearance on the lip. If you fill in your entire lip with the lip liner, your lipstick will last throughout the day! In addition to being vegan free, these lip liner pencils are also gluten-free!

## 6. Pacifica’s Color Quench Lip Tint

![Pacifica’s Color Quench Lip Tint](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/12/6_pacificas-color-quench-lip-tint_230x507.jpg)

This all natural lip tint adds a subtle, shimmery color to the lip while also adding moisture! It has a natural flavor and comes in a 100% recycled tube. Trade in your boring colorless lip balm for this pretty option! This lip tint comes in 5 amazing flavors: guava berry, vanilla hibiscus, blood orange, sugared fig, and coconut nectar.

## 7. Obsessive Compulsive Cosmetics Loos Colour Concentrates

![Obsessive Compulsive Cosmetics Loos Colour Concentrates](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/12/7_obsessive-compulsive-cosmetics-loos-colour-concentrates_328x328.jpg)

These pure color pigments from Obsessive Compulsive Cosmetics add shimmer and color to the eyes, lips, cheeks, and body! You can apply them alone or mix them to create a custom color. I love how versatile this product is, but all the products created by OCC are 100% vegan and cruelty-free so choose your favorite!

Purchasing vegan makeup products is a simple way to help end animal cruelty. These high-quality makeup products are great additions to your makeup kit! Do you purchase vegan makeup products? What is your recommendation?
