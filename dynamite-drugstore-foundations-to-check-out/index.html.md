# 7 Dynamite Drugstore Foundations to Check out ...

Say what you will about drugstore foundations, but they’ve come a long way since their humble beginnings. These days, don’t be surprised to see cutting edge formulas in drugstore foundations that rival their high end counterparts as well as other bargain beauty products. Even if you’ve never trusted drugstore makeup to provide you with the proper range of shades, coverage and staying power before; I think you’ll be pleasantly surprised by these seven drugstore foundations that are blowing away the competition.

## 1. L’Oreal True Match Super Blendable Makeup

![L’Oreal True Match Super Blendable Makeup](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zo/cv/u2/hffuxpm7_299x294.jpg)

One of the top drugstore foundations in beauty aisles right now is L’Oreal’s True Match foundation. The great thing about this foundation is that it was created to match your skin tone and texture so you get a precise color match that works well with your particular skin. If you’re like me and have the hardest time finding a foundation that is your true blue match, you’re going to adore this affordable yet high tech color matching foundation. Comes in 33 different shades and works with all skin types.

## 2. Neutrogena Nourishing Long Wear Foundation

![Neutrogena Nourishing Long Wear Foundation](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ti/tu/wv/ccwsa7s1_409x389.jpg)

Neutrogena is another trustworthy name in the drugstore makeup industry and their foundation is not to be missed. As you can probably guess from the name, the formula is nourishing and long lasting. It’s also noted to instantly cover up flaws and improve your skin tone and texture with continued use. It’s kind of like a skin care treatment within your makeup! Many people have noted that the formula is on the thicker side, so if you’re looking for something lightweight, pass on this one.

## 3. Maybelline Fit Me Foundation

![Maybelline Fit Me Foundation](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uc/cx/s5/yt17jnt4_399x333.jpg)

Maybelline’s Fit Me Foundation comes in a gel-liquid formula that is lightweight and allows your skin to breathe. But that’s not all, Fit Me is also known for an impressive range of shades. Four of them are formulated especially for women of color and contain 5D pigment technology that balances your skin’s natural tones. This is another great option for ladies who have a hard time finding the right shade of foundation.

## 4. Revlon Colorstay Makeup for Combination/Oily Skin

![Revlon Colorstay Makeup for Combination/Oily Skin](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gn/d3/qk/pnt7ufyl_279x462.jpg)

If you have combo or oily skin, finding a foundation that’s not going to change colors on you or fade away after a few hours can be challenging to say the least. Lucky for us, Revlon’s Colorstay foundation was created with skin types likes ours in mind. This drugstore foundation was made to stay strong without fading or caking while it absorbs oil so our faces stay perfectly made up sans shine. It offers medium to full coverage and Revlon says it stays up to 24 hours, but I personally wouldn’t want to test it out that long.

## 5. Covergirl Outlast Stay Fabulous 3-in-1 Foundation

![Covergirl Outlast Stay Fabulous 3-in-1 Foundation](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mj/an/wh/a94r2ax3_299x388.jpg)

This next drugstore foundation is perfect for those who are always in a rush and don’t have time for multiple steps and products in the morning. CG’s 3-in-1 foundation combines the power of primer, concealer and foundation all in one so you get the flawless coverage, staying power and skin-evening properties of the three products in one easy, breezy step. Get smooth, seamless coverage in less time with less products!

## 6. Garnier Skin Renew Miracle Skin Perfector BB Cream

![Garnier Skin Renew Miracle Skin Perfector BB Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wi/ku/lg/o9csnlfg_349x284.jpg)

Although BB Cream isn’t the same thing as foundation, this is a great alternative to foundation if you’re looking for something lightweight and hydrating. Garnier’s BB Cream has even been deemed the best drugstore BB cream by some beauty experts and addicts. I agree that it’s a great value and offers lots of skin care and beauty benefits, but my only gripe is that it only comes in two shades! While the product offers solutions to common skin care issues like discoloration and dryness, I think it’ll garner even more rave reviews once there are more shades added to the line!

## 7. Rimmel Lasting Finish 25 Hour Foundation

![Rimmel Lasting Finish 25 Hour Foundation](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/0j/vh/yz/bj0i4k54_311x600.jpg)

If you’re looking for affordable drugstore makeup, Rimmel is a fab brand to try out. They have some pretty rad lipsticks, eye makeup and of course, foundation. Their Lasting Finish 25 Hour Foundation comes in 10 shades and promises to stand up to heat, sweat, stress and humidity without any problems! Your skin will feel hydrated and lightweight as you face the long day or night ahead.

Everyone loves finding a good bargain and these drugstore foundations are sure to please even the pickiest of beauty addicts. Drugstore makeup companies are constantly coming out with new and exciting products, so it’s worth your while to check out drugstore beauty aisles every now and then. Who knows, you just might find your new must-have beauty product and you won’t go broke in the process! What is your absolute favorite drugstore foundation?
