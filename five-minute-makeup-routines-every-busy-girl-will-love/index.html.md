# 5 Minute Makeup Routines All Busy Girls Will Love ...

Even if you love spending time in front of the mirror applying makeup, you won't always have an hour to spare. If you have to run out the door, then you'll only have a few minutes to get your look right. The next time that you're in a rush, here are some of the best five minutes makeup routines that all of you busy girls will love:

## 1. Basic 5 Minute Makeup Routine

This makeup tutorial will work for any woman. All you have to do is dab some product beneath your eyes in order to get rid of any dark shadows and around the nose to get rid of any redness. Then you can curl your lashes and place a little bit of mascara on them. Finally, add a little bit of cream blush and lipstick, and you're all finished!

## 2. Late for School Look

If you're obsessed with applying concealer and perfecting your eyebrows, you'll love this tutorial. It'll show you how to make your face look problem free in a hurry. Of course, this tutorial will show you more than how to do your makeup in a rush. It'll also show you how to do your hair.

## 3. Light Makeup Routine

This isn't a full faced makeup look. It's just a simple makeup routine that you can use when you don't feel like wasting too much time in the morning. All you have to do is cover up your blemishes, add some definition to your brows, add some eyeliner and shadow, and then dab on some lipgloss.

## 4. Chic and Simple

This makeup routine is perfect for when you're going to work or school, because it'll make you look sweet and chic at the same time. Even though it'll only take you five minutes to complete, it'll look like you've spent an hour in front of your mirror.

## 5. Fresh Face in 5 Minutes

This is the perfect five-minute makeup routine to use before a date. You should start by applying sunscreen to your face in order to prevent your skin from becoming damaged. Then you should put on the usual products, like concealer, powder, and eyeshadow. Make sure you complete the look by finding a pretty, powerful lipstick that will make your mouth stand out.

## 6. Natural Looking Makeup Routine

This routine is perfect for ladies who like wearing light makeup. The woman in this tutorial talks about the importance of your eyebrows, and believes that they're one of the first things you should work on when you only have a few minutes to get ready. After all, they can make your whole face look different.

## 7. 5 Minute Eye Makeup

If you want your eyes to stand out, then there's no reason to waste time painting your lips and adding on blush, because they'll take away the focus from your peepers. That's why you should use this makeup routine that solely focuses on the eyes. The woman in the tutorial will show you how to make the most gorgeous wings possible.

You don't have to spend hours in front of the mirror to make your face look perfect. If you know what you're doing, your makeup routine should only take five minutes. Which one of these makeup routines are you dying to try first?
