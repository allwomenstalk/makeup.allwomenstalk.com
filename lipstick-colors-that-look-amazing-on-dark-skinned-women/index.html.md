# 27 Lipstick Colors That Look Amazing on Dark Skinned Women ...

![](https://img.allw.mn/content/j2/y7/l9s8uf9f55ac2e02d143c391671274_500x309.gif)

Confession: I have a somewhat unhealthy obsession with lipstick! Pink, purple, red, nude, and anything in between and beyond I LOVE! Unfortunately, though, most cosmetic brands cater to a primarily caucasian market. But don't worry, I've found several lipsticks that look amazing on dark skinned women!
