# 9 Gorgeous Eye Shadow Looks Created with the Must\-Have Eyeshadow Palettes of 2017 ...

If you want to update your eye shadow look check out the latest eyeshadow palettes of 2017. It can get tedious wearing the same eye look over and over again, maybe it's time to change your eyeshadow palette.

I absolutely love eyeshadows; they’re a fun and colourful way to add drama to your eyes. I personally like to do my research before splurging out on a new palette. There’s a mixture of textures from matte to metallic and different colours from pastels, nudes, and bold colours. Whatever look you are going for, check out the compilation of eye shadow looks that can be created with these new palettes of 2017.

## 1. Too Faced Natural Love Ultimate Neutral EyeShadow Palette at 4.42

This is a limited edition eyeshadow palette of 2017 with a total of 30 eye shadows. It contains 7 mattes, 13 shimmer and 10 sparkle eye shadows. These are all natural looking shades. Shannon creates a gorgeous soft bronze copper neutral look that you can totally rock for date night.
Price: $59 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 2. Kat Von D Pastel Goth at 4.43

If you want to be more adventurous with your eye look, try out this palette. It contains 8 pastel shades. They do look vibrant in the palette but once you apply them they do have a pastel look. The shades cover the rainbow spectrum. Atleeeey creates a smokey pastel purple eye paired with a pink in the inner corners of the eye. This really is a statement look.
Price: $38 at [katvondbeauty.com](https://www.kvdveganbeauty.com/pastel-goth-eyeshadow-palette/29374V1.html)

## 3. Tarte Make Believe in Yourself EyeShadow Palette at 2.00

This is a magical eye shadow palette. It is a Kaleidoscopic, unicorn-inspired eyeshadow palette. With 10 luminous eye shadows that can also be used for strobing and as a cheek highlight. It covers a wide range of colours from copper, metallic lavender, turquoise and golden pink. Victoria creates a foiled mystical blue look using the eyeshadow colour in ‘dream’. The colours are vibrant and show up easily on the skin.
Price: $40 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 4. NARSissist Loaded EyeShadow Palette at 7.41

This is warm-toned palette with 12 velvety shades that are highly pigmented to create a rich look. This eye shadow look is glamorous and has a bold glitter cut crease. As with all warm toned palettes, the looks are very wearable.
Price: $59 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 5. Maybelline 24K Nudes at 2.42

This one is value for money being a drugstore eyeshadow palette it does a good job. The colours are infused with metallic gold pigments and it contains 12 eye shadow shades. It is a versatile eye shadow palette where you can create very wearable looks. Melissa creates a Smokey gold glitter eye that you can easily recreate.
Price: $11.99 at [ulta.com](http://www.ulta.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 6. Stila Matte N Metal Eyeshadow Palette at 6.09

The Stila Matte N Metal eyeshadow palette contains 12 colors. It has 6 matte shades and 6 metallic shades. You can play around with the different textured eye shadows. The colour ranges from pink, rose gold to cool toned colours and golden bronze. These colours will flatter all skin tones. Serein creates a simple look with a wash of colour over the eyelids.
Price: $49 at [sephora.com](https://goo.gl/YtIxda)

## 7. Urban Decay after Dark Palette at 0.50

As the name suggests this is for after dark not for day wear.
It is a jewel-toned palette with 10 shades. The eyeshadows themselves are duo-toned and metallic shimmers. You can really get creative with this palette. What I like about the YouTuber ‘My eye shadow is odd’ is that she creates 5 different looks with the palette while reviewing it. This shows the versatility of the palette and can also give you some design inspiration.
Price: $49 at [urbandecay.com](http://www.urbandecay.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 8. Too Faced Peanut Butter and Honey EyeShadow Palette at 1.47

This is a limited edition palette that contains 9 matte, shimmer, and creamy shades. The colours are warm neutrals as the name suggests peanut butter and honey. These shades are very wearable. Jkissa creates a funky orange and yellow warm toned look. Proving that you can also get creative with the go-to warm colours.
Price: $36 at [toofaced.com](https://goo.gl/OZgnNu)

## 9. Smashbox Cover Shot Eye Palette in Ablaze at 1.15

This is a purse-size palette and ideal to take with you on vacation or when traveling. It contains 7 shades from metallic to bold and matte. These are highly pigmented and are easily bendable. As the name suggests it contains warm copper and red fiery colours. Melrae creates a beautiful scarlet red and gold look that’s absolutely gorgeous.
Price: $29 at [sephora.com](https://goo.gl/lbtRJD)

I personally love eye shadows as it’s a way of being creative and expressing yourself. These Eyeshadow palettes of 2017 do not disappoint. There are a wide variety of colours and hues to chose from. The Tarte unicorn inspired Make Believe in Yourself palette, to the pastel bold colours of Kat Von D Pastel Goth palette and not forgetting the wearable warm shades of NARSissist Loaded Eye Shadow Palette.

Whatever look you are going for I hope these tutorials have got your inner makeup geek exploring.
