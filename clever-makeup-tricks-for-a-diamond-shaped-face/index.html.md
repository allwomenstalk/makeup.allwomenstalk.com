# 7 Clever Makeup Tricks for a Diamond Shaped Face ...

Have you been dying to find all of the top makeup tricks for a diamond shaped face but can't seem to find the tricks that work for you? Well all you diamond-shaped faced girls out there, I've got all of the top makeup tricks for a diamond shaped face that really work and can make your face look absolutely beautiful! So girls, you ready to take a dive and explore my top 7 makeup tricks for a [diamond shaped face](https://hair.allwomenstalk.com/hairstyles-for-your-diamond-shape-face/)?

## 1. Warm Blush

When you have a diamond-shaped face, you constantly want to make sure that you are defining your cheekbones to really make them stand out. Warm blush colors can do that! Going with peach and apricot colors is a great way to really [draw](https://diy.allwomenstalk.com/drawing-websites-to-visit-when-youre-bored/) attention to your cheekbones and is one of my top makeup tricks for a diamond shaped face!

## 2. Silvery Eyeshadow

Did you know that when you have a diamond-shaped face, silvery eyeshadow can actually draw out your shape and your eyes? This makeup trick for a diamond shaped face is a great way to really make your shape stand out! Just a little swipe of silver across your lids can do wonders!

## 3. Use a Highlighter

Using highlighter for your face is another great makeup trick for a diamond shaped face that a lot of people don't know about! Highlighting under your cheeks and along your jawline is a great way to make your face appear more even, instead of focusing on the width of your forehead.

## 4. Create Width with Your Cheekbones

Another great way to take the width away from your forehead is to create some width with your cheekbones. What that means is that you actually want to do upwards brush strokes along the apples of your cheeks, all the way to your temple. This will create a width in your face and will take away from your forehead!

## 5. Pale Lipstick

Did you know that pale lipstick is another great makeup trick for a diamond shaped face? It can draw all kinds of attention to your lips and away from your forehead! One note, keep in mind your skin color. You don't want to have super pale lipstick with pale skin!

## 6. Remember Your Shade

Remember what I said about keeping your skin tone in mind? No matter the shape of your face, you always want to make sure that you are finding foundation, eyeshadow colors and even lipstick that matches your shade of skin! For me, I have to be wicked careful because I have very pale skin!

## 7. Bronzer

Finally girls, the last makeup trick for a diamond shaped face that I'm going to talk about is bronzer! Again, take your skin tone into account, but bronzer can definitely help draw the attention away from your forehead and really center it in on your face!

So girls, there you have it! All of my top makeup tricks for a diamond shaped face! Do you have any other makeup tricks for a diamond shaped face that you'd like to share?
