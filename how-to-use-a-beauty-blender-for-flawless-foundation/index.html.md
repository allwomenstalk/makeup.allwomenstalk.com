# How to Use a Beauty Blender for Flawless Foundation ...

If you've never used a beauty blender before, you're missing out. It's the easiest way to apply your foundation, moisturizer, and even SPF without leaving any lines. It's a miracle product that won't cost you much, so you should go out and buy one ASAP. If you're not sure how they work, here are a few tips on how to use your beauty blender for a flawless foundation:

## 1. Wet Your Beauty Blender with Water

![Wet Your Beauty Blender with Water](https://img.allw.mn/content/uz/ld/q9li2xhd561528b51ed37045231230_728x385.gif)

The first step to using your beauty blender is to soak it in water. It doesn't matter if you place it in a bucket of water or run it underneath a faucet, as long as you let it soak up as much water as possible. The material acts like a sponge, so the more water it contains, the bigger and fluffier it will look.

## 2. Squeeze out the Excess Water

![Squeeze out the Excess Water](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ov/bs/n3smwq6u561529a4af932554437622_600x401.jpg)

Once you get your beauty blender wet, you should wait a few moments, and then squeeze out the excess water. While you want your beauty blender to be saturated at first, you don't want water to be dripping from it while you're applying your makeup. That would just make a mess.

## 3. Put Your Foundation on the Back of Your Hand

![Put Your Foundation on the Back of Your Hand](https://img.allw.mn/content/mi/v1/jy32hsr3561528b9d81a9189617814_700x467.gif)

Grab your foundation and place it on the back of your hand or on a paper plate--whatever you usually do in order to get it out of its container. Of course, if you're not worried about making a mess, you could just skip the next step by placing the foundation on your beauty blender in the first place.

## 4. Dab the Beauty Blender into the Foundation

![Dab the Beauty Blender into the Foundation](https://img.allw.mn/content/b0/i6/ksha38k5561528bdf2543492143856_600x399.gif)

Now that your foundation is out of the container, you want to dab the large end of your beauty blender into the product. There's no reason to put the product on the sides of your blender or even on the small tip, because it won't be used. You're going to stick to using the large end for now.

## 5. Bounce the Beauty Blender along Your Skin

![Bounce the Beauty Blender along Your Skin](https://img.allw.mn/content/pj/zc/hzk2893w561528b65c1d9970588505_405x270.gif)

The technique that you use is incredible important. You don't want to drag your beauty blender along your face. You want to bounce it along your skin, like a ball. It might feel funny at first, but it's the best way to get your foundation all over your face without leaving any lines.

## 6. Use the Pointed Tip for Small Spaces

![Use the Pointed Tip for Small Spaces](https://img.allw.mn/content/pl/we/epc2we6x561528b725417771974515_405x270.gif)

Beauty blenders have one large end and one small tip for a reason. While the large end should be used to cover your cheeks and forehead with foundation, you should use the small tip to get the difficult to reach areas beneath your eyes and around your nose. That way, you're never going to miss a spot.

## 7. Drag Your Beauty Blender to Erase Mistakes

![Drag Your Beauty Blender to Erase Mistakes](https://img.allw.mn/content/a5/lc/gkj1r5ol561528b8edd50471154874_650x433.gif)

If you mess up your makeup somehow, you should stop bouncing your beauty blender and start dragging it. When you drag it, it'll act like an eraser, which means it can make your blush or foundation lines disappear. Isn't that convenient?

Beauty blenders are cheap and easy to use, but they can change your life. You'll never have to worry about whether your foundation looks natural again, because it always will! Have you ever used a beauty blender before? Do you love them or hate them?
