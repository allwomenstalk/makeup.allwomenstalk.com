# 7 Wonderful Tips for Dramatic Eye Makeup ...

Finding the right tips for dramatic eye makeup is hard! You don't want to be too dramatic and you want to make sure that you keep it classy. Well, if you are looking for all of the right tips for dramatic eye makeup that will keep you classy and looking beautiful, I've got 'em! I've got the top 7 tips for dramatic eye makeup that will make your eyes pop and your makeup flawless!

## 1. Pick a Dominate Shade

The first tip for dramatic eye makeup that we are going to explore is all about picking a dominate shade. You absolutely want to make sure that the shade that you are picking flatters your eye color and goes with your outfit. Just pick a shade that works with the look you are going and you'll be fine!

## 2. Intense Eyeliner

When you are looking for tips for dramatic eye makeup, you want to start with really intense eyeliner. This will not only bring out your eyes completely, but it is a great way to ensure that the color is enhanced. Because I have green eyes, plum is my choice of eyeliner and the more intense I put it on, the better my eyes look!

## 3. Thick Mascara

With intense eyeliner comes thick, thick mascara. Now, I am not saying that you should coat your lashes to be clumpy, but you want to make sure that you are bringing out the color of your eyes and framing them with eyelashes that look like baby doll eyelashes. For me, I use Stiletto mascara and let me tell you, it works wonders!

## 4. Long Eyelashes

Remember how I mentioned thick mascara? Well ladies, this tip for dramatic eye makeup is all about the long eyelashes! Lengthening mascara is out there and you want to make sure that you find one that brings out the most of your length!

## 5. Have a Theme

The next tip for dramatic eye makeup all revolves around the theme. You want to make sure that there is some rhythm or reason to your eye makeup. A theme works great for this!

## 6. Keep the Flow Throughout the Rest of You Face

When you are doing your makeup, why not carry your eye makeup theme throughout your entire face? This will not only really help with your overall look, but you'll look incredible! This tip for dramatic eye makeup is definitely one to follow!

## 7. Toned down Lipstick

Finally, one of the major tips for dramatic eye makeup is to tone down your lipstick a bit when you are doing really dramatic eyes. The reason is because it can make you look a little too made up if you overemphasize your eyes as well as your lips. Just a quick swipe of some lip gloss or some shine and you should be fine!

Whenever you are looking for tips for dramatic eye makeup, keep these in mind! Dramatic eyes are so awesome and can really bring out the color of your eyes in the best possible way. So ladies, what other tips for dramatic eye makeup can you share?
