# 8 Makeup Collections of 2013 I Cant Wait for ...

With all of the makeup collections of 2013 out there coming out, how are we going to be able to pick our favorites? Girls, if you're having problems finding the right makeup collections of 2013 to claim for your favorite, don't worry! I've got your back. Below are my top 8 makeup collections that are going to blow your mind! So, you ready to see what makeup collections I absolutely love for this new year coming up?

## 1. Chanel

Ah girls, the brand new PRINTEMPS PRÉCIEUX DE CHANEL collection for 2013 is their spring collection and it's absolutely beautiful! This is one of my top makeup collections of 2013 and I am so excited for it! It's got all kinds of precious pinks, beautiful plums and just screams spring all over! It's simple yet complex, beautiful and elegant.

## 2. MAC

I am always excited for MAC's brand new makeup collections, no matter what year it is and this year is no different! Their spring collection is not only amazing, but it's called Apres Chic, which just sounds cool! If you are looking for bold colors, bright golds and shimmering eye shadows, you've got to take a look at this makeup collection!

## 3. NARS

NARS is another brand that I absolutely love and I can't wait for it to come out. Their spring 2013 is definitely a winner and it's one collection that I know I'm going to have to buy as a whole! It's going to feature some beautiful satin lip pencils, some amazing creamy concealers and some awesome light-reflecting powders!

## 4. Laura Mercier Arabesque

Laura Mercier is one of those makeup designers that I can't seem to stop buying! Her collections are incredible and her 2013 spring collection is not one to disappoint at all! It's got some dusty pinks, some beautiful rosy colors and it all around looks beautiful!

## 5. Bare Escentuals

Now, I am actually a huge fan of Bare Escentuals. I love their makeup, I think that it works and covers beautifully and I think that it is one of the few mineral makeup brands that really does what it promises. This new collection dubbed the 'remix' collection is incredible! It's going to be all about fun and classical, bold and bright!

## 6. Lancome

Who doesn't love Emma Watson? If you are wondering exactly what Emma has to do with Lancome, well, she's the Lancome girl, y'all! She's going to be the one that is going to head up their brand new 2013 spring collection that has all sorts of sparkle, shine and shimmer! It's going to be ultra-feminine and so, so pretty!

## 7. Dior

I think that Dior makeup collections are typically so brilliant and so bold and – this one is no exception! The Cherie Bow collection for spring 2013 is full of everything you'd ever want out of a spring collection! Lots of dusty pinks, brilliant pastels and tons of shimmer and shine!

## 8. Shiseido

Finally girls, the last makeup collection for 2013 that I am over the moon about is Shiseido! Now, this isn't a makeup brand that I've brought a whole lot in the past, but this collection? It's going to be ideal! It's going to capture everything that spring is about and the makeup is going to be amazing!

So, these are some of my favorite makeup collections for 2013 that I can't wait for! Do you have any other ones that you're dying to know about? That you can't wait for? Give 'em up!
