## Savoring the Experience: How to Use

Using the Lip Reset is an experience in itself. It’s not just about applying a lip balm; it’s about creating a moment of self-care in your busy day. Here's a little glimpse into how to make the _most_ of it:

- **Preparation**: Begin with clean, makeup-free lips.

- **Application**: Gently apply a generous layer of your chosen Lip Reset flavor using your fingers or a clean applicator.

- **Wait & Indulge**: Let the mask settle in and work its magic, ideally leaving it on overnight for maximum impact.

- **Feel the Transformation**: Upon waking, gently wipe off any excess product, and enjoy the _smoothness_ and _hydration_ that remain.

This little ritual is not just about treating your lips but is a gentle reminder to slow down and savor life’s little pleasures.
