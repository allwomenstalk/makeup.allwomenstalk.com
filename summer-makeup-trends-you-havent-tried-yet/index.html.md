# Summer Makeup Trends You Havent Tried Yet ...

Summer makeup trends can sometimes be hard to identify. Summer is a weird time for makeup in general, because sometimes the weather’s too hot to even allow for the different trends and styles you want to wear. However, these summer makeup trends are verified sweat-proof. They can easily be adjusted and altered to avoid any unsightly streaks, and they incorporate a ton of fun summer colors into your look that you probably haven’t tried yet!

## 1. The No Makeup Look

![The No Makeup Look](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vf/pi/g11sn86k55697b57856f2369940210_600x900.jpg)

There are a lot of reasons why summer is a great time to stop wearing makeup altogether. First of all, the warm, sometimes humid air isn’t always conducive to heavy makeup routines and products. Plus, when the weather’s hot, wearing that much makeup can clog your pores. On top of all of that, the sun has probably given you a natural glow, and why would you want to cover that up with heavy makeup? If there were any time to try to forgo makeup or wear minimal makeup, summer would be that season!

## 2. Bright Blue Eyes

![Bright Blue Eyes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tm/s8/w72x6emb55697bdf2b9af360385470_600x827.jpg)

Summer is all about bright colors; so let that show in your makeup look. Either create a dramatic aquamarine blue eye look, or simply line your eyes with blue liner. No matter which direction you decide to go in, this look is sure to turn heads!

## 3. Play up Your Bronze

![Play up Your Bronze](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j3/ug/wliggc4755697c86e5f2b265624646_600x800.jpg)

Like I said before, you probably have a pretty beautiful natural glow from those summer beach days! Play it up with your favorite bronzer. Even if you don’t want to wear a lot of makeup this summer, a slightly glitter bronzer is sure to play up your natural skin tone!

## 4. Try Pastel Eyeshadow

![Try Pastel Eyeshadow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z0/ly/rrgzap1y55697dd60b545370831166_600x901.jpg)

Pastels are such a fun way to incorporate some color into your summer makeup look. They’re more muted if you’re not into bright colors, and the look of pastel eyeshadow is just such a fresh look. It’s clean but still so cute!

## 5. Creative Eye Liner

![Creative Eye Liner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n4/ic/js7838h15569817ea1f52219878848_600x900.jpg)

Get creative with how you draw on your eyeliner this summer. You can easily make a huge statement with simple eyeliner you already own if you draw it on in a completely new and different way!

## 6. Go Glitter

![Go Glitter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oa/zk/sq4xgxxf556982eb44137452530252_600x900.jpg)

Glitter is already a huge trend right now, but it’s especially great in the summer. Even if it’s just a little touch of glitter, you’ll add a great touch of sparkle and shimmer to your summer look! Even if you don’t have a little bit of tan, you can easily fake it with some fun glitter in your makeup!

## 7. Bring Twiggy’s Look Back

![Bring Twiggy’s Look Back](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r1/mp/oban1ipi5569838640cf7777494351_600x795.jpg)

Twiggy’s iconic look has been all over the runways this summer. If you’re looking to bring something mod and retro into your makeup look, do it with Twiggy’s iconic eye makeup look!
