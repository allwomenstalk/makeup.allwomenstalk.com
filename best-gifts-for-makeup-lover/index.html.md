# 9 Best Christmas Gifts for Makeup Lovers ...

Are you looking for some **Christmas gifts for makeup lovers**? We all know a girl who lives for new makeup products and loves trying out new looks. The obvious gift choice for these ladies is makeup, right? Whatever your budget, there are loads of **Christmas gifts for makeup lovers** that you can put under the tree this year. You might want to get yourself something too!

## 1. Eyeshadow Palettes

![eyebrow, eye shadow, eyelash, eye, beauty,](https://img.allw.mn/content/q7/iy/potg8lor5a2c56a274b37956046187_500x296.gif)

Changing up the color on her lids is really fun so an eyeshadow palette is one of the best **Christmas gifts for makeup lovers**.

## 2. Lip Glosses

![hair, blond, human hair color, beauty, eyebrow,](https://img.allw.mn/content/ye/iz/j22h1mqn5a2c56eed8917553616770_500x281.gif)

What makeup lover doesn't love trying out new lip glosses? Any color will bring a holiday smile to her face.

## 3. Lipstick

![lip, singing, mouth, girl, organ,](https://img.allw.mn/content/r2/iu/fjdi8irn5a2c574569d06647198405_400x224.gif)

Lip gloss is great, but a really smooth lipstick is a gift that just about any woman will appreciate. With so many choices, you shouldn't have any trouble finding her perfect shade.

## 4. Mascara

![nose, eyebrow, chin, cheek, lip,](https://img.allw.mn/content/tj/ci/nwj85nhw5a2c577b241e0088511846_500x270.gif)

If long and lush lashes are what she covets most, mascara is a wonderful gift idea.

## 5. Cheek Color

![eyebrow, human hair color, lip, beauty, cheek,](https://img.allw.mn/content/xu/be/u8g21lum5a2c58e1ad5ed470382653_650x366.gif)

A rosy glow is what any girl wants and you can help her get the look she loves with any of these cheek products. Beautiful!

## 6. All-in-one Palettes

![eyebrow, eyelash, skin, beauty, chin,](https://img.allw.mn/content/pn/sj/fb2idtgo5a2c59214d827186059596_479x269.gif)

What could be better than a big case of makeup that has everything she needs in one place? Probably nothing. Which is why you should give her one of these for Christmas this year.

## 7. Makeup Tools

![brush, finger, hand, nail, lip,](https://img.allw.mn/content/ss/qz/b2rv8d3j5a2c589f95b9c800791805_650x362.gif)

Of course, that makeup lover in your life can't apply her new products if she doesn't have the right tools. Here are some great additions to her collection.

## 8. Cosmetics Bags

![lip, eyebrow, beauty, lipstick, cheek,](https://img.allw.mn/content/jj/vb/myso6zup5a2c597ad4489359667949_500x200.gif)

Anyone who has a huge collection of makeup needs a place to keep all that stuff. Any of these bags are perfect and will make her holiday so much better.

![black and white, monochrome photography, photography, emotion, human,](https://img.allw.mn/content/ef/cv/a60fegtv5a2c59d794794749544277_480x270.gif)
