# How to Use Red and Green in Your Makeup Routine ...

It might sound strange to use red or green makeup, but there are definite benefits to both. It’s a good idea to know what you’re doing before you give it a try, but once you figure it out, you are going to love experimenting with these fantastic colors. Ready to try red and green? Read on for some great tips for getting both colors just right. Have fun!

## 1. Try Green on Your Eyes

![Try Green on Your Eyes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f7/f0/zbvsethj566f7898a3c56181053600_600x900.jpg)

Green works well with several eye colors and can really make your eyes pop. Green eye shadow is a great choice and you can choose from shades that range from olive to emerald. Not ready to go green all over? Try a dark [green eyeliner](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/best-eyeliners-for-green-eyes/) in place of your usual black. You’ll be surprised at how much of a fabulous difference this can make. A green smoky eye is always a good option too!

## 2. Pair Green Eyes with Bright Red Lips

![Pair Green Eyes with Bright Red Lips](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/os/f9/mc96o1hl566f78b5ed04b007979309_600x600.jpg)

Red and green are on opposite sides of the color spectrum, which means they typically complement each other well. Mixing green eye shadow with red lipstick is a fantastic look for many ladies. You might need to mix and match to find the two that look best with your complexion and skin tone.

## 3. Try a Green Concealer to Cover up Blemishes

![Try a Green Concealer to Cover up Blemishes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lg/zi/n8qbs8bg5671011ba2477997224397_500x750.jpg)

Most facial blemishes are a bit red and again, green is on the opposite side of the color wheel. That means they cancel each other out. So when you cover red blemishes with a green tinted concealer, you cover the spots and minimize the redness at the same time. This simple step can give you tons of confidence because you won’t have to worry about acne or age spots from messing up your look.

## 4. Go Ahead and Try Red on Your Eyelids

![Go Ahead and Try Red on Your Eyelids](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rp/y1/dikbrbuy5671017b6cd04951413384_600x835.jpg)

You might wonder how you could possibly pull off red eye shadow, but it’s something that is gaining popularity. Just a bit of red eye shadow can give you a really cool new eye look that no one is will be rocking as well as you are. Many people love to combine red and green on their eyelids. It’s perfect for Christmas parties!

## 5. Glam Things up with Super Shiny Red Gloss

![Glam Things up with Super Shiny Red Gloss](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hw/k9/jbof81jd5671025ea741f942263014_500x669.jpg)

Red lipstick is fabulous, but if you want a look with more impact, try a super shiny red lip gloss. True, this look will require a bit of touching up throughout the day, but it will be totally worth the effort. Shiny, red lips are great for work and play so there’s really no place where you can’t pull off some shiny red lip gloss. So stock up today!

## 6. Stain Your Cheeks with Reddish Pink Blush

![Stain Your Cheeks with Reddish Pink Blush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sw/tx/l310lups5671041a7444b153480284_500x717.jpg)

Pink blush is perfect for just about any girl! If you have a deeper skin tone, you can pull off a darker red tinted blush. Lightly dust if on the apples of your cheeks and pair it with red lips and green eyes for a stunning look that will turn heads everywhere you go.

## 7. Pair Red and Green Makeup with Formal and Casual Looks

![Pair Red and Green Makeup with Formal and Casual Looks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/al/s7/gi2hedat56710481822e9442031704_450x800.jpg)

Red and green makeup pairs perfectly with your favorite little black dress, but it also looks great with your weekend jeans and sweater style. The best part about this color combination is that you can experiment with it and find your favorite ways to combine the two.
