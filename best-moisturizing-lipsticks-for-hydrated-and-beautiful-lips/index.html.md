# 15 Best Moisturizing Lipsticks for Hydrated and Beautiful Lips ...

Lipstick is one of those things that can totally transform your look with just a quick swipe. It can easily brighten your face, balance out your look and draw attention to your sexy pout. One of the most important qualities of this must-have beauty product is that it actually feels good on your lips. No matter how great the lipstick looks on, if it dries out your lips or you have to constantly reapply it, you deserve better! Take a look at some of these moisturizing lipsticks that are highly rated for feeling as good as they look.

## 1. Rimmel Moisture Renew Lipstick

![Rimmel Moisture Renew Lipstick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/22104033_0404_599x539.jpg)

If you’re looking for a lipstick that’s more like a lip care product with some color, Rimmel’s Moisture Renew lipstick will not disappoint. Many times we sacrifice comfort for color, but with vitamin enriched Moisture Renew, your lips will be smoother and softer with each wear. Contains SPF 20.

**Price:** $6.99 from [ulta.com](http://www.ulta.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 2. Bobbi Brown Creamy Lip Color

![Bobbi Brown Creamy Lip Color](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/22104032_5906_249x247.jpg)

Long-wearing, creamy and comfortable are just some of the words used to describe this highly rated moisturizing lipstick. Shea butter and plant-based conditioners as well as 13 dreamy colors make this lipstick an irresistible treat for the lips and the eyes.

**Price:** $24.00 from [bobbibrowncosmetics.com](http://www.bobbibrowncosmetics.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

![Tom Ford Beauty Lip Color](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/22104032_8385_349x519.jpg)

His highly sought after beauty products garner rave reviews for their selection of gorgeous shades, moisturizing formula and highly pigmented lip colors. Dress up your lips in exotic soja seed extract, Brazilian murumuru butter and chamomilla flower oil.

**Price:** $49.00 from [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Chanel is known for making classics and their beauty line is full of must-haves! Rouge Coco Shine is one of their most popular and most loved products. Maybe it’s the long-wearing formula, the sheer yet striking color or the deeply hydrating qualities that make this lipstick an instant classic.
