# Travel\-Sized Sephora Products to Keep on You at All Times ...

Like most of the female population, I have an addiction to Sephora. However, because I’m cheap, I often find myself avoiding the regular-sized products and going right for the travel sizes near the cash register. They’re great whether you just want to try out a few new products or a new brand, or you simply actually have to travel and want to keep them handy. This list will provide you with items for both, no matter how you decide to use them!

## 1. NARS Blush and Bronzer Duo

![NARS Blush and Bronzer Duo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o0/pp/ggy9ljd055b23017af38d701211893_600x344.jpg)

This NARS blush and bronzer duo has been one of my favorite go-to products for as long as I can remember. I’ve always loved the full size version, so the travel size is infinitely better. I personally think that the travel size is perfect because it can easily be thrown in your purse for quick touch ups!

## 2. DryBar Detox Dry Shampoo

![DryBar Detox Dry Shampoo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/br/n1/iw88s7je55b2302763a3a189443565_521x489.jpg)

Dry shampoo is an absolute necessity, especially when you’re on the go, but you’d be surprised just how few companies sell their dry shampoo in a travel size. DryBar is one of the most popular hair care lines out there right now, and everyone, including celebrities, raves about their dry shampoo. This is a great purchase, not only to travel with, but to keep in your purse for quick touch-ups in the office bathroom.

## 3. Sephora Collection Express Cleansing Wipes

![Sephora Collection Express Cleansing Wipes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sn/j3/aaqjbhhy55b2303f77b86139378885_450x400.jpg)

You really can’t go wrong with keeping makeup wipes in your purse, and with the $4 price tag on these, they’re hard to pass up. If you’re looking for a new makeup wipe to take with you on the go, pick these up the next time you’re waiting in line at Sephora!

## 4. Makeup Eraser

![Makeup Eraser](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k1/f6/nzr83gjw55b2305c1b450916827472_600x480.jpg)

The last time I was at Sephora, this “makeup eraser” intrigued me no end. It’s such a simple concept-warm water and a washcloth, but the washcloth has magical powers that remove all of your makeup easily. This is a great product to keep with you in your purse if you’re opposed to makeup wipes.

## 5. Tarte Play It Safe Essentials Kit

![Tarte Play It Safe Essentials Kit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ew/vw/qx69x0rr55b2309e7035d947988610_450x329.jpg)

Whether Tarte is your favorite makeup brand or you’ve never tried it and want to, this makes it possible. I love when makeup brands come up with little starter kits like this, because it lets you try out a bunch of new products for a great price (this one is $24) or you can just get smaller versions of your favorite products for a great deal!

## 6. Dr. Jart+ Water Replenishment Cotton Sheet Mask

![Dr. Jart+ Water Replenishment Cotton Sheet Mask](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xf/m4/yr1ljmxh55b230b9a8bae599691942_600x475.jpg)

This sheet mask is great after you’ve spent the day in an airport or on a very long flight. Don’t you just hate the drying feeling that comes along with flying? If you’re looking for a way to combat it, keep one of these handy when you’re traveling and indulge in a little bit of pampering when you get to your final destination. You’ll feel refreshed in no time!

## 7. Coola Sport Continuous Spray SPF- Fresh Cucumber

![Coola Sport Continuous Spray SPF- Fresh Cucumber](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/et/l4/sjfn4vxw55b230d167a9f022962491_460x433.jpg)

I’ve always been interested by water sprays, but I’ve never felt like any were really worth the purchase until I came across this one. This cooling spray not only has a fresh, relaxing spa scent, but it also has SPF 30. I think it’s perfect for traveling days, but even more perfect for days spent on the beach or in the sun. It’s not a chore to apply sunscreen when you’re also getting a refreshing cucumber mist out of it!

What is your favorite Sephora travel size? Let me know what you love and I should try in the comments!
