# 10 Lipsticks with SPF Ideal for Summer ...

Summer is here and with summer, comes lots and lots of sun – why not fight the sun with some lipsticks with SPF built right inside? If you're on the look-out for some great lipsticks with SPF, I've got the list right here! My top 10 lipsticks with SPF include everything from super affordable all the way to a little more expensive and designer. Which one will you pick ladies?

**Price:** $2.50 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Avon is a brand that you would expect the highest quality right? Well, this affordable lipstick not only has the quality, but this is one lipstick with SPF that I'd totally buy in bulk! The colors are plenty and it is a lip plumping lipstick!

![Bobbi Brown Rich Lip Color SPF 12](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/06/2_bobbi-brown-rich-lip-color-spf-12_250x292.jpg)

**Price:** $12.99 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

The next lipstick with SPF that we're going to talk about will not only keep your lips smooth, but it will also protect them! The sun won't stand a chance against this incredible lipstick – and it goes on super smooth too! How cool is that?

![Vincent Longo Lipstain SPF 15](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/06/3_vincent-longo-lipstain-spf-15_500x500.jpg)

**Price:** $23.00 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

I absolutely adore anything that is Vincent Longo, but his lipstick with SPF? It's incredible! It feels so good on, stays hydrated and the fact that it has SPF that will protect my lips from the sun? That's even better!

## 4. Yves Saint Laurent Pure Lipstick SPF 8

![Yves Saint Laurent Pure Lipstick SPF 8](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/06/4_yves-saint-laurent-pure-lipstick-spf-8_300x300.jpg)

## 5. Almay Hydracolor Lipstick, SPF 15

![Almay Hydracolor Lipstick, SPF 15](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/06/5_almay-hydracolor-lipstick-spf-15_500x500.jpg)

**Price:** $2.25 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

I think that Almay is one of the top affordable brands out there and this lipstick? It's got SPF 15 in it! You can wear it to the beach, out for a day on the town or even as a great lipstick with SPF for a date! Let me know what you'd use this awesome lipstick for ladies!

## 6. Elizabeth Arden 8 Hour Cream Lip Protectant Stick SPF 15

![Elizabeth Arden 8 Hour Cream Lip Protectant Stick SPF 15](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/06/6_elizabeth-arden-8-hour-cream-lip-protectant-stick-spf-15_350x350.jpg)

**Price:** $8.25 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Elizabeth Arden is one of those brands that you just want to have in your makeup bag at some point and this lipstick with SPF is the ideal lipstick for your bag! It's got SPF of 15, which is pretty high and great for a day on the beach!

## 7. Chanel Aqualumiere Sheer Colour Lipshine Lipstick SPF 15

![Chanel Aqualumiere Sheer Colour Lipshine Lipstick SPF 15](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/06/7_chanel-aqualumiere-sheer-colour-lipshine-lipstick-spf-15_250x250.jpg)

**Price:** $18.00 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

I love Chanel lipsticks, I think that they feel super smooth and they are super hydrating on my lips! The fact that this lipstick with SPF has actual SPF in it is amazing! Try it ladies, it's so worth the price!

## 8. Korres Mango Butter Lipstick SPF 10

![Korres Mango Butter Lipstick SPF 10](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/06/8_korres-mango-butter-lipstick-spf-10_221x380.jpg)

If you're looking for a sheer and super lightweight lipstick with SPF, this is it! It's vibrant, it's glossy and it's got just a hint of color. It's the perfect lipstick for the summer and for the beach!

## 9. Neutrogena MoistureShine Lip Soother with SPF 20

![Neutrogena MoistureShine Lip Soother with SPF 20](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/06/9_neutrogena-moistureshine-lip-soother-with-spf-20_500x500.jpg)

**Price:** $6.39 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Are you looking for a lipstick with SPF that has a ton of gloss to it? Lots of shine? This one is it! It's actually a lip gloss and it's got so much shine, so much shimmer and so much color to it, it'll make you stand out on the beach!

## 10. Maybelline Moisture Extreme - SPF 15 Sun Screen

_ **Price:** $5.00 at amazon.com_

Finally, the last lipstick with SPF that we're going to talk about is the moisture extreme lipstick from Maybelline! This lipstick feels so good on, is feather light in weight and doesn't bleed off. Try it ladies!

There you have it! My top 10 lipsticks with SPF. I have tried all of these and these lipsticks with SPF have saved my lips on more than one occasion. So ladies, what's your favorite lipsticks with SPF?
