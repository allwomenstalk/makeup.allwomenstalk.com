# Celebrity Inspired Makeup Tutorials You Need to Try ...

Celebrities may have professional makeup artists around to paint their faces, but that doesn't mean that you can't copy their looks. Even if you aren't skilled with eyeliner or lipstick, it won't take long for you to master the following celebrity inspired makeup tutorials. All you need is a little bit of time on your hands and a bag full of different products. Without further adieu, here are a few celebrity inspired makeup tutorials you need to try before you step out of the house:

## 1. Rihanna

There's no one hotter than Rihanna, which is why she's the perfect person to model your makeup after. If you want to create a bold look, then this tutorial will show you how to use dark colors without looking like you're in a Halloween costume. However, it's super daring, so it's best to wear the style when you're going somewhere special, like a big party or an anniversary dinner.

## 2. Kylie Jenner

Despite your opinion on the Kardashians, you have to admit that they're beyond stylish. Even though she's young, Kylie is well on her way to becoming a fashion icon, which is why she's a great person to look to for makeup inspiration. This tutorial will show you how to create one of her earthy looks that comes across as both casual and classy.

## 3. Nicki Minaj

Do you want to stand out from the crowd? Then it's time to wear makeup like Nicki Minaj. You don't see many people walking around with bright pink lips and yellow eyeshadow, so you won't have to worry about looking unique. You're guaranteed to be the only one in the room with the style.

## 4. Megan Fox

Megan Fox is one of the sexiest women alive, so why wouldn't you want to look like her? This tutorial will show you how to get bold black eyes, smooth looking skin, and kissable lips. By the time you're finished creating the look, your confidence will be higher than it's ever been before.

## 5. Beyonce

This tutorial will show you how to create a nighttime look that's perfect for dancing to Single Ladies at the club. Beyonce has amazing cheekbones and dark eyebrows, which is why they'll be the focus of this look. Of course, you'll also learn how to create shimmery eyes and blemish free skin.

## 6. Lana Del Rey

This look isn't all that hard to complete, especially once you get the hang of doing your eyeshadow, because it's the most difficult part of the routine. After you master the eye shadow application process, it won't take you long to do your eyebrows, apply your blush, and stick on your fake lashes. Once you do, you'll look just like Lana.

## 7. Selena Gomez

Here's a sweet look that you can wear on a first date or at a friend's party. This tutorial will show you how to master a classic smokey eye with bold pink lips. There's even a bonus section to this video where you'll learn how to do your hair in order to look just like Selena did in her famous music video.

You don't need to hire a makeup artist in order to look as fabulous as your favorite celebrities do. What star do you wish you could look like?
