# Sizzling Hot Makeup Trends for Everyday Use ...

If you’re all about keeping up with the trends of the makeup world then this article’s for you. These are 7 sizzling hot makeup trends that are perfect for everyday use. It’s always exciting when you can learn new ways to wear makeup. You never know which new trend is going to be the perfect one for you.

## 1. Wear a Nude Lip

![Wear a Nude Lip](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ps/ki/o45kikh6559452be6c948632612242_400x600.jpg)

I’m so in love with this trend and it is hot, hot, hot right now! For a long time, I didn’t think I could pull off a nude lip because I’m a very fair skinned blonde and I felt nude lips just blended in and left me looking bland. But I found that’s not the case if I move the color up and allow my eyes to be the focus. The truth is that there’s a nude for everyone. Don’t give up if you haven’t found yours yet!

## 2. Rethink Blue Eyeshadow

![Rethink Blue Eyeshadow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gh/ff/cqsl4er7559452e0169cc339349908_500x750.jpg)

When most of us think blue eyeshadow, we have visions of that horrendous peacock blue of the 1960s. It’s completely cringe-worthy. But don’t write blue eyeshadow off totally; it can look very pretty when done correctly. Try doing a smoky eye with navy instead of black and see how beautiful the results are. This look pairs perfectly with a nude lip, too.

## 3. Master the Winged Eyeliner Technique

![Master the Winged Eyeliner Technique](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/by/vo/vwfpr4sl559453459e2f4857877706_499x750.jpg)

Winged eyeliner is a lovely and dramatic look, but it can be a bit difficult to learn. There are some great tutorials on Pinterest that can teach you this technique, though. I learned how to wing my eyeliner through one of these tutorials. This look pairs wonderfully with a bold lip. This is a vintage look that’s made a big comeback.

## 4. Go for a Fuller Brow

![Go for a Fuller Brow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/no/kz/gn599wn95594535d01813185943680_500x636.jpg)

Embracing a fuller brow takes some getting used to if you’ve been a long time member of the skinny eyebrow club. It’s an adjustment, to be sure. But once you get used to your new look, you’ll like it better. Look at some comparison pictures of women with only one eyebrow filled in and you’ll see a huge difference. Eyebrows truly are the frame for your face.

## 5. Mimic Marilyn Monroe’s Mole

![Mimic Marilyn Monroe’s Mole](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ws/hb/qqqi4lmv559453e77d59b377266383_470x688.jpg)

I’ve always loved this unique beauty mark of Marilyn Monroe’s. Other women have had beauty marks that they’ve played up too, such as Cindy Crawford. Instead of covering up every spot on your face, consider playing one up. They really are quite stunning and give you a look that’s all your own. It’s a sexy addition to your makeup routine.

## 6. Contour like a Pro

![Contour like a Pro](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mh/rz/la8kc7yf5594540f63b95279764083_500x500.jpg)

Contouring is the skill to have if you want to be on trend with your makeup. Feel inadequate? Don’t worry, it’s a talent that can be learned. There’s a very popular blogger who’s absolutely phenomenal at explaining contouring concepts. You can do a search for the topic on her blog, [maskcara.com](https://www.seintofficial.com/en), and find a wealth of helpful information on the subject.

## 7. Stay Classy with a Red Lip

![Stay Classy with a Red Lip](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gk/bf/mx3i98hv55945464f2a18957237697_600x900.jpg)

Red lipstick is both classic and classy. It’s a trend that ebbs and flows but has never really gone out of style. Red lips are hot and the perfect choice for date night. That being said, you do want to wear your red lips with some caution. It’s important to make sure you’re choosing a shade of red lipstick that’s flattering for your skin tone. You also may want to tone down your eye makeup a bit when you’re wearing red lips to allow them to be the focal point of your face.

These are 7 sizzling hot makeup trends that you can wear every day. What makeup trends do you love right now? I can’t wait to read your comments!
