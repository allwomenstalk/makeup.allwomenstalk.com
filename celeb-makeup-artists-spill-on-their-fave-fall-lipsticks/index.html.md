# Celeb Makeup Artists Spill on Their Fave Fall Lipsticks!

Your fall shopping list is about to get longer because you NEED these lipsticks! Some of Hollywood's hottest makeup artists have spilled on their favorite trends and brands so you can't wrong with any one of these:

## 1. Lipstick Queen

_ Daniel Martin, the man who's in charge of making Kate Bosworth and Jessica Alba look as fierce as they do, shared his favorite lipstick! "My favorite is Lipstick Queen Silver Screen Lipstick (lipstickqueen.com_) in Made It. It's a deep berry with a moisturizing finish."

## 2. Tom Ford

![Tom Ford](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s9/c5/ho47pc57_336x404.jpg)

Rose Byrne's makeup artist, Georgie Eisdell, is all about Tom Ford. She said,"I am loving Tom Ford Cherry Lush and Wild Ginger ([tomford.com](https://www.tomford.com/beauty/lips/)). I love the brightness of both; the pink in Cherry Lush and the cool shade of orange in the Wild Ginger. You can really wear them during the day to put a pep in your step or keep it fun and fresh for the evening."

## 3. NARS

![NARS](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dy/o2/j4ri8r01_333x385.jpg)

Beau Nelson (Nicole Richie and Nina Dobrev's makeup artist) can't stop raving about NARS! "The new NARS Audacious lipsticks ([narscosmetics.com](http://www.narscosmetics.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)) are incredible but the standout shade for me for fall is Lena-a vivid almost neon orange red that looks incredible with a strong brow and a neutral face."

## 4. L'oréal

![L'oréal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z4/eu/vnfc96n9_540x504.jpg)

Fiona Stiles, who's known for her beautiful makeup on stars like Jennifer Garner and Elizabeth Banks shared her current obsession: "My new favorite color for fall is from L'Oréal. They have a new range of Colour Riche Glossy Balm out ([lorealparisusa.com](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.lorealparisusa.com/en/products/makeup/lips/lip-gloss-balm/colour-riche-glossy-balm.aspx)), and the color I am loving for fall is called Petite Plum. What I'm crazy about is the texture; it feels like a balm, but looks like a lipstick. The color is vivid, but has a little transparency to it, so it doesn't feel too intense, and it gives just the right amount of a super flattering plummy color. When a darker color is too opaque it goes goth really quickly, but this color is that perfect chic plum that's easy to wear and feel cozy, like a cashmere sweater."

## 5. Mark

_ Jamie Greenberg, makeup artist to The Big Bang Theory's Kaley Cuoco said: "Mark's All Butter Now in Bare All (avon.ca_). It's the perfect nude hue for fall. Nudes tend to look dry on the lip but his one is extra hydrating because they added shea, cocoa, and mango seed butters-so super creamy!"

Which of these lipsticks just topped your fall shopping list?
