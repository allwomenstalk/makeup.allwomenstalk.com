# 9 Brand New Beauty Products to Add to Your Beauty Wish List ...

With a new season comes new beauty products. While fall is already in full force, there are tons of new beauty products coming out all the time, so many that it’s hard to keep up! I mean, as much as we love makeup it’s still hard to keep track of them of all and remember what we want to add to our beauty collection. But not to worry, allow me to show you nine of the most wanted beauty products you’ll want to add to your beauty wish list this season.

## 1. Giorgio Armani Maestro Fusion Makeup Compact

![Giorgio Armani Maestro Fusion Makeup Compact](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/12002300_4844_450x450.jpg)

One of the new beauty products for fall 2013 that caught my eye was this cream foundation. I rarely use cream foundations because they just don’t do it for me, but this one I could see loving. If you’re familiar with Armani’s Maestro Fusion liquid makeup, which is basically perfection in a bottle, then you’ll adore this compact. You get silky, even coverage that doesn’t look cakey and feels like a second skin.

## 2. Neutrogena MoistureSmooth Color Stick

![Neutrogena MoistureSmooth Color Stick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/12002300_9850_449x430.jpg)

Neutrogena’s Color Stick is a lip crayon that hydrates your lips and leaves a pretty wash of color behind. I think it resembles Clinique’s Chubby Stick Lip Color lot but for less than half the price! Aside from this beauty product allowing me to live out my dream of coloring my lips with what looks like a crayon, it promises to give you smoother lips in three days.

## 3. Sephora + Pantone Universe Elemental Energy Eye Shadow Palette

![Sephora + Pantone Universe Elemental Energy Eye Shadow Palette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/12002300_5129_249x246.jpg)

If you decide to invest in some new makeup this fall, make it this delightful eye shadow palette! I haven’t been that crazy about the Sephora + Pantone collabs thus far, but this one is pretty darn irresistible. I love that you get 16 eye shadows that are all inspired by the four elements and come in different finishes and textures.

## 4. Giorgio Armani Rouge Ecstacy Lipstick

![Giorgio Armani Rouge Ecstacy Lipstick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/12002301_6846_332x394.jpg)

This is the second Armani beauty product to make this beauty wish list and it’s for a good reason. This alluring lipstick is a CC lipstick! The CC craze has finally crossed over to lipstick and I couldn’t be happier. They call this a hybrid product that moisturizes and calms like a lip balm and has the vivid color and staying power of a lipstick. Choose from 35 delightful colors.

## 5. Covergirl Ink It! Perfect Point plus Eyeliner in Golden Ink

![Covergirl Ink It! Perfect Point plus Eyeliner in Golden Ink](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/12002300_3781_409x384.jpg)

Another beauty product for your new makeup shopping list is this glitteringly gilded eyeliner. These beauties are thin enough for a super precise cat eye that applies like a liquid liner but the color has the staying power of a tattoo! If gold isn’t your thing, there’s also black, aquamarine, brown, charcoal and grey. Easy to use, lasts for up to 12 hours and comes in gorgeous colors-sign me up!

## 6. Hourglass N° 28 Lip Treatment Oil

This fancy beauty product is a lip treatment oil that helps smooth your lips, combat dryness, shield against the elements, strengthen the moisture barrier and improve the texture and shape of your pout. It's enriched with plant oils, essential oils and vitamins to deliver a decadent treatment for your lips. Use it alone or layered, day or night.

## 7. Pro-X by Olay Professional Microdermabrasion + Advanced Cleansing System

![Pro-X by Olay Professional Microdermabrasion + Advanced Cleansing System](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/12002301_6553_419x470.jpg)

If you’ve been wanting a Clarisonic but just can’t see yourself paying that much, Olay has a fine substitute for you. This cleansing brush cleanses, exfoliates and performs microdermabrasion on your face all in the comfort of your own home. You’ll see an improvement in skin texture, tone and see a more radiant complexion. The Thermal Crystal Polisher that’s included in the kit is said to exfoliate 7 times better than a scrub. Pretty impressive, right?

## 8. Essie Cashmere Bathrobe Nail Polish

![Essie Cashmere Bathrobe Nail Polish](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/12002301_0795_129x263.jpg)

As much as I’d love a cashmere bathrobe, my bank account doesn’t agree so I’ll gladly settle for this captivating nail polish. This is one of the nail polishes in Essie’s fall collection and they call this shade a true flannel gray. The color pretty much looks the same on your nails as it does in the bottle, it’s a dark gray with some silver shimmer.

## 9. Drybar Detox Dry Shampoo

![Drybar Detox Dry Shampoo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/12002301_7693_249x244.jpg)

The last beauty product I wanted to share with you isn’t exactly new makeup, but it’s a new product for your hair that I think you’re going to love. Drybar is all about the perfect blowout and what other product is essential to prolong a perfect blowout than dry shampoo? This dry shampoo is making waves with hair mavens for its lovely fragrance and its ability to create body, absorb oil and blend in with hair colors.

I’m so excited about these new beauty products and can’t wait to get my hands on them! Playing with makeup and trying new products is seriously one of my favorite things to do. Do you have any new beauty products for fall that you’ve got your eye on? Would you try any of these?
