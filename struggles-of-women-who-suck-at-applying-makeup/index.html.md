# 7 Classic Struggles of Women Who Suck at Applying Makeup ...

Not all of us are pros when it comes to applying makeup. After all, it's much harder than it looks. [Women's Health Mag](https://www.womenshealthmag.com/beauty/a19938062/makeup-struggles/) knows that, which is why they've created a list of all of the things women with poor makeup skills have to deal with:

## 1. Doing and Re-Doing Your Winged Eyeliner Six Times (on Each Side!) before Throwing in the Towel

![person, barechestedness, man, male, muscle,](https://img.allw.mn/content/fx/o3/jpd0sula587a3583e2005003131886_500x297.gif)

You could waste an entire hour doing this.

## 3. Praying Your Eyebrows Are Naturally on Fleek-Because You Have No Idea How to Properly Fill Them in

![Praying Your Eyebrows Are Naturally on Fleek-Because You Have No Idea How to Properly Fill Them in](https://img.allw.mn/content/j7/qo/r7pizd5e564f31bf84b99816804943_500x216.gif)

Makeup isn't meant to cover hair, anyway.

## 4. Getting Really Excited about Going to Sephora…and then Getting Really Overwhelmed

![Getting Really Excited about Going to Sephora…and then Getting Really Overwhelmed](https://img.allw.mn/content/vn/yz/ject7qtw564f31c03f939381266120_500x294.gif)

Everyone that works there is unbelievably beautiful. It's intimidating.

## 5. Buying Cool Brightly Colored Makeup...but Being Too Scared to Use It

![Buying Cool Brightly Colored Makeup...but Being Too Scared to Use It](https://img.allw.mn/content/fw/d5/sysze35l564f31c0a1a97152841089_462x260.gif)

You're not daring enough to pull off some of the looks you'd like to try.

## 6. Never Having Your Lipstick Stay on for Longer than an Hour or Two
