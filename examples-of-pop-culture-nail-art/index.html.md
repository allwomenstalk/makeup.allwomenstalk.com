# 9 Creative Pop Culture Nail Art Ideas You Must Try

I absolutely love pop culture nail art! It is super neat and incredibly fun to try. I've gathered 9 creative examples of pop culture nail art that you definitely should check out. While some of these might not be the most practical for everyday life, they are fantastic if you are heading to a special screening of a movie, a fan convention, or even getting ready for Halloween. Creating these looks is super fun and gives you a chance to express your fandom!

Before we jump into the list, here are a few tools you might need to recreate these detailed designs:

- A fine-tipped detailing brush

- Dotting tools for perfect circles

- High-quality top coat to seal your art

## 1. Little Mermaid Nails

![Little Mermaid Nails](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/07/1_little-mermaid-nails_500x523.jpg)

I love [The Little Mermaid](https://movies.disney.com/the-little-mermaid)! It is by far one of my favorite classic Disney movies, and these nails are super cute. While it might be hard to type with 3D elements for a while, this pop culture nail art example is adorable. Who can resist a tribute to Ariel and under-the-sea themes? Try to duplicate this one if you want to make a splash!

## 2. Lord of the Rings Nails

![Lord of the Rings Nails](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/07/2_lord-of-the-rings-nails_500x376.jpg)

Lord of the Rings is absolutely one of my favorite film series. I have read all of the books and am always ready for a marathon of the movies! These pop culture nails are incredibly detailed, super fun, and represent the epic journey perfectly. Do you think you could wear something this intricate all day? If you love fantasy looks, you might also want to check out some [magical makeup looks](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/magical-makeup-looks/) to match.

## 3. Beauty and the Beast Nails

![Beauty and the Beast Nails](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/07/3_beauty-and-the-beast-nails_500x530.jpg)

Beauty and the Beast is another pop culture nail art example that I just can't get over. These nails are super cute and are the ideal representation of the beloved classic. The sparkles really just make the design that much better, catching the light beautifully. Would you give these a try if you had the right polishes?

## 4. Nightmare on Elm Street Nails

![Nightmare on Elm Street Nails](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/07/4_nightmare-on-elm-street-nails_500x438.jpg)

Oh Freddy, you are even making your way into pop culture nail art, aren't you? The [Nightmare on Elm Street](https://www.warnerbros.com/movies/nightmare-elm-street) movies were some of the scariest films I saw when I was younger, and these nails are ideal for a creepy costume! They feature iconic elements like his striped sweater. Would you be willing to rock these terrifying nails for a day?

## 5. Up Nails

![Up Nails](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/07/5_up-nails_500x517.jpg)

Up is such a heartwarming and cute movie, and these nails really make me want to watch it over and over again. They are so much fun and flirty! Although they are very detailed, if you have the patience, they would be a rewarding project to recreate. This balloon-filled example of pop culture nail art is definitely one of my favorites.

## 6. Snow White Nails

![Snow White Nails](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/07/6_snow-white-nails_500x510.jpg)

With all of the different Snow White adaptations out there, it is a great excuse to revisit the original animated movie and try out some Snow White nails. I love this design and think the little apples and character details are by far the cutest things. It is a timeless look for any Disney fan.

## 7. Tetris Nails

![Tetris Nails](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/07/7_tetris-nails_500x518.jpg)

While this pop culture nail art example might not be practical at all, it is still really cool, isn't it? I love [Tetris](https://tetris.com/) and having a retro game detailed out across my nails is such a clever idea. It brings back great memories of classic arcade gaming.

## 8. Spongebob Squarepants Nails

![Spongebob Squarepants Nails](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/07/8_spongebob-squarepants-nails_500x572.jpg)

Who doesn't love Spongebob? This show became a huge hit and remains one of the best and most recognizable cartoons out there. Why not show the characters from Bikini Bottom some love on your nails? The bright yellow and playful faces are guaranteed to make you smile.

## 9. Harry Potter Nails

![Harry Potter Nails](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/07/9_harry-potter-nails_500x462.jpg)

Finally, we get to my all-time favorite pop culture nail art example: the Harry Potter nails! This tribute to the magical world is awesome, and the Deathly Hallows symbol is beautifully done. If you want to show your Hogwarts pride, this is the perfect way to do it. For more magical inspiration, look at our [favorite Harry Potter beauty products](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/harry-potter-beauty-products/).

There you have it! My top 9 pop culture nail art examples that might not always be practical, but are undeniably awesome. What are some of your favorite pop culture nail art designs that you have seen or tried?
