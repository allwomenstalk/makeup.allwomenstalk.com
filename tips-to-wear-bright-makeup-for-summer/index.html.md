# 7 Tips to Wear Bright Makeup for Summer ...

How to wear bright makeup in summer, do you know all of the ways? I didn't either until I picked up some tips and tricks! Ladies, if you are looking for ways on how to wear bright makeup in the summertime, I've got the low-down! Below, I'm going to go over all of the different ways on how to wear bright makeup this summer without overdoing it!

## 1. Line Your Eyes in Color

![Line Your Eyes in Color](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/06/blue_499x751.jpg)

Lining your eyes in a bright and bold color is a great way to learn how to wear bright makeup in summer! It's also super easy to do! For me, it's all about the bright green. It really brings out the natural green hues in my eyes – the best!

## 2. Tomato Color Lipstick

![Tomato Color Lipstick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/06/r_586x880.jpg)

Believe it or not, but a little red lipstick with some orange in it is the trick on how to wear bright makeup in the summer! It's also a great way to really brighten up your entire face! Try this one ladies, I can promise that you will love it and will probably wear this lipstick all throughout the summertime!

## 3. Highlight the Inner Corner of Your Eyes

![Highlight the Inner Corner of Your Eyes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/06/in_550x689.jpg)

One neat trick when you are learning how to wear bright makeup in the summer is actually highlighting the inner corner of your eyes. Just take a little bit of your brightly colored eyeshadow and dot in the inner corner of your eyes. This will draw attention to your eyes and it looks so cool!

## 4. Don't Forget the Outer Corner of Your Eyes

![Don't Forget the Outer Corner of Your Eyes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/06/o1_500x753.jpg)

Let's say you don't want to do the inner corner, you want to do the outer corner. That's fine too! This is one of the greatest ways when you are learning how to wear bright makeup! Just a little dot of that bright shadow along the inner or outer corner and you'll see a huge difference in how your eyes look!

## 5. Peach Colored Blush

![Peach Colored Blush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/06/p2_500x668.jpg)

Blush is one of those products that you might not think about when you are thinking about tips on how to wear bright makeup but it can absolutely brighten up your face! Just a little peach colored blush along the apples of your cheeks can absolutely highlight your cheekbones and really bring out any and all of your features!

## 6. Purple Eyeshadow

![Purple Eyeshadow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/06/pie_470x705.jpg)

I actually love purple eyeshadow and I gotta say ladies, it is one of the best ways on how to wear bright makeup in the summer! Purple is hot this summer and if you want an easy way to brighten up your makeup, this is the way to do it! Whether it is bright, brilliant purple or pale, lilac purple, just use a little and see what happens!

## 7. Colored Mascara

![Colored Mascara](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/06/mas_500x750.jpg)

Finally, the last tip on how to wear bright makeup throughout the summer that are we are going to talk about is colored mascara. There are tons of different colors that you can wear that are mascara and I promise, they all will look beautiful!

Whenever you are looking for ways on how to wear bright makeup throughout the summer, just remember, it can be something small! Just a splash of bright color is a great way to tie your makeup into summer. So ladies, what other tips on how to wear bright makeup for the summer do you have? Share!
