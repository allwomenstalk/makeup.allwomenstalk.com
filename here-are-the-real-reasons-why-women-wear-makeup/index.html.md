# Finally an Answer! the Real Reasons Women Wear Makeup ...

People have a lot of opinions about women, makeup, why we wear it, how we look in it … the list goes on. Not to say that women are the only people who wear makeup, of course, but we are generally the ones who receive opinions about. People tell us we don't need to wear so much. That we look better without makeup. That we look tired when we're not wearing any. The truth is that there are dozens upon dozens of reasons why women wear makeup, and they rarely have to do with gaining approval from anyone else.

## 1. We Really do It for Ourselves

![We Really do It for Ourselves](https://img.allw.mn/content/m6/wd/k05ila1257006c226a7ed154806179_500x270.gif)

First and foremost, know this: we wear makeup for ourselves. Not for other women, not for boys, but for ourselves. We pick out the products we want. We apply them the way we like. We wear makeup for ourselves.

## 2. Plus, It's Pretty

![Plus, It's Pretty](https://img.allw.mn/content/sj/jf/yfi1h8fq57006c233c4ff060396569_480x270.gif)

Makeup is gorgeous. I mean, in and of itself, makeup is beautiful. Have you ever seen a brand new tube of lipstick? Please.

## 3. We Use It to Express Ourselves

![We Use It to Express Ourselves](https://img.allw.mn/content/xy/hk/uo3u9m1t57006c2453b19278107935_500x281.gif)

Makeup can be an art form. It's a means of expression. This is how some of us choose to express ourselves.

## 4. We Can't Always Correct Everything

![We Can't Always Correct Everything](https://img.allw.mn/content/j9/tk/ytb8wf4e57006c24e5c30079484835_500x283.gif)

Sometimes a girl needs a little help with a spot or an under-eye circle. DEAL WITH IT.

## 5. We Can See Every Version of Ourselves

![We Can See Every Version of Ourselves](https://img.allw.mn/content/vp/yb/s043anns57006c24ebb21447965333_540x304.gif)

Every woman has a range of selves inside of her. Makeup allows us to see some of those different versions of ourselves. There's the no-makeup look, the natural makeup look, the professional look, the date night look, the glam look, and the super fancy formal look – just to name a few.

## 6. Sometimes, Wearing Makeup is Just Easier

![screenshot, No,, Ajus, don't, have,](https://img.allw.mn/content/pu/hb/s0ontk8m58cbb78735b94833616441_500x279.gif)

Because when we don't, everybody starts asking if we're sick or tired or angry or whatever, and that ish is tiresome.

## 7. It's Zen Time for Us

![It's Zen Time for Us](https://img.allw.mn/content/x3/ew/sxlrbi9m57006c252c90d122355183_500x280.gif)

Putting on makeup is zen. It's therapeutic. You can get lost in the process and take time for yourself. It's kind of lovely.

## 8. It Makes Some of Us Feel More Complete

![It Makes Some of Us Feel More Complete](https://img.allw.mn/content/vi/mv/nbfctz2457006c2557777277487920_479x269.gif)

Some women simply feel more like themselves when they're wearing makeup. And that's cool. Why? Because it's their choice, that's why.

## 9. It Can Increase Our Confidence

![It Can Increase Our Confidence](https://img.allw.mn/content/q3/vi/xzqwfsct57006c265731b930804798_500x279.gif)

Other women feel more confident with makeup. That's cool, too. Because it's their choice.

## 10. It's Fun to Play with

![It's Fun to Play with](https://img.allw.mn/content/ma/cr/tzu4oxhb57006c28c4d17368213540_480x272.gif)

True story. I don't wear a lot of makeup because I'm lazy and not good at applying it, but even I get in the mood to play around with [eyeliner looks](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/these-eyeliner-styles-are-perfect-for-any-and-all-occasions/) and new colors sometimes.

## 11. Experimenting with It is Fun

![Experimenting with It is Fun](https://img.allw.mn/content/hm/r7/gezbev8q57006c26414c3797982272_500x281.gif)

Trying new formulas, testing out new products, putting together different color combinations – makeup experimentation is bomb!

## 12. We like to Try Every New Product out There

![We like to Try Every New Product out There](https://img.allw.mn/content/po/e8/bc2cbh5f57006c26eaffa448822150_500x282.gif)

Plus, yes, it is often quite tempting to try out all the revolutionary cosmetics that pop up seemingly every single day.

## 13. We Change Our Makeup with the Seasons

![We Change Our Makeup with the Seasons](https://img.allw.mn/content/pq/at/h2zqy3fc57006c285ce2b942826830_500x281.gif)

We change our clothing with each new season. We may change up our hairstyle. And yes, we change our makeup, too. We have to. Spring colors do not look good in autumn.

## 14. It Complements Our Looks

![It Complements Our Looks](https://img.allw.mn/content/iv/uo/p4u5i8xu57006c28eeea1540440790_500x210.gif)

The right makeup can take a hairstyle or an outfit and make it next level. Sometimes makeup is all about polish.

## 15. It's a Form of Performance and Gender Play

![It's a Form of Performance and Gender Play](https://img.allw.mn/content/oq/lr/jn1psi8s57006c29e4537775653774_625x345.gif)

Makeup is transformative. It's practically magical.

## 16. It Makes Us Feel Badass

![It Makes Us Feel Badass](https://img.allw.mn/content/rb/dq/qvo257mx57006c2b3c19a960388988_479x269.gif)

Daring red lip. Killer winged 'liner. The perfect mascara formula. We walk out like warriors when our faces are in place.

## 17. In Fact, Red Lip Can Change Your Whole Life

![In Fact, Red Lip Can Change Your Whole Life](https://img.allw.mn/content/z9/em/opd3lium57006c2c16412664455008_500x281.gif)

You can go from girl-next-door to femme fatale. The point is, makeup allows us to be whatever we want to be.

However, there's usually only one real reason why women wear makeup – we do it because we want to. If we don't wear it, it's because we don't want to. We are not hiding, we are not lying, and we are not trying to impress you. It's just that we are warrior goddesses and this is our war paint. But tell me – why do you wear makeup?
