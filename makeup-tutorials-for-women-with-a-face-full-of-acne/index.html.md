# 10 Makeup Tutorials for Women with Acne Problems ...

We all wish we had clear skin. Of course, it's hard for some of us to accomplish that feat. It doesn't matter how many acne products we use or what kind of pillowcases we sleep on. No matter what, our faces just won't stay clear. Of course, that isn't a problem, because makeup exists! Here are a few makeup tutorials for women with a face full of acne:

## 1. Acne Coverage

Believe it or not, some of the women you're jealous of because of their "perfect" skin are actually covered in pimples. You just can't see them, because they know how to cover them up. Don't worry, because you can learn how to do the same thing! If you follow the advice in this video, then you'll be able to reduce the redness on your face and cover up all of those pesky pimples.

## 2. Foundation Routine

This makeup routine will show you how to use foundation to cover up all of your pimples. However, you shouldn't be ashamed of what your skin looks like without makeup. The woman in this video is super self-conscious about the way her skin looks before her morning routine, but she was still willing to show the world her face. That's something you should do, too.

## 3. Glowing Full Face

This video is perfect for any woman who has a lot of acne or acne scarring. It'll make your skin look healthy, almost like it's glowing. Even though this tutorial requires a lot of makeup products, it'll help you feel more confident in your skin.

## 4. How to Cover Acne and Scars

Listen to the words that the woman says in the beginning of this video. Nobody is perfect. Even if someone looks like they're flawless, they have things that they're insecure about. That's why you shouldn't feel bad when you step in front of the mirror each morning. Everyone else has insecurities, just like you do.

## 5. Everyday Makeup (Full Coverage)

This makeup routine will help you cover up your acne scarring and make your pores look smaller than usual. It'll also show you how to make your brows look beautiful and how to create a cute eye look. Basically, this video will give you all the info you need to look gorgeous.

## 6. Get Ready with Me

Unlike most women, the woman in this video likes to do her eye makeup before she does her foundation. Of course, you can do your makeup in whatever order that you think works the best. It doesn't matter, as long as you're happy by the time you're finished.

## 7. Acne Coverage Foundation Routine

This tutorial is perfect for women with oily and acne prone skin. It'll help you cover up your "flaws," so that you can feel more confident in your skin. Before you put on any of your makeup, make sure you use a good moisturizer in the mornings and before you go to bed. Then you can start working with your primer and foundation.

## 8. Full Acne Coverage Routine for Red, Blotchy Skin

Get a glowing look even when your skin isn't completely perfect. Cover blemishes, redness, and eczema with this every day makeup look.
