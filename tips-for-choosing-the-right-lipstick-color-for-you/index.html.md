# 7 Tips for Choosing the Right Lipstick Color for You ...

If you have been a little shy about wearing finding the right lipstick you might be asking how to choose the right lipstick color for you. With so many different shades and tones, it’s no surprise we women can get a little overwhelmed at the makeup counter. Follow these 7 tips for choosing the right lipstick color for you and you will be on your way to having a perfectly painted pout.

## 1. Undertone

The first step in how to choose the right lipstick color is to know your undertone. If you have more of a cooler undertone choose colors with a blue tint like rich reds and pale pinks. Warmer undertones can wear corals, red-orange and browns. If you have a neutral undertone you are safe with any lip color.

## 2. Time of Day

Knowing how to choose the right lipstick color for you also depends upon the time of day or the occasion. Lighter colors are best for daytime. Also consider matte or cream finishes over gloss for daytime. Dark glossy colors are great for evening dates and nights out.

## 3. Eye Makeup

How’s your eye makeup? If you are going for a dramatic eye look, tone down your lip color. Go for a neutral lip or lighter color. If your eyes are softer feel free to go bold with your lip color.

## 4. Keep It Simple

If you have ever wondered how to choose the right lipstick color for you, here is a simple trick. Look no further than your own lips. You are guaranteed a home run if you choose a lip color a couple shades darker than your natural lip color.

## 5. Test out New Colors

Discount and drug stores have a wide variety of lipsticks and brands. They are less expensive than department store brands. Feel free to buy a couple of different colors to try something new. In most cases if you aren’t happy with your purchase you can always return the lipstick and get your money back.

## 6. Ask an Expert

If you need further guidance you can always ask the girls at the makeup counters in the mall. They are there to help you and would love to assist in finding the perfect lipstick color. You can ask them all your questions on how to find the right lipstick color for you.

## 7. Going Neutral?

Neutral lip color shouldn’t be the same shade as your skin. It should the same color as your natural lips. If your neutral lip color looks more like concealer it’s all wrong. That’s a look that only works on the runway.

I hope these 7 tips on how to find the right lipstick for you has helped you determine what lip colors to consider during your next shopping trip. Keep them in mind the next time you pick out a new lipstick. Also don’t be afraid to try something new. What tips do you have for picking out the perfect lipstick color?
