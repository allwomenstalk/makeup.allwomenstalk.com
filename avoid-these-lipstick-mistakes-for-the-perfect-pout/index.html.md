# Avoid These Lipstick Mistakes for the Perfect Pout ...

Many women get quite obsessed with using lipstick. Even if we get it wrong frequently, we will still confess to owning more than 30 different shades of lippie: some dark vampy shades; bright and cheerful daytime colors; the glossy stuff for when we want to be alluring; some matte, some whacky; and some neutral thrown into the mix. There you have most women's bathroom cabinet at one glance!

But, is your inner goddess confidence undermined by unhelpful jitters over how to apply lipstick correctly and what colors will be most suitable for the occasion? Fear not, there are some simply steps that can help your inner critic and you to gain the confidence you need to wear a variety of hues for different occasions. Here are 8 of the most common lipstick errors your inner goddess may not even have noticed you were making:

## 1. You Aren’t Exfoliating and It Can Spoil the Finished Look

Chapped, flaky and dry lips won't look attractive, no matter how scrumptious your lipstick may be. Exfoliating before applying lipstick will leave your lips smooth, without dryness or excess flakes. Be sure to regularly moisturize your lips, when not wearing lipstick.

## 2. You Don’t Prime Your Lips before Applying Lipstick

For the flawless skin effect you'll need to prime your skin prior to applying foundation and the same holds true for your lips. There are makeup brands out there that offer lip primers for added smoothness and longer lasting color. You could also use Chap Stick on your lips - apply a quarter of an hour prior to applying lippie for the best result. Lipstick looks its best when applied to perfectly smooth lips.

## 3. You Aren’t Applying Your Lipstick Correctly

Lipstick can be applied the wrong way. Start at the centre of your mouth, which helps you to gain a steady hand and venturing outside of your lips' outline. Whatever you do, avoid getting color into the corners of your mouth!

## 4. You Don’t Understand Why Lip Liner is Important

Not everybody likes lip liner - too many examples of freaky vampire-style dark lip liners can put even the most daring lippie fan. However, lip liner is rather important, since bright or dark lipstick colors can easily bleed. Lip liner will keep your lippie where it's supposed to be and you'll find it's also easier to apply. Choose a nude lip liner, if you're worried about looking to obvious.

## 5. You Forget to Check if You Have Lipstick on Your Teeth

It's just not sexy! Immediately after applying lippie make sure it's not covering your teeth. Simply blot your lips with a gentle touch to remove excess lipstick color which has a tendency to travel to one's teeth.

## 6. You’re Missing the Vital Step of Setting Your Lipstick

Lipstick lasts longer on your lips if you cover your lips with tissue and then dust with a translucent setting powder. You could also use a makeup setting spray or lipstick setter, which some brands produce. You'll achieve a slightly more matte look by using lipstick setter and even your morning cup of coffee won't harm your lippie.

## 7. You're following Color Rules That You Don’t Have to

There are numerous color rules out there: avoid lipstick, if you have large lips, don't apply dark lipstick to [thin lips](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/awesome-makeup-tips-for-thin-lips/). Ignore all of these rules and simply wear what you feel comfortable with.

## 8. You Allow a Lack of Confidence to Interfere

There's nothing so sexy as a confident girl! Don't be intimidated by the choice of lipstick colors or color rules out there. Embrace the color spectrum you like and try different shades until you've found the hues that suit you best.

Now you know, I’m sure you’ll have the best looking pout in town! What favorite shades are currently in your makeup bag?
