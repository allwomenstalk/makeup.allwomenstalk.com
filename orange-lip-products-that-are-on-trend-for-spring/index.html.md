# 7 Orange Lip Products That Are on\-trend for Spring ...

As a beauty blogger, part of my job is to try and report on the latest beauty trends, but when I found out that orange lips are in this Spring, I cringed at the thought of stocking up on orange lip products. I'm glad I gave orange a chance, because I think it's a misunderstood lip colour, and there is a world of orange lipsticks, glosses, and tints to suit every skin tone! So if you're shying away from orange lips, take heart - I've compiled the boldest oranges, purest peaches, and mildest tangerines. There's bound to be something for you somewhere on this list! Here are 7 orange lip products that are on-trend for Spring!

## 1. Red Apple Lipstick Sunkissed Lipstick

![Red Apple Lipstick Sunkissed Lipstick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f9/yo/a5blg4cf_600x518.jpg)

This light, bright true orange is beautiful on fair-skinned girls and redheads, and can be applied full force for a bold orange lip, or sheered out for a sexy stain. Gluten and paraben-free, this non-toxic lipstick looks ravishing alone or paired with Diamond Sands lip gloss, to add a nude/gold shimmer. Perfect for Summer, this is one of my go-to orange lip products!

## 2. Kjaer Weis Sweetness Lip Tint

![Kjaer Weis Sweetness Lip Tint](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yn/m7/sd38767w_600x574.jpg)

This high-end green beauty house is well-known for luxurious, modern lip tints, which lend lips a sheer wash of color that can be built up for more intensity. Formulated with lemon peel oil and organic beeswax, this orange-coral is perfect for those who want to make an orange lip statement that's not too bold but not overly subdued. One of the best orange lip products there is!

## 3. Just Pure Minerals Merengue Lipstick

![Just Pure Minerals Merengue Lipstick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rt/z0/xq8xht04_168x500.jpg)

A bright, pale tangerine, this shade is great for many different skin tones. Very pigmented, but with just enough orange to try out the trend without committing to an all-out pumpkin-orange lip. A hint of pretty shine makes this non-toxic, minty lipstick just perfect. Mix it with Sophia lipstick to lighten it up and create an almost-pastel peachy nude.

## 4. Vapour Organic Beauty Tease Elixir Plumping Lip Gloss

![Vapour Organic Beauty Tease Elixir Plumping Lip Gloss](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w3/xv/mxr5o4ds_506x500.jpg)

Formulated with organic plumping liposomes, this gorgeous hot coral gloss not only boosts your pout with a pretty plump naturally, but also imparts a gorgeous, non-stick shine with lots of vibrant color. Try it alone for a more subtle hint of orange or layer it over the pretty Pin-Up Siren lipstick for the perfect coral.

## 5. ILIA KARMA CHAMELEON LIPSTICK CRAYON

![ILIA KARMA CHAMELEON LIPSTICK CRAYON](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zo/s9/wnq81rf5_600x525.jpg)

Quick and easy to apply, this lip crayon is packed with moisture and rich, vibrant color! Karma Chameleon is a bold, bright coral enriched with sesame, rose hip and papaya oils.

## 6. JUST PURE MINERALS SAMBA LIPSTICK

![JUST PURE MINERALS SAMBA LIPSTICK](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ow/lx/jixr6py3_157x500.jpg)

I had to include Just Pure Minerals in this feature one more time, because this feisty fiesta of orange is too good to pass up. Not for the faint of heart, this vibrant reddish-orange is as beautiful as it is affordable, and looks great paired with the Tangelo Dream blush. Of course, if you're going for a more subdued look, you could sheer this out, but where's the fun in that?

## 7. Burt's Bees Apricot Lip Shimmer

![Burt's Bees Apricot Lip Shimmer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vw/i5/quk63e15_515x500.jpg)

A hint of peachy-nude loaded with sexy shimmer? Yes, please! Packed with Shea butter and coconut oil, this natural lip tint will pretty up your pucker with gorgeous, shiny moisture!

Whether it's bold or laid-back, I think the orange color family is perfect for Spring and Summer lips. What's your take? Will you be rocking orange lips this year? Or maybe you already have a favourite orange lipstick. Let me know!
