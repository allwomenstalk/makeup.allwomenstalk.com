# 7 Best Lip Balms for Kissable Lips ...

The best lip balms leave your lips feeling soft and kissable. There is something on this list for every girl, no matter what your budget or special concerns may be. I believe everyone needs a good lip balm on hand to moisturize and soothe your lips to prevent them from getting chapped. So if you are looking to try out some of the best lip balms, give the ones on this list a try!

## 1. C.O. Bigelow – Rose Salve

_ One of the best lip balms is C.O. Bigelow’s Rose Salve. I have been obsessed with this stuff since I first tried it years ago. It is formulated with rose extract that conditions and soothes the lips. Even better, you can use this lip balm on your cuticles, face, elbows, knees, and dry skin patches as well. As if you needed more reasons to love this product, it was made in the USA and not tested on animals. You can get this amazing lip balm at bigelowchemists.com_.

## 2. EOS – Smooth Sphere Lip Balm

![EOS – Smooth Sphere Lip Balm](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lk/fz/0ipdgr7w_400x271.jpg)

Who doesn’t love EOS Lip Balm? Miley Cyrus even featured this amazing lip balm in one of her music videos. In case you aren’t sure what’s so great about it, I will fill you in. First of all, the shape of this lip balm makes application precise and easy. Secondly, this lip balm protects your lips from harmful UVA/UVB rays. Thirdly, it is packed full of Vitamin E, shea butter, and jojoba oil that will keep your lips soft and smooth. Lastly, it is paraben and gluten free, not tested on animals, and has a 100% natural flavor. You can get this wonder lip balm at [ulta.com](http://www.ulta.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 3. Beecology – Lemon Lavender Lip Balm

_ This lip balm smells so fantastic, you will want to eat it right up. I love the combination of lemon and lavender and it works so well in this lip balm. This product is smoothing and softening, leaving your lips absolutely kissable. Another thing I love about this lip balm is that when you read the ingredients list, you can understand every word. You will fall in love with this natural lip balm! You can get it at beecology.com_.

## 4. Burt’s Bees - Beeswax Lip Balm

![Burt’s Bees - Beeswax Lip Balm](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i2/oc/o1bjgt55_230x230.jpg)

Although Burt’s Bees has added several other kinds of lip balm to their line throughout the years, their original Beeswax Lip Balm still remains the best. Since 1991, this lip balm has been helping people condition their lips. The peppermint oil in the product gives your lips a slight, pleasant tingle and the Vitamin E enriches your lips. You can get this award-winning lip balm by visiting [burtsbees.com](http://www.burtsbees.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 5. Marc Jacobs Beauty – Lip Lock Moisture Balm

![Marc Jacobs Beauty – Lip Lock Moisture Balm](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kr/ox/rt819uct_450x447.jpg)

Talk about keeping your lips kissable, Marc Jacob’s Lip Lock Moisture Balm will do just that! This buttery lip balm is formulated with SPF18, so you won’t have to worry about your lips getting sunburned. Aside from protecting your lips, it also keeps them hydrated and smooth. The minty smell and flavor will have you licking your lips all day long! The lip balm is loaded with natural oils and butters including shea, avocado, andiroba as well as others. You will be obsessed with applying this lip balm! You can get it exclusively in store or online at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 6. ChapStick – Classic Cherry

![ChapStick – Classic Cherry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o3/yz/ibit7p49_320x324.jpg)

This ChapStick is so good, even Katy Perry sang about it! ChapStick is one of the original masterminds behind lip balm, which is why we sometimes refer to all lip balms as ChapStick. ChapStick has many flavors and kinds of lip balm, but there is just something about Classic Cherry. It heals and prevents chapped lips all while softening your lips for a silky feel. You can find this amazing ChapStick at your favorite drugstore.

## 7. Jack Black – Intense Therapy Lip Balm

![Jack Black – Intense Therapy Lip Balm](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w3/ub/eitdzf1y_450x434.jpg)

This lip balm by Jack Black is essential for any girl who spends a lot of time outdoors. They tested this lip balm in the most extreme conditions and found that it protects your lips from the harshest elements: sun and wind. This product is infused with green tea that enriches your lips with essential antioxidants. There is also shea butter and avocado oil that hydrate your lips. The Vitamin E leaves your lips feeling soft and kissable! It’s formulated to be free of sulfates and phthalates and also protects your lips from UVA/UVB rays. You can get this lip balm at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

If you are looking for silky soft lips, than you must try out these lip balms! No matter which one you choose, I guarantee you will love the way it makes your lips feel.
