# 7 Gorgeous Drugstore Lipsticks to Wear This Fall ...

You don’t have to spend a lot of money in order to get a good lipstick, and these 7 drugstore lipsticks are proof of that! These lipsticks will leave you looking your best without hurting your wallet. These brands make quality drugstore lipsticks that you’ll feel good about buying.

## 1. Revlon - Color Burst Lip Butter

![Revlon - Color Burst Lip Butter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/26153819_5640_600x886.jpg)

Revlon is one of my favorite brands, and their drugstore lipsticks are no exception. I specifically chose their lip butter because of how silky soft it leaves your lips feeling once it’s applied. According to a study performed by Revlon, 94% of women found their lips to be smoother, softer, and hydrated. This lipstick is packed with mango, shea, and coconut butter, which is how the product got its name. The lipstick goes on sheer to medium to create the perfect buildable appearance. While all of the colors in the collection are gorgeous, my personal favorite is “Candy Apple” because red lips are always in season.

## 2. Maybelline – Color Sensational Color Whisper Lip Color

![Maybelline – Color Sensational Color Whisper Lip Color](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/26154037_9030_599x596.jpg)

Color Whisper by Maybelline is perfect for girls who want a sheer lipstick. The color pigments are mixed with gel in order to create a translucent color on the lips. Color Whisper is the perfect way to create a subtle statement with your makeup. It’s a great way to try out wearing a new color you are unsure about, like their “Oh La Lilac” shade. My favorite lipstick in this collection, however, is “Go Nude.” A clear nude lip goes great with any makeup or outfit.

## 3. L’Oreal – Colour Riche Colour Caress

![L’Oreal – Colour Riche Colour Caress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/26154159_1718_599x589.jpg)

I know some girls who don’t like wearing lipstick because they think it feels sticky and smothering. If that’s how you feel, than you’ll probably love the Colour Riche Colour Caress line. The lipsticks are formulated to go on light and airy to provide optimum comfort for your lips. You don’t have to sacrifice color quality with this lipstick either. There are many beautiful shades, but I’m a big fan of “Fiery Veil” for autumn. The brilliant orange is the perfect accessory to your fall wardrobe.

## 4. Revlon - Super Lustrous Lipstick

![Revlon - Super Lustrous Lipstick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/26154231_7097_599x942.jpg)

If you want your lips to shine, look no further than Revlon’s Super Lustrous Lipsticks. They are packed with luster to keep your lips looking bright and glossy all day long. Revlon uses a LiquiShine complex that’s loaded with Abyssinian Oil in order to ensure the lipstick is light reflective. You will love your lips this fall if you try out the gorgeous purple “Plum Velour” shade from this line. The coverage is buildable so you can make it as dramatic as you want.

## 5. Rimmel London – Moisture Renew Lipstick

![Rimmel London – Moisture Renew Lipstick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/26154306_9531_599x766.jpg)

With colder weather on the way, it’s important to keep your lips hydrated. Luckily, Rimmel London has made this easy with their Moisture Renew Lipstick. This collection is guaranteed to reduce dryness by up to 65%. You’ll look great while keeping your lips healthy too, because the colors in this collection are stunning. The lipstick is also packed with vitamins to aid in the health of your lips. I just can’t get enough of the “Coral Shimmer” shade. This was a staple of mine this summer and I love it so much, I may just continue wearing the summery shade all winter long.

## 6. Covergirl – LipPerfection Lipcolor

![Covergirl – LipPerfection Lipcolor](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/26154336_4588_599x573.jpg)

Covergirl is one of my personal favorite drugstore beauty lines, so it’s no wonder I’m obsessed with this lipstick line. I think you’ll love it too, because it’s formulated to make kissable lips in just 7 days. The color is rich and will transform your entire look. Some of the lipsticks in this collection are packed with glittery shine, such as “Croquette,” my favorite LipPerfection Lipcolor. Your lips will thank you for wearing this lipstick!

## 7. E.l.f. – Essential Lipstick

![E.l.f. – Essential Lipstick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/26154721_0701_599x548.jpg)

Essential Lipstick by e.l.f. is great for girls on a budget because it only costs $1. Yes, you read that right. For the price of a gumball you can have gorgeous lips. Known for affordable beauty products, e.l.f. has a loyal band of customers who will swear by this lipstick. Each of the shades are gorgeous and have great reviews. Currently, I am loving the shade “Voodoo” for it’s rich red brown color and fun name. I have a feeling this will be one of my lipstick staples this fall.

I hope you’ll love these gorgeous drugstore lipsticks as much as I do. It’s nice to be able to find quality lipsticks that won’t cost a fortune. Do you use any of these lipsticks? If not, what is your favorite drugstore lipstick?
