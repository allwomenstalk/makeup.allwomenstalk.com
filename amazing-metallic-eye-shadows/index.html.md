# 9 Amazing Metallic Eye Shadows ...

Metallic eye shadows are a really fun and festive way to dress up your eyes! I love that you can easily add some shimmer to an otherwise ordinary look or give your eyes a really dramatic look. Even if you’re intimidated by all that shine, there are so many different shades and types of metallic eye shadows that there’s bound to be a perfect match for you!

## 1. L'Oreal HiP Studio Secrets Professional Metallic Shadow Duo in Shocked

![L'Oreal HiP Studio Secrets Professional Metallic Shadow Duo in Shocked](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/01/1_loreal-hip-studio-secrets-professional-metallic-shadow-duo-in-shocked_300x300.jpg)

Some of the best drugstore metallic eye shadows I’ve found are from L’Oreal. Their HiP Studio Secrets line combines rust and gold to create a super flattering look for fall or anytime you want a look that really warms up your eyes. These shadows are super pigmented and you can’t beat drugstore prices!

## 2. Milani Baked Metallic Eye Shadow in Blue My Mind

![Milani Baked Metallic Eye Shadow in Blue My Mind](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/01/2_milani-baked-metallic-eye-shadow-in-blue-my-mind_224x216.jpg)

This vibrant blue eye shadow reminds me of the clear waters of the ocean! This baked eye shadow can be worn wet for a more intense look or dry for a sheerer wash of color. This color would make a fabulous smoky blue eye or even blended with other bright colors to really makes eyes pop!

## 3. Sephora Collection Outrageous Prisma Chrome Metallic Eyeshadow in Golden Taupe #3

![Sephora Collection Outrageous Prisma Chrome Metallic Eyeshadow in Golden Taupe #3](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/01/3_sephora-collection-outrageous-prisma-chrome-metallic-eyeshadow-in-golden-taupe-3_250x250.jpg)

This metallic eye shadow is almost too pretty to use! As much as I’d love to preserve the quilted design on the eye shadow, the golden taupe color is just too stunning to not wear! This is formula is said to combine all the elements of baked, cream and powder textures for a flawless finish every time!

## 4. Lancôme 'Color Design' Sensational Effects Eye Shadow in All That Sparkles

![Lancôme 'Color Design' Sensational Effects Eye Shadow in All That Sparkles](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/01/4_lanc-me-color-design-sensational-effects-eye-shadow-in-all-that-sparkles_328x328.jpg)

This shimmery eye shadow would make a great all-over color or to create a silvery smoky eye. Lancôme makes outstanding eye shadows that are super smooth, easy to blend and highly pigmented. All That Sparkles is a long-lasting shadow that has the perfect amount of sparkle that will really make eyes command attention!

## 5. NYX Single Eye Shadow in Rust

![NYX Single Eye Shadow in Rust](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/01/5_nyx-single-eye-shadow-in-rust_250x250.jpg)

This is for all the ladies who love their rusted red colors. Feel free to play around with this color, you might like to wear the shade as a sheer, all over color or layered over primer to really intensify the look. This shadow is said to be a long-wearing formula that can withstand long hours of wear even with oily eyelids!

## 6. Urban Decay Eye Shadow in Roach

![Urban Decay Eye Shadow in Roach](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/01/6_urban-decay-eye-shadow-in-roach_216x232.jpg)

Don’t let the name of this metallic eye shadow fool you. Roach is an amazing, deep burgundy and metallic brown. You can depend on Urban Decay’s eye shadows to deliver incredible color payoff, crease resistant and easy to blend colors with minimal fallout. Sounds like a dream to me!

## 7. Too Faced Exotic Color Intense Shadow Singles in Petals to the Metal

![Too Faced Exotic Color Intense Shadow Singles in Petals to the Metal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/01/7_too-faced-exotic-color-intense-shadow-singles-in-petals-to-the-metal_192x200.jpg)

Petals to the Metal is a fabulous metallic brown eye shadow mixed with blue that looks as pretty in the pan that it does on your eyes. This unique and unexpected color is highly pigmented and easy to blend on its own or with other complementary colors. What an alluring blend of colors!

## 8. Tarte Amazonian Clay Waterproof Cream Eyeshadow in Seashell Pink

![Tarte Amazonian Clay Waterproof Cream Eyeshadow in Seashell Pink](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/01/8_tarte-amazonian-clay-waterproof-cream-eyeshadow-in-seashell-pink_250x250.jpg)

This shimmering light pink cream shadow might seem demure and reserved but it packs a powerful punch of color. This shimmery shadow can easily be worn alone all over the lid, to highlight the brow bone or blended with other colors to create a gorgeous velvety look.

## 9. Maybelline Color Tattoo 24HR Eyeshadow in Painted Purple

![Maybelline Color Tattoo 24HR Eyeshadow in Painted Purple](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/01/9_maybelline-color-tattoo-24hr-eyeshadow-in-painted-purple_410x410.jpg)

Maybelline’s Color Tattoo line has an incredible collection of creamy metallic eye shadows in the most stunning shades. While the 24-hour wear is up for debate, these long-wearing eye shadows offer creaseless, intense color that all skin types can wear.

Eye shadow is one of the most important beauty products we use. It can really transform our look from bashful to bold with just a simple swipe of the brush! Any of these metallic eye shadows would make a great addition to your makeup kit to create tons of different looks. Do you wear metallic eye shadows?
