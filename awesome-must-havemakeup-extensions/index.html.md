# 17 Awesome Must\-Have Makeup Extensions ...

I've always believed that beauty is found from within, but why not look beautiful inside and out?! Makeup is used to accentuate our natural beauty! Now and then it's nice to get "glamified" with a little extra help to amplify our beauty! Here are some awesome must-have (or must-try) makeup extensions!

## 1. INDIVIDUAL EYELASH EXTENSIONS

Eyelash extensions are one of the popular fads in makeup today! Extensions provide a full, voluminous set of lashes all day!

## 2. SEWN-in HAIR EXTENSIONS

Hair extensions are a dream come true for anyone with short/medium length hair who longs to have long hair temporarily! Track extensions are a long length of hair sewed into regular hair.

## 3. EYEBROW EXTENSIONS

Want brows on-fleek all day, every day?! Now you can have full, thick brows with extensions!

## 4. FALSE STRIP EYELASHES (FALSIES)

Falsies, or strip eyelashes, are a drugstore steal! Get full, quick lashes with these easy pick-ups!

## 5. LOWER LASH EXTENSIONS

Like the top lashes, get full, voluminous lower lashes and make your eyes stand out!

## 6. MICROBLADING BROWS

Want permanent brows on-fleek!? Try microblading! It gives a natural, full look for brows!

## 7. ARTIFICIAL NAILS (ACRYLIC)

Acrylic nails give nails pizzazz! With the different styles for acrylics, you can transform your nails into a variety of shapes!

## 8. GEL POLISH

Gel polish is similar to acrylic, but gel nails are stronger, shinier, and more durable! Gel can go on natural nails or acrylic!

## 9. CLIP-in HAIR EXTENSIONS

Want long hair, but not permanent? Try clip-in extensions- place them on whenever and take them off whenever!

## 10. GLUE-in HAIR EXTENSIONS

Glue-in extensions are popular and great! A strand of extension is glued to natural hair and fused with a hot tool. They last for about 3-6 months!

## 11. TAPE-in HAIR EXTENSIONS

Tape-in hair extensions are good to last between 5-6 months depending on care, plus they're luscious and gorgeous! The extensions and natural hair are connected between two bits of sellotape, creating an image of lengthy hair!

## 12. MICRO-BEAD HAIR EXTENSIONS

Single strand micro-bead extensions are done by clamping little beads to your own hair! They can last between 3-4 months!

## 13. HALO HAIR EXTENSIONS

A halo hair extension is a weft of hair on a clear line that is placed on the crown of your head and covered with natural hair.

## 14. YOUNIQUE EYELASHES

Want to accentuate your natural lashes? You can! Use Younique 3D fiber mascara to give natural lashes length and volume!

## 15. LIP PLUMPER

Want Kylie Jenner lips without the needle? Now you can get them by utilizing a lip plumping lip stick or gloss! Always make sure to do your research on products like these to ensure your safety first!

## 16. COLORED CONTACTS

Colored contacts complete an overall look by providing a faux eye color! One of the longest trends still going strong!

## 17. FAUX EYEGLASSES

Faux glasses have come to light as a must-have extension or accessory to complete an overall makeup look! Real or not, girls look fab rocking these eye statements!
