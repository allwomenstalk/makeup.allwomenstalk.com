# Hottest Fall Lipsticks Already Hitting the Shelves ...

Are you getting tired of wearing the same old lipstick? It might be time to retire that summer lipstick and swap it out for something a little more fall appropriate. Even though some parts of the world might still be soaking up the sun, I say it’s never too early to start planning your next beauty move! We’ve rounded up some of the hottest lipsticks for fall 2016 that we think you’re going to love and the best part is that they’re already available for you to scoop up. Take a look at the hottest lip colors to wear this fall and let us know your favorite in the comments!

## 1. Lancôme L'Absolu Rouge in 359 Hypnotique

![nail polish, lipstick, cosmetics, lip, LANCOME,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xe/ib/h6kkcqm057d36c5f65ee7891781625_480x599.jpg)

Deeper lip colors are synonymous with fall and this grape-hued lip color is to die for! I am in LOVE with this lipstick. It’s not just because of the gorgeous color, but also because it’s formulated with the same technology as Lancôme’s best-selling moisturizer so it keeps lips super moist which is perfect for fall. This rich lipstick lasts up to 8 hours and contains vitamin E to keep lips supple and smooth.
Available at [ulta.com](http://www.ulta.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for $32.

## 2. Anastasia Beverly Hills Liquid Lipstick in Carina

![pink, lip, product, lip gloss, cosmetics,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qp/yd/w5angpik57d36c5f6aa83904412668_500x500.jpg)

Next up, we’ve got a liquid lipstick from none other than the queen of brows, Anastasia Beverly Hills. This liquid lipstick is said to have a next level matte finish with tons of staying power but won’t leave your lips feeling parched. Cherry red lips were all over the fall runway and it’s such a classically pretty shade, no matter what season you’re in.
Available at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for $20

## 3. Tom Ford Lip Contour Duo in Fling It on

_ Leave it to Tom Ford to create a genius lip product in the most amazing shades. Although all of the lip colors are incredibly beautiful, Fling It On is a nude-y pink color that you can wear now and later in the year. The unique thing about this product is that you’ve got a lip shaping pencil on one side and a full coverage lipstick on the other. This super slim duo makes application and touch-ups a breeze.
Available at tomford.com_ for $53

## 4. Yves Saint Laurent Vinyl Cream Lip Stain in Burgundy Vibes

![Yves Saint Laurent, perfume, cosmetics,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b7/qs/jcx5yytk57d36c60016f5676777372_450x447.jpg)

Pigmented lips are expected to be big in the fall and I’m loving the burgundy vibes we’re getting from YSL. Don’t let the name fool you. It’s called a cream lip stain but the swatches I’ve seen are highly pigmented, opaque, and just the right amount of glossy. The unique diamond-shaped applicator makes application even easier and you can get full coverage in just one swipe.
Available at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for $36

## 5. NARS Audacious Lipstick in Kate

![lipstick, cosmetics,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/it/u6/y8g1l7jq57d36c5fee059746791602_450x448.jpg)

This soft [purple lipstick](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/tips-on-how-to-wear-purple-lipstick-like-a-pro/) is deep enough for fall but also bright enough to wear now. Audacious is Nars’ newest addition to their line of lipsticks that’s said to be satiny smooth and instantly dress up your lips in full coverage color that’s moisturizing and comfortable to wear all day. This is a fun color that’ll brighten up any outfit!
Available at [sephora.com](https://goo.gl/lYMqtr) for $32

## 6. The Estée Edit Mattified Lipstick in Sunday Vibe

![lipstick, cosmetics, eye,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tc/gr/yl82cd6d57d36c605c09b432921402_426x512.jpg)

Despite its appearance, this is actually a matte lipstick that contains some jojoba oil that allows you to stay on trend without having to suffer through dry lips. This peachy-nude color is perfect for those time you want to emphasize your natural lip color.

Available at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for $22

## 7. Bite Beauty Multistick in Cocoa

![product, skin, cosmetics, eye, lipstick,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wu/z9/yfx557xt57d36c61379b6612734156_450x446.jpg)

If you're a fan of the brown lips of the '90s, what better time to work it into your makeup rotation than in fall? This isn't your ordinary brown lipstick, though. Bite Beauty's Multistick goes on creamy and wears like powder on the eyes, lips, and cheeks.

Available at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) $24

OK, ladies, you've gotta tell us which lipstick color you'd wear. As much as I'm drawn to the deeper, darker shades, I always tend to go for nudes. What about you?
