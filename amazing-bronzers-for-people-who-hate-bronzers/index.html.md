# 7 Amazing Bronzers for People Who Hate Bronzers ...

This one’s for all the ladies out there who don’t believe there are stunningly amazing bronzers that will work for you. Whether you hate dealing with bronzers or you’re a beginner, I’ve rounded up what I think are the best bronzers for anyone who shudders at the thought of walking around with telltale streaks or muddy-looking cheeks. I know, bronzers can be tricky to work with and if you don’t choose the right shade, even the most amazing bronzers can make your complexion look unnatural. Get to know some of these gorgeous bronzers and show off your new found glow!

## 1. Guerlain Terracotta Bronzing Powder

![Guerlain Terracotta Bronzing Powder](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ql/nc/aovbu3hj_375x368.jpg)

Guerlain’s Terracotta Bronzing Powder has been on must-have lists for years. Honestly, I think it’s been around longer than I’ve been wearing bronzer and it’s still a favorite for many makeup lovers. What makes this one of the most amazing bronzers is that it’s formulated not to leave any lines or marks and leaves a hint of moisture. With eight stunning shades that suits skin tones of all types, you can put your bronzer fears aside and rock that naturally sun-kissed look you desire. Available at Sephora for $52.

## 2. Wet N Wild Color Icon SPF 15 Bronzer

![Wet N Wild Color Icon SPF 15 Bronzer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xw/ec/o3nsxc33_315x305.jpg)

Some of the best bronzers in stores today cost less than $10, so don’t let the price prevent you from giving bronzer a chance. Wet N Wild makes one of the most popular drugstore bronzers and for less than $5, it’s definitely something to experiment with. This is a no-nonsense bronzer that provides long lasting, blendable and buildable color with sun protection. Available at Walmart for $3.93.

## 3. ESTEE LAUDER Bronze Goddess Powder Bronzer

![ESTEE LAUDER Bronze Goddess Powder Bronzer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a7/0r/y4jlfsnb_450x274.jpg)

With Estee Lauder’s Bronze Goddess Powder Bronzer, you can rock a golden glow whenever and wherever you want. Lightly dust this on your face, neck and décolletage for an alluring, natural glow that gives you color without any unnecessary shimmer. This product is oil-free and formulated to keep shine at bay so there’s no need to worry about excess shine. Available at Sephora for $36.

## 4. Covergirl Queen Collection Natural Hue Minerals Bronzer

![Covergirl Queen Collection Natural Hue Minerals Bronzer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fy/ke/fqx49g8g_300x297.jpg)

This is another immensely popular drugstore bronzer and judging from the rave reviews, it’s just as good as a department store brand bronzer at a fraction of the price! This mineral bronzer comes in three natural shades made especially for darker skin tones and controls oil, blends flawlessly and even comes with a money-back guarantee! Available at Drugstore.com for $6.63.

## 5. MARC JACOBS BEAUTY O!Mega Bronze

![MARC JACOBS BEAUTY O!Mega Bronze](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ui/qs/gqi2cjqi_450x416.jpg)

Marc Jacobs should’ve forayed into the world of beauty a long time ago because all of his products are getting nothing but praise from the beauty gods. Case in point, O!Mega Bronze. This baby comes in one universal shade with advanced pigment technology to impart a seamless, matte glow to your face and body. It’s silky smooth, lasts all day and looks totally natural. Available at Sephora for $49.

## 6. Rimmel Natural Bronzer

![Rimmel Natural Bronzer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hf/gh/mwcvv8mo_600x545.jpg)

Rimmel London makes another great starter bronzer. It comes in three natural-looking shades that’ll help warm up your complexion in seconds. Use this matte bronzer to contour or just give your skin a natural sun-kissed look at a bargain price. Mix some of this with some body lotion and warm up your entire body! Available at Walmart for $3.97.

## 7. TARTE Amazonian Clay Matte Waterproof Bronzer

![TARTE Amazonian Clay Matte Waterproof Bronzer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w2/tg/x65gz35q_450x448.jpg)

I’m not sure what it is about Amazonian clay, but all of Tarte’s products that contain it are nothing short of amazing. Take this bronzer for instance, it’s matte yet won’t leave your face looking dull or muddy and you can easily blend it and build it up. Another fab feature is that this bronzer is waterproof so you can rock it at the pool or beach and you’ll still be looking fierce even after a long day out. Available at Ulta for $30.

Don’t let your fear of streaky or orange-y looking bronzer prevent you from trying out this incredible beauty product. Bronzer can slim your features, give you a natural-looking tan and give you that sexy glow that looks like you just got back from an island vacay. I think any of these products would make a great starter bronzer for anyone looking to broaden their makeup horizons. Which bronzer do you think is the best for a bronzer novice?
