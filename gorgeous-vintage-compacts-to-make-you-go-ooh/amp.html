1 Green Art Deco

Via Let Me Powder My Nose

When it comes to vintage compacts, Art Deco was definitely the golden age. Art Deco was a style of the 1920s-1940s characterized by bold colors and geometric lines.

***

Green Art Deco compacts were incredibly popular during the 1920s-1940s, and were often made with enamel, chrome, and Bakelite. They featured bold colors and geometric lines, and were often decorated with precious stones or intricate designs. Many of the designs were inspired by the Art Deco movement, which was a popular art style of the time. Today, these vintage compacts are highly sought after by collectors.
