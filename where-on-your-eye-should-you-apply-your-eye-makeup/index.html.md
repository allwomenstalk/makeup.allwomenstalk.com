# Where on Your Eye Should You Apply Your Eye Makeup ?

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/be/kj/zfxof4en565318e31ab11834327231_600x450.jpg)

You'll never make a mistake applying makeup again, as long as you know where all of your products are meant to go. If you're not sure, [Good Housekeeping](https://www.goodhousekeeping.com/beauty/makeup/a35631/how-to-apply-eye-makeup-guide/) has you covered.

## 1. Brow Bone

![Brow Bone](https://img.allw.mn/content/zv/hl/w9fnn7yv565318bae537b184333759_500x227.gif)

"Add a matte or shimmery pale eyeshadow directly under the brow" to make your eyebrows pop.

## 2. Lid

![eyebrow, face, eyelash, eye, nose,](https://img.allw.mn/content/v1/mg/eqsqcpbn587a2c9c28b03335491109_500x310.gif)

Your main shade of shadow should go here.

## 3. Crease

![Crease](https://img.allw.mn/content/i5/wb/y2m7u3oz565319132f0a3299109784_498x277.gif)

Apply a darker shade here to add dimension to your eye.

## 4. Outer V

![Outer V](https://img.allw.mn/content/u6/vp/dgij5sh2565318bae2bc5207613355_500x244.gif)

This is the furthest spot you should place dark shadow.

## 5. Upper Lashline

![Upper Lashline](https://img.allw.mn/content/fa/yf/u47oa57p565318bc3fb47375857114_500x281.gif)

Use eyeliner, eye shadow, or mascara here.

## 6. Waterline

![Waterline](https://img.allw.mn/content/bl/z8/rqtzvbnj565318bc0091c023332995_250x131.gif)

Create a smokey look with dark liner or open up your eyes with a nude liner.

## 7. Lower Lash Line

![color, face, cheek, eye, eyebrow,](https://img.allw.mn/content/dk/aw/uil0fl2y587a2c9c5a286666347449_500x280.gif)

You can place liner and shadow here to define the eyes.

## 8. Tear Duct

![Tear Duct](https://img.allw.mn/content/cw/bm/ass91q1l565318bbbb630961941663_498x214.gif)

This is where you can place shimmery shadow to make your eyes look more wide and awake.
