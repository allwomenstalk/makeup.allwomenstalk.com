# 11 Awesome Makeup Tips for Green Eyes ...

**Makeup tips for green eyes** are a game-changer, aren't they? Honestly, if I had a dollar for every time someone with [mesmerizing green eyes](#1) asked me for makeup advice, I'd probably be sipping mimosas on a beach somewhere instead of writing intros. A quick glance in the mirror reassures me that the struggle-and joy-of making those irises pop is very real. As we sail through 2024, let's dive into some brilliant makeup tips that will not only make your green eyes sparkle but also turn more than a few heads.

One of the most exciting things about having _green eyes_ is the versatility of _eyeshadow_ palettes you get to play with. Seriously, the range is like being a kid in a candy store! From shimmering golds to deep purples, the world is your oyster. And speaking of oysters, did you know that the right _eyeliner_ can make your eyes look as lustrous as pearls? We're talking about colors like [bold blues and intense blacks](#3). Yup, it's a thing, and it works.

Now, let's talk _mascara_. Oh, the drama a good coat of mascara can add! Think of it as the cherry on top of your _makeup_ sundae. Going waterproof? Great! Opting for volumizing? Even better! And while we're at it, remember that eyeliner technique you were skeptical about? Trust me, put the cats back in cat-eye! The effect on _green eyes_ is undeniably chic. And hey, if you mess it up, just call it a "smoky eye" and move on with your fabulous self.

You know, a [highlighter](#4) can be your best friend or your worst enemy, like that roommate who knows all your secrets. When used correctly, it can make your eyes glow like you've just emerged from a high-end spa. But use too much and you'll look like a disco ball from the 80s-fun at parties, not at brunch. The secret lies in subtlety and knowing exactly where to swipe.

A little personal anecdote: I've been experimenting with [different blush shades](#8) since the 90s. And guess what? I recently discovered that a soft peach shade does wonders for green eyes. Seriously, where was this tip when I was channeling my inner grunge queen?

Considering the buzz around makeup trends in 2024, incorporating just a few savvy tips from these sections will revolutionize your makeup game. So, buckle up and let’s embark on this fabulous journey together. Who knows? Your green-eyed glam may become the talk of the town!

P.S. Spoiler alert: We’ve got some other exciting tips lined up like [perfecting the art of blending eyeshadow](#7). Trust me, you won’t want to miss it.

## 1. Warm Bronze

![hair,eyebrow,face,nose,hairstyle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u3/xz/b8tgc_hair_eyebrow_face_nose_hairstyle_420x584.jpg)

Green eyes are all about the warm colors, the bronzes and golds and I gotta say, bronze eyeshadow is ideal for green eyes! This makeup tip for green eyes really works beautifully and will really make the green of your eyes pop right out! Trust me ladies, it's beautiful!

## 2. Copper Tones

![eyebrow,hair,face,nose,beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/08/22.jp_eyebrow_hair_face_nose_beauty_445x600.jpg)

Just like bronze, this makeup tip for green eyes is all about the warmer tones. Copper tones work great for any and all green eyes because it really makes them pop and really makes them look beautiful. For my eyes, I always layer on a bit of shimmer to really let my eye color deepen!

## 3. Play with Purples

![eyebrow,face,color,cheek,eye,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/08/32.jp_eyebrow_face_color_cheek_eye_500x635.jpg)

Purples and greens are two colors that go really well together! Ladies, if you are looking for the right makeup tips for green eyes, play with the purple eyeshadows and the violet eyeliners, see what works best for you!

## 4. No Silver

![eyebrow,face,hair,cheek,nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/an/jt/tlve8_eyebrow_face_hair_cheek_nose_375x500.jpg)

One color that does not exactly work for green eyes is silver. Now, I am not talking about jewelry of course, but silver and gray eyeshadows do not exactly look all that great with green eyes. They conflict a little bit. Stick to warm colors and plums.

## 5. Skip the Black Liner

![eyebrow,face,hair,cheek,eye,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q9/un/ooxlo_eyebrow_face_hair_cheek_eye_500x700.jpg)

Now, I wouldn't say that you have to skip the black liner all of the time, but this makeup tip for green eyes is all surrounding making the green pop. Plum and violet eyeliner is much better for green eyes and really will make the green in your eyes stand out that much more!

## 6. Darker Greens

![hair,face,eyebrow,nose,hairstyle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gv/s5/whce3_hair_face_eyebrow_nose_hairstyle_343x480.jpg)

While a lot of makeup tips for green eyes say you need to avoid all greens, I don't think that is true! If you have light green eyes, framing them in darker green or allowing some dark green eyeshadow is great! Remember, just go darker or lighter than your own green eyes color.

## 7. Peach Blush

![color,face,pink,white,red,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ml/lr/t0hc4_color_face_pink_white_red_533x800.jpg)

Another makeup tip for green eyes is using peaches and cream blush. Whether it is cream or it is powder, I gotta say, peaches and cream blush really will make your eye color pop out!

## 8. Red-Based Shadows

![face,eyebrow,hair,red,cheek,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xh/r8/tumqt_face_eyebrow_hair_red_cheek_600x900.jpg)

For your eye shadows in general, try to find colors that are red-based rather than blue-based. Red-based colors bring out the beautiful green in your eyes even more!

## 9. Pile on the Mascara

![color,hair,face,human hair color,nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l8/jx/etmvg_color_hair_face_human-hair-color_nose_427x640.jpg)

Green-eyed girls, don't be afraid to pile on the mascara! Green eyes are so rare and breathtaking that you should want them to be noticed! You should wear extra-lengthening mascara and attract attention to your eyes.

## 10. Brown Mascara

![hair,eyebrow,face,nose,black hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b6/q7/zbbr5_hair_eyebrow_face_nose_black-hair_399x600.jpg)

Brown mascara can look subtly beautiful on women with green eyes! It's simple, yet makes a considerable difference in your appearance!

## 11. Pinks and Plums

![eyebrow,face,cheek,nose,eyelash,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ef/ak/hqjof_eyebrow_face_cheek_nose_eyelash_340x500.jpg)

Finally ladies, the last makeup tips for green eyes is to use the pinks and plums! Whether it is in your eyeliner or in your eyeshadow, incorporate the colors somehow and it'll really make your eye color pop!

So ladies, there are all of my makeup tips for green eyes, what tips do you have? Have you ever used any of these makeup tips for green eyes? Give 'em a try!
