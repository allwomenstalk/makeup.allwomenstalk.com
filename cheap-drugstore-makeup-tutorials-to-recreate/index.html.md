# 7 Cheap Makeup Tutorials for Girls Short on Cash but Full of Glam ...

Not all of us are rich enough to buy Urban Decay makeup palettes and Kylie Jenner's lip kit. We stick to using the eyeshadows and lip glosses that we find in our local drugstores, because they're just as gorgeous. If you're sick of listening to beauty gurus talk about fifty dollar products and need an affordable way to do your makeup, here are a few cheep drugstore makeup tutorials that you should enjoy:

## 1. Full Face Drugstore Makeup

This tutorial will show you how to create a makeup look that's sexy, but soft. When you're finished, you're going to end up with pouty pink lips, soft cheeks, and a sexy cat eye. Of course, you're also going to be applying concealer and setting powder in order to make your skin look as flawless as possible.

## 2. Flirty Drugstore Makeup

Technically, this video was made for Valentine's Day, but you don't have to wait for February to roll around again to do your makeup like Zoella does in this tutorial. It includes a sexy red lip, golden cheeks, and subtly sultry eye makeup. It's perfect to wear during any day of the year, because it'll make you look fun and flirty.

## 3. Waterproof Drugstore Makeup

It'll be summer before you know it. That's why you should think about buying some waterproof makeup. That way, you can take a dip in the pool while wearing the same makeup that you're walking around on the beach with. This tutorial will show you which concealers, cream eyeshadows, and eyeliner to buy if you want to enter the water without ruining your beautiful makeup.

## 4. Everyday Drugstore Makeup

If you love wearing soft, pink colors, then this is another tutorial that you're going to adore. After it, it'll work well for any skin color and for any occasion. You can wear it to lunch or to work, and when it's time for you to go out on the town, all you have to do is throw on some dark liner and you're ready for the nightlife.

## 5. Affordable Drugstore Makeup Tutorial

If you're not a fan of wearing light colors, then this tutorial will show you how to wear dark colors. This look is done with cool tones and an affordable makeup palette that won't hurt your wallet. Of course, if you only want to buy a few shades from the palette, you can buy each one for two dollars per piece.

## 6. "No Makeup" Tutorial - Drugstore

This tutorial will show you how to even out your skin tone by covering up your dark circles and red spots. Once your foundation and concealer are applied, the rest is easy. You're only going to apply a little bit of lip color, mascara, and blush. It's easy, which means that anyone can do it!

## 7. Pink Drugstore Makeup

Here's one last tutorial that'll show you how to create a hot makeup look with only drugstore products. It'll teach you how to do your brows, contour your face, and create perfect cat eyes. That means that you should take the time out to watch it, because you might learn some valuable information.

You don't need to spend hundreds of dollars in order to look beautiful. Your makeup can still look amazing while using affordable products from the drugstore. What's your favorite (cheap) makeup product?
