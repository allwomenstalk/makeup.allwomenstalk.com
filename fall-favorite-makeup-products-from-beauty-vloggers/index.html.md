# Fall Favorite Makeup Products from Beauty Vloggers ...

YouTube is one of the best places to look for makeup tutorials. It doesn't matter if you're a master at applying eyeliner or just a novice, because there are videos for every type of woman. If you're looking for new products to buy, you should follow advice from your favorite beauty vloggers. They've tested loads of different products, so they know what they're talking about. Here are a few of their best suggestions:

## 1. YSL Lipstick #54

![YSL Lipstick #54](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hu/cu/dpj6r78w5606b69495198178431461_336x392.jpg)

$36 at [yslbeautyus.com](http://www.yslbeautyus.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

You've probably heard of Zoella before. She always has plenty of helpful advice on hair, makeup, and fashion. That's why you should take her advice and buy YSL Lipstick 54. She claims that it has a beautiful bright color and is super moisturizing, which means it'll keep your lips feeling fresh all day long.

## 2. Sally Hansen Xtreme Nail Color

![Sally Hansen Xtreme Nail Color](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h5/uo/w4airds15606b694b14c9805849906_480x464.jpg)

$3 at [bit.ly](https://rstyle.me/n/srvvry2tw)

Angela Lanter loves to paint her nails. When she does so, her favorite product to use is Sally Hansen Xtreme Nail Color. The brand comes in all sorts of cute, sparkly colors, so you'll have a variety to pick from. The best part? They're super cheap, so you can buy a handful of them at a time.

## 3. Olay Silky Berry Cleansing Body Wash

![Olay Silky Berry Cleansing Body Wash](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ev/il/i7ubtj825606b697c6e2f014195968_600x569.jpg)

$18 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This is a suggestion from everyone's favorite lady, Bethany Mota. She recently won Choice Web Star at The Teen Choice Awards, so you know that she has great advice. According to her, this cleansing body wash smells delicious. Not only that, but it will keep your skin feeling soft and smooth all day long.

## 4. Kat Von D Tattoo Liner

![Kat Von D Tattoo Liner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cg/r0/aymmjcg15606b696a9208359053158_600x572.jpg)

$19 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

If you've been on the lookout for a good eyeliner, Ingrid Nilsen suggests buying Kat Von D's brand. The application of it is effortless, it creates dramatic lines, and it will stay on for the entire day. What more could you ask for in an eyeliner?

## 5. Tanya Burr Cosmetics Lashes

![Tanya Burr Cosmetics Lashes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z7/ru/rovdj8ru5606b696a5aec101489340_600x593.jpg)

$5.32 at [falseeyelashes.co.uk](https://ui2.awin.com/closedmerchant.php?mid=26593&aid=85386)

It shouldn't surprise you that Tanya Burr thinks that you should buy fake lashes from her own line. They're called Bambi Eyes, because they'll make you look as adorable as a baby deer. These handmade lashes even come with lash glue, so you don't have to worry about buying it separately.

## 6. Gigi Gorgeous Wild Madagascar Vanilla Body Lotion

![Gigi Gorgeous Wild Madagascar Vanilla Body Lotion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bf/c0/l2c06w0z5606b69598fed269918079_280x343.jpg)

$12.50 at [bathandbodyworks.com](https://www.bathandbodyworks.com/blocked.html?url=L3Byb2R1Y3QvP3Byb2R1Y3RJZD0zNzc5ODI0Ng==&uuid=be574ff4-090e-11ed-ac0d-544d7672546b&vid=)

Gigi Gorgeous truly is gorgeous. That's why you should follow her advice and buy Wild Madagascar Vanilla Body Mist or Wild Madagascar Vanilla Body Lotion. Gigi is obsessed with delicious scents, so the fact that these products have her seal of approval is a huge deal. They're bound to make your nose happy.

## 7. Eyeko Eyeliner by Alexa Chung

![Eyeko Eyeliner by Alexa Chung](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zz/og/msfvxbpn5606b695d05b7527794470_600x750.jpg)

$20 on [eyeko.com](http://www.eyeko.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

You might know Marzia as Pewdiepie's gorgeous girlfriend. Of course, she's so much more than that. All you have to do is look at her to realize that she knows a lot about beauty. Her suggestion is to buy Eyeko Eyeliner by Alexa Chung. It's what keeps her looking fabulous, and it could do the same for you.

All of these women know a thing or two about beauty, which is why you should put these products on your shopping list. Who is your favorite beauty vlogger on YouTube?
