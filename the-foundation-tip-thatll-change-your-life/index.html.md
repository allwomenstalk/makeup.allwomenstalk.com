# The Foundation Tip That Will Completely Change Your Life ...

Wayne Goss has a popular YouTube channel for a reason. He really knows what he's talking about when it comes to makeup. In order to follow his lifesaving foundation tip, all you'll need is moisturizer, powder, and foundation.

Basically, you're going to start by moisturizing your skin. After that, you're going to take a loose powder and brush it over your moisturizer. Then you're going to apply your foundation! That's right, you'll be applying your foundation after your powder. It'll hold your makeup in place all day long. Plus, it'll fill in your pores.
