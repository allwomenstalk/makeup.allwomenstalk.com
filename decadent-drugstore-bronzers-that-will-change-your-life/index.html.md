# 7 Decadent Drugstore Bronzers That Will Change Your Life ...

In case you weren’t aware, drugstore bronzers are one of the best kept beauty secrets around. Sure, there are lots of reasons to want to buy higher end beauty products, but why spend the extra money when you can get the same color and quality for so much less? Bronzers aren’t just reserved for your summer makeup anymore. They’re an indispensable beauty product that you can use to contour, give your skin a sun-kissed look no matter what time of the year or define certain features. If you’re new to drugstore bronzers or are just looking for something new to try, don’t miss out on these drugstore bronzers that could change your makeup and your life!

## 1. Neutrogena Healthy Skin Blends Natural Radiance Bronzer

![Neutrogena Healthy Skin Blends Natural Radiance Bronzer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lv/kw/ly/l9ylcbzi_499x461.jpg)

Neutrogena makes one of the most highly rated drugstore bronzers around. The great thing about this particular bronzer is that it contains multiple shades so you can customize your look as the seasons change and it matches various skin tones. It’s also formulated with vitamins so your skin will love wearing it just as much as you do!

## 2. Essence Sun Club All-in-One Bronzing Highlighter

![Essence Sun Club All-in-One Bronzing Highlighter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ym/mb/nm/eccoyupg_599x284.jpg)

Although I’ve never seen Essence at any drugstores, I have seen it at Ulta and they make a very affordable bronzer. Their All-In-One Bronzing Highlighter combines the beauty of a bronzer and highlighter to make getting your glow on quick and easy. It also comes in two different shades, one for blondes and one for brunettes, so picking your ideal bronzer just got even easier.

## 3. Rimmel Waterproof Natural Bronzer

![Rimmel Waterproof Natural Bronzer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/du/iq/az/0xa60fm2_599x544.jpg)

This next drugstore bronzer is a must-have for hot days or days by the water. It might seem too soon to be thinking about the beach, but hot, sunny days will be back before you know it, girl! Rimmel’s bronzer is waterproof, slightly shimmery and contains minerals so you get a glorious, natural-looking bronzed look that lasts and lasts. You won’t want to miss out on this one!

## 4. Hard Candy so Baked Bronzers

![Hard Candy so Baked Bronzers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g1/jq/jn/i2mo15a9_500x500.jpg)

I love that Hard Candy became a drugstore beauty brand because so many people can afford to try their latest and greatest products now. So Baked Bronzer is a gorgeous, shimmery, matte bronzer that comes in four different shades. Although it’s called a bronzer, some people have found that it works better as a highlighter so depending on your particular skin tone, you might find these to be on the lighter side. Either way, it’s super easy to blend and goes on evenly for a radiant glow.

## 5. NYC Sun 'N' Brown Bronzing Powder

![NYC Sun 'N' Brown Bronzing Powder](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yh/h6/w9/ln1gfaks_499x496.jpg)

NYC’s bronzer comes in multiple shades of bronze and highlights so you can easily add dimension to your face and create a healthy, golden glow. Swirl your brush onto all the colors or pick and choose your favorite shades to apply. This affordable bronzer is smooth, lightweight and incredibly easy to blend so you’re left with a beautiful bronzed look without the sun damage or damage to your wallet.

## 6. Wet N Wild Color Icon Collection Bronzer SPF 15

![Wet N Wild Color Icon Collection Bronzer SPF 15](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/px/ot/yd/mi99f913_499x450.jpg)

This next drugstore bronzer has somewhat of a cult following. Tons of beauty gurus on YouTube as well as beauty bloggers rave about how pigmented and long-lasting this bronzer is. It comes in three different shades and conveniently contains SPF 15 which can help protect your skin from the sun’s harmful rays. Although all of the bronzers mentioned are budget-friendly, this is one of the cheapest yet most effective bronzers you can get.

## 7. Maybelline Fit Me Bronzer

![Maybelline Fit Me Bronzer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vt/kc/uy/lckp7ibo_499x359.jpg)

For the ladies looking for the most natural and lightweight bronzer, Maybelline Fit Me Bronzer might be your perfect fit. Swipe this bronzer on your cheeks and temples for an even, natural tan look that won’t fade on you midday and will still allow your skin to breathe. The three shades are perfect for a flawless faux-glow any day of the week.

The next time you’re shopping makeup, make sure you check out some of these fab drugstore bronzers! I think you’ll find that these bronzers are not only super inexpensive, but they’re quite comparable in quality to many of the more expensive higher end brands on the market. If you’re new to bronzers, trying a drugstore bronzer first is a good way to try it out and see if it’s something you’d like to invest in later. Do you have a favorite drugstore bronzer?
