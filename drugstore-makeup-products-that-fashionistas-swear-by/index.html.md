# 7 Drugstore Makeup Products That Fashionistas Swear by ...

High end makeup is fabulous and most of us have our faves that are worth the splurge. But there are also some good quality drugstore makeup products. It’s all about what you’re open to trying. If you’re feeling adventurous or just need to watch your makeup budget, these are the makeup products fashionistas swear by!

## 1. Maybelline Instant Age Rewind Dark Circle Concealer + Treatment

![Maybelline Instant Age Rewind Dark Circle Concealer + Treatment](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xz/ym/k0ghvq2z55b88787b4f85380408601_600x397.jpg)

This is the drugstore concealer that you hear the most buzz about. It’s amazing at covering up dark circles, which is the number one result most of us want from our concealer. Don’t pay any attention to the fact that this product contains the words “age rewind.” It’s an amazing concealer for any age! If you also need concealer for blemishes then you may want to pick up a separate product for that since the focus of this one is on getting rid of those raccoon circles we all hate!

## 2. Rimmel London Match Perfection Light Perfecting Radiance Foundation

![Rimmel London Match Perfection Light Perfecting Radiance Foundation](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rz/ry/ecg0t1rt55b887f6b534f158560637_450x450.jpg)

This foundation is loved by none other than beauty blogging queen Zoella, whom I adore! She’s quite a fan of this foundation, which gives it all the endorsement I need. I love a foundation that gives my skin a luminous finish and this one fits the bill. It also contains SPF 18 so you’ll know you’re protected from the sun, keeping your skin both safe and lovely. It’s also in the $5 price range, which means more money for other things you love.

## 3. Maybelline Dream Bouncy Blush

![Maybelline Dream Bouncy Blush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tl/i5/g3dcyvs755b891065026c370949970_400x392.jpg)

This is going to be my next blush purchase. I have to confess that I’ve recently developed quite the crush on cream blushes and this one meets the criteria. There are 10 beautiful shades to choose from. It’s an easily buildable blush so you can choose whether you wear the slightest flush or a fuller blush look. I’m so excited to get this product!

## 4. NYX Nude on Nude Palette

![NYX Nude on Nude Palette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vu/mh/zffjflvz55b8911f8d01c014673290_540x416.jpg)

This lovely little palette offers you 20 neutral shades of eye shadow so you can create endless combinations of looks. The shades range from light to dark. It also comes with a bonus lip palette of 10 glosses. A mirror is built in so you can do your eye makeup wherever you are. While I’ve never tried this palette, I’ve had great experience with NYX products.

## 5. Maybelline Eye Studio Lasting Drama Gel Eyeliner

![Maybelline Eye Studio Lasting Drama Gel Eyeliner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zd/rk/gj8u7vnz55b8913cca92c590695286_400x311.jpg)

This is the gel eyeliner to buy. Reviews of this product are stellar. It’s even rumored to be a favorite of the beautiful singer Adele, whose makeup I’m absolutely in awe of. It’s smudge-proof, waterproof and can last for a total 24 hours. This is Maybelline makeup product number 3 that’s made it onto this list so kudos to them for having many popular, beloved products!

## 6. L’Oreal Voluminous Butterfly Mascara

![L’Oreal Voluminous Butterfly Mascara](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mn/gj/n8vimeba55b8915885123269211611_400x400.jpg)

Prepare to fall in love! This magical mascara will have you wondering where it’s been all your life. L’Oreal is known for producing remarkable mascaras and this one’s no exception. The brush is uniquely shaped, which allows you to fan your lashes out completely. The result is a full, voluminous look, which we all love.

## 7. Revlon Lipstick in Various Shades

![Revlon Lipstick in Various Shades](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rf/ab/w23ysbwe55b891767044e115512213_433x521.jpg)

Revlon has the market cornered on drugstore lipsticks. They’re smooth, pigmented and stay put for hours on end. If the budget’s a little too tight for a MAC lipstick this month then try a Revlon. I hesitate to name one shade that’s better than the others because there are so many gorgeous shades to choose from. Why settle for just one lippy anyway?

These are some makeup products fashionistas swear by. What’s your fave drugstore makeup? I can’t wait to learn what you love!
