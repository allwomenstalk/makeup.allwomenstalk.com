# Breaking News! Whats in Kylie Cosmetics Summer Vacation 2017 Collection ?

In what is sure to be another sensation, Kylie Jenner has announced the Kylie Cosmetics' new Summer 2017 Vacation Collection. Everything about it is vacation-inspired so you'll find everything sun, sea and sand in the colors.

What's in the mega Kylie Cosmetic Vacation 2017 collection?

Revealed by Kylie on her Snapchat because where else? she showed the entire range, which includes brand new products and new colors for existing and beloved products. And to ensure you get the best idea she swatched the shades while lounging by the pool.

The highlight surely has to be the Take Me On Vacation eyeshadow palette. Summer mermaids are going to love the sandy, ocean-inspired, and jewel-like shades and all in one slim and totable case complete with brush and mirror.

But let's take a look at everything in the Kylie Cosmetics' new Summer 2017 Vacation Collection.

(And give thanks to Bustle.com for bringing it to our attention)

It's such a quality mix of bright and neutral shades.

![text, advertising, font, brand, banner,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x2/c8/x0ujbicr593fb9e5f16a3119702522_500x640.jpg)

Take me away!

There is also the June Bug Lip Kit, which is an electric purple matte that will pop against bronzed skin, since Jenner and her brand really love purple mattes. The Lip Kit family is full of colors in the purple family. There's the deep plum and gothy Kourt K. There is the vampy Love Bite Lip Kit, which is a plummy brown. Spice is a raspberry plum.

![perfume, brown, beauty, product, cosmetics,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ak/hr/rng927yx593fbaa137dd3956719923_500x640.jpg)

June Bug is a bright, electric purple Lip Kit. It really pops and will look amazing when paired with sun-kissed or faux glow summer skin.

There are two Super Glitter Glosses in Glitz and Glamour.

![face, cheek, pink, nose, lip,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uu/ot/lm87vlai593fb9e602bed475032218_500x640.jpg)

They are golden and bronzy shades.

There are two Send Me More Nudes lip sets.

![text, organ, brand, eye, pattern,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lv/y9/uvd7se0b593fbaa128437735285086_500x640.jpg)

Each has the same four shades - Naked, Bday Suit, Commando, and Bare- and these nudes come in both the Velvet and the Matte texture because Jenner wanted to give her customers choices.

![brown, pattern, brand, design, beige,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y0/be/m5qa9uvw593fb9e667828353789099_500x640.jpg)

The brand new items are the Ultra Glows, which are loose powder highlighters. They come in three shades named after tropical locales, such as Tahiti, Santorini, and Fiji.

![finger, nail, arm, hand, pattern,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ip/gu/y6r50p3x593fbaa154c92452497751_500x640.jpg)

They glimmer and glow.

![skin, arm, finger, leg, hand,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wq/fe/gja5txjo593fb9e6939ca145019707_500x640.jpg)

That glow is lit.

The Skinny Dip Face Duo consists of a bronzer and a highlighter for the face.

![label, brand, pattern,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qh/zx/qqeislzh593fb9e6dcd92787545758_500x640.jpg)

The Wet Set Pressed Powder set is four pans of pressed powder highlighter named Privacy Please, Unbothered, Do Not Disturb, and Get-A-Way.

![brown, eye, product, organ, wood,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y1/eg/yh30xasp593fbade300a4950747562_500x640.jpg)

The Wet Set powders are dry, but feel wet when you apply them, per Jenner. They are super silky and can be worn on your face or any part of your body. If you are going to a wedding or an event this season and want shimmery legs or decolletage, the Wet Set will be your new best friend.

![text, brown, font, calligraphy, brand,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v6/ao/w1n0zoh4593fbade4d111799156368_500x640.jpg)

The products were formulated to make you look good while on vacation or not! These Snaps indicate that Jenner has another hit on her well-manicured hands.

The [Kylie Cosmetics Summer 2017 Vacation Collection](https://kyliecosmetics.com/en-us) drops on Thursday, Jun. 15 at 6 p.m. ET/3 p.m. PT on the brand's site, which is its sales platform.
