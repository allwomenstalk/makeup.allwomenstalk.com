# Top 8 Long\-lasting Lip Colors ...

Finding good, long-lasting lip colors can be quite a challenging feat. You want to find a lipstick that lasts but won’t dry out your lips or look cracked and cakey. Well ladies, I scoured the interwebs to find the best and the brightest lasting lip colors that will feel as good as they look. Check out my picks for the top eight long-lasting lip colors.

## 1. Covergirl Outlast All-Day Lipcolor

![Covergirl Outlast All-Day Lipcolor](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/01/1_covergirl-outlast-all-day-lipcolor_176x368.jpg)

One of the most popular long-lasting lip colors from the drugstore is Covergirl’s Outlast. Outlast comes in two steps, with one being the lip color and the other being the moisturizing top coat to seal in color and shine. The Outlast collection comes in over 40 colors, so there should be a splendid shade for everyone!

## 2. Bite Beauty Luminous Crème Lipstick

![Bite Beauty Luminous Crème Lipstick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/01/2_bite-beauty-luminous-cr-me-lipstick_250x250.jpg)

Bite Beauty’s long-wearing lipstick not only looks good but is also good for you! Each tube of lipstick contains as much resveratrol as there are in five glasses of red wine! Resveratrol is an antioxidant that provides anti-aging benefits and can keep lips looking youthful and luscious!

## 3. Stila Stay All Day Liquid Lipstick

![Stila Stay All Day Liquid Lipstick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/01/3_stila-stay-all-day-liquid-lipstick_250x250.jpg)

If you’re more of a lip gloss kind of girl, you’re probably very aware that, although they look great, they do a poor job of staying on our lips. Stila’s Stay All Day Liquid Lipstick offers the high gloss shine of a lip gloss with the staying power of a lipstick! Swipe this baby on and enjoy up to six hours of shine!

## 4. Make up for Ever Rouge Artist Intense

![Make up for Ever Rouge Artist Intense](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/01/4_make-up-for-ever-rouge-artist-intense_250x250.jpg)

This long-lasting lipstick is said to contain 50% more pigment than traditional lipsticks and will deliver luxurious color in just one swipe. This [hydrating lipstick](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/best-moisturizing-lipsticks-for-hydrated-and-beautiful-lips/) comes in matte, pearly and satin finishes for a variety of looks that need minimal reapplication.

## 5. Kat Von D Everlasting Love Liquid Lipstick

![Kat Von D Everlasting Love Liquid Lipstick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/01/5_kat-von-d-everlasting-love-liquid-lipstick_250x250.jpg)

Ladies have compared this long-wearing lipstick to be more like a tattoo than an actual lipstick. This liquid lipstick goes on shiny but dries to a gorgeous matte finish. If you don’t like carrying makeup around in your purse, this lipstick is an essential to give you long-lasting color that lasts all day.

## 6. Revlon Super Lustrous Creme Lipstick

![Revlon Super Lustrous Creme Lipstick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/01/6_revlon-super-lustrous-creme-lipstick_300x300.jpg)

If you’re looking for a great long-wearing lipstick that works beautifully for everyday and special occasions, choose one of Revlon’s 39 shades of crème lipstick! This creamy, moisturizing lip color is highly pigmented, applies evenly and stays true to color.

## 7. Yves Saint Laurent ROUGE VOLUPTÉ Silky Sensual Radiant Lipstick

![Yves Saint Laurent ROUGE VOLUPTÉ Silky Sensual Radiant Lipstick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/01/7_yves-saint-laurent-rouge-volupt-silky-sensual-radiant-lipstick_250x250.jpg)

There are so many reasons to love this long-lasting lipstick. From the gorgeous packaging and range of vibrant colors, YSL’s Silky Sensual lipstick provides smoothing, plumping and lasting color that just won’t quit. Formulated with SPF 15 and their exclusive color-shine complex, your lips have never experienced luxury like this!

## 8. L'Oreal Infallible LipColour

![L'Oreal Infallible LipColour](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/01/8_loreal-infallible-lipcolour_300x300.jpg)

This long-wearing lip color promises to offer up to 16 hours of wear while keeping lips moist and conditioned. This lipstick comes in a cool little mirrored kit with the lip color on one side and a conditioning top coat to seal in color and leave a glossy finish.

We have lots of options when it comes to long-lasting lipstick and I hope this list will help you find your perfect match! I think the second best thing to finding your signature shade of lipstick is to find one that wears comfortably and you don’t have to constantly reapply! What’s your favorite kind of long-lasting lipstick?
