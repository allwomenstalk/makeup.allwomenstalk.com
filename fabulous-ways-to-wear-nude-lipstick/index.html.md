# 8 Fabulous Ways to Wear Nude Lipstick ...

The naked lip is in right now, but if you’re wondering how to wear [nude lipstick](https://allwomenstalk.com/hi/5705cc040c4fe7be538b45c3/), you’re not alone. It can be tricky to find just the right shade for your skin tone, but I can help! I’ve scoured my favorite makeup shop, Sephora, and I’ve found a range of nude lippies that will look gorgeous with any complexion! Here are 8 fabulous ways to wear nude lipstick.

## 1. Sephora Ultra Shine Lip Gloss

![Sephora Ultra Shine Lip Gloss](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/05/1_sephora-ultra-shine-lip-gloss_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $14.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Sephora’s lip gloss delivers on what the name implies ultra shine, with loads of gorgeous long-wearing pigmentation. The best nude shade in the line is called Shiny Perfect Nude, and it looks great with dramatic smoky eye makeup!

## 2. Sephora Rouge Shine Lipstick

![Sephora Rouge Shine Lipstick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/05/2_sephora-rouge-shine-lipstick_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $12.00 at [sephora.com](https://goo.gl/GFKKnv)
Vitamin E and natural oils work together to hydrate your lips while rich pigments give your lips a shiny, supple nude look… the color I like best for nude lips is called Tenderness… which is great for rosy complexions. Golden Girl is perfect for olive and darker skin tones… choices, choices!

## 3. Urban Decay Lip Junkie Lipgloss

![Urban Decay Lip Junkie Lipgloss](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/05/3_urban-decay-lip-junkie-lipgloss_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $19.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
As f the glorious glimmer shine wasn’t enough, Urban Decay had to add a lush vanilla-mint scent and flavor to this lip gloss! It’s one of my favorite ways to wear nude lipstick - Midnight Cowboy. Again, wear this with a very dramatic plumy or grey smoky eye, and leave the bronzer out of the equation.

## 4. Yves Saint Laurent Rouge Volupté

![Yves Saint Laurent Rouge Volupté](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/05/4_yves-saint-laurent-rouge-volupt_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $34.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Protect your pucker with SPF 15, and look gorgeous doing it with another of my favorite ways to wear nude lipstick, Nude Beige. The name really says it all, doesn’t it? This hue looks great with all complexions, especially warm olive tones.

## 5. Lorac Couture Shine Liquid Lipstick

![Lorac Couture Shine Liquid Lipstick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/05/5_lorac-couture-shine-liquid-lipstick_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $22.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Is there such a thing as a way to wear nude lipstick that’s good for your lips, keeping them hydrated, happy, and oh-so-kissable? Yes! This Lorac liquid lipstick contains mother-of-pearl for shimmer and shine; jojoba oil and sweet almond oil for hydration lips; and oriza sativa bran extract to keep lips nourished and protected. The best nude hue in the line is called Vintage… It’s gorgeous!

## 6. Bobbi Brown Lip Color

![Bobbi Brown Lip Color](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/05/6_bobbi-brown-lip-color_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $23.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you’re not into glimmer and shine, this is the best way to wear nude lipstick. It’s a matte finish, though the color is still rich and lush, and it contains vitamins C and E. The hue for you is probably the one called Beige. Easy to remember, right?

## 7. Too Faced Lip of Luxury

![Too Faced Lip of Luxury](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/05/7_too-faced-lip-of-luxury_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $20.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
How luxurious! This nude lipstick (called Totally Nude) actually contains champagne… so decadent! It also contains shea butter, which keeps your lips soft and supple. Hooray for shea!

## 8. Tarte LipSurgence Natural Lip Stain

![Tarte LipSurgence Natural Lip Stain](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/05/8_tarte-lipsurgence-natural-lip-stain_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $24.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you fell in love with Tarte’s cheek stains, you’ll love these lip stains just as much! They keep your lip color vibrant all day, without reapplying. The best way to wear nude lipstick in this line is a color they call Joy… but Peaceful is a safe bet, too.

Wow, so many ways to wear nude lipstick… how will you ever choose just one? I know I couldn’t… I have the Too Faced Lip of Luxury and the Lorac Liquid Lipstick in my shopping basket right now! Which of these ways to wear nude lipstick do you like best, and how will you wear it? Do tell!
