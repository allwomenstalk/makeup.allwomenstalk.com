# Look like a Victoria Secret Model with These Makeup Tutorials ...

Do you struggle with applying your makeup so that it looks professional? Or maybe you're just tired of wearing the same products on your face day after day. Either way, YouTube has the solution to your problem. There's no reason for you to be unsatisfied with your beautiful face when there are so many different ways for you to apply your makeup. Here are some Victoria Secret inspired makeup tutorials that'll make you look as fabulous as any supermodel:

## 1. Adriana Lima

Here's a tutorial that was inspired by Adriana Lima. It shows you how to create an elegant look that's perfect for any party. The Youtuber shows you how to do your lips, how to fill in your brows, and how to make your eyeshadow look fabulous. Even though it takes a little while to create the look, the time you wasted will be worth it when you see just how gorgeous your face is.

## 2. Miranda Kerr

Is Miranda Kerr your favorite supermodel? Then you should try to recreate her style by following the tips in this tutorial. It'll help you create a super sweet look that's perfect for a day at school or a night out on the town. It really works for any occasion or any time of day, so make sure you pay close attention.

## 3. Candice Swanepoel

Candice Swanepoel is about as gorgeous as it gets, which is why you should try to recreate her look. This tutorial doesn't require you to do anything too crazy to your pretty face, which means that you can feel free to steal the style whenever the mood strikes. You're bound to get plenty of compliments on it.

## 4. Heidi Klum

When you're discussing Victoria Secret models, how can you forget about Heidi Klum? Although this tutorial will show you how to create another pink lip, similar to the videos before it, it's worth the watch. Why? Because of the gorgeous eye makeup that'll make you stand out in a crowd.

## 5. Adriana Lima – Valentine’s Edition

Here's another video inspired by Adriana Lima. However, this one is a Valentine's Day version. Even though February 14th has passed, you can still rock this look at any time of year. As long as you're a fan of bold looks, you'll love the outcome.

## 6. Fashion Show 2013

This video will show you how to do more than your makeup. It'll also show you how to create beautiful, Victoria Secret inspired waves in your hair. It's not as difficult as it looks, so make sure you give it a try. Even if you mess up at first, don't be afraid to give it another go until you get it just right.

## 7. Fashion Show 2014

Here's another video that'll show you how to do your hair, as well as your makeup. It's inspired by the 2014 Victoria Secret Fashion show, so you know that everything will come out looking fantastic. The best part? The look is fancy enough to wear to a party but natural enough to wear around town.

You don't have to be a famous model with a professional makeup artist in order to look fabulous. As long as you give yourself enough time in the morning, you can make yourself look as amazing as any woman on the runway. Who's your favorite Victoria Secret model?
