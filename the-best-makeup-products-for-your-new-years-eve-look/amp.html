9 Must-Have Beauty Products to Slay Your New Years Eve Makeup Look ...

Laura Mercier Caviar Stick Eye Colour in Sugar Frost • Maybelline New York the Nudes Palette • Nars Dual Intensity Eyeshadow in Sycorax • Tom Ford Ultra Length Mascara • Dolce &amp; Gabbana Eyebrow Pencil • More ...

By Chantel

If you're here it's because you're looking for the best makeup products for your New Year's Eve look. New Year’s Eve is just around the corner and for most of us it’s a special time to get all glammed up with sparkle, shimmer, and dark sultry makeup as we celebrate the beginning of a new year. Whether you are hitting the town with your significant other or partying with your girls these are the best makeup products for your New Year's Eve look.

1. Laura Mercier Caviar Stick Eye Colour in Sugar Frost

This is one of the best makeup products for your New Year's Eve look! This multitasking eyeshadow stick delivers both trouble-free application, high definition and long-lasting color. Long wearing, transfer proof, and crease resistant, the color glides seamlessly onto lids and blends and layers beautifully with a rich pigment payoff. It features a myriad of shades and finishes to deliver endless options for effortlessly beautiful eyes that stand out both day and night. This colour stick is great as a highlighter in the corner of your eye and along the waterline.
$29.00 at sephora.com

2. Maybelline New York the Nudes Palette

This 12-shade eyeshadow palette features dazzling colors. From bold beiges and sultry sands to brazen bronzes and tempting taupes. This makeup palette is perfect for creating versatile eye makeup looks. Use alone or blended together, there are enough combinations to take you to New Year’s Eve
$10.99 at ulta.com

3. Nars Dual Intensity Eyeshadow in Sycorax

Mesmerizing dual intensity eyeshadow features a luxuriously smooth transformative texture. This one has 17 ready to wear and rebelliously romantic sophisticated shades to express a riveting range of eye drama. Use your finger to smudge it along your lash line to add a bit of smoky drama.
$29.00 at macys.com

4. Tom Ford Ultra Length Mascara

Bring out the big guns this New Year’s Eve with this ultra-black mascara. It features a long-wearing formula to impart the ultimate in luxurious length and extraordinary wear to even the thinnest lashes.
$45.00 at sephora.com

5. Dolce & Gabbana Eyebrow Pencil

Give your brows a beautiful, tailor-made shape with this eyebrow pencil by Dolce & Gabbana Beauty. The dual application liner brings natural definition to your brows by enhancing the shape and density to suit your look. It doesn’t even require sharpening and provides you with consistent, even color that lasts for up to 12 hours thanks to its water-resistant formula. Brows on FLEEK!
$45.00 at shop.nordstrom.com

6. Tom Ford Traceless Foundation Stick

This unique cream foundation formula features the nourishing Tom Ford infusing complex. The portable stick format achieves versatility in the application for sheer, medium, or full coverage while remaining beautifully natural and undetectable. When used as a concealer it swiftly hides imperfections leaving a matte finish.
$85.00 at shop.nordstrom.com

7. Giorgio Armani Maestro Liquid Summer

This innovative Armani Beauty liquid bronzer provides amazing color and brightens and applies weightlessly on the skin. Blend it all over cheekbones and forehead for a sun-kissed effect.
$64.00 at neimanmarcus.com

8. Clinique Chubby Stick Cheek Colour Balm in Amp’d up Apple

Creamy, mistake-proof cheek colour creates a healthy-looking glow in an instant. It's also oil-free. Dot it onto the apples of your cheeks. Fingertip blend along cheekbones, toward hairline. Layer to build colour.
$23.00 at macys.com

9. Estee Lauder Pure Color Envy Sculpting Lipstick in Tumultuous Pink

It's super creamy and glides on effortlessly and covers evenly. Sensually soft and smooth, luxuriously comfortable, 6-hour wear. The perfect complement to your sparkly party dress. $32.00 at esteelauder.com
