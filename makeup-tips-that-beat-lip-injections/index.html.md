# 17 Makeup Tips That Beat Lip Injections ...

You don't need to get plastic surgery in order to feel beautiful. If you're unhappy with your natural lips, there are little things you can do to make them appear fuller. So instead of getting lip injections, you should follow these makeup tips:

## 1. Use Peppermint Oil

![Use Peppermint Oil](https://img.allw.mn/content/d1/xo/rsrhaxuj5630d61615481028480505_500x281.gif)

Peppermint can work wonders on your lips. You can either buy peppermint oil to rub over them, or you can buy lip products that contain spearmint or peppermint in them. Either way, it'll make your lips look fuller.

## 2. Exfoliate Your Lips

![Exfoliate Your Lips](https://img.allw.mn/content/nq/md/qkliu7ih5630d61101afb856410688_500x235.gif)

Your face isn't the only thing you should be exfoliating every morning. Don't forget to exfoliate your lips when you're standing in front of that mirror.

## 3. Apply Highlighter to Your Cupid’s Bow

![Apply Highlighter to Your Cupid’s Bow](https://img.allw.mn/content/iq/zb/j4b5l5lc5630d61106089292811368_500x253.gif)

Your cupid's bow is the area in the middle of your top lip. If you place some highlighter on it, you can create the illusion of a fuller mouth.

## 4. Use Products with Beeswax

![Use Products with Beeswax](https://img.allw.mn/content/ui/vq/vaj1w8l25630d61200c9e942098318_500x258.gif)

Find lipgloss that contains beeswax in it. It will make your lips look fuller while stopping them from looking chapped.

## 5. Use Pink Toned Lipstick with Dark Lip Liner

![Use Pink Toned Lipstick with Dark Lip Liner](https://img.allw.mn/content/xn/gs/jrjw4gec5630d61344f78220080782_480x270.gif)

You can create an illusion with your makeup. Use a pink toned lipstick on your lips, but line it with a color that's slightly darker.

## 6. Try Puckering Exercises

![Try Puckering Exercises](https://img.allw.mn/content/zz/if/hmmptgno5630d61205624122552317_400x225.gif)

Your arms and abs aren't the only parts of you that need exercise. There are a few facial exercises for your lips you should try. Find them at [livestrong.com](https://www.livestrong.com/article/181988-facial-exercises-for-the-lips/).

## 7. Give Yourself a Lip Massage with Lip Balm

![Give Yourself a Lip Massage with Lip Balm](https://img.allw.mn/content/c2/se/e3qn4imm5630d61250018632409045_500x281.gif)

Back massages aren't the only relaxing type of massage. You can actually massage your lips with your lip balm.

## 8. Use Cinnamon Oil on Lips

![Use Cinnamon Oil on Lips](https://img.allw.mn/content/ll/e6/y1kl6ln65630d613151ce797191340_500x350.gif)

Essential oils are lifesavers. That's why you should buy some cinnamon oil and use it on your lips.

## 9. Brush Lips with Your Toothbrush

![Brush Lips with Your Toothbrush](https://img.allw.mn/content/gx/j8/n52sgydi5630d61644874301340781_480x270.gif)

When you brush your teeth, don't forget to brush your lips. It'll increase the blood flow, which will make your lips look bigger.

## 10. Drink a Lot of Water

![Drink a Lot of Water](https://img.allw.mn/content/ld/u3/tvdrgr945630d618988cb409055686_500x275.gif)

Water hydrates your body, which will make your skin look brighter and your lips look fuller. It's good for you in every way!

## 11. Rub Honey on Lips

![Rub Honey on Lips](https://img.allw.mn/content/ez/eq/d70ga54n5630d6e25cd16009281624_400x225.gif)

Before you hop in the shower, you should rub honey on your lips. Let the steam go over your face, and then wash off your lips before you end your shower.

## 12. Use Coconut Oil

![Use Coconut Oil](https://img.allw.mn/content/x5/y1/eogomrsl5630d6e2a267c036521977_500x205.gif)

Buy some coconut oil and rub it on your lips before bathing and after bathing. It'll create the look you've been hoping for.

## 13. Avoid Dark Colored Lipsticks

![Avoid Dark Colored Lipsticks](https://img.allw.mn/content/xf/jg/uh3nvqbx5630d61411a5e110210053_500x281.gif)

You know how black clothing can make your body look slimmer? Well, dark colored lipstick can make your lips look slimmer.

## 14. Fill in Lips with Nude Liner

![Fill in Lips with Nude Liner](https://img.allw.mn/content/zm/yx/zf1fpn0h5630d61430d8e967371105_320x180.gif)

Before you apply your lipstick, you should fill in your lips with nude liner. That way, the lipstick color will pop more.

## 15. Use Shimmery Glosses

![Use Shimmery Glosses](https://img.allw.mn/content/d6/yp/j2maczoi5630d6156706c736971632_500x333.gif)

Shimmery glosses do the opposite of dark colored glosses. They'll make your lips look larger.

## 16. Mix Cayenne Pepper with Lip Gloss

![Mix Cayenne Pepper with Lip Gloss](https://img.allw.mn/content/r7/ny/lsiibgyw5630d61fc27fe718055011_500x206.gif)

Cayenne pepper can plump your lips. That's why you should mix a small amount of it with your lip gloss.

## 17. Buy Lip Plumping Lip Gloss

![Buy Lip Plumping Lip Gloss](https://img.allw.mn/content/a9/rh/x2je0hjq5630d61745dc7690353544_500x269.gif)

You don't have to use any homemade remedies, because you can simply buy lip plumping lip gloss. It's available in any store, so you shouldn't have any trouble finding it.

Now you won't have to head to the doctor for lip injections! What other makeup tips do you have for creating fuller lips?
