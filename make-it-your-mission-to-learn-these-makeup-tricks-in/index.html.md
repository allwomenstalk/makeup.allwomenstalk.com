# Make It Your Mission to Learn These Makeup Tricks in 2016 ...

You want to look your best, don't you? That's why you need to work on your makeup technique. According to [Fashion Magazine](https://fashionmagazine.com/beauty-grooming/makeup-tricks-2016/), you should learn how to master these makeup tricks in 2016:

## 1. Dark Lipstick

![Dark Lipstick](https://img.allw.mn/content/ba/a4/wq3ydf3u5672d310dae32913239924_500x208.gif)

You shouldn't stick to wearing nude lips or light lips. Try going bold by wearing dark lipstick. It doesn't have to be black, but it should be drastic.

## 2. Winged Liner or Graphic Liner

![Winged Liner or Graphic Liner](https://img.allw.mn/content/a4/uf/bbxgisir5672d3114179b077458041_570x321.gif)

It's difficult to make your winged eyeliner match. However, it's possible if you keep on trying.

## 3. Braids

![Braids](https://img.allw.mn/content/mz/my/ylxvditm561afeda2c89d983533541_500x332.gif)

Learn how to do french braids, rope braids, fishtail braids, and waterfall braids.

## 4. Nail Art

![Nail Art](https://img.allw.mn/content/ey/ge/g084jonz5672d47d7f8ee154187971_720x405.gif)

If you try to nail your nail art, but still can't master it, then you should buy some fake nails to stick on yourself.

## 5. Smoky Eye

![Smoky Eye](https://img.allw.mn/content/m7/qn/ylu3wsvg5672d3122182e135634466_500x281.gif)

This is the perfect party look. That's why you need to watch some tutorials on YouTube to find out how to do it right.

## 6. Eyebrows

![Eyebrows](https://img.allw.mn/content/lc/d7/fy3zwq6v5672d3123dbae928419458_500x269.gif)

Everyone's obsessed with eyebrows nowadays. That's why you should learn how to pluck them and fill them in.

## 7. Fuller Lips

[https://media.giphy.com/media/111jnJg0QK7fri/giphy.gif](https://media.giphy.com/media/111jnJg0QK7fri/giphy.gif)
We have Kylie Jenner to thank for this trend. Even if you don't buy her lip kit, you should try your best to make your lips look fuller.

Are you a pro at any of these things?
