# 7 Little Known Tips on How to Apply Bronzer like a Pro ...

How to apply bronzer like a pro will give you the upper hand when it comes to summer skin this season. Wearing bronzer is a great way to even out skin tone and mask imperfections. It’s also ideal for creating the appearance of a natural, sun-kissed glow. Knowing how to apply a bronzer can involve anything from choosing the right shade to application and contouring. Take a look at the following few tips on how to apply bronzers like a pro.

## 1. Wear a Slightly Darker Foundation

If you’re wearing foundation under your bronzer, switch to one that’s slightly darker than your skin tone. Wearing a base colour that’s one shade darker than your natural skin tone is will help enhance the impact of the bronzer and create contour. It’ll help you appear tanned without being too overdone.

## 2. Build up Colour

With most make up products, the key is to gradually build up colour. Applying one coat of colour can create a heavy-handed look, which is hardly ever a good thing in the make up game. Gradually building up colour when it comes to [how to apply bronzer](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/tips-to-apply-bronzer-like-a-pro/) will allow you to blend and shade as needed.

## 3. Not Just for Your Cheeks

Work an all-over glow by applying bronzer to other parts of your face, not just your cheeks. To avoid a muddy, Jersey Shore look, only sweep the slightest hints of bronzer to the parts of your face that the sun would naturally hit – areas such as the bridge of the nose and the forehead.

## 4. Contour

Create a contoured effect by focusing on applying bronzer to the hollows of your cheeks. You want to apply the bronzer in an area from your temple to the corner of your lips. When looking at how to apply bronzer this way, start with a contouring brush and gradually build up colour with a larger blusher brush. Blend well so that the overall effect is subtle. Avoid applying bronzer on the apples of your cheeks.

## 5. Mix and Match

If you can’t find the right shade of bronzer for your skin, try mixing a few different colours together to get the perfect shade. You’ll find that quite a few bronzers come in palettes with multiple shades. If you have lighter skin, look for a palette with pink tones in it. Those with medium skin tones can generally get away with most shades but golden and apricot shades are great for bringing out warmth. If you’ve got darker skin then go for a rich, shimmery bronzer.

## 6. Naturally

A number of bronzers contain some kind of shimmer, which can sometimes be a bit distracting for an ‘everyday’ face. If you want a more natural look then go for a matte formulation without any shimmer. When looking at how to apply bronzers, matte formulations are also a great choice for contouring.

## 7. The Right Tools Count

To apply bronzer, you’ll need the right tools. Sometimes the brushed and applicators they provide with a product just don’t cut it. Instead, you’ll want a big blusher brush and a contouring brush.

These are just a few tips for how to apply bronzer to help you achieve that bronzy summer glow. Some you may already know of, and others you may not. Do you have any tips for how to apply bronzers that you’d like to share?
