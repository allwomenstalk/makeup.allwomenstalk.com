# 7 Best Drugstore Primers to Use Right Now ...

The very best drugstore primers deliver awesome coverage, blur out imperfections, even out skin tone and help keep skin matte at a nice price that won’t break the bank. Having a good primer is one of the keys to having flawless makeup, so while it’s important to get a high quality primer, it doesn’t necessarily mean you have to go out and buy a majorly expensive primer. Take a look through these products, which are some of the best drugstore primers around, and get gorgeous now!

What makes Rimmel’s Stay Matte Primer one of the best drugstore primers? This super lightweight formula minimizes the appearance of pores, controls excess oil for up to 8 hours and never feels heavy or greasy. Use it alone or under any type of foundation and forget about enlarged pores and shiny T-zones. A little goes a long way, so you get even more bang for your buck!
