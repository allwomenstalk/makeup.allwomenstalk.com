# 5 Sexy Lipsticks to Try This Valentines Day ...

Need some **sexy lipsticks for Valentine's Day**? V-Day is a perfect excuse to update your lipstick collection (if you even needed one). From vampy reds to subtle nudes, these 5 shades are sure to impress are are the best **sexy lipsticks for Valentine's Day**.

## 1. Buxom Full-on Lip Stick in Brooklyn

![beauty, cosmetics, lipstick, product, product,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u1/ho/ixqd23zx5a7f4cdb67ed3100039035_450x450.jpg)

[sephora.com](https://www.sephora.com/product/buxom-full-on-lip-stick-P243804?skuId=1195643&icid2=buxom_lp_bestsellers_carousel_us:p243804)

Buxom's Brooklyn is a gorgeous matte fig color. It's plumping and tingles ever so slightly on your lips. Even better, it's got a pointed tip, so you won't need to worry about smudges. Just apply and go! This is one of the very best **sexy lipsticks for Valentine's Day**.

## 2. Nyx Simply Vamp Lip Cream in Enamored

_ nyxcosmetics.com_

Feeling femme fatale? Try Nyx's Simply Vamp Lip Cream in Enamored. This rich, silky brown-burgundy is definitely Bond Girl material. Like Brooklyn, it's got a pointed tip, so you won't have to worry about smudges.

## 3. Tarte Color Splash Hydrating Lipstick in Escape

![cosmetics, product, lipstick, product, product design,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r7/jx/s7r719mf5a7f4d2d16ebd973102019_540x540.jpg)

[tartecosmetics.com](https://tartecosmetics.com/shop/makeup/lips/lipstick-liners/)

Tarte's Color Splash Lipstick in Escape is a classic lustrous rose. Tarte also offers a slightly darker, cooler alternative (Vacay) for ladies with pink, red, or bluish undertones. How can you go wrong?

## 4. Fresh Sugar Lip Treatment SPF 15 in Rosé

![cosmetics, product, lipstick, product, product design,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b9/p1/jangwqhj5a7f4d45383a6879648483_540x540.jpg)

[sephora.com](https://www.sephora.com/product/sugar-lip-treatment-spf-15-P57002?skuId=1190529&icid2=fresh_lp_bestsellers_carousel_us:p57002)

Sometimes, less is definitely more. Fresh's Sugar Lip Treatment in Rosé provides a light, smoothly shining pop of color that tastes as good as it looks. It's moisturizing, has SPF 15, and smells of flowers (you know, like the ones you're holding). Even better, you won't have to wipe it off before kissing bae.

## 5. Maybelline Color Sensational Insti-Matte Nude in Almond Rose

![lipstick, cosmetics, product, product design, product,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zs/de/nnroqes85a7f4d7073c56884265138_400x400.jpg)

[maybelline.com](https://www.maybelline.com/lip-makeup/lipstick/color-sensational-the-mattes-matte-finish-lipstick-makeup)

Nude lips go with everything. For a bold, [feminine look](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/super-simple-tips-to-look-more-feminine/), try Maybelline's Insti-Matte Nude Lipstick in Almond Rose. It's a vibrant, creamy, and wonderfully warm color. For a softer, more classic nude, try Hot Sand or Purely Nude.
