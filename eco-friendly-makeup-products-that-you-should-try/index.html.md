# 7 Eco\-Friendly Makeup Products That You Should Try ...

Eco-friendly makeup is becoming more popular and easier to find, which is great because it doesn’t contain ingredients that could be harmful to you or the environment. When you are looking for eco-friendly makeup, one of the best places to go is the Environmental Working Group’s database for cosmetics. The Environmental Working Group (EWG) has rated thousands of products and given them a score of zero to ten. Those with a zero are considered the safest and most eco-friendly makeup products. All of the following makeup products have received low ratings, and you can feel good about using any one of them.

## 1. BareMinerals Eyecolor

![BareMinerals Eyecolor](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/me/pu/iykjf49p_450x359.jpg)

bareMinerals Eyecolor is a great option for eyeshadow if you are looking for eco-friendly makeup. With a rating of three from the EWG, this eyeshadow is much safer than most on the market. In addition to having a low rating from the EWG, bareMinerals Eyecolor is also a great choice for eyeshadow because it can be used wet or dry. This means you can have either opaque of translucent color, making this eyeshadow perfect for both day and evening wear.

## 2. Tarte Skinny SmolderEYES Intense Amazonian Clay Waterproof Liner

![Tarte Skinny SmolderEYES Intense Amazonian Clay Waterproof Liner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cd/z9/xdy2bdtd_450x439.jpg)

Tarte Skinny SmolderEYES Intense Amazonian Clay Waterproof Liner received the EWG’s rating of one, which makes it a very good choice for a safe nontoxic eyeliner. This eyeliner does not have any parabens or phthalates. Using this eyeliner from Tarte will not only reassure you about what you are putting on your face, it will also give you beautifully defined eyes!

## 3. Tarte Amazonian Clay and Annatto Body Bronzer

![Tarte Amazonian Clay and Annatto Body Bronzer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ze/f4/qjd7kgy5_450x448.jpg)

Bronzers are a great way to give your skin a sun kissed glow, but not all bronzers have safe ingredients for your skin. The Amazonian Clay and Annatto Body Bronzer from Tarte received a rating of two from the EWG, which means it is a good choice for an eco-friendly bronzer. Using this bronzer will give you a beautiful glow without any parabens, sulfates, synthetic fragrances, petrochemicals, phthalates, or GMOs.

## 4. Smashbox Full Exposure Mascara

![Smashbox Full Exposure Mascara](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rm/fp/maspmbyo_450x445.jpg)

Smashbox Full Exposure Mascara is a volumizing and lengthening mascara that will make your lashes look absolutely stunning. Aside from giving you voluminous lashes, this mascara will also give you soft silky lashes from the conditioning olive oil that it contains. The Full Exposure Mascara does not have any parabens, and received a rating of two from the EWG.

## 5. Too Faced Perfect Lips Lip Liner

![Too Faced Perfect Lips Lip Liner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z9/hx/ke41z2sn_450x447.jpg)

The Perfect Lips Lip Liner from Too Faced is a great lip liner if you are looking for eco-friendly options. It has a rating of one from the EWG, and it defines lips beautifully. This lip liner comes in five colors, and it doesn’t have any parabens, sulfates, synthetic fragrances, petrochemicals, phthalates, GMOs, or triclosan.

## 6. BareMinerals Blush

![BareMinerals Blush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t1/tn/iekebf5z_450x351.jpg)

Wearing blush is a wonderful way to bring color into your face. bareMinerals blush is fantastic blush with safe ingredients. It received a rating of one from the EWG. Aside from having a great rating from the EWG, this blush is wonderful because it comes in six colors and is great for all skin types.

## 7. Ellis Faas Creamy Lips Lipstick

![Ellis Faas Creamy Lips Lipstick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tv/ox/bzvvacaq_450x448.jpg)

Ellis Faas Creamy Lips lipstick is a long-wearing lipstick that has rich pigment. There are nine beautiful colors to choose from, and none of the colors contain fragrance or flavors. Also, there are not any parabens in the Ellis Faas Creamy Lips lipsticks. This beautiful lipstick received a rating of one from the EWG, so you can feel good about wearing it.
