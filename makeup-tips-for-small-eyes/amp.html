1 Groomed Eyebrows

The first makeup tip for small eyes is all about groomed eyebrows. Truthfully girls, you've got to keep your eyebrows up and groomed beautifully to make sure that your eyes stand out and look larger! For me, I keep them a little on the thin side and make sure that I don't have any stray hairs. That highlights my eyes and makes them pop!

***

Eyebrows can be a great way to frame your face and draw attention to your eyes. When it comes to small eyes, it's important to keep your eyebrows groomed and well-maintained. This will help to make your eyes appear bigger and brighter. Aim to keep your eyebrows thin and tidy, removing any stray hairs that may be present. You can also use eyebrow makeup to add definition and further enhance your look. For a more natural look, use a brow powder or pencil to fill in sparse areas and create a more defined shape. With the right grooming and makeup techniques, you can make your eyes look bigger and more beautiful.

2 Pale Eye Shadow on Lids

Did you know that pale eyeshadow can actually enhance the size and shape of your eyes? This tip for small eyes is all about putting pale eye shadow along your lid. It can really be any color that you want, but make sure that it is pale. For me, I use a pale pink or purple and draw out the green in my eyes. Use the color that works best for your eye color!

***

Small eyes can be enhanced with the right makeup look, and one of the best ways to do this is with the use of pale eyeshadow. Pale eyeshadow can help to make eyes look larger and brighter. For small eyes, the best colors to use are light pinks, purples, and blues, as they will help to draw out the color of the eyes. When applying eyeshadow, make sure to use a light hand and blend the colors together. Start with the lightest color on the inner corner of the eye and blend it outwards. Then, use a slightly darker color on the outer corner of the eye and blend it back in. This will help to create a more natural look.

When applying eyeshadow, it is also important to use the right brush. A flat eyeshadow brush will help to apply eyeshadow evenly and blend it out, while an angled brush will help to create a more defined look. It is also important to use an eyeshadow primer, as this will help to keep the eyeshadow in place and prevent it from creasing.

3 Use Light Colors to Brighten Eyes

In turn, you can actually use light colors to brighten up your eyes and make you appear more awake! This makeup tip works especially well for me in the morning, when I am really tired. It makes my eyes appear super awake and alert without me having to try too hard!

***

Light colors are great when you want to make your eyes look brighter and more awake. This makeup tip is especially helpful for those mornings when you’re feeling tired and need to look alert. Light colors can be used to make your eyes appear larger, more awake, and more vibrant. For example, using light shades of pink, peach, or champagne on the lids and inner corners of your eyes can really open them up. To further enhance the effect, use a highlighting shade on the brow bone and inner corners of your eyes. This will make your eyes appear more awake and alert. Additionally, you can use a light shade of eyeliner to make your eyes look larger and more awake. Using a light shade of eyeliner on the bottom lash line will open up your eyes and make them look bigger. Lastly, mascara on the top and bottom lashes can really make your eyes pop and look more awake.

4 Dark Colors to Outer Corners of Eyes

If you are looking for a way to make your eyes really stand out and look beautiful, this tip is just for you! Just swipe a bit of that dark colored eye shadow along the outer corners of your eyes. That will really make them pop and can even draw out the color of your eyes! Trust me, I use this one all the time!

***

Dark colors can be a great way to make your eyes stand out. When applied to the outer corners of the eyes, dark colors can draw out the color of your eyes and make them pop. This tip is especially useful for those with small eyes, as dark colors can create the illusion of larger eyes.

When applying dark colors to the outer corners of your eyes, it’s important to use a light hand. Start with a small amount of eye shadow and gradually build up the color until you achieve the desired effect. It’s also important to blend the eye shadow outwards and upwards to get a natural look.

To make the eyes appear even larger, try using a light colored eye shadow in the inner corners of the eyes. This will create a brightening effect and make the eyes look bigger. You can also use a light colored eyeliner along the lower lash line to give the illusion of larger eyes.

Finally, don’t forget to use mascara. Curling the lashes and applying several coats of mascara will help to open up the eyes and make them look bigger. It’s also important to use a waterproof mascara to avoid smudging and ensure that your makeup lasts all day.

5 Light Colors under Brow Bone

Remember what I said about light colors and how they can make your eyes appear that much more awake and alert? Well, if you use light colors under your brow bone, it can actually make your eyes appear that much bigger too! Truthfully, this technique is an obsession of mine. It works so well and nobody knows that I have small eyes!

***

Using light colors under the brow bone can be a great way to make small eyes appear bigger. The technique is simple and easy to do. First, you need to apply a light eyeshadow color to the area just under your eyebrow. This area is often referred to as the brow bone. When you apply the eyeshadow, blend it up and out to create a subtle highlight. The light colors will draw attention to the area and give your eyes a larger look.

It's important to use the right colors when you're applying eyeshadow. For small eyes, you want to choose light and neutral shades. Shades like beige, taupe, and ivory will work best. Avoid any colors that are too dark or too bright, as they can make your eyes look even smaller.

In addition to using light colors, you can also use eyeliner to make your eyes appear bigger. When using eyeliner, try to stick to neutral colors like brown or black. Apply a thin line close to the lash line, and then smudge it out with a blending brush. This will create a subtle definition that will make your eyes appear bigger.

6 Line Your Upper Lash

Do you ladies line along your upper lash line? I didn't do it until I realized that when you do, you are actually making your eyes appear so much bigger! This tip for small eyes totally enhances my eyes and really makes them look lovely and large! Below, I have some eyeliner tricks that'll enhance your eyes even more!

7 Make Line Pass Outer Corner of Eye

Remember when I said there would be some eyeliner tricks? That's what this tip is all about! You can actually use your eyeliner to go past the outer corner of your eyes, almost in a cat-eye shape. It'll draw your eye out a little bit and make it appear much larger!

***

This technique is great for those with small eyes, as it creates an illusion of elongation and can make the eyes appear bigger. Make sure to use a liquid or gel eyeliner for this trick, as it will be easier to create a precise line. When applying the eyeliner, start from the inner corner of the eye and gradually make the line thicker as you reach the outer corner. Then, extend the line slightly beyond the outer corner of the eye to complete the cat-eye shape. Finally, use an eyeliner brush to blend the line for a more natural look. With a little practice, you'll be able to achieve a perfect cat-eye look every time!

8 Line Lower Lash Line

You also want to make sure that you are lining your lower lash line too. Not just your waterline, but also your lash line. Truthfully, this one took a while for me to master, but once I got it down, it totally worked and it looks beautiful every single time!

9 Smudge

Another great makeup tip for small eyes that all revolves around eyeliner is to smudge your liner a little bit! This can be done on the upper and lower lids. Now, don't make it look too smudgy, but just enough. For me, I use my smudge stick on the end of my eyeliner pencil and I smudge very lightly.

10 White Eyeliner on Inner Rims of Eyes

This makeup tip is all about brightening up your eyes and really making them pop! White eyeliner right around the inner rims of your eyes can actually make your eyes appear super large and doll-like. It also make them appear super wide awake. This is the best way to mask a hangover or a bad night's sleep!

***

White eyeliner is a great way to make small eyes appear larger and brighter. When applied to the inner rims of the eyes, it can make them look doll-like and wide awake. This is an especially useful trick for those who have had a late night or are suffering from a hangover and want to look more alert.

When applying white eyeliner to the inner rims of the eyes, make sure to use a light hand. It's best to use a thin line of eyeliner, as this will help to avoid the raccoon look. Also, be sure to use a high-quality eyeliner that won't irritate your eyes.

To complete the look, use a black eyeliner on the upper and lower lash lines. This will help to define and frame the eyes, making them appear larger. If you want to add more definition to the eyes, use a dark eyeshadow on the upper and lower lash lines. This will create a more dramatic look.

Finally, apply a few coats of mascara to the lashes. This will help to make the eyes appear larger and more defined. With these simple makeup tips, you can easily make small eyes appear larger and brighter.

11 Curl Lashes

Did you think that your lashes wouldn't featured in makeup tips for small eyes? No way! Your lashes can actually really make your eyes appear super big. What I do every morning to make my eyes look bigger is curl my lashes. I use a lash curler, but there are tons of other ways to do it too!

***

Did you think that your lashes wouldn't be featured in makeup tips for small eyes? No way! Your lashes can actually really make your eyes appear super big. What I do every morning to make my eyes look bigger is curl my lashes. I use a lash curler, but there are tons of other ways to do it too! Heating your eyelash curler slightly with a blow dryer before using it can give you an extra lift. After curling, apply a couple of coats of volumizing mascara to enhance the effect further. False lashes or lash primers can also provide that extra oomph for a more dramatic look.

12 Volume the Lashes up

Another great way to make your eyes appear bigger is to use a volumizing mascara! Truthfully, I have used all sorts of different mascaras, but my favorite is Maybelline's Stiletto. Not only does this mascara length, but it really makes them lush and pretty! Give it a try girls!

13 Apply Two Coats of Mascara

When you are applying your mascara, don't just do one coat. Instead, make sure that you do at least 2 coats of mascara. That way, you know that you'll be getting not just more volume, but your lashes will stand out that much more! Give this a try girls, and let me know how it works out for you!

14 Put the Most Mascara on Middle and Outer Corners of Eyes

One mascara trick that really works to make your eyes appear a lot bigger, is to make sure that you put your mascara on the middle and outer corners of your eyes. This makeup tip for small eyes really works for anyone at all! Just be careful when you are applying your mascara to keep it away from the inner corner.

***

Mascara is an essential part of any makeup look, and it can be especially helpful when it comes to making small eyes appear larger. When applying mascara, the key is to focus on the middle and outer corners of the eyes. This will create the illusion of a larger eye area, as the mascara will draw attention to the corners of the eyes.

When applying mascara to the outer corners of the eyes, make sure to use an upward motion to create a lifting effect. This will help to open up the eyes and make them appear bigger. Additionally, be sure to use a light hand when applying mascara to the inner corners of the eyes, as too much product can make the eyes look smaller.

When applying mascara to the lashes, make sure to use a zig-zag motion when applying the product. This will help to separate and define the lashes, and will also help to create the illusion of bigger eyes. Additionally, be sure to use a lengthening mascara, as this will help to extend and thicken the lashes, making the eyes appear larger.

15 Use Liquid Highlighter on Cheeks

While a lot of these techniques for small eyes revolve around your eyes, why not try to involve your entire face? Using liquid highlighter along your cheekbones is a great way to highlight your eyes and your cheekbones. Typically, when I use this trick, I'll actually use a pale, pale pink. It totally works!

16 Avoid Darker Eye Shadow Colors

When you are using eye shadow, make sure that one of the tips that you are following is to avoid any of the darker eye shadow colors. What this means is that you should ditch the dark shadows and take up lighter hues. Do you remember in the earlier tips about lighter eye shadow? Take that to heart!

***

When it comes to makeup for small eyes, it is important to remember that dark colors can be very overpowering. Darker eye shadow colors can make the eyes appear even smaller, so it is best to avoid them. Instead, opt for lighter hues such as beige, ivory, or light pink. These colors will enhance the eyes without making them look too small.

When applying eye shadow, make sure to start with the lightest shade and then gradually build up to the darker shades. This will help to create a more subtle look, which is ideal for small eyes. For a more dramatic look, you can use a darker color on the outer corner of the eye and blend it inwards.

It is also important to remember that the size of the eye shadow brush matters. When using a smaller brush, you will be able to apply the eye shadow more precisely and avoid using too much product. This will help to create a more natural look.

When it comes to eyeliner, a thin line close to the lash line is best. This will help to define the eyes without making them look too heavy. You can also use a white or light colored eyeliner on the lower lash line to make the eyes look brighter.

17 Use Eyeliner Tricks

With all of the different tips for small eyes out there, you've got to remember that there are tons of eyeliner tricks out there that you can use! Cat-eyes are a great way to draw out your eyes and really make them appear that much bigger!

***

Eyeliner is a makeup staple and can be a great way to enhance the size of small eyes. To create the perfect cat-eye look, start by lining your upper lash line with a dark eyeliner. You can use a pencil, liquid, or gel liner depending on your preference. Make sure to line the entire lash line, from the inner corner to the outer corner. Then, draw a thin line from the outer corner of your eye that extends slightly beyond the outer corner. Make sure to keep the line thin and close to the lash line. You can then thicken the line towards the outer corner to create a winged effect. Finally, smudge the line a bit to soften the look.

If you’re looking for a more subtle look, you can use a lighter shade of eyeliner and just line the upper lash line. This will give your eyes definition without making them look overly dramatic. You can also use a white or nude eyeliner to line the lower lash line. This will help to brighten up the area and make your eyes look bigger.

18 No Thick Lines

Finally girls, the last makeup tip for small eyes that we're going to talk about is thick lines. When you are trying to make your eyes appear much larger, make sure that there are no thick lines and instead, make sure that you do some smaller, thin lines.

So girls, there you have it! My top 18 makeup tips for small eyes that can really make them appear larger! So, what other techniques for small eyes do you have to share? Give 'em up!
