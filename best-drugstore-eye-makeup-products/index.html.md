# More Bang for Fewer Bucks with Drugstore Eye Makeup Products ...

Sometimes, it can be hard to figure out which drugstore eye makeup products are good, and which ones are not so good. I’m always trying out new drugstore eye makeup products, whether it’s primer, eyeshadow, or even eye makeup remover. I feel like I’ve tried it all at this point. I’ve compiled a list of my favorite drugstore eye makeup products, so if you’re looking for the best of the best, you’ve come to the right place!

## 1. Sonia Kashuk Dramatically Defining Liquid Eyeliner

![Sonia Kashuk Dramatically Defining Liquid Eyeliner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lm/ea/syzu2u7l_410x395.jpg)

I sing my praises of this liquid eyeliner all too often, so if you read my articles, you know how much I love this liner. I used to try any eyeliner out there, but after finding this eyeliner almost a year ago, I haven’t tried anything else. It stays on all day, with no smudging whatsoever. It's the best eyeliner I've ever used, out of both high end and drugstore eye makeup products. It’s perfect for a day at work or a night on the town, and anything in between. It is my go-to eyeliner, no matter the occasion.

## 2. Almay Eye Makeup Remover Pads

![Almay Eye Makeup Remover Pads](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t1/zd/jkhp5sgh_450x448.jpg)

I think everyone has tried these eye makeup remover pads at one point or another, but if you haven’t yet, go to a drugstore right now and pick some up. They’re perfect for everyday use, but I also love to bring the smaller containers on trips with me. On busy days where I don’t have time to cleanse my face thoroughly, I know that I always have the small pack handy just in case.

## 3. Maybelline New York Fit Me Concealer

![Maybelline New York Fit Me Concealer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rz/xg/kv8iz2mw_138x500.jpg)

Technically, this is not an eye makeup product. However, I use it as an under eye concealer, and it covers up all of your secrets. Believe me. I love using it to cover up blemishes, but after a long night, this concealer works wonders for your under eye circles.

## 4. Physicians Formula Nude Palette

![Physicians Formula Nude Palette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ij/i4/xlgv3ez8_400x320.jpg)

If you want to try out Urban Decay’s Naked Palette, but don’t necessarily want to spend a small fortune on it, try Physicians Formula’s Nude Palette. The colors are definitely the closest match you’ll find to the Naked Palette, and with an exponentially lower price tag, it’s hard to pass up this deal.

## 5. Rimmel Extra Super Lash Mascara

![Rimmel Extra Super Lash Mascara](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rz/ik/q0h6vowz_600x570.jpg)

It seems like everywhere you turn, people are praising this mascara all over the Internet. If you’re looking for mascara to last all day, at a fraction of the price of designer and big name mascaras, Rimmel’s Extra Super Lash mascara is definitely worth checking out!

## 6. L’Oreal HiP Studio Secrets

![L’Oreal HiP Studio Secrets](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/of/mp/nv3wla1f_600x644.jpg)

Over the years, I have come to the decision that L’Oreal makes the best drugstore eyeshadow on the market. No matter what kind it is, their eyeshadow is always super pigmented, stays all day, and they always have the prettiest shades. L’Oreal’s HiP Studio Secrets is by far my favorite drugstore eyeshadow, but it can be pretty tough to find in stores.

## 7. Wet N Wild Fergie Eyeshadow Primer

![Wet N Wild Fergie Eyeshadow Primer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kd/zl/f9uehw7n_500x427.jpg)

I love trying out different eyeshadow primers, but I don’t love the price tag that most of them come with. This Wet n Wild Eyeshadow Primer is different, though. It’s barely a few dollars, and it works just as great as some of your favorite high-end eyeshadow primers!

What are your favorite drugstore eye makeup products? I’m always looking for new drugstore makeup products; so let me know your favorite products in the comments!
