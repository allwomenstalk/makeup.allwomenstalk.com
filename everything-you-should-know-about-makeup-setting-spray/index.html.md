# Everything You Should Know about Makeup Setting Spray ...

Makeup setting spray is a magical product that promises to keep your makeup in place all day long. And all night too, if you’re planning on hitting the town tonight. Just like with anything you put on your skin, it’s a good idea to understand the product before using it. That’s why I went to the experts and gathered all the pertinent info about makeup setting spray so that you can decide whether it’s right for you or not. Here’s everything you need to know.

## 1. The Spray Should Be the Last Step in Your Makeup Routine

![The Spray Should Be the Last Step in Your Makeup Routine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g5/cj/x1yoekb4564bc38f079b6389557493_500x498.jpg)

Setting spray makes a similar promise to what primer promises. However, unlike primer, the spray is formulated to be the last step in your routine. You need to complete your entire look and then use the setting spray to hold it all in place. It works by preventing your cosmetics from absorbing into your skin so that they stay where they belong until you’re ready to wash them off.

## 2. You Only Need a Small Amount of the Spray

![You Only Need a Small Amount of the Spray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q9/h1/x4n1yof2564bc3fdb853f608988791_500x486.jpg)

When it comes to makeup setting spray, more is not better. In fact, you want to go easy on the makeup spray or it won’t do the job it’s designed to do. When you’re ready to use the spray, lightly mist it on top of your makeup to help it stay put. That’s all you have to do!

## 3. Makeup Setting Spray Contains a Wide Range of Ingredients

![Makeup Setting Spray Contains a Wide Range of Ingredients](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ia/j9/zfmzgptt564bc4645f553990247006_500x517.jpg)

Not all makeup setting sprays are created equal and you’ll need to read labels to find the one that will work best for you. Most are made with a base of water and alcohol and then the ingredients that create the film on your face that prevents the makeup from fading or rubbing off. You may need to experiment with products before you find the one that works best for you.

## 4. Some Makeup Setting Sprays Are Sticky

![Some Makeup Setting Sprays Are Sticky](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i3/zz/kzj7p48z564bc4e5bf47f241662639_500x500.jpg)

Imagine spraying hairspray on your skin. That sticky residue is what holds your hair in place. Not all makeup setting sprays will do this, but some of them will leave your face feeling sticky and coated in a thin layer of residue. If you notice this, try a different spray because that’s not comfortable or good for your skin.

## 5. You Might Not Need the Spray Every Single Day

![You Might Not Need the Spray Every Single Day](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bj/oq/n3z6061z564bc57a8f762160810383_500x488.jpg)

If, for the most part, your makeup looks the same at the end of the workday as it did when you got to the office, you probably don’t need setting spray. If you’re planning to have dinner with clients or hit happy hour with the girls, but won’t have time to refresh your makeup, it’s a good day for setting spray. In general, you can use it every day, but you may find that you only need it when you’ll be out all day and all night.

## 6. Some Setting Spray Are Designed for Wearing While You Sleep

![Some Setting Spray Are Designed for Wearing While You Sleep](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bw/v1/dqiiv1vb564bc64c65e10444242905_500x500.jpg)

This goes against everything you know about wearing makeup to bed, but some setting sprays are made so that you can sleep in your makeup and wake up looking gorgeous. Manufacturers say these products are great for a sleepover with a new guy or when you have to go all night looking fab. Would you sleep with a setting spray on your face?

## 7. Setting Sprays Aren’t Right for Every Girl

![Setting Sprays Aren’t Right for Every Girl](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a7/va/brstk7y8564bc68bea8a0221504621_500x500.jpg)

Just because a product exists doesn’t mean it’s a good choice for just anyone. If you have sensitive skin or specific skin conditions, you may not benefit from the spray. Because many of them contain alcohol, you may notice that your skin gets dry when you use it often.
