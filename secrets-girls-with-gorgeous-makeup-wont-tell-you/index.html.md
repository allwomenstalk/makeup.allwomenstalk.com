# 7 Secrets Girls with Gorgeous Makeup Wont Tell You ...

All of us have seen girls with gorgeous makeup and wondered what it is exactly that sets them apart. How are they so fabulously talented with makeup? The truth is that there’re secrets behind their makeup look and you can learn them too. Soon you’ll be the girl with makeup everyone envies!

## 1. They’re Experimental with Makeup

![hair, human hair color, face, eyebrow, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ql/kl/wkrqmwmq586601963acb9364182356_600x662.jpg)

Girls with gorgeous makeup have their secrets and this’s one of them. They’re experimental when it comes to makeup. They love to play with makeup and try new looks and techniques. They aren’t afraid to try a winged eyeliner in purple or mix foundations to achieve the perfect shade. They don’t box themselves in when it comes to makeup application.

## 2. They Study Makeup

![hair, face, black hair, pink, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k6/n0/i1hcd4ro586601cc2b69c594777811_600x600.jpg)

Another thing girls with gorgeous makeup do is study it. They’re very interested in makeup and can happily spend an hour figuring out why a certain makeup look is unique. They can easily spend hours watching makeup reviews or tutorials on YouTube. And the beauty is that this, as well as the other 6 secrets, are things you can do, too.

## 3. Not Everything Makes the Cut

![eyebrow, face, hair, cheek, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ho/gh/ylnfm8kw586602531f904230757702_600x600.jpg)

A girl with fabulous makeup doesn’t accept mediocre products. That doesn’t mean she always pays top dollar, either. But she won’t settle for makeup that doesn’t deliver the promised results. You can be choosy about your products, too. If you’re not satisfied with a makeup product you purchased then return it for something you do love.

## 4. They Know Their Best and Worst Features

![hair, face, nose, black hair, lip,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iq/uj/q9v6rt0l5866020b825d0523326763_600x600.jpg)

Girls with gorgeous makeup have become very aware of their best and worst features. They know how to play up the features they loves and downplay the ones they don’t. You can do the same thing! If you hate your lips but love your eyes then focus on having beautiful eye makeup and wear a nude lip product. It’s all about directing attention where you want it to go.

## 5. They Have a Passion for Makeup

![hair, human hair color, face, black hair, eyebrow,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ob/ih/niavup3a5866026d93b15658060931_600x600.jpg)

Having a passion for makeup will almost always result in having makeup that others admire. Someone who has gorgeous makeup is passionate about it and finds makeup to be fun and enjoyable. It’s not a chore to endure. Open yourself up to the world of makeup and be open to enjoying it more rather than just going through the same boring routine each morning. Discovering how makeup can make the most of your beauty is something to be passionate about.

## 6. They Know Where to Splurge and Where to save

![eyewear, hair, glasses, face, vision care,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ui/k4/cv0znbnj586602ab3f569522051547_600x600.jpg)

While girls with gorgeous makeup don’t care to shell out the cash for a quality product, don’t assume they don’t save where they can. I’ve talked to endless women about makeup products that look lovely on them. Many times, the makeup product that sets them apart is a drugstore brand. The trick is in knowing where to splurge and where to save. Everyone is going to have different opinions about this but for me, I tend to go drugstore for mascara, liner and lippies and spend more for the products that enhance my skin such as foundation, blush, highlighter and contouring products.

## 7. They’re Brave when It Comes to Makeup

![eyebrow, face, cheek, lip, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/el/ka/ozthfgar5866030ebf513466694231_600x600.jpg)

You have to be a little brave when it comes to makeup. It’s good to step out of your comfort zone a little bit. This doesn’t mean you have to wear anything crazy but why not try something new? Go for a coral lipstick instead of the same pink you’ve worn the last 5 years. Being brave in your makeup choices pays off.

These 7 secrets can help you have the gorgeous makeup look you want. Which of these secrets are you going to try? Do you have any secrets of your own to share?
