# How to Make DIY Lipstick out of Bubble Gum ...

If you don't have any cute lipsticks laying around your house, then you can create one yourself. All you'll need is two pieces of gum, food coloring, and vaseline. Start by mixing all of the ingredients together, and then put them in the microwave to melt them. Once that's done, then leave it in your fridge for five minutes.
