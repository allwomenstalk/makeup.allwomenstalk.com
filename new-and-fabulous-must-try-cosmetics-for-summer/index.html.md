# 7 New and Fabulous Must\-Try Cosmetics for Summer ...

This summer, there are some new must try cosmetics for a fresh beauty look. These must try cosmetics will give you a bright and glowing look for the warm months. There have been a plethora of new products introduced this season, but these are my favorites!

## 1. The Lipstick Queen’s Hello Sailor Lipstick

![The Lipstick Queen’s Hello Sailor Lipstick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/07/1_the-lipstick-queens-hello-sailor-lipstick_400x400.jpg)

Hello Sailor is an innovative new lipstick from The Lipstick Queen that appears to be navy in the tube, but goes on as a sheer deep berry shade. Blackberry and wine colored lips have been a huge trend lately, and this lipstick is the perfect shade for summer due to its lightweight, silky texture and sheer appearance! This lipstick is loaded with Vitamin E for super-moisturization in addition to gorgeous color, making it a definite choice on this must try cosmetics list.

## 2. NARS Shimmer Eyeshadow in Euphrate

![NARS Shimmer Eyeshadow in Euphrate](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/07/2_nars-shimmer-eyeshadow-in-euphrate_536x448.jpg)

The grunge makeup trend made a comeback with this year’s Met Gala theme, and there’s nothing sexier than adding a silver sparkle to your smokey eye. Enter NARS Shimmer Eyeshadow in Euphrate. This light-reflecting eyeshadow is perfect for adding a highlight to your eyes, and even looks great smudged below your bottom lashes. Use a dampened brush for a more dramatic shade of silver. This shadow blends super easily and is a great addition to your summer makeup look.

## 3. Stila Countless Color Pigments in Center Stage

![Stila Countless Color Pigments in Center Stage](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/07/3_stila-countless-color-pigments-in-center-stage_314x316.jpg)

Not only does Stila’s Countless Color Pigment in Center Stage look gorgeous on the skin, it’s beautiful in the package and will look great on any vanity table! This product includes 3 bright colors that are swirled together in the pan. You can apply each color separately or blend 2 together for a custom shade. Center Stage boasts gorgeous blue, pink, and coral.

## 4. MAC Temperature Rising Bronze Body Oil

![MAC Temperature Rising Bronze Body Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/07/4_mac-temperature-rising-bronze-body-oil_375x500.jpg)

Get a jump on your summer glow with MAC’s Bronze Body Oil from their new Temperature Rising collection. This oil provides a glamorous sun effect by offering a shimmery, yet sheer sheen on the skin. In addition to the bronzy glow, your skin will be softened, smoothed, and moisturized. Apply a thin layer and you won’t feel greasy, as the oil soaks right into the skin!

## 5. Face Stockholm for J.Crew Blush Stick in Rose Flush

![Face Stockholm for J.Crew Blush Stick in Rose Flush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/07/5_face-stockholm-for-j-crew-blush-stick-in-rose-flush_375x500.jpg)

Summer is the perfect season for sporting a rosy flush on the cheeks, and Face Stockholm for J.Crew’s Blush Stick in Rose Flush provides the ultimate hue. This creamy blush will give your skin a dewy look that is ideal for the summer months. Blend it onto the skin after you apply your foundation, but just before you set your makeup with face powder for the best application. Apply it using your fingertips since the warmth from you skin will allow for an easier application of the creamy texture.

## 6. Chantecaille Just Skin Tinted Moisturizer

![Chantecaille Just Skin Tinted Moisturizer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/07/6_chantecaille-just-skin-tinted-moisturizer_300x400.jpg)

Nothing is more uncomfortable than wearing heavy foundation when it’s extremely hot outside. Summer is the best time of year for tinted moisturizer. Chantecaille’s Just Skin Tinted Moisturizer provides a sheer, even coverage while protecting your skin from the sun’s harsh rays with SPF 15. This product is an anti-irritant that provides antioxidants to the skin and includes honeysuckle, green tea, and sodium hyaluronate to lock in moisture.

## 7. MAC RiRi Lipstick in RiRi Woo

![MAC RiRi Lipstick in RiRi Woo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/07/7_mac-riri-lipstick-in-riri-woo_600x920.jpg)

MAC’s Rihanna makeup collection is definitely a big hit this summer! My favorite piece of the collection is the lipstick in RiRi Woo-a cool matte red shade that is perfect for summer. Summertime is great for wearing bright makeup shades and you can never go wrong with a red lip!

These must try cosmetics for summer will refresh your look! Which new cosmetics will you be wearing this summer? Have you already tried any of the products on this list?
