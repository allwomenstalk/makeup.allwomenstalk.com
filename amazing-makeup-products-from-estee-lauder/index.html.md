# 7 Amazing Makeup Products from Estee Lauder ...

I have been using makeup products from Estee Lauder for years, and have found several products that I return to time and time again. I am a girl who loves makeup. I love the colors, and I love trying new application techniques. So, it is very important to me that the products I use are of good quality. The makeup products from Estee Lauder never disappoint me, and I am always very happy with the products I purchase.

## 1. Ideal Light Brush on Illuminator

![Ideal Light Brush on Illuminator](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/25131400_2769_409x360.jpg)

The Ideal Light Brush on Illuminator is a little gem I found about a year ago. It hides dark circles and instantly brightens up your face. I can’t believe how awake I look when I use this illuminator. I love the brush tip pen that makes application a breeze. I’m not exaggerating when I say I go from looking tired to looking wide awake in under a minute! This really is one of the [best makeup products](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/best-makeup-products-everyone-is-buying-right-now/) from Estee Lauder!

**Price:** $27.50 at [esteelauder.com](http://www.esteelauder.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 2. Pure Color Eye Shadow

![Pure Color Eye Shadow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/25131401_1951_409x336.jpg)

One of my biggest pet peeves with eye shadow is that the colors don’t always show up like I would like them to. The Pure Color Eye Shadow always has a vibrant color that I can see after I apply it. This eye shadow also doesn’t crease, and it stays on for a long time. I am up very early in the morning, and I don’t go to bed until late. Yet, despite my long hours, this eye shadow always looks the same in the evening as it did early in the morning!

**Price:** $21.00 at [esteelauder.com](http://www.esteelauder.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 3. Automatic Eye Pencil Duo

![Automatic Eye Pencil Duo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/25131400_2374_409x396.jpg)

I may love makeup, but until recently I shied away from eyeliner. It is one of those things that is very easy to mess up. However, on a whim I tried the Automatic Eye Pencil Duo from Estee Lauder and discovered that I could actually apply it and look good! Because it is creamy, it goes on really easy, and it has a smudger on the end that can erase mistakes. This eye pencil also comes with a refill, which is great because you only have to purchase the refills when you have used up the pencil.

**Price:** $26.00 at [esteelauder.com](http://www.esteelauder.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 4. Automatic Brow Pencil Duo

![Automatic Brow Pencil Duo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/25131400_6270_174x262.jpg)

I am blonde with very fair skin, which means my eyebrows are not very prominent. An eye brow pencil makes my eyebrows stand out more, which is like the icing on the cake when I am wearing makeup. Like the eye pencil, the Automatic Brow Pencil comes with refills, which is a perk I really like. It also has a brow brush on one end that helps evenly distribute the pencil and shapes your brows at the same time. If you have light or sparse brows, this brow pencil just might become your new best friend!

**Price:** $26.00 at [esteelauder.com](http://www.esteelauder.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 5. Sumptuous Extreme Lash Multiplying Volume Mascara in Extreme Black

![Sumptuous Extreme Lash Multiplying Volume Mascara in Extreme Black](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/25131400_0582_409x408.jpg)

This mascara is by far the best I have ever used! It actually gives the effect of false lashes by creating a lot of volume and a lot of length. The Sumptuous Extreme Mascara is fortified with a vitamin complex that conditions lashes. It also is very long lasting. It is not waterproof, but I can attest to the fact that it doesn’t smudge and will even stay on if you have just a few tears!

**Price:** $26.00 at [esteelauder.com](http://www.esteelauder.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 6. Pure Color Blush

![Pure Color Blush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/25131401_8378_409x355.jpg)

I have natural pink undertones in my skin, which means I have a hard time finding a blush that shows up on my skin without looking clownish. The Pure Color Blush by Estee Lauder actually provides a sheer color that looks like a natural blush. Another benefit of this blush is its ability to provide a natural glow. Whenever I use it, I always look fresh faced with a healthy pink glow. Like many of Estee Lauder’s products, this blush is long lasting, so you don’t have to worry about touch ups.

**Price:** $30.00 at [esteelauder.com](http://www.esteelauder.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 7. Pure Color Sensuous Rouge in Cheating Heart

![Pure Color Sensuous Rouge in Cheating Heart](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/25131401_2425_409x377.jpg)

I normally don’t recommend a specific color for lipstick, but this is a beautiful berry red that would look good on most skin tones. I absolutely love red lipstick, and this lipstick is the [best red lipstick](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/best-red-lipsticks-you-simply-must-have/) I have ever used. It is very long lasting, and it doesn’t bleed. Due to the fruit extracts and shea butter that are in the lipstick formula, it also is very moisturizing. There is also a vanilla scent to the lipstick, which is like the cherry on top!

**Price:** $28.00 at [esteelauder.com](http://www.esteelauder.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
