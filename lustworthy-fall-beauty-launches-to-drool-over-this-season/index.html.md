# Lustworthy Fall Beauty Launches to Drool over This Season ...

Warning! Reading this post is going to make you want to go on a major beauty buying spree so proceed with caution. No, really, it’s that serious! I always get excited about new beauty products but it’s especially exciting when a brand is launching new products for a new season. Prepare yourselves, because I think you’re going to love these new fall beauty launches as much as we do!

## 1. Carmex Comfort Care Lip Balm

_ One of the most common beauty issues we face in the fall is chapped lips. It’s colder and windier so our poor lips really take a beating. Carmex just released a new line of lip balms that contain colloidal oatmeal and fruit seed oil for pampered lips any time of the day. What’s even more exciting is that they comes in watermelon, mixed berry, and sugar plum. Carmex’s original formula is excellent, but it’s so blah. Their newest lip balm will be great worn alone or underneath lipstick to prime the lips!

Available at target.com_ for $2.79 for a pack of 2

## 2. Hourglass Ambient Light Correcting Primer

![brown, skin, product, lotion, cream,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qa/po/afdbtvgw57d4af8087267671217450_426x576.jpg)

I think we’re all well aware of how important a good primer is to a flawless face of makeup. The newest addition to Hourglass’ lineup is a light correcting primer that neutralizes, smooths skin, blurs imperfections, absorbs instantly, and imparts a soft, lit from within glow. Pick from three flattering colors that you can wear alone for a little boost or under your foundation.

Available at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for $44

## 3. Yes to Tomatoes Clear Skin Detoxifying Charcoal Facial Wipes

![product, espresso, multimedia, MAKEUP, SWIPE,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ye/ul/xnlgfj7i57d4af8178da7477468595_600x559.jpg)

I’m just a little obsessed with charcoal. I mean, it works wonders internally and externally so I couldn’t be happier to see Yes To Tomatoes adding charcoal to their already amazing facial wipes. These babies are affordable, gentle, and thoroughly cleanse skin so you don’t have to worry about fussing with your skin care products after a long day. Cleanse and detox your skin in one easy step!

Available at [ulta.com](http://www.ulta.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for $5.99

## 4. PAT McGRATH LABS Lust 004 Lipstick Kit

![pink, beauty, product, cosmetics, eye,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t3/na/mzrfaguh57d4af810f386283321994_450x447.jpg)

Yes, yes, yes! First things first, this packaging is soooo pretty. Second, anything that celeb makeup artist Pat McGrath touches, or in this case designs, is destined to be amazing. This 5-piece kit comes with 2 matte lipsticks, a clear gloss, gold pigment, and red glitter. You can create ombre lips, wine-colored lips with high shine, or even add the glitter for major sparkle and shine. There are so many options!

Available at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for $60

## 5. Lancôme Glow Subtil Silky Crème Highlighter

![face, brown, eye, cheek, face powder,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n3/jj/v62413dt57d4af819510e894040076_450x368.jpg)

Highlighter has become a must-have beauty product because of its ability to instantly elevate a look. What other product can give you a gorgeous glow and sculpt your features? Lancôme’s Crème Highlighters comes in 5 stunning shades that are easy to apply with a brush or your fingers so your face is illuminated and radiant.

Available at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for $30

## 6. It Cosmetics Superhero Eye Transforming Antiaging Super Palette

![eye, eye shadow, beauty, product, organ,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cy/kf/j5qzpwhe57d4af82393c9421221487_600x305.jpg)

OMG!! Is this not one of the best-looking palettes you’ve ever laid your eyes on? Aside from that, this palette was created by plastic surgeons and each eye shadow contains anti-aging ingredients that your delicate eye area will love. Another plus is that this palette contains eye foundation which is something brand new to the beauty game. It color corrects, neutralizes, and smooths for easy application.

Available at [ulta.com](http://www.ulta.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for $42

## 7. MAC Liptensity Lipsticks

![lipstick, product, lip, cosmetics, organ,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ct/ze/voa74uk057d4af82e3512933659088_600x278.jpg)

MAC lipsticks are legendary and just when you thought they couldn’t get any better, they come out with a brand new line of their most pigmented lipsticks yet. There will be 14 Liptensity lipsticks ranging from nudes to violets. Unlike other lipsticks, the shade you see in the tube is exactly what will show up on your lips, how cool is that?!

Available at [maccosmetics.com](https://goo.gl/4L4cQ7) for $21
