# 7 NYX Cosmetics Products You Will Love ...

If you've never tried NYX Cosmetics products, now's the time to start. Founded in 1999 by 26 year old Toni K, the NYX line has beautifully pigmented products at extremely affordable prices. Toni was inspired to start the makeup line because she and her friends wanted to be able to look their best and not spend a fortune. Check out some of these awesome NYX Cosmetics products and get ready to be wowed by their look and affordability:

## 1. High Definition Photo Concealer Wand

![High Definition Photo Concealer Wand](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mm/lu/jf9rkks3_400x400.jpg)

This has to be one of my all time favorite NYX Cosmetics products. Not only does it do an incredible job concealing, it also lightens and brightens every area it touches, without being shimmery or sparkly. It also doesn't crease or cake, making it seem like an absolute miracle in a bottle.

## 2. Butter Gloss

![Butter Gloss](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zk/ng/0btwxpvw_410x400.jpg)

Want the look of a gloss without the sticky, gooey feel? Then you've got to try NYX's Butter Gloss. With a super-high level of color pigmentation and a wide range of colors, it'd be silly not to pick up 1, 2, or 7 of these incredible glosses at an unbeatable price.

## 3. Retractable Eyeliner

![Retractable Eyeliner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r8/yj/02djnc1r_400x32.jpg)

It may seem a bit difficult to achieve the highlighted look of an under eye pencil or a bright eyeliner, but with the NYX Retractable Eyeliner, it's easy as pie. The consistency of the pencil is a bit pliable, so you can glide the color right on without dragging or getting the jagged edge look. I recommend the Turquoise Blue shade for an amazing pop of color.

## 4. Butter Lip Balm

![Butter Lip Balm](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w3/ez/kir6o3mu_400x400.jpg)

Honestly, every "Butter" lip product from NYX is awesome, but like the glosses, there's something about the Butter Balm that just screams gorgeous. For a subtle pop of color that's also hydrating and makes your lips feel super soft, pick up one of these babies.

## 5. Soft Matte Lip Cream

![Soft Matte Lip Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a5/s2/j59fniip_410x400.jpg)

Matte lip colors are all the rage lately, and this lip "cream" is perfect to get the job done. At first, the creamy color glides on beautifully, then later transforms into more of a matte stain. The color is amazingly long-lasting, even strong enough to power through hours of drinking and eating.

## 6. Powder Blush

![Powder Blush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/or/xw/lhfmf8sq_410x391.jpg)

It has such a simple name but really stands above so many high-end products with its color payoff and long lasting wear. The signature quilting of the product helps to transfer the blush flawlessly onto your skin, and the wide range of colors helps any skin tone achieve that natural flush. You truly only need to dip your blush brush once into the compact to get enough product to make your whole face light up.

## 7. Eye Shadow Palette

![Eye Shadow Palette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eo/ly/up9ec1xt_410x268.jpg)

This palette could be viewed as a dupe for Urban Decay's Naked2 Eyeshadow Palette, but for less than half the price! Indulge yourself in ten shades of taupe and bronze and prepare to wow everyone that lays eyes on yours. But hurry, this palette is limited edition so snatch up these amazing hues that go with just about anything before it's too late!

NYX has an absolutely amazing line of products, and I highly recommend checking them out! They can be found online, at Ulta, Target, and several other stores. Have you tried any NYX products yet? What have you liked the best?
