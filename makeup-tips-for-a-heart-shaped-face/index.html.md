# 7 Makeup Tips for a Heart\-Shaped Face ...

Makeup tips for a heart-shaped face are all over the internet but how do you know which ones actually work? Oh you heart-shaped faced girls out there, you’ve got one of the most unique and different shaped faces in the world! I’ve got all of the makeup tips for a heart-shaped face right here and below, we’ll learn how to pair that beautifully shaped face with some awesome makeup techniques! So girls, you ready to explore the top makeup tips for a heart-shaped face?

## 1. Natural Brow

![Natural Brow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/07/13_800x921.jpg)

The very first makeup tip for a heart-shaped face that we’re going to talk about is a natural brow. You girls don’t have to do a thing to your arch or try to extend your brow. Just some waxing to a natural curve and you’re all set!

## 2. Widen Chin with Concealer

![Widen Chin with Concealer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/07/23_500x656.jpg)

Did you know that you can actually even out the shape of your face by widening your chin with a bit of concealer? Just a few dabs in the right place and this makeup tip for a heart-shaped face will make your face that much more even all the way around!

## 3. Avoid Lots of Blush

![Avoid Lots of Blush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/07/33_800x1199.jpg)

One makeup tip for a heart-shaped face that lots of women don’t follow is the blush. I love blush – but I have a round face. If you have a heart-shaped face, just avoid using a lot of blush ladies! Just a little swipe!

## 4. Contour

![Contour](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/07/43_613x481.jpg)

Contouring is a great way to really shape your face the way you want it or highlight your shape. Use a little bronzer and contour it up! Trust me, it makes a difference!

## 5. Vertical Sweep of Blush

![Vertical Sweep of Blush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/07/53_800x1095.jpg)

If you are going to use blush, one makeup tip for a heart-shaped face is to actually use a vertical sweep. Just sweep your blush brush upwards and you’ll see just how much that one sweep highlights the beauty of your heart shape!

## 6. Dark Eyeliners

![Dark Eyeliners](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/07/62_1280x1707.jpg)

Dark eyeliners is another way you can draw people right to your eyes and can actually allow a focal point of your face. Dark browns, deep blacks and even some navy colors are all great eyeliner colors for your face!

## 7. Lip Liner

![Lip Liner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/07/72_690x470.jpg)

Finally ladies, the last makeup tip for a heart-shaped face that we’re going to talk about is lip liner! When you have a heart-shaped face, you want to make sure that you have a great focal point for your face and your lips could totally be that! So girls, start lining your lips before applying lipgloss!

There you have it! All of the makeup tips for a heart-shaped face out there! What other makeup tips for a heart-shaped face do you have? Any to add?
