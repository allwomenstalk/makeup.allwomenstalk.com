# 7 Splendid Makeup Tips for Tomboys ...

Being a tomboy doesn't mean that you can't wear makeup – all you need are the right makeup tips for tomboys! I know what you are thinking, "If I'm a tomboy, why would I want to read about makeup tips for tomboys?" The thing is, every girl needs a little makeup sometimes. So whether you are just trying to go with the natural look or you hate makeup, take a look at my tips for tomboys below!

## 1. Natural Colors

I know that when you are a tomboy, you might not want to go with a whole lot of colors, especially pinks, so why not go with something super natural? This is one of the top makeup tips for tomboys! You don't have to have a ton of colors on if you don't want to, just go with some earthy tones and hues that are natural for your skin color!

## 2. Small Bit of Eyeliner

Adding a small bit of eyeliner can actually go a really long way. If you are feeling really daring, you can even try out a colored eyeliner. For me, I just use eyeliner on my top lid when I am heading to work, so all you tomboys out there, that might be a good start for you!

## 3. Go with a Color You like

When you are picking out colors for your eye shadow or even your eyeliner, go with the color choices that you like. If you like black, go with black, if you like blue, go with blue, but find a color that you'll actually wear. That's the important thing. If you like dark blue, buy a navy pencil or eye shadow!

## 4. Little Mascara if You Dare

I know that mascara is hard to put on, especially if you aren't use to it, so tomboys, if you don't want to do mascara, that's okay. Just eyeliner will make your eyes pop and stand out, but mascara will really draw attention to your eyes. Personally, it took me a really long time to get how to put mascara on.

## 5. No Blush

Wearing blush is something that takes a lot to master to put on, so if you are looking for something quick and something easy, just skip the blush. You might not really need it for the small bit of makeup you are wearing and who knows, maybe your natural pink undertones will show through on your cheeks!

## 6. No Bronzer

Bronzer is another one of those makeup products that takes a lot to apply and to learn how to apply correctly. For me, I have absolutely no idea how to put on bronzer – not to the point that it looks great anyway! Bronzer can add more definition to your cheekbones, but if you don't mind your cheeks the way they are, no need for bronzer!

## 7. Light Powder

Finally, the last thing that you can do if you are looking for super easy makeup tips for a tomboy is just a little dusting of powder over your face. This will take away any shine at all! Just a small bit around your whole face will give it a glow that really makes you stand out!

So, you don't have to be a girly girl to wear makeup! Just use a couple of these makeup tips for tomboys that are simple and quick! What other tips do you ladies have? Give 'em up!
