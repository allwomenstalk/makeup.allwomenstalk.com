# 7 Gorgeous Coral Lip Glosses ...

The hottest trend in lip color this season is more than a color, it’s an attitude! Coral lip gloss is so on-trend, and flatters so many complexions, you’ve just got to try one. But which coral lip gloss to try, and which to skip? I can help. Here’s a sneak peek at the hottest coral lip glosses of the summer… pucker up and let’s go!

## 1. Buxom Big & Healthy Lip Polish

![Buxom Big & Healthy Lip Polish](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/05/1_buxom-big-healthy-lip-polish_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $18.00 at [sephora.com](https://goo.gl/fmdA3d)
While there’s an entire palette of colors, the “Debbie” hue is my favorite, all coral glossy and shimmery. “Destiny” is also pretty, and seems made for olive complexions… both are sheer and provide a slight plumping effect, and contain vitamins A and E for long-lasting lip hydration.

## 2. Nars Lip Gloss

![Nars Lip Gloss](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/05/2_nars-lip-gloss_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $24.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
With 30 shades to choose from, it might be difficult to decide which of these glosses is right for you… but remember, stay focused! We’re all about the coral lip gloss, so try “Orgasm” or “Wonder,” or (even better?) “Super Orgasm.”

## 3. Dior Addict Lip Polish

![Dior Addict Lip Polish](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/05/3_dior-addict-lip-polish_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $30.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This is my favorite coral lip gloss of the summer, with a built-in primer for the lushest pout ever! The coral color is vibrant, like the name implies - Glow Expert. And the color is ideal for almost any complexion, from peaches and cream to deep olive.

## 4. Smashbox Reflection High Shine Lip Gloss

![Smashbox Reflection High Shine Lip Gloss](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/05/4_smashbox-reflection-high-shine-lip-gloss_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $19.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Does any word in the English language conjure summer more than “watermelon”? That’s why this coral lip gloss is so aptly named, with the same gorgeous coral color, with a hint of pink. It’s so shimmery!

## 5. Philosophy Papaya Passion Punch Lip Shine

![Philosophy Papaya Passion Punch Lip Shine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/05/5_philosophy-papaya-passion-punch-lip-shine_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $10.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I’ve been a fan of Philosophy lip gloss for years, and my favorite shade for the coral lip gloss trend is the “Papaya Passion Punch,” which is spot-on for the trend, and even tastes and smells as sweet as the real thing. Also, note the price - can you think of a less expensive way to try the trend?

## 6. Tarte Maracuja Divine Shine Lip Gloss

![Tarte Maracuja Divine Shine Lip Gloss](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/05/6_tarte-maracuja-divine-shine-lip-gloss_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $21.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Keep your pucker hydrated and happy with maracuja oil, the key ingredient in this gloss, and keep it on-trend with the gorgeous coral color, called “Bliss.” This gloss is ultra-glossy, and the applicator “pen” helps avoid a mess.

## 7. Sephora + Pantone Universe Color of the Year Lipgloss Set

![Sephora + Pantone Universe Color of the Year Lipgloss Set](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/05/7_sephora-pantone-universe-color-of-the-year-lipgloss-set_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $20.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Sephora and Pantone have teamed up to provide their take on the coral lip gloss trend with this tangerine-hued set, featuring four lush lippies. Hooked on the hue? The set is also available in matching nail varnishes and face make-up.

With so many gorgeous coral lip glosses to choose from, all of them equally on-trend and lush, how will you ever choose just one? I’d choose the Dior “Glow Expert” … But if you could choose just one, which would you choose, and why? Do tell!
