# 8 Great Ways to Make Your Eyes Look Bigger ...

Learning the ways to make your eyes look bigger with makeup is a great lesson! Your eyes can be used for all sorts of things – such as drawing in your favorite boy! There are tons of different ways to make your eyes look bigger, but there isn't really a compiled list anywhere – until now! I've got the top 8 ways to make your eyes look bigger by using makeup! So girls, try it!

## 1. Stencil Your Brows

You can actually use your eyebrows to really make your eyes appear bigger, did you know that? This way to make your eyes look bigger is easy! All it takes is a stencil for your brows! Just stencil them a little bit and make draw them in and you'll see just how big and beautiful your eyes can look!

## 2. Long Lashes

Another great way to make your eyes look bigger is long, long lashes. You can actually get this from your mascara! For me, Stiletto from Maybelline (I can't rave enough about this mascara!) is the best for lengthening my eyelashes! Give it a try ladies and see just how long your lashes really are!

## 3. Layered Eyeshadow

While shadow eyeshadow is great, layering up your eyeshadow is another great way to make your eyes look bigger! Just do a swipe of one color along your lash line, another color on your lid and another swipe of the same color along your lash line along the crease. This will give your eyes some dimension!

## 4. Blue-Hued Eyeliner

Did you know that blue-hued liner can actually make your eyes appear bigger no matter what color you have? Just get a little bit of navy liner and see just how big your eyes can be! I have green eyes and the blue really makes my eyes pop!

## 5. Double-up on Mascara

Remember how I said that your eyelashes need to be long? Well girls, you can actually double-up on the mascara (as long as you not getting those nasty spider lashes!) to really make your lashes longer! Just a couple coatings of eye mascara can really make a huge difference and is a great way to make your eyes look bigger!

## 6. Curl Your Lashes

Curling your lashes is another secret that a lot of women don't think about. Just take your lash curler and curl up your lashes to really make your eyes pop and appear that much bigger and brighter!

## 7. Follow Your Crease

Another way to make your eyes look bigger is to actually follow along the crease with your eyeshadow. This will increase the drama in your eyes and will truly make them appear to be much bigger than they are!

## 8. Light Colors

Finally ladies, instead of using dark eyeshadow that can make your eyes appear smaller, try something a little lighter! This way to make your eyes look bigger really does work – I use it all of the time! So ditch the dark and go with something light!

There are so many different ways to make your eyes look bigger, you just have to find the way that works for you! So girls, what ways to make your eyes look bigger are you going to start using?
