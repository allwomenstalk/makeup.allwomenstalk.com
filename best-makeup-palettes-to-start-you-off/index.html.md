# 9 Best Makeup Palettes to Start You off ...

**Makeup Palettes** are fun to play with and they come in handy so many times! All the colors you need to create a certain look are all handy right there. I'm an aspiring makeup artist, so I know the importance of **makeup palettes**. Even if you don't wear makeup on a daily basis, I would suggest you invest in a few good **makeup palettes** so that you do have a range of colors that work well together when you do decide to wear makeup. Here's a few great **makeup palettes** to start you out!

## 1. Urban Decay Naked Palette

![Urban Decay Naked Palette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tw/uz/p5wenho054c9308f65e56160818439_248x128.jpg)

**PRice:** $48.00 at [sephora.com](https://goo.gl/THhYYm)
Everybody needs one good makeup palette that has natural, neutral colors in it that work for nearly everyone. This is one of the best makeup palettes to have since it offers a variety of textures including shimmer, matte, and glitter so you won't get bored. You'll love these colors!

## 2. Lancome Color Design 5 Shadow & Liner Palette

![Lancome Color Design 5 Shadow & Liner Palette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gb/kh/kr0lsfh554c930901706c658516126_232x200.jpg)

**Price:** $48.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I love these Lancome palettes because they come with your shadow, highlight and liner along with a good blending and crease color. You can choose from a range of colors, but my favorite is the purples. Great for creating a smoky eye look for a night out!

## 3. Kat Von D True Romance Eyeshadow Palette Adora

![Kat Von D True Romance Eyeshadow Palette Adora](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gn/wu/u66i6enh54c93090b8929670274389_250x128.jpg)

**Price:** $15.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I'm loving Kat Von D makeup palettes right now! These richly pigmented cream and powder shadows are on sale right now. At originally $34, you are getting a great deal! One side has frosty whites and silvers, and the other has sexy black and maroon shades. You can create a myriad of looks with one of these palettes!

## 4. Sephora Collection Pro Lesson Palette: Smoky Eyes

![Sephora Collection Pro Lesson Palette: Smoky Eyes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l7/gk/boggc5fo54c9309176bd3622355088_250x248.jpg)

**Price:** $28.00 at [sephora.com](https://goo.gl/7r1MS1)
We all need a makeup palette to create a good, basic smoky eye and this is just the one! At $28, it's a great steal too. And the instructions are printed directly on the palette, so it's great for beginners! This palette comes with 6 shadows so you have more than enough to create different looks.

## 5. Stila Haute in the Hamptons-Collectible Beach Palette No. 2

![Stila Haute in the Hamptons-Collectible Beach Palette No. 2](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bd/i0/ed2kn1j154c9309244766404441174_250x240.jpg)

**Price:** $14.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This has got to be one of my favorite makeup palettes ever! You will love these breezy, beachy colors as much as I do. They are just enough to bring a sheer wash of color to your eyes without being over the top. Perfect for a day out on the lake or a date or anything that's not too formal. This palette also comes with a cheek color and a bronzer!

## 6. Makeup Forever Professional 5 Camouflage Cream Palette No. 1

![Makeup Forever Professional 5 Camouflage Cream Palette No. 1](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j7/xv/fub2j7ri54c930930c0ce382070842_248x72.jpg)

**Price:** $36.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This has got to be one of the best palettes for concealing. You can cover anything and everything from under-eye circles, and acne to discoloration and redness. You have got to add this palette to your collection right away! I absolutely love it.

## 7. Nars Blush/Bronzer Trio

![Nars Blush/Bronzer Trio](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ln/lc/wiapnx1054c93093ab0a1558769137_250x242.jpg)

**Price:** $55.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I am in love with Nars products and their makeup palettes are no exception! This blush and bronzer palette has colors formulated to work nicely together and includes a highlight as well. You can even use the highlight on the inner corner of your eyes and your temples if you like! Your face will be radiant!

## 8. Sephora Collection Harmony Gloss 3

![Sephora Collection Harmony Gloss 3](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i3/gl/s9l4xbiv54c93094641b7598430926_248x64.jpg)

**Price:** $12.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Of all the gloss palettes I've ever tried, I have to say this one is the best. Sephora glosses are perfect-not too sticky and not too thick. The perfect amount of shine and glitter! This palette comes with a nice brush for mixing the perfect custom shade.

## 9. Sephora Collection Colorful Artist Lipstick Palette

![Sephora Collection Colorful Artist Lipstick Palette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tl/q5/xzeqe4gr54c930950f09a702750952_248x192.jpg)

**Price:** $13.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
For big nights out and special occasions, you need a range of lipsticks to create the ultimate shade and this makeup palette will provide you with just that! Mix and match to create your perfect shade, then top off with gloss if you like! And at $13, it's a fab deal since you get 15 shades! This is one of the most comprehensive makeup palettes I've seen!

As you can see, there are so many great **makeup palettes** to start you off! Now the problem is choosing one! And whether you are new to makeup or have been wearing it forever, you can always enjoy shopping for **makeup palettes** with new and fun colors.
