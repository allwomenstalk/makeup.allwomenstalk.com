# Unbelievably Cheap Makeup Steals from E.L.F. Makeup ...

The E.L.F. (eyes, lips, face) brand has changed the world of makeup. It burst onto the scene in 2004 and has been making headlines and gaining in popularity ever since. They offer unbelievably low prices (some items as low as $1), making their quality products a steal! The most difficult part of writing this article was narrowing the choices down to only 7. These are the sizzling hot must try products!

## 1. E.L.F. Studio High Definition Powder

![E.L.F. Studio High Definition Powder](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iw/n2/s72d5l3955b03fdae9a37912735972_600x533.jpg)

This powder has stellar reviews and has been making headlines for being completely fabulous! It’s even been mentioned in “Redbook” and “National Health” magazines. This powder hides lines and imperfections and gives your face a flawless airbrushed look. It does so much more than keep down shine. You can choose from sheer, shimmer or corrective yellow to suit your skin’s specific needs.

## 2. E.L.F. Studio Baked Highlighter

![E.L.F. Studio Baked Highlighter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g2/rn/cc502zpf55b03ff52c6f3665996923_600x550.jpg)

Why pay $32 for a high end highlighter when you can get this one for $3? E.L.F.’s baked highlighter in Blush Gems is a great dupe for MAC’s Mineralize Skin Finish in Soft & Gentle. You can add the shimmer you want without killing your makeup budget. It can be applied wet or dry for whatever effect you prefer. It’s also made of good-for-your-skin ingredients like vitamin E, Sunflower and Grape.

## 3. E.L.F. Studio Eyebrow Kit

![E.L.F. Studio Eyebrow Kit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/da/pf/bgofylcm55b0400d78413646675501_600x533.jpg)

The last few years have taught us the importance of full eyebrows. They’re the frame for your face and need attention. This little kit has all you need to fill them in and shape them. It’s a duo of a wax and setting powder. It comes in 4 distinct shade combos so there’s one for everyone.

## 4. E.L.F. Essential Eye Crease Brush

![E.L.F. Essential Eye Crease Brush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wf/j9/dcggo2oe55b0404820c26654544911_600x590.jpg)

This amazing beauty tool is only $1. I’m not even kidding! I bought this because my high end crease brush needed replacing. I’ll admit I was a bit skeptical but was soon amazed by the results I could achieve with this brush. It’s wonderful at concentrating color and is touchably soft.

## 5. E.L.F. Studio Lip EXFOLIATOR

![E.L.F. Studio Lip EXFOLIATOR](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p8/bk/eocn5gcj55b040619f3cb430063137_600x573.jpg)

Don’t you hate exfoliating your lips with those messy scrubs? That’s not an issue with this product. It’s in the form of a lipstick so you simply apply it then rinse it off with a wet cloth for lovely, soft lips. In addition to exfoliating your lips, it also moisturizes them with ingredients like Shea butter and jojoba oil. Chapped, dry lips will be a thing of the past!

## 6. E.L.F. Studio Pout Perfecter in Glow

![E.L.F. Studio Pout Perfecter in Glow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/of/it/xdbcqie955b040795490c292429127_600x592.jpg)

May I just say how much I adore this product? It’s a shimmery gloss that creates the illusion of fuller lips. It sells for an unbelievable $3 and is undoubtedly worth so much more than that. It lasts forever, too. As a blonde, I love to wear it over nude and muddy pinks to add a hint of shimmer. My lovely brunette sister wears it over her favorite plummy lipstick and it’s absolutely stunning!

## 7. E.L.F. Studio Makeup Remover Cleansing Cloths

![E.L.F. Studio Makeup Remover Cleansing Cloths](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/if/ep/otfmieji55b0408dbc767481942528_600x483.jpg)

I’ll admit that I prefer a cleanser and water makeup removal process, but there are some nights you’re just too exhausted for that! When that happens to you, this is the perfect way to remove your makeup while lying in bed. These cleansing cloths remove all makeup, including stubborn waterproof mascara. They’re a good choice for sensitive skin and are formulated with aloe to prevent dryness. They’re a must for every girl’s nightstand!

These are 7 of the many amazing products by E.L.F. Which ones are you planning to try? Do you have a favorite E.L.F. product? Please share!
