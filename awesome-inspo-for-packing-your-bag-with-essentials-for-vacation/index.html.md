# Awesome Inspo for Packing Your Bag with Essentials for Vacation ...

When it comes to packing for vacation, I never seem to know what to bring! What I think I need I actually don't, and for what I didn't think I'd need I seem to find myself running to the nearest store. Here are some ideas on essentials for your carry on bag before you go on your next vacation.

## 1. Makeup Essentials

![product, beauty, cosmetics, product, health & beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zz/sf/q9t3uf0n5956ca5717799129313009_550x383.jpg)

![product, product, product design, cosmetics,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gj/v3/a5q4m5pj5956ca2adf8ac057517872_600x400.jpg)

Always pack the makeup essentials: Foundation, mascara, eyeliner, eyeshadow, lipstick. From there, you need to pack essential brushes and other accessories you use on the regular (i.e. eyelash curler, cover up, lip plumper etc.)

## 2. Skincare

![product, product, skin care, product design, cosmetics,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yj/d8/fhfqfvpe5956cac01a1dd454405917_500x386.jpg)

![product, product,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ua/y5/r8y74zec5956cab2d33e5557103968_600x411.jpg)

![product, product,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ru/pa/xpi7spyw5956ca442ef69180893807_600x393.jpg)

Whatever your skincare regime is, make sure you pack accordingly. Toner, moisturizer, scrub, whatever you use on the daily - don't forget them! Unless, of course, you can live without them or find them easily accessible at your destination!

## 3. Carry on Items

![bag, product, handbag, fashion accessory, product,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ez/ac/exny6ffz5956cb5c15551356285557_600x600.jpg)

Taking a plane is always difficult, but sometimes a pure necessity. Be sure to check guidelines since they seem to be ever changing, and follow regulations when packing your carry on bag for maximum in-flight comfort.
