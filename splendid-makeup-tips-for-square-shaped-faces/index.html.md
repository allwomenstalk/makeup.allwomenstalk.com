# 8 Splendid Makeup Tips for Square Shaped Faces ...

Locating the ideal makeup tips for square shaped faces isn't easy! I've scoured the internet ladies and I know that there isn't a place that has all of the makeup tips for square shaped faces all in one place! So girls, you ready to learn all of the makeup tips for square shaped faces that really work? I've got them below! I'm going to explore what works, what doesn't and what you should start doing for your makeup!

## 1. Low Light Your Cheekbones

When you have a square shaped face, you want to find makeup tips for a square shaped face that actually draws attention away from the fact that your face is square. That's what having low light on your cheekbones will do! Just make sure that your cheeks aren't blushed too high and that you don't [draw](https://diy.allwomenstalk.com/drawing-websites-to-visit-when-youre-bored/) too much attention to them and you'll notice a huge difference in how your face looks!

## 2. Blend

One of the tricks to makeup tips for a square shaped face is all about the blending. You want to make sure that your foundation is blended really well, that your powder matches and that your blush blends in and makes your face feel warm. If you don't blend ladies, makeup can actually make your face appear that much more square! You don't want that!

## 3. Light Blush

Remember how I said that you should have low highlighting on your cheeks? Well girls, another makeup tip for a square shaped face is all about blush. You want to make sure that you keep the blush light and airy. You don't want to cake it on too heavy, instead, you want to make sure that there is just a small swipe of blush instead of a lot.

## 4. Bright Lipstick

Another great makeup tip for a square shaped face all revolves around bright lipstick. Highlighting your lips with a bold and brilliant lipstick is a great way to draw attention away from the fact that your face is square. Just a swipe of some brilliant pink or a beautiful red can really highlight your lips perfectly!

## 5. Highlighting on Forehead

Do you know where you should highlight whenever you have a square shaped face? Your forehead! This will actually allow your square shape to fade away a little bit. Just a little highlighting with a powder that is lighter than your actual skin tone can make all of the difference in the world girls!

## 6. Don't Blush Too High

Because your face is square, if you blush too high on your cheekbones, it can actually cause it to seem a bit more square. So girls, my next makeup tip for square shaped faces is all about keeping your blush down, to the apples of your cheeks. Give it a try!

## 7. Plump Your Lips

Remember how I said that allowing your lips to stand out is a great makeup tip for square shaped faces? Well girls, plumping up your lips is another great way to do that! I love any and all lip plumping products, especially lip gloss! What's your favorite lip plumping product?

## 8. Dark Eyeshadow Works Best

Finally ladies, you should definitely draw some attention to your eyes! Dark eyeshadows and smoky eyes are a great way to really pump up the beauty of your shape and draw attention to your eyes. I love blacks, deep purples and even gray colors, all of which make fantastic smoky eyes!

So girls, what makeup tips for square shaped faces do you use everyday? Any of the ones I have listed here? Give them a try and let me know how they work!
