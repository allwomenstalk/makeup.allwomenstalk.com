# 7 Simple Makeup Looks to Effortlessly Spice Up Your Routine

If you're looking for fresh ways to **spice makeup** routines, look no further. Sometimes we can get stuck in a rut, applying the same look day in and day out. It's easy to stick with what you know and feel most comfortable with, but little tips and tricks will really help liven up your look and accentuate your best features. Whether you're aiming for a **burnished bronze skin** glow or just want to experiment with **fun makeup looks**, this list will help you fall out of that boring routine! Check out these **simple makeup looks** to revamp your daily style.

| Makeup Look | Best For | Difficulty |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Glitter Eyes | Night Out | Medium |
| Ombre Lips | Daily Wear | Easy |
| Cat Eyes | Classic Elegance | Hard |

## 1. Glitter Eyes

![Glitter Eyes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yo/x9/ns35yvha_600x900.jpg)

One of the best ways to spice up your makeup is by adding a bit of glitter to your eyes. A dab of glitter right in the center of your eye shadow will help add depth and dimension. If you're a bit wary of trying out this shimmery look, start with a light silvery-white shade and dab it with your finger right on the center of the eyelid. If you'd like to take it a step further, try a glitter eyeliner and line your top lash line. This technique is a great way to transition from a daytime look to something more [dazzling for the evening](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/how-to-apply-glitter-eyeshadow/).

## 2. Metallic Smokey Eye

![Metallic Smokey Eye](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j2/ab/0b0wlokl_600x902.jpg)

For a really gorgeous and smoldering look perfect for summertime, grab a deep bronze metallic shadow and smudge the color just below your lash line as well as on your eyelid. Bronze is a super-flattering shade for every eye color and adds drama without being too dark. It's the perfect way to achieve that sought-after **burnished bronze skin** aesthetic that looks so healthy and radiant.

## 3. Ombre Lips

![Ombre Lips](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zl/xw/n9f2zojq_500x750.jpg)

To achieve this look, choose a deep red matte color and follow the outside of your lips, leaving the centers untouched. Next, grab a lighter red and fill in the center of your lips. Blend the two colors together and voilà—ombre lips. This can also be done with plum-lavender and deep mauve-pink color combinations, among many others. Just have fun with it! Using a high-quality [matte lipstick](https://www.maccosmetics.com) can make all the difference in longevity.

## 4. Cat Eyes

![Cat Eyes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bm/ka/phkgglic_600x857.jpg)

The cat eye look has been around for a while, but it really does make a big difference in **simple makeup looks**. If you're usually an eye pencil user, try swapping it out for a liquid liner—we recommend [Sephora's waterproof eyeliner](https://www.sephora.com) for a smudge-proof finish. Trace your upper lash line, winging it out at the edges. If you have a bit of a shaky hand, start in the middle of your eyelid and move the wand in back-and-forth motions until your whole lash line is filled. Next, take a straight-edge, like a piece of clear scotch tape, create an angle on the outer edge of your eye, and trace it. You can find more tips on mastering the wing in our guide on [perfecting your eyeliner](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/best-liquid-eyeliners-for-cat-eyes/).

## 5. Neon Beauty

![Neon Beauty](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/te/p2/lys521ob_450x675.jpg)

Neon colors on your face may seem a bit scary, so start off small. Choose a neon eyeliner and line your lower or upper lash line (not both!) or choose a neon eye shadow and lightly buff it onto your lower lid with a small, fluffy brush. The latter will give the bright look you want without being too bold. It's one of those **easy fun makeup looks** that instantly makes a statement.

## 6. Stunning, Simple Pout

![Stunning, Simple Pout](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zk/oo/yhoqs9oo_600x800.jpg)

For a **just bitten lip look**, take a berry color or a color that's just a few shades darker than your natural lips, dab your finger on the lip color, and lightly tap it onto just the center of your lips. This will add such a natural and beautiful look, especially if you're not a big fan of bold lips. It’s the ultimate low-effort, high-impact technique for daily wear.

### Essential Tools for Your Makeup Kit

- Precision Liquid Eyeliner

- Metallic Bronze Eyeshadow Palette

- Matte and Satin Lipsticks

- Waterproof White Eyeliner

## 7. White Liner

![White Liner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/er/xz/mbmco98u_600x896.jpg)

You'd be amazed how much lining your water line (the inner, bottom rim of your eye) with white eyeliner will open up your eyes. Choose something waterproof, like an option from [Ulta Beauty](https://www.ulta.com), for maximum long-lasting wear, and trace the waterline a few times until the color truly shows up. Be careful not to go overboard or you could end up looking a bit like Twiggy!

Makeup is fun to play with, and your look can truly be transformed with just a few simple products and styles. I hope you enjoyed this list of **fun makeup ideas** to help revamp your routine. Whether you are trying a **simple 60s makeup** vibe or something more modern, the key is to have confidence. What tricks do you like to do to liven up your makeup?
