# 9 ⃣ Celeb Makeup Artists to Follow on Insta Right Now ...

If you want a better shot at copying your favorite celeb's makeup looks, why not follow their makeup artists on Instagram? They're super-stars in their own right, really, and they deserve a little time in the spotlight! Here are a few celebrity makeup artists to follow in Instagram, stat!

## 1. Pat McGrath

![color, face, cheek, eyebrow, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uf/bz/qogdu23k57ee6132c8b76961289138_600x687.jpg)

Follow her at [@patmcgrathreal](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
Her clients - Katy Perry, Kendall Jenner, and Rihanna, to name a few - are known for their dramatic, ever-changing looks, so I always wonder: what could she do for me?

## 2. Mario Dedivanovic

![eyebrow, face, hair, nose, cheek,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pd/f4/jb1xz3lb57ee61c17eae4107405747_600x600.jpg)

Follow him at [@makeupbymario](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
If you're a fan of the glowing skin and dramatic eyes all the Kardashian/Jenner sisters share, you've got to give credit where credit is due: good genes and Mario.

## 3. Sir John

![hair, person, beauty, hairstyle, singer,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ko/n7/cl4f62j157ee624bed8ef200130213_480x480.jpg)

Follow him at [@sirjohnofficial](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
Give this man a medal! He makes up a few of our celeb icons, like the gorg Chrissy Teigen and Karli Kloss, and then there's his client Beyoncé, too.

## 4. Pati DuBroff

![hair, human hair color, face, blond, eyebrow,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wh/mr/d1wsm8km57ee638f539e1812349242_600x693.jpg)

Follow her at [@patidubroff](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
With a wide range of clients and looks - we're talking Miley Cyrus to Charlize Theron - Pati's done some amazing work.

## 5. Kelsey Deenihan

![hair, face, nose, hairstyle, black hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tk/v6/e4e69bly57ee62965344a304080213_600x749.jpg)

Follow her at [@kdeenihan](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
Two words: Sarah Hyland. Two more: Lucy Hale. Both lovelies are Kelsey's clients, and she's always sharing behind-the-scenes shots.

## 6. Mai Quyhn

![hair, black hair, person, photography, supermodel,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d7/bw/lm1eb05t57ee62cc243da943840572_600x600.jpg)

Follow her at [@storyofmailife](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
Extra points for the killer Instagram handle, and also for her impressive client list, which includes the gorg Jessica Chastain and Emma Stone.

## 7. Gucci Westman

![hair, face, black and white, nose, beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h2/z4/vy8y0d2057ee62fa0464b891625289_600x600.jpg)

Follow her at [@gucciwestman](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
In a word: classic. Her clients - including Reese Witherspoon, Cameron Diaz, and Christy Turlington - are all classic beauties, with smooth skin and smoldering eyes.

## 8. Beau Nelson

![hair, human hair color, face, eyebrow, brown,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qt/yu/irnpv1ku57ee6325880a8754631539_600x600.jpg)

Follow him at [@beau_nelson](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
Beau's responsible for setting Hollywood trends - like Nina Dobrev's red lips - and his feed is always a great place to get a makeup tip or three.

## 9. Charlotte Tilbury

![eyebrow, hair, face, nose, cheek,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e9/ps/gxxyia9s57ee63733c59e418220249_600x748.jpg)

Follow her at [@ctilburymakeup](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
Another artist with a long list of classic beauties as clients (like Sienna Miller, Blake Lively, and Sofia Vergara), I love Charlotte's frequent posts.
