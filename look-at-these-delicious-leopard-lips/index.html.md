# Look at These Delicious Leopard Lips ...

Are you tired of painting your lips plain red or pink or peach? Well, the new Leopard print trend will help you switch things up. According to [Seventeen,](https://www.seventeen.com/beauty/makeup-skincare/news/a37981/leopard-lips-are-the-fierce-beauty-look-you-need-to-see/) it's actually easy to replicate.

All you have to do is put on your regular matte lipstick, let it dry, and apply dots with a liquid liner over it. You can even finish it off with a little bit of glitter.
