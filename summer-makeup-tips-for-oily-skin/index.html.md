# 7 Summer Makeup Tips for Oily Skin ...

**Summer Makeup Tips for Oily Skin** really come in handy, especially when the temperatures crest past the 90s and it seems like you start sweating as soon as you leave the house. Whatever type of skin we have, it causes us difficulties. When you have oily skin, summer can be a real problem, which is also why **summer makeup tips for oily skin** are so essential. The heat makes you sweat, and your makeup seems to slide off your face as soon as you put it on. It’s tempting not to bother at all, but fear not – you don’t have to put away your makeup bag until the weather cools down. It’s a question of using the right products – so here are some **summer [makeup tips](https://allwomenstalk.com/make-up-tips-to-look-older/) for oily skin**.

## 1. Cleansing Routine

As we all know, but probably ignore at times, it’s important to start with a good cleansing routine. Of all the summer makeup tips for oily skin I found for you, this is the most important. It is especially important in summer, when you may be producing more oil than normal. Use a good cleanser and toner, but treat your skin gently so that you don’t encourage it to produce yet more oil.

## 2. Oil-Free Moisturiser

Every skin needs moisturiser, oily skin included. However, if that’s the type of skin you’re blessed with, then make sure you choose a brand that’s specially formulated for it. You don’t need to add yet more oil, so look for an oil-free moisturiser.

## 3. Primer

One of the best summer makeup tips for oily skin is to use primer before foundation and eyeshadow. Just like an artist, you need to prepare your canvas, and that’s where primer comes in. It helps your makeup to last longer – don’t forget to apply a little to your eyelids as well, so that your eye makeup will last longer.

## 4. Powder

The best friend of the woman with oily skin! Although you will need powder throughout the year, you are bound to need it even more in summer. Along with the primer, it will help set your makeup. As with moisturiser, do be sure to pick an oil-free formulation.

## 5. Blotting Paper

Another of the most helpful summer makeup tips for oily skin is to always be prepared. In the heat of summer, there are bound to be times when you need to touch up your powder and deal with the shine that’s starting to appear. If you don’t want to carry a powder compact with you, then keep a little book of blotting papers handy. These are quick and easy to use, and take up no space at all in your bag.

## 6. Spritz

Are you feeling hot and sweaty? That’s not a good look in summer, and for women with oily skin it’s even worse. Spritz your face with a mineral spray to cool yourself down. It won’t ruin your makeup, as the spray is very fine – in fact, it will help to set it.

## 7. Shimmer? No Thanks!

Many summer makeup tips for oily skin deal with what not to do, and this is one of them. If you already have a problem with oily skin, then avoid any products with a shimmery or shiny look. Otherwise you’ll give yourself the very look that you’re trying to avoid! Lip gloss will be fine, as lips don’t tend to be oily, but give other products a miss.

Try these **summer makeup tips for oily skin** and you will look stunning regardless of your skin type! Remember, the advantage of having oily skin is that it is less prone to wrinkles! So it’s not all bad news. Still, when the heat is baking and you feel like you're sweating half to death, it definitely pays to have some great **summer makeup tips for oily skin**. If you have oily skin, are there any products that you’ve found useful? While you're at it, start sharing your own **summer makeup tips for oily skin**! You can never have too many!
