# How to Make the Hottest MOTD Looks All Your Own ...

#MOTD – aka Makeup of the Day – photos are my life. I don't wear a lot of makeup, see, but I love it all the same. I love getting inspo from the women and men who share their fierce looks so that on the occasions I do put on makeup, I have new ideas to experiment with. Plus, well, I love beauty – beautiful people, beautiful clothing, beautiful scenery. Anyway, these are my favorite looks of the moment. They're fierce and flawless and definitely deserving of the #MOTD tag.

## 1. Lush Lips

That is some talented over-lining, I tell you what. I keep thinking that's Kylie Jenner. It's not secretly Kylie, is it?

## 2. The Eyes Have It

Oh, they most certainly do have it. This girl is stunning and she plays up her eyes to perfection.

## 3. A Wing and a Prayer

Winged eyeliner is a lasting trend, but that doesn't mean you can't give it an inventive twist – like winging the eyeliner on your bottom 'lids instead.

## 4. Speaking of Wings ...

Those gorgeous eyes are about to fly away, her wings are so on-point.

## 5. Thinking Pink

The right color of lipstick will make your lips glossy, pouty, and so lush. So, so lush. Luscious. Damn, girl.

## 6. Blending It in

_BRANDI [つじまよ] on Instagram: “ Chopped my hair off and went back to black... Thank you so much @chrisdoesmyhair Eyes: @anastasiabeverlyhills buttery and deep…”_

A lot of #MOTD shots can teach you loads about blending. Look at the eye shadow here. How perfect is that?

## 7. Envy These Lips

If you ever think that you can't pull off a bold, unexpected lip color, think back to this 'gram. You can wear any color if you have the confidence to rock it.

## 8. Monochromatic

This woman is a vision of autumnal hues. Look closely. See how her eye makeup and lipstick both match her hair, her eyebrows, and even her freckles? That's so pretty!

## 9. Those Lips, Though

This is how you wear matte lipstick, man. Those eyelashes are fierce, too.

## 10. Eyebrow Envy

I experience eyebrow envy all the time. This is one of those times. Also, that smile is brighter than a summer's day!

## 11. Making Glasses Work

I remember forcing myself to wear contacts because I didn't think I looked pretty wearing my glasses. Word to the wise: you look gorgeous in your glasses.

## 12. How to Combine Soft and Glam

I love everything about this, but her eyes are particularly arresting. That makeup works, honey.

## 13. All of This

All of it. Every bit. The lips, the eye makeup, the eyebrows, the hair. Girl, let's be friends. I need this prettiness in my life.

## 14. Vintage with a Twist

I see some double winged eyeliner, y'all! I cannot do that with any amount of skill.

## 15. This Pout

Sometimes all you need is some eyeliner and a bold, dramatic lip.

## 16. Going Green

The hints of green at the corners of her eyes are amazing. They draw the right amount of attention, brighten her eyes, complement her skin, and look incredible with her full, pink lips. Love!

## 17. Eyelashes Forever

Those are clearly at least a foot long.
