# 9 on\-Trend Spring Nail Colors ...

**Spring Nail Colors** allow you to get a bit more festive in your color matching. Offset your outfits with sweet pastels or punchy neons. Both types of spring [nail colors](https://nails.allwomenstalk.com/unique-nail-colors-sure-to-get-attention/) are stylishly on-trend for the season. Take a look at just some of the most on-trend spring nail colors below.

## 1. Tangerine - Essie Nail Polish in ‘Orange, It’s Obvious’

![Tangerine - Essie Nail Polish in ‘Orange, It’s Obvious’](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/01/1_tangerine-essie-nail-polish-in-lsquo-orange-it-rsquo-s-obvious-rsquo_163x250.jpg)

**Price:** $8.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you haven’t heard already, tangerine has been named the color of the year by world renowned color authority Pantone. Get your tangerine fix by updating your **spring nail colors**. This nail polish by Essie is a great example.

## 2. Pale Neutral - Nails Inc. Nail Polish in ‘Basil Street’

![Pale Neutral - Nails Inc. Nail Polish in ‘Basil Street’](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/01/2_pale-neutral-nails-inc-nail-polish-in-lsquo-basil-street-rsquo_250x250.jpg)

**Price:** $9.50 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Nude and neutral colors will always be popular in the spring time. A pale neutral color is the perfect accompaniment to this spring’s colorful and sometimes busy color palette.

## 3. Pistachio - Dior Vernis Nail Lacquer in ‘Waterlily 504’

![Pistachio - Dior Vernis Nail Lacquer in ‘Waterlily 504’](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/01/3_pistachio-dior-vernis-nail-lacquer-in-lsquo-waterlily-504-rsquo_250x250.jpg)

**Price:** $23.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Pistachio colours are another example of on-trend **spring nail colors**. It’s a fresh favourite and just screams spring dressing. Match your pistachio nails with a crisp white dress this spring.

## 4. Multi Glitter - Deborah Lippmann Glitter Nail Colour in ‘Happy Birthday’

![Multi Glitter - Deborah Lippmann Glitter Nail Colour in ‘Happy Birthday’](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/01/4_multi-glitter-deborah-lippmann-glitter-nail-colour-in-lsquo-happy-birthday-rsquo_163x250.jpg)

**Price:** $18.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Glitter nail polishes will still be on the top of many hit lists this spring. Add an extra dimension to your nails by choosing a multi-colored glitter polish.

## 5. Butter Yellow - Sally Hansen Nail Polish in ‘Mellow Yellow’

_ **Price:** $2.82 at drugstore.com_
In keeping with the airy, mellow vibe of spring, a light nail polish is another option for your spring nail polish collection. Yellow is notorious for being hard to match to skin tones, so make sure you try it on in store to make sure it suits you.

## 6. Fuchsia - Essie Nail Polish in ‘Tour De Finance’

![Fuchsia - Essie Nail Polish in ‘Tour De Finance’](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/01/6_fuchsia-essie-nail-polish-in-lsquo-tour-de-finance-rsquo_163x250.jpg)

**Price:** $8.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
**Spring nail colors** don’t always have to be about sweet pastels and neutral shades. Fuchsia shades are one way to amp up your look. If you’re into making a bit of a statement, then a fuchsia shade is the way to go.

## 7. Lilac - Essie Nail Polish in ‘to Buy or Not to Buy’

![Lilac - Essie Nail Polish in ‘to Buy or Not to Buy’](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/01/7_lilac-essie-nail-polish-in-lsquo-to-buy-or-not-to-buy-rsquo_163x250.jpg)

**Price:** $8.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Another pastel **spring nail color**, lilac is a feminine favourite. Wear one with a slight shimmer to add a hint of glamour to your look.

## 8. Neon - Love & Beauty Nail Polish in ‘Neon Coral’

_ **Price:** $2.80 at forever21.com_
Neon accents are on-trend for spring. When choosing your **spring nail colors**, keep an eye out for the neon version of your favourite shades. Team your neon nail polish with a fairly neutral outfit to really make them stand out.

## 9. Blue - Love & Beauty Nail Polish in ‘Periwinkle Blue’

_ **Price:** $2.80 at forever21.com_
Variations on blue are another one of the must-have **spring nail colors**. From electric blue to more sea-green shades, a blue nail is your ticket to spring dressing.

When it comes to **spring nail colors**, there’s something to suit everyone. Whether you prefer to keep it simple in neutral colors or stand out from the crown in neon brights, these spring nail colors will have you covered. Which of these spring nail colors do you like best?
