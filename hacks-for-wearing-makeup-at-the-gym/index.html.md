# 6 Simple Hacks for Wearing Makeup at the Gym ...

If you are going for a run or hitting the gym, you will get all sweaty, which makes it impossible to wear makeup without a mess. That's why you need these magical **hacks for wearing makeup at the gym.** No more leaving with makeup all over the place and no more having to go out without any makeup at all. You aren't going to believe how great you look, even after an hour on the treadmill.

## 1. Go for a Colored Lip Balm Instead of Gloss/ Lipstick

![beauty, black hair, supermodel, model, girl,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yu/na/wpvo3e44599941b2e158a102491105_600x600.jpg)

This is one of the best **tips for wearing makeup at the gym** because you may not be aware, but your lips always need to be hydrated. Apart from drinking plenty of water, your lips need some extra care, such as moisturizing. Applying a colored lip balm gives you an amazing glossy shine to your lips, along with protection from dryness. The best part? The gloss stays put at the gym way better than lipstick will.

## 2. Mix Your Sunscreen/ Day Cream with Liquid Foundation

![eyebrow, beauty, chin, black hair, lip,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i9/ta/kpuhf0fm59994588c61b2691785066_600x749.jpg)

Those with dry skin usually apply cream before foundation, but who knew that blending cream with foundation can do wonders? It gives you the perfect shade of color that suits your complexion and provides a glow on your face that doesn’t look anything like makeup, but looks completely natural instead. This duo makes it fast and easy to give your face the glow you love, even if you're at the gym.

## 3. Choose BB Cream over Foundation

![eyewear, glasses, eyebrow, vision care, human hair color,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/in/n8/swlblj8t5999549025e22879473296_600x749.jpg)

Beauty Balm Cream (BB Cream), also known as Complexion Care Cream solves all your problems, providing you with a mix of sunscreen, foundation and moisturizer. It is a trend that has been followed up by most girls at the gym these days. It is easy to apply and doesn’t consume a lot of time.

## 4. Pick a Waterproof Mascara

![eyebrow, beauty, lip, fashion model, chin,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xu/tc/mbm5ntnx599955bcb11c4696086100_600x600.jpg)

Mascara can catch the attention so easily by making your eyes look stunning, and beautiful at the same time. But it doesn't mix well with a majorly sweaty workout. A waterproof mascara ensures that the flawless eye make-up will sit on your face even if you sweat.

## 5. Get Your Eyebrows Threaded

![human hair color, beauty, black hair, brown hair, long hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gh/ij/u82z2o0i5999568cba42a298075247_600x672.jpg)

Instead of using an eyebrow pencil, you may want to get them threaded. It lasts up to 15-20 days, and it keeps your eyebrows on point, even if you hit the gym every single day. Many salons offers this service. It is a simple time saving hack that will make you look splendid even when you're in Zumba.

## 6. Opt for a Very Thin Line of Eyeliner

![human hair color, black hair, muscle, girl, long hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ny/u2/pnonrazk599956c53e7ed032367382_600x540.jpg)

Applying a very minimal amount of eyeliner is a good way to get the look without using so much that it runs all over when you exercise. And it looks great with your favorite workout outfit.
