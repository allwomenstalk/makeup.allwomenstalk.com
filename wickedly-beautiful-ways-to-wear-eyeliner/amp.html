Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best eyeliner styles for everyday wear?
Classic thin lines or soft smudged looks are perfect for a natural, everyday style.
How can I make my eyeliner last all day?
Use a primer before applying eyeliner and opt for waterproof formulas to prevent smudging.
Which eyeliner works best for sensitive eyes?
Look for hypoallergenic, fragrance-free eyeliners designed specifically for sensitive eyes.
How do I choose the right eyeliner color?
Pick shades that complement your eye color, like brown for blue eyes or plum for green eyes.
What’s the easiest way to apply winged eyeliner?
Start with small strokes from the outer corner, gradually extending the wing and connecting it to the lash line.
Can I wear eyeliner if I have hooded eyelids?
Yes! Use thin lines close to the lash line and consider tightlining for a subtle effect.
How do I fix mistakes when applying eyeliner?
Use a cotton swab dipped in makeup remover for quick corrections without starting over.
Is gel eyeliner better than liquid eyeliner?
Gel eyeliners offer more control for smudged or bold looks, while liquid eyeliners are great for sharp, precise lines.
How can I make eyeliner look less harsh?
Smudge the liner slightly or use softer colors like brown or gray for a gentler effect.
What tools are best for applying eyeliner?
A fine-tipped brush or pencil is ideal for precision, while angled brushes work well for gel liners.
