# 10 Tips to Keep Your Lips Kissably Soft ...

Learning how to **keep lips soft is hard**! I bite, chew and pick at my lips all of the time! That is actually what inspired this post because I was dying to find out how to keep lips soft and kissable all of the time! So – without anymore stalling, let's talk a little bit about how to keep lips soft and beautiful looking so your man will want to kiss you all of the time!

## 1. Use Coconut Oil

Did you know that there are tons of different natural ways to keep lips soft? Using a bit of coconut oil on your lips is a great way to keep them hydrated and beautiful! Remember though, you don't have to use a whole lot, just a small amount of coconut oil once a week will do!

## 2. Sugar & Honey Scrub

Now, I've done a ton of research on how to **keep lips soft and every** single bit of research mentions a sugar and honey scrub. Not only does this sound delicious, but ladies, it's so good for your lips! The sugar gets rid of the dead skin and the honey hydrates and coats! It's the best of both worlds!

## 3. Chapstick before Bed

So just before you head on over to bed, one of the tips that I do to keep my lips soft is use chapstick just before I head to bed. Slathering on a bit of chapstick just before getting into bed will not only keep your lips soft while you sleep, but it stays on throughout the night!

## 4. Lemon & Sugar Scrub

That sugar and honey scrub above is great for about once a week and you can alternate with using the lemon and sugar scrub too. This one is great if your lips are not just a little oily but if you have a ton of dead skin. These is also a great lip plumper too!

## 5. Clove Oil for Plump Lips

Speaking of plumped up lips! If you're looking for an all natural, instant lip plumper, just add some clove oil to your lips and instantly, they will seem bigger and loo more lusicous! Trust me ladies, this is one of the ways to **keep lips soft and plump that** works!

## 6. Almond Paste for Glow

If you want lips that glow and that are super bright in color, this is the way to do it! Almond paste will give your lips not just a beautiful sheen, but the color will be brighter, your lips will glow and naturally, they will be softer!

## 7. Avoid Sun

One of the biggest things that you have to remember whenever you are dealing with keeping your lips soft is avoiding the sun. Sun can actually suck everything out of your skin and especially your lips. If you plan on being out in the sun a lot, make sure that you have your chapstick ready!

## 8. Don't Bite

Biting my lips is absolutely one of my worst habits. It's also one of the habits that I've learned to break since it does cause my lips to become super dry and peal a lot! So ladies, if you want soft and kissable lips, don't bite them!

## 9. Water Yourself

Drinking up to 8 glasses of water a day is a great way to ensure that not only your lips but also your skin healthy and hydrated. This is one that I have to work on!

## 10. Use Baby Oil

Finally, the last tip to keep lips soft that we're going to talk about is baby oil. Using just a little bit of baby oil on your lips is a great alternative to chapstick and it works better!

As you can see, there are tons of different tips and tricks that you can **use to keep lips soft**, you just have to find the ones that work for you! For me, I need to stop biting my lips and I do the scrubs once a week. So ladies, do you have anymore tips to share on how to keep lips soft?
