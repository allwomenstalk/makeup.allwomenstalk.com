# 8 Makeup Tips to Make You Look Prettier ...

Did you know that there are actually makeup tips to make you look prettier out there? Makeup is supposed to enhance your beauty, girls, but if you do it wrong, the results can be devastating! So, if you are in the market for some makeup tips to make you look prettier, you've got to take a look at these! Whether it is something small like choosing the right colors or picking out the right foundation texture for you – these are all important things!

## 1. Choose the Right Colors

This is one of the most important makeup tips to make you look prettier, loves! The right colors for your skin tone and for your eye color are all things that you're going to want to take into consideration. Whether it is choosing some violets for your green eyes or even just opting for some beautiful blushes to enhance your hair color – choosing the right colors is so important!

## 2. Don't Plaster Foundation on

Whenever you are putting on your foundation, the most important step that you're going to have to remember is not to plaster on the foundation. There is a difference between concealing and actually caking your foundation on. Remember, just a thin layer can work wonders, girls!

## 3. Balm before Lipstick

This is one makeup tip to make you look prettier that I actually love! Putting on a touch of balm before you swipe on your lipstick will leave your lips feeling absolutely beautiful and will leave them silky soft! It'll also help hold the pigment on your lips, so that way the color will stay longer.

## 4. No Thick Eyeliner

Thick eyeliner can be great in some instances, but if you are heading to the office, you don't need a huge line of thick eyeliner. Just a thin line can do wonders for your eyes and really draw them out! So girls, ditch the thick lines and go with something a bit subtler.

## 5. Brush Lashes before Mascara

Are you a mascara queen like me? I love mascara and I swear, I don't go a day without it! If you are anything like me, then you've got to remember this makeup tip to make you look prettier! Just a quick swipe of an eyebrow brush through your lashes will make them separate and will make them appear longer and thicker!

## 6. Experiment with Different Eyeliner Colors

Always, whenever you are looking for new ways to draw out your beauty, experiment with makeup! Don't be scared to try new colors, girls! Trust me, I never thought that I would like deep greens, but they really draw out the green in my eyes!

## 7. Pump up Your Lips

There are tons of different lip plumpers out there to try and they work! Maybelline is my favorite brand of makeup and I've got to say, their lip pumpers are amazing! After one swipe, my lips feel incredible and look way bigger!

## 8. Powder to Set

Finally girls, the last makeup tip to make you look prettier that we're going to talk about is using powder! Just a little translucent powder can work wonders for your skin and can make it appear completely flawless. It also helps set the rest of your makeup so it lasts all day.

Makeup was never meant to be caked and plastered on, girls, it's supposed to enhance your own beauty. So, what other makeup tips do you use to make yourself look prettier? Any to share?
