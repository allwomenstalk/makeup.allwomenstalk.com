# The Best Eye Shadow Palettes for Fall ...

New season, new makeup! I’m so excited to show you some of the best eye shadow palettes that we can get our hands on this fall. There are so many amazing new products coming out and eye shadow palettes are one of my favorites because they make creating custom looks a breeze! Plus, if you often get ready on-the-go, eye shadow palettes make it so simple to get gorgeous quick and without fussing with a bunch of beauty products. Scroll through the latest and greatest eye shadow palettes this season and get ready to fall in love!

## 1. Olivia Palermo X Ciaté London Smokey Suedes Eye Palette

![Olivia Palermo X Ciaté London Smokey Suedes Eye Palette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ko/z7/dndclv4h5623243c77184329043201_600x300.jpg)

If you’re a fan of fashion and beauty, this palette is your dream come true! Fashion icon Olivia Palermo teamed up with Ciaté London to create the fabric-inspired Smokey Suedes palette. This palette contains all the high-impact shades you’ll need for a subtle daytime look or a night out on the town.
Available at Sephora for $39

## 2. Maybelline up in Smoke Palette

![Maybelline up in Smoke Palette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h7/d0/xmrtlcoz56232437433a3287259905_600x320.jpg)

Maybelline created this limited edition palette that’s so much more than just eye shadow. In addition to the four eye shadows, there’s a liner, blush, highlighter, and lip color so you have all your essentials in one convenient kit. There’s another limited edition palette called Gilded in Gold with lighter neutrals if that’s more your style. Either way you go, it’s a great value!
Available at Ulta for $14.99

## 3. L'orÉAL Color Riche La Palette Nude 1

![L'orÉAL Color Riche La Palette Nude 1](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dm/is/x515gbj05623243927d61862233411_450x215.jpg)

Another fab drugstore eye shadow palette that’s sure to be your go-to this fall is L'Oreal’s La Palette Nude 1. It’s got a gorgeous collection of nude shades for a range of looks from understated chic to all-out bold. The back of the package has a great guide on how to create a trendy nude ombre to a sexy, smoked-out look.
Available at Walmart for $15.84

## 4. Anastasia Beverly Hills Artist Palette

![Anastasia Beverly Hills Artist Palette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dj/x0/gru9ynb85623243bb2f9d638752350_600x220.jpg)

In case you’re looking for something with more color variety, Anastasia Beverly Hills has you covered. The Artist Palette contains 12 rich, pigmented colors ranging from a velvety navy blue to a shimmery orange shadow and a shimmery green. Just because it’s fall doesn’t mean you have to stick with dark colors!

## 5. SEPHORA COLLECTION Jem and the Holograms: Truly Outrageous Eyeshadow Palette

![SEPHORA COLLECTION Jem and the Holograms: Truly Outrageous Eyeshadow Palette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g5/w6/mlvo0xha56232437e8514315588257_300x256.jpg)

For a truly outrageous look this fall, turn to Sephora’s Jem and the Holograms eyeshadow palette. Channel your inner rock star with this collection of 24 glitter, shimmer, and matte eye shadows that was inspired by the original cartoon and the new film. An entire Jem and the Holograms makeup collection is said to be in the works so keep your eye out for it!
Available at Sephora for $39

## 6. Sonia Kashuk Grand Bazaar Color Palette

![Sonia Kashuk Grand Bazaar Color Palette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y9/bz/gi8ofpt65623243aee8b5706799858_600x720.jpg)

Inspired by the markets in Istanbul, Sonia Kashuk’s new Color Palette comes in a sleek burgundy plaid case and contains four universally flattering eye shadows and two blushes. These wearable earthy pink tones will warm up your complexion and make it easy to go from day to night.
Available at Target for $19.99

## 7. E.l.f. Studio Mad for Matte Eyeshadow Palette

![E.l.f. Studio Mad for Matte Eyeshadow Palette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/br/am/ie9zrz7h5623243d4867c103564645_600x270.jpg)

If you’re more of a matte eye shadow fan like myself, you’ll appreciate this collection of 10 matte colors that are perfect for shading, highlighting and defining your eyes. You can even use the darker shades as an eyeliner for a soft, feminine look and you can’t beat the great price!
Available at eyeslipsface.com for $6

## 8. Urban Decay Naked Smoky

![Urban Decay Naked Smoky](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ws/de/ex8m95oj5623243c481f2068674975_600x530.jpg)

We can’t talk about eye shadow palettes without mentioning Urban Decay. They have had some of the best-selling palettes for years and they’re latest, Smoky, is packed with everything you need to design your ideal smoky eye. It even comes with a detailed guide with a how-to on four incredible smoky eye looks.
Avaialble at Sephora for $54

## 9. Bobbi Brown Greige Eye Palette

![Bobbi Brown Greige Eye Palette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cw/ub/jvoq9vzj5623243cea60a295645257_415x238.jpg)

Last but not least, we’ve got this gorgeous Greige Eye Palette from Bobbi Brown. These complementary shades are super wearable, silky smooth, and can be used to create sexy, statement-making eyes that compliments all skin tones. Play with colors and textures without having to worry about creasing or flaking.
Available at Sephora for $70

There are so many great options, it’s hard to choose just one! An eye shadow palette will come in really handy with all the fall events and holidays coming up. Do you buy new makeup when the seasons change or do you stick with your tried-and-true favorites?
