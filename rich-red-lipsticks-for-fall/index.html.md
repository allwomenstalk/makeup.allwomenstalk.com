# 9 Rich Red Lipsticks for Fall ...

This season bright and dark colors ruled the runways when it came to beauty trends, and undoubtedly lips reigned supreme with rich red lipsticks for fall. Traditional reds took a vampy, dark and romantic turn this season, whereas high fashion hues took on an orange crush flavor for ultimate bold beauty looks. Although it can be daunting to choose the correct lip tint for your skin tone, don’t shy away this season from crimson palettes, as a red pout is always a standout. Shop these 9 rich red lipsticks for fall to fire up your beauty formula.

## 1. Nars "Heat Wave" Semi Matte Lipstick

_ **Price:** $30 at narscosmetics.ca_
A fiery intense red is a strong, bold color that is fierce competition for fall’s usual deeply dark shades. A full-bodied colour such as this "Heat Wave" pick from Nars is best paired with subdued muted eyes for the ultimate attention grabbing rouge. Add some warmth to your fall beauty look for a sultry change against the cold.

## 2. Lancôme L’Absolute Rouge Lipstick in "Rouge Velour"

**Price:** $29.50 at [lancome-usa](http://www.lancome-usa.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)[.com](http://www.lancome-usa.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This "Rouge Velour" shade from Lancôme is the perfect shade of ruby red with flecks of subtle glitter throughout. Nothing draws more attention to your luscious lips than hints of shimmer and shine that will have you standout in a crowd. Wear this shade for fall cocktail parties and evening soirees to amp up your regular daytime red.

## 3. L’Oreal Paris Infallible Le Rouge in "Refined Ruby"

_ **Price:** $9.99 at lorealparis.ca_
Now this is a ruby red you can wear for daytime. This shade comes with all the high shine without the shimmer, making it ideal and suitable for day-wear. Add this shade into your daily beauty routine for a glamorous new look for fall.

## 4. Yves Saint Laurent Rouge Volupté Lipstick in "Red Taboo"

![Yves Saint Laurent Rouge Volupté Lipstick in "Red Taboo"](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/09/4_yves-saint-laurent-rouge-volupt-eacute-lipstick-in-red-taboo_600x920.jpg)

**Price:** $34 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
A bold pop of colour with an orangey coral undertone, this bright red shade is a definite standout. In a sea of deepened hues and muted colours, a vivid fiery shade is sometimes needed to create a cheery pop of colour amongst fall jewel tones. The brightness makes this lipstick an ideal candidate for fall or summer beauty trends, giving it versatile status. This "Red Taboo" tint by Yves Saint Laurent is sure to always heat up your look.

## 5. Tarte Glamazon Pure Performane 12-Hour Lipstick in "Wild-Cool Red"

![Tarte Glamazon Pure Performane 12-Hour Lipstick in "Wild-Cool Red"](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/09/5_tarte-glamazon-pure-performane-12-hour-lipstick-in-wild-cool-red_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $34 at [sephora.com](https://goo.gl/9MSUQu)
A cool red tint has more blue-based undertones, which suits a wide variety of women making this "Wild Cool Red" shade ultra wearable. The cool tones give this lipstick a more muted appearance in comparison to other rich reds. If you’re someone who shies away from bold colour and still wants to try and add a red lip into your beauty regime for fall, then this Tarte lipstick is the ideal option for you since it’s not too bold and has just enough colour to spice up your look.

## 6. Estée Lauder Vivid Shine Lipstick in "Fireball"

![Estée Lauder Vivid Shine Lipstick in "Fireball"](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/09/Untitled-11_244x318.jpg)

**Price:** $25 at [esteelauder.com](https://goo.gl/PIR5wU)
Take your fall beauty routine to new heights with a blazing bright lipstick. "Fireball" by Estée Lauder is another orangey coral undertone option with high shine and soft shimmer that is also super wearable during the warmer seasons. The brilliant shine and shimmer adds depth to your precious pout and makes this vibrant shade a style standout.

## 7. Giorgio Armani Rouge D’Armani Lipstick in "Red 401"

![Giorgio Armani Rouge D’Armani Lipstick in "Red 401"](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/09/Untitled-2_284x358.jpg)

**Price:** $30 at [giorgioarmanibeauty-usa.com](https://www.giorgioarmanibeauty-usa.com/makeup/lips/lipstick/rouge-d%E2%80%99armani-matte-lipstick/A2126.html)
A true classic red lipstick is something every girl should have in her makeup arsenal. The simplicity of a timeless lipstick can take you from day to night flawlessly without any effort at all. A classic red tint is one like this Armani pick that will have you staying chic regardless of the season or trend.

## 8. Chanel Rouge Allure in "Pirate"

![Chanel Rouge Allure in "Pirate"](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/09/chanel_350x350.jpg)

**Price:** $34 at [chanel.com](http://www.chanel.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
For fall it is essential to have one deep, rich tone to set the earthy mood this season is all about. The darker tints add a romantic touch to your look especially when paired with a smokey eye. This Chanel lipstick in "Pirate" suits the gothic romance trend of the season, giving you a little depth and mystery to play up.

## 9. Dior Rouge Lipcolor in "Cherry Red"

![Dior Rouge Lipcolor in "Cherry Red"](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/09/9_dior-rouge-lipcolor-in-cherry-red_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $36 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
For you pink lipped ladies that love your rose, a cherry red hue has a rosy undertone allowing you to break out of the box a little while still donning your favourite shade. The rosy mix of red and pink perfects an ultra feminine shade that is subtle and soft yet still sexy. If you’re having trouble finding your perfect rich red shade to match your skin tone, a cherry red option is the ideal pick for you since it combines the best of both worlds into one magnificent colour.

Whether you want to create attention-grabbing style or add a touch of all-out glam, you can’t go wrong with the latest rich red lipsticks for fall. What is your go-to rich red shade this season?
