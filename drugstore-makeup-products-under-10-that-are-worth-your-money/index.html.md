# 6 Drugstore Makeup Products under $10 That Are Worth the Buy ...

I'm here to tell you all about some **drugstore makeup products under $10 that are worth your money**.

Let’s face it. I’m a girl who loves a good highlighter and lip color that will pop. But I’m also a girl on a budget. While I’m a fan of all the things major and expensive beauty lines have to offer, I’ve found a love for cosmetic lines that I can purchase at my local drug store. I’m not a professional MUA, but these have been a win for me every time. Here are the best **drugstore makeup products under $10 that are worth your money**.

## 1. E.L.F. Berry Sorbet Lip Matte Lip Color: $3

![beauty, cosmetics, product, lipstick, product,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lg/k4/zqzltch85a909872032b3254787974_450x450.jpg)

I experimented with E.L.F.’s Berry Sorbet lip color after I realized my makeup wasn’t translating into snapshots on social media. I’m pretty simple so there were times I thought my face would be beat for the heavens, but no one else knew I was actually wearing makeup. I discovered a bold lip was the key that I was missing. I fell in love with Berry Sorbet and under colors from E.L.F. like the Party in the Buff and Wine Tour. Even though they’re only $3, they last for quite a few hours! This is one of my top favorite **drugstore makeup products under $10 that are worth your money.**

## 2. Milani Strobelight Instant Glow Powder: $8

![product, material, powder, powder, product design,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v0/fn/ig7wt8ro5a9098f71ed3b610500262_450x450.jpg)

This is arguably my most favorite makeup purchase so far. A little definitely goes a long way with this product. I purchased it after I realized my simple and formal makeup looks were missing that pop of glow. I purchased it from CVS 3 months ago and still have plenty left! Granted, I don’t wear a full face of makeup consistently. But when I do, this product is always part of the routine and has yet to let me down.

## 3. Milani Stay Put Brow Color: $6.19

![beauty, brush, cosmetics, product, product,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v3/qa/m8fwdrta5a90991414c2a482518511_520x520.jpg)

Clearly, Milani is a favorite of mine. I have had thin eyebrows for as long as I can remember. Once they actually started growing in properly, I used the Milani Stay Put Brow Color to fill them in all the way. Interestingly enough, I’ve been getting lots of compliments on my brows since then. This is just more proof that makeup has the power to make subtle changes.

## 4. COVERGIRL Perfect Point plus Eye Liner: $4.49

![product, product, pen, office supplies, cosmetics,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m3/ut/pgztewfh5a90993c50ce9406483460_520x520.jpg)

I’ve been told that I have small eyelids. So, while I don’t use a ton of eyeliner, this is my go-to. It’s long-lasting, doesn’t smear and is super easy to put on, especially on those days when I’m racing the clock to be on time for work.

## 5. NYX Professional Makeup First Base Primer Spray: $7.99

![product, product, liquid, skin care, spray,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rx/it/ui951pkh5a909987e4d9f798464162_600x600.jpg)

I have not turned back since I started using this primer! It really sets the tone for a good makeup day. I feel like my skin immediately starts glowing as soon as I spray it on. Thankfully, this is another product where you don’t need a ton to make a huge difference. I love it because it’s not one that I have to rub on my face, just a few quick sprays and I’m good!

## 6. COVERGIRL TruBLEND Liquid Foundation

![product, product, perfume, liquid, cosmetics,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ay/sc/pf4um6kh5a9099c07706b170306136_500x500.jpg)

I finally wrapped up my seemingly never-ending search for a good foundation with this one. I have always found it so difficult to find a foundation that not only matches but enhances the traits of my skin. While it’s oily in some areas, powder foundation just hasn’t been a win for me. During one of my many makeup shopping sprees, I picked up this gem and it really does live up to its name. It matches my skin tone perfectly.
