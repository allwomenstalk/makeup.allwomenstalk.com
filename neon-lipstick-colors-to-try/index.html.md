# 9 Neon Lipstick Colors to Try ...

If you're getting bored with your regular old makeup look, why not spice it up with some neon lipstick colors? Neon is a surefire way to create a bold look. If you know how to work it, any color will look amazing on you, no matter how bright or vibrant! Give some of these neon lipstick colors a try!

## 1. Lime Crime's No She Didn't

![Lime Crime's No She Didn't](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/05/1_lime-crimes-no-she-didnt_350x500.jpg)

My absolute favorite of all neon lipstick colors! I just love the color blue, so finding a lipstick as bright as this one makes my day. The key to rocking this color is to keep your outfit minimalistic and the rest of your makeup very natural. A little mascara and eyeliner, and you're set! This lipstick can be purchased from limecrimemakeup.com for $15.99.

## 2. OCC Lip Tar in Nylon

![OCC Lip Tar in Nylon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/05/2_occ-lip-tar-in-nylon_250x250.jpg)

Obsessive Compulsive Cosmetics' lip tars have quickly become my new favorite type of liquid lipstick. They go on so well and last forever. What's not to love about this neon pink? I can imagine so many adorable outfits this can be paired with, both daytime and nighttime. You don't have to be afraid to match other bright colors to this, since pink goes well with lots of things. This lip tar can be found at occmakeup.com as well as online and in-stores at Sephora for $18.00.

## 3. Lime Crime's Countessa Fluorescent

![Lime Crime's Countessa Fluorescent](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/05/3_lime-crimes-countessa-fluorescent_350x500.jpg)

This is a shade of pink with some more purplish undertones that'll definitely turn some heads. I can see this color paired really well with a pair of jeans and whatever shirt you think would match best. It's also a shade you could use to give a pop of color to a little black dress and some black heels. You'll look amazing and everyone will be admiring your great sense for color! It's available from limecrimemakeup.com for $15.99.

## 4. M.a.C.'s Hayley Williams Lipstick

![M.a.C.'s Hayley Williams Lipstick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/05/4_m-a-c-s-hayley-williams-lipstick_600x920.jpg)

M.A.C. doesn't disappoint with its new special edition Hayley Williams lipstick, a shade inspired by the Paramore singer's own hair. I am LOVING this color of orange. This bright color would actually look fantastic on any of you very fair-skinned ladies, as it'll bring out the brightness in your face. It's available from maccosmetics.com for $15.00.

## 5. Lime Crime's New Yolk City

![Lime Crime's New Yolk City](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/05/5_lime-crimes-new-yolk-city_350x500.jpg)

Okay, don't freak out. You're probably wondering, yellow? Really? YES, yellow! Any color can look dazzling if you do it right, and yellow is no exception! I see this shade as more of a summer look, although it's up to you how you'd like to wear it. Get it at limecrimemakeup.com for $15.99.

## 6. Lime Crime's Airborne Unicorn

![Lime Crime's Airborne Unicorn](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/05/6_lime-crimes-airborne-unicorn_350x500.jpg)

Who doesn't love purple? I think purple is a color that just shouts out confidence. If you can wear this lipstick in public and make it look effortless and glamorous, then you have reached the status of fashion goddess. The best part is this shade goes really well with just about any skin color. It's available at limecrimemakeup.com for $15.99.

## 7. OCC's Harlot Lip Tar

![OCC's Harlot Lip Tar](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/05/7_occs-harlot-lip-tar_250x250.jpg)

Red should be a part of every woman's makeup kit, but you probably weren't thinking a red like this! Because OCC's Lip Tars are so thick on application, this color goes on like, well, tar. And since it's so vibrant, your lips will instantly pop! Purchase it from occcosmetics.com or online and in-stores at Sephora for $18.00.

## 8. Lime Crime's Cosmopop

![Lime Crime's Cosmopop](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/05/8_lime-crimes-cosmopop_350x500.jpg)

More orange, but this time a bit lighter! I'm in love with this color--I own it, and wear it all the time. It's a more subdued neon, if you're not into being too flashy. This lipstick is one of my favorites! It's available at limecrimemakeup.com for $15.99.

## 9. Lime Crime's Retrofuturist

![Lime Crime's Retrofuturist](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/05/9_lime-crimes-retrofuturist_350x500.jpg)

I've never seen a red this bright and vibrant, although I'm sure glad I did! Red is a staple, and you can do so much with it, but one this bright will certainly get some attention! Get it from limecrimemakeup.com for $15.99.

Look at all those neon lipsticks! There was quite a bit of Lime Crime on here, because LC happens to specialize in bright and neon colors. But which shade was your favorite? If you liked one, I dare you to take a chance and wear it out!
