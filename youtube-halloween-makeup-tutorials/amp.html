Amazing YouTube Halloween Makeup Tutorials ...

Unicorn • Mermaid • Clown • Day of the Dead • Cat • More ...

By Deeceebee

Are you ready to see some of the best YouTube Halloween makeup tutorials?

Whether you’re inspired by the fabulous females of 2017’s pop culture or the hottest trends of the year, there are some great ideas for Halloween costumes this year. And to make your costume memorable and recognizable, the right makeup is a must. These YouTube Halloween makeup tutorials will have you screaming – not in horror, but for joy.

1. Unicorn

Unless you have been living under a rock this year, you will know that unicorns have been rocking 2017! Follow this stunning tutorial if you want to spend Halloween in a glittery, multi-coloured haze!

2. Mermaid

Mermaids are also super trendy this year, but you need to make sure that you follow a brilliant tutorial like this to avoid looking like the wrong kind of scaly!

3. Clown

Thanks to the massive success of It this year, every Halloween party is going to be filled with creepy clowns! Make sure you stand out from the crowd with this cool and cute, but still creepy tutorial!

4. Day of the Dead

The Day Of The Dead is a Mexican October tradition, and there’s no getting away from the fact that the makeup designs look amazing! Check this one out for some superb inspiration.

5. Cat

The ‘sexy cat’ costume it something that has become almost a parody when it comes to girls attending Halloween parties, but if you really go all out and focus on the makeup as well as the cute ears and outfit, then you won’t be judged for replicating a classic!

6. Sally

Sally from The Nightmare Before Christmas is an iconic Halloween character, and because she is animated, her features are perfect for being replicated by amazing makeup techniques. This one takes a little time, but it’s so, so worth it!

7. Maleficent

Angelina Jolie brought Sleeping Beauty villain Maleficent back in to the spotlight a few years back, and ever since the character has been as mainstay in Halloween and fancy dress parties all over the world. Follow this tutorial for perfect results!

8. Wonder Woman

Leading the charge of the female superheroes, Wonder Woman crushed the box office in 2017. If you intended to follow in the red booted footsteps of the warrior princess, you need to get that makeup exactly right.

9. Belle

Who doesn't want to dress like Emma Watson? As the star of Disney's live-action Beauty and the Beast, you can now channel her character for the night with this makeup tutorial.

10. Daenerys Targaryen

Dress as Daenerys, Princess of House Targaryen, and you'll be ready to claim your throne. Don’t forget to find a killer braid hair tutorial to complete your look.

11. Bellatrix Lestrange

Is there anything more chilling than opening the door to a Death Eater on a dark, dark Halloween night. They don’t come more spine-chilling (or mad!) than Voldemort’s most loyal disciple.

12. Rey

We may have lost one princess from the Star Wars universe (RIP Carrie Fisher) but Rey is more than shaping up to fill in Leia’s shoes.

13. Winifred Sanderson

Aww go on, admit it. You’d much rather watch Hocus Pocus than any of those slasher Halloween horrors. If you want to combine garish makeup with a crushed velvet renaissance dress you need this tutorial.

14. Veronica Lodge, Betty Cooper and Cheryl Blossom

If you have become obsessed by the girls from Riverdale (and let’s face it, everyone is), they make for some easy costumes this year. Match your outfit with the right makeup.

15. Zoya the Destroyer

If you dare venture out in a leotard as Zoya, you also need to tease your hair and wear dark lipstick and makeup. Here’s how to get that Zoya look.
