# 9 Eyeliner Looks All Ladies Need to Try ASAP for a Hot New Look ...

I LOVE eyeliner! In fact, I can't imagine ever excluding eyeliner from my makeup routine and although the winged liner is my all time favourite, I do love trying out different styles every now and then. Check out this video by "naturallybellexo" with 9 different [eyeliner looks](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/these-eyeliner-styles-are-perfect-for-any-and-all-occasions/)! So apply some eye primer, get your gel liner out and let's get started.

## 1. Simplicity

Simply follow the natural shape of your eye.Of course, you can go as thick or as thin as you like.

## 2. Classic

On to the classic winged liner. Start with the first style and when you get to the end, simply wing out your liner as shown. Yes, this one takes some practise but it's so beautiful!

## 3. Double Trouble

Time to get a little more creative! This one is a double winged liner look that is perfect for a night out.

## 4. Basic

Start from the middle, stick as close to your lash line as possible and when you get to the end, drag the line down. Simple!

## 5. Drama

As the name suggests, this one is pretty bold!

## 6. Smooth

A simpler version of the winged liner that is perfect for an eyeliner noob.

## 7. Bold

This one is thick, dramatic, and awesome!

## 8. Fishy Tail

This one is absolutely adorable and I can't wait to try it!

## 9. Feline

In this look, you line both your upper and lower lashes, for a pretty cat eye look.

Don't forget the mascara! Which one was your favorite eyeliner look?
