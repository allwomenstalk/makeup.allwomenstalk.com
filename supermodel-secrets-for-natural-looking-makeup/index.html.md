# Supermodel Secrets for Natural Looking Makeup ...

Want some **supermodel secrets for natural looking makeup**? Makeup is something that every girl (and every fabulous guy!) has to go on their own individual journey with. From playing with your mother’s lipstick as a kid to buying your first mascara as a teen, there is something really fun and experimental about makeup in general, and the great thing about it is that the trends are always changing and the products are always improving! Of course, when it comes to makeup inspiration, supermodels are the obvious pick. If you can’t learn something from the likes of Kate Moss, Cara Delevingne and Tyra Banks, then where can you learn it from!? Here are some **supermodel secrets for natural looking makeup**.

## 1. Perfection Illusion

Your skin doesn’t actually have to be perfect; the trick is to make it look like it is anyway! It is all about achieving a glowing base and an even skin tone, and to make things look as natural as possible. Your two main products should be tinted moisturiser and highlighter. Don’t be afraid to build up coverage over breakout areas and acne scars. Just because you are putting a lot of product on your face, it doesn’t mean that it can’t look natural. Treating your skin with care is one of the best **supermodel secrets for natural looking makeup**.

## 2. Groomed Brows

You can get away with pretty much anything if you have a pair of well-groomed eyebrows! As long as your brows are on fleek and stunningly maintained, you can opt for a really minimal makeup look overall. It’s all about following the natural shape of your brow and producing a nice solid arch. Don’t go too crazy with adding wings and high tips because you will start to look more drag queen than supermodel!

## 3. Mascara

If you know how to use mascara in an effective way, then you can get away with minimal makeup in other areas. A good quality mascara product and application will draw immediate attention to your eyes which in turn will give you the opportunity to tone things down in terms of blush and lipstick. You can’t go wrong with a good black mascara, but you can also try out different colours like blues and browns.

## 4. Contour Shade as Eye Shadow

This is a great supermodel tip! You can add lots of natural looking dimension to your face if you use your contour shade and as eyeshadow. Applying it in the traditional ‘3’ formation can really effectively reduce the size of your forehead and slim down your side profile, and when you have done this, take some of the product on your finger and blend a little bit into your eyelids to create a really quick and natural looking sultry eye!

## 5. Subtle Lip

To achieve a more natural looking lip, the experts always suggest opting for a matte finish, along with a colour choice that accents your natural features rather than a bold red or a deep black. Anything in the pastel range is perfect for a subtle lip, and the matte finish is much more natural than a high gloss.
