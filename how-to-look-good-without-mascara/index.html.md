# How to Look Good without Mascara ...

If you forget your mascara at home, don't worry. You can still make your eyes look gorgeous without the product. Just follow [Harper's Bazaar](https://www.harpersbazaar.com/beauty/makeup/a12753/how-to-look-good-without-mascara/) tips to look gorgeous without mascara.

## 1. â€‹â€‹Keep a Lash Curler in Your Bag

![â€‹â€‹Keep a Lash Curler in Your Bag](https://img.allw.mn/content/al/ot/xcxx3rkz563a6ded4e012927343764_500x310.gif)

You can touch up your eyes throughout the day by curling them a bit. It'll make a much bigger difference than you'd think.

## 2. Opt for Long-wearing Eyeliner or Really Thin Liquid Liner

![Opt for Long-wearing Eyeliner or Really Thin Liquid Liner](https://img.allw.mn/content/e0/yi/rqieljeq563a6deb3c582795329936_500x280.gif)

This will help make the base of your lashes appear thicker. Then it'll look like you're wearing mascara when you're not.

## 3. Use Beige Eyeliner inside the Eye

![Use Beige Eyeliner inside the Eye](https://img.allw.mn/content/g9/x1/l2skn1tb563a6deb88952012706580_500x303.gif)

This will help give your eyes the illusion of being wider. So don't forget to buy a beige eyeliner in addition to your favorite black liner.

## 4. Trace Your Lash Lines with Pencil in a Pale Golden Champagne Color

![Trace Your Lash Lines with Pencil in a Pale Golden Champagne Color](https://img.allw.mn/content/he/rt/mr122m0k563a6debcddcf459269464_470x269.gif)

This is for all of you ladies who already have dark lashes. It'll create some sexy contrast.

## 5. Press Dark, Matte Shadow against the Root of the Lashes

![Press Dark, Matte Shadow against the Root of the Lashes](https://img.allw.mn/content/q4/x6/gd08mhuu563a6debbc08d295080757_500x227.gif)

This will help define your eyes. It's the best substitute for mascara when you don't have it on you.
