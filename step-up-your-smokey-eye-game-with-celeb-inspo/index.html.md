# Step up Your Smokey Eye Game with Celeb Inspo ...

Whether you're out on the town or just going to the mall, a [smokey eye](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/how-to-create-a-reverse-smokey-eye/) can easily amp up your look. Dark makeup is very popular this season because it instantly makes you look edgier. These celebrities have perfected their smokey eye looks and you can use them as inspiration for next time!

## 1. Ashley Olsen

![Ashley Olsen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x6/nj/em8oxihh_485x687.jpg)

Ashley's golden smokey eye looks absolutely perfect on her!

## 2. Olivia Wilde

![Olivia Wilde](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fs/q5/ba3n7vxf_420x620.jpg)

Since Olivia already has such bright eyes, she doesn't need to go overboard with the dark makeup.

## 3. Cara Delevingne

![Cara Delevingne](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nu/uq/cgbhgxmw_403x604.jpg)

Cara doesn't need any help getting attention to her eyes but her dark brown/black look is stunning.

## 4. Emma Roberts

![Emma Roberts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lz/gf/bonmojkd_500x689.jpg)

Emma's dark eye makeup and her choppy haircut are the perfect pairing!

## 5. Keira Knightley

![Keira Knightley](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hm/ze/co4xnt56_600x900.jpg)

Keira proves that you can have fun with different colors, like this blue one!

## 6. Mila Kunis

![Mila Kunis](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j2/s1/a78x4rhn_600x885.jpg)

Mila's no stranger to the smokey eye trend!

## 7. Lily Collins

![Lily Collins](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/st/ui/cqcnkah1_435x580.jpg)

Lily's dark smokey eye demands attention.

## 8. Camilla Luddington

![Camilla Luddington](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ru/bm/yi9ujq3m_396x594.jpg)

Grey's Anatomy's Camilla Luddington doesn't need much eye makeup to make her eyes stand out.

## 9. Blake Lively

![Blake Lively](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u0/a1/obx8b35u_600x900.jpg)

Blake's smoldering Cannes makeup is perfect to recreate this fall!

## 10. Kristen Stewart

![Kristen Stewart](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ir/f3/jyf9eof7_433x594.jpg)

Kristen always keeps her makeup very simple but she always makes sure to make her eyes pop!
