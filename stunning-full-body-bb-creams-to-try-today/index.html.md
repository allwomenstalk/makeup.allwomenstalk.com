# 7 Stunning Full Body BB Creams to Try Today ...

If you're ready to cover up pesky flaws on your legs or arms, try one of these full body BB creams. Yes, the BB creams you love to cover up blemishes on your face are now being designed for your entire body. Many celebrities are already swearing by them. Just like the facial versions, these full body BB creams actually help improve your skin as well as provide cover. Basically, they're foundation for your body.

## 1. Jergens BB Body Cream

![Jergens,beauty,skin,lotion,hair coloring,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wm/pe/vxiw8_jergens_beauty_skin_lotion_hair-coloring_179x450.jpg)

Jergens BB Body Cream is one of my favorite full body BB creams and comes in two shades. The price is affordable and it does an amazing job at covering spider veins, light scars and other flaws. I like that it blends evenly and looks completely natural. Plus, it reduces the appearance of flaws over time, moisturizes and helps firm skin. I often use it instead of my regular body lotion, especially on my legs.

## 2. Perfekt Body Perfection Gel

![Per Fekt,skin,lotion,product,cream,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vt/mb/nwyaa_per-fekt_skin_lotion_product_cream_300x296.jpg)

I love Perfekt Body Perfection Gel since it comes in three shades and is water resistant. The last thing you want is your leg cream running when you step out in the rain. It offers light coverage and gives you an instant sun-kissed look. Over time, it works to help firm and tone the skin while improving the look of flaws. I personally love how quickly it dries while leaving absolutely no streaks behind.

## 3. Urban Decay Naked Skin Body Beauty Balm

![skin,lotion,product,cream,skin care,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jd/x5/uipxc_skin_lotion_product_cream_skin-care_570x523.jpg)

The Naked Skin Body Beauty Balm is designed to instantly blur away flaws while tightening and toning your skin. Plus, you get a gorgeous glow. The only problem is it's only available in one shade. However, the shade adjusts to all skin tones to give everyone stunning skin. I love that the longer your use it, the better your skin starts to look. Its lightweight formula dries quickly and makes your skin seem naturally beautiful.

## 4. Votre Vu Silk Stockings

![lotion,skin,product,skin care,VOTRE,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rz/rr/u9gq4_lotion_skin_product_skin-care_votre_500x370.jpg)

Votre Vu Silk Stockings gives you the look of flawless legs like you'd get with stockings, but without wearing annoying pantyhose. Its extreme hydration formula is full of vitamin E along with natural extracts such as St. John's Wort and chamomile to give your legs a silky smooth texture. It provides light coverage that instantly makes small scars and veins seem to disappear. It's only available in one shade, but applying extra layers will deepen the coverage and color.

## 5. Miracle Skin Transformer Tinted Body Lotion

![skin,lotion,cream,skin care,McN,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zo/e0/fbzd8_skin_lotion_cream_skin-care_mcn_380x553.jpg)

If you really want to transform the look of your skin, look no further than Miracle Skin Transformer Tinted Body Lotion. I love how hydrating the formula is. Plus, it not only tightens skin, but has SPF 20 to protect me when I'm out in the sun. You have three shades to choose from to get the perfect look and coverage. It dries to a smooth, powdery finish almost instantly. Over time, you will notice your skin's texture improving.

## 6. Tarte Maracuja Rainforest Glow Instant Matte Waterproof Body Perfector

![product,cosmetics,lotion,flavor,maracuja,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t2/s3/meipv_product_cosmetics_lotion_flavor_maracuja_600x358.jpg)

For the best coverage possible, Tarte Maracuja Rainforest Body Perfector offers the fullest coverage of any on the list. Don't let the darker color fool you. It blends perfectly into your skin to give you a light bronzed look, while covering up imperfections. It dries almost instantly and is completely streak free. The vitamin C and Maracuja oil rich formula brightens and hydrates skin, leaving skin looking stunning even after you take it off.

## 7. Caudalie Divine Legs Tinted Body Lotion

![lotion,product,skin care,CAUDA,MBES,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xt/ja/hh8fs_lotion_product_skin-care_cauda_mbes_380x558.jpg)

My favorite part about Caudalie Divine Legs Tinted Body Lotion is the scent. Don't get me wrong, it covers beautifully, but it leaves a light grapefruit and vanilla scent that'll instantly make you fall in love with it. It doesn't dry as quickly as the rest, but you will be ready to go within a few minutes. I love the natural glow and super soft skin left behind. Plus, the majority of the ingredients are all natural.

Why let your face have all the benefits of a BB cream? Give your body some love too and look nearly flawless by trying a few of these BB creams made for your body. They all run between $10-$50. What is your favorite BB body cream?
