# The Eye Makeup Style Best Suited to Your Zodiac Sign ...

They say that the eyes are the window to the soul, and if that’s true, you don’t want your personal windows to be without the best looking curtains possible! I’m talking, of course, about eye makeup. I think it’s my personal favourite part of the makeover process because you do so much to transform your entire look when you change your eyes! Here is **the eye makeup style best suited to your zodiac sign**.

## 1. Aries

![Face, Eyebrow, Eyelash, Hair, Eye,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ny/jd/bp6vcznk5c9fbdc66d4c0058691157_600x708.jpg)

Fire, fire, fire, that’s what you need in your style to match your personality! The best thing for an Aries is something like a bold ombre burgundy look. You should be bringing as much drama to your eyes as you can.

## 2. Taurus

![Hair, Face, Eyebrow, Lip, Beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qe/ra/xxo9dzhy5c9fbe3247b17348011437_600x749.jpg)

You possess quite a lot of natural beauty so you don’t need to go overboard with your eye makeup. Pick whichever colour you like. The main thing for you need to remember is that subtle is best for you.

## 3. Gemini

![Hair, Face, Hairstyle, Eyebrow, Lip,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rq/uq/s6xgb0zm5c9fbe9e7af4b857214240_600x600.jpg)

Heavy eyeliner is the best look for a Gemini. You always look best when you look kind of dramatic and seductive, so a heavy liner is a great way to bring immediate atmosphere to your makeup.

## 4. Cancer

![Face, Hair, Eyebrow, Lip, Cheek,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j9/vb/a5aolia85c9fbf5eb1e38649340707_600x750.jpg)

Your eyes are your best feature by far, so you want to highlight them as much as possible. Cute pinks are the colours that you should be aiming for because they will make your eyes pop without taking away from their natural allure.

## 5. Leo

![Eyebrow, Hair, Face, Eyelash, Eye shadow,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ef/sy/p9f7gz5h5c9fc022ad093142382565_600x649.jpg)

You are the absolute life of the party, so your eye makeup needs to be able to grab the attention when you are talking to someone! If you are heading out on the town, don’t be afraid to try a show stealing graphic look.

## 6. Virgo

![Hair, Face, Lip, Eyebrow, Skin,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vq/cu/iao305mo5c9fc364a832d836532567_600x600.jpg)

You aren’t the most adventurous when it comes to makeup, so there is no need to go wild. The thing that you should be paying most attention to is a good quality mascara that really adds volume to your lashes.

## 7. Libra

![Face, Hair, Eyebrow, Lip, Blond,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ld/x6/rvm8ra6i5c9fc5462d314791067551_600x600.jpg)

You are a master of the casual, boho chic look, so for a Libra nothing looks better than a simple, sexy, smoky eye.

## 8. Scorpio

![Face, Hair, Eyebrow, Lip, Beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lv/sd/yennq3ez5c9fc5bde84e7122168446_600x750.jpg)

Winged eyeliner is the thing that looks best for a Scorpio. It adds to that sense of sophistication and elegance that you naturally have.

## 9. Sagittarius

![Hair, Face, Eyebrow, Beauty, Lip,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s3/v9/fdy57zb55c9fc6fbb852c125560044_600x750.jpg)

The song Dancing Queen could probably have been written for you; you aren’t happy unless you are out partying! The best thing to accompany this life of the party personality is glitter, glitter, and more glitter!

## 10. Capricorn

![Face, Eyebrow, Hair, Lip, Eyelash,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l6/d7/vuk4hoex5c9fc7796f020047894804_600x750.jpg)

You are the kind of girl who loves to vamp it up at any opportunity, so nothing suits your style and attitude more than a set of killer cat eyes. When you go bold and sexy, you really can’t go wrong!

## 11. Aquarius

![Face, Hair, Eyebrow, Lip, Skin,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cs/n0/rkketnk95c9fc795e7901472849000_600x749.jpg)

You are up for anything in life, and you like to express that through your makeup. How about trying a cool futuristic metallic eye? Why stick to the old traditions when you could be out there starting trends for yourself?

## 12. Pisces

![Hair, Face, Eyebrow, Eyelash, Lip,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ai/kj/grp4xtlc5c9fc84424d36546408004_600x750.jpg)

You possess an ethereal kind of personality that comes across as very dreamy and mysterious, so something adventurous and pretty like glossy blue lids can give you an almost mermaid like aesthetic!
