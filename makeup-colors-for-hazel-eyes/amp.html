7 Makeup Colors for Hazel Eyes ...

By Heather • 16 Comments

Finding the right makeup colors for hazel eyes isn't easy! Hazel eyes are so unique, so different and so pretty, it's hard to figure out exactly what colors go with these unique and different eyes! Well girls, I've got the top makeup colors for hazel eyes that will bring out the greens and grays that you have, while still keeping that refreshing brown and blue! So ladies, you ready to see what makeup colors for hazel eyes are out there?

Table of contents:

  1. Gold
  2. Mauve Pink
  3. Plum
  4. Olive Green
  5. Violet
  6. Gray
  7. Dark Blue

1 Gold

Did you know that gold actually looks amazing with hazel eyes? This is definitely one of the top makeup colors for hazel eyes because not only does it bring out the flecks of brown, but it'll also bring out the flecks of green! Try just a swipe of gold dust on your lids ladies and you'll see a huge difference!

***

Gold is a great choice for makeup for hazel eyes, as it brings out the flecks of brown and green. For a subtle look, try a light dusting of gold shimmer on the lids. To make the look more dramatic, a gold or bronze eyeshadow can be applied in a smoky eye look. For a more natural look, try a gold eyeliner to make the eyes pop. To complete the look, add some gold highlighter to the inner corners of the eyes and the brow bone. Gold is a versatile color that can be used to create different looks for hazel eyes.

2 Mauve Pink

Pink is another makeup color for hazel eyes that looks amazing! Mauve pink though actually brings out all of the different greens that you have in your eyes! Just a few little swipes and an outline of pink around your eyes and ladies, you'll see just how beautiful hazel eyes can be!

***

Mauve pink is a great color choice for hazel eyes as it helps to bring out the different greens in the eye. Applying a few swipes of mauve pink around the eyes can really enhance the beauty of hazel eyes. This color is also great for creating a subtle yet glamorous look. Mauve pink can be used in a variety of makeup looks, from a natural everyday look to a dramatic evening look. It pairs well with other colors, such as browns and purples, and can also be used to create a smokey eye. Mauve pink is a versatile color that can be used to create a variety of looks for hazel eyes.

3 Plum

I actually have green eyes and plums really brings out the green in my eyes – so of course, plum colors are some of the top makeup colors for hazel eyes because they look great with all colors! Give a try with some plum eyeliner and see what a difference it makes to your hazel eyes!

***

Plum is a versatile color that complements hazel eyes due to its ability to bring out the green tones in them. This makes it a top choice for hazel-eyed individuals looking to enhance their eye color with makeup. Plum can be incorporated into a makeup look through the use of eyeliner, eyeshadow, or even lipstick. Its deep and rich hue adds depth and dimension to hazel eyes, making them stand out. Additionally, plum is a great color for all skin tones, making it a universally flattering choice for those with hazel eyes. So, don't hesitate to try out some plum makeup and see the stunning results it can bring to your hazel eyes.

4 Olive Green

If you are looking for your eyes to be a little more goldish brown, olive green is the perfect makeup color for hazel eyes that will help bring out the natural browns you have in your eyes! Give it a try ladies!

***

Olive green is a versatile makeup color for hazel eyes that can enhance the golden-brown tones in the eyes. The color is a blend of green and brown, making it complementary to the natural hues in hazel eyes. It can also help bring out the green flecks in hazel eyes, creating a unique and eye-catching look. This color is suitable for both daytime and nighttime makeup looks, making it a must-have in any hazel-eyed woman's makeup collection. Additionally, olive green is a great choice for those with hazel eyes who want to add depth and dimension to their eye makeup without being too overpowering.

5 Violet

Violet is a color that looks good with brown eyes, green and even blue eyes – and guess what? With hazel eyes, you have a mixture of all of those colors! Looking to bring out the real beauty of your eyes? Give a try to violet eyeliner or even violet eyeshadow!

***

Violet is a versatile color that complements hazel eyes due to its ability to enhance the different colors present in hazel eyes. The varying shades of green, brown, and blue in hazel eyes can be brought out by using violet makeup. Violet eyeliner can add a pop of color to the eyes, while violet eyeshadow can create a smoky and sultry look. Additionally, violet is a cool-toned color, making it a great choice for hazel eyes with warmer undertones. It can also make the eyes appear brighter and more vibrant. Overall, violet is a great choice for hazel-eyed individuals looking to enhance their natural eye color.

6 Gray

Are you looking to bring out the beautiful blue in your hazel eyes? Gray can do it! Just a little gray eyeshadow or even a little gray eyeliner can really change the entire color of your eyes!

***

Contrary to what you might think, gray isn't a dull or lifeless color when it comes to makeup! It can be a chic and elegant choice especially for those with hazel eyes. The key is to find the right shade—a silver gray can add a subtle sparkle, while a charcoal gray will provide a smoky, mysterious effect. It's all about playing with different hues to see how they highlight the green, gold, or brown flecks in your eyes. And don't be afraid to experiment with matte, shimmer, or metallic textures to really let your eyes stand out and shine!

7 Dark Blue

Finally, the last makeup color for hazel eyes that we're going to talk about is dark blue. Dark blue eyeliner is a great addition to any and all makeup bags because it will bring out those blues and those deep grays in your eyes. Just try it!

There you have it all of you hazel-eyed girls! These makeup color for hazel eyes can work with any of the colors mixed right into your hazel eyes and really make them stand out! So girls, what makeup colors for hazel eyes do you like best?

***

Hazel eyes are a unique eye color that can be difficult to enhance with makeup. The mixture of brown, green, and gold tones can be tricky to work with, but with the right colors, you can really make your hazel eyes pop.

Dark blue is a great choice for hazel eyes, as it can bring out the blues and grays in the eyes. The dark blue hue will help the other colors in the eye stand out. To achieve the best look, try using a dark blue eyeliner and pair it with a neutral eye shadow. A light brown shadow will help to bring out the gold tones in the eye, while a dark brown will bring out the brown tones.

Another great option for hazel eyes is purple. Purple is a great color to use if you want to bring out the green tones in your eyes. A light purple or lavender eye shadow will really bring out the green in your eyes, and a purple eyeliner will add a nice pop of color.
