# 7 Hottest Ways to Rock Dusty Rose Color for Girls Who Dont Know How ...

Dusty rose seems to be cropping up all over the place. Hair color, beauty products, and Adam Levine and Behati Prinsloo’s baby! OK, maybe I’m getting a little carried away. But, we’re here to talk about all the ways you can wear this beautiful and versatile color this season. Dusty rose is one of those colors that you can wear on your lips, lids, cheeks, and then some and it just looks SO damn pretty! Honestly, you really can’t go wrong. So, now that you’re officially ready to start rocking this super flattering color, let’s get into the products!

## 1. MAC Eyeshadow Palette in Dusty Rose X 9

![eye, eye shadow, face, organ, human body,](https://img.allw.mn/content/wz/jc/c1732asa57f98b4d64de8157380810_426x640.gif)

We’re starting off this list of products with one of the prettiest palettes you’ll lay your eyes on. I’m just dying to swatch these babies but it’s from MAC so you know they’re going to be so good! All of these colors are so complementary and you can go subtle for work or go all out and dramatic for date night. You’ve got so many options with these 9 pans!

## 2. Anastasia Beverly Hills Liquid Lipstick in Dusty Rose

![cosmetics, lip gloss, product, lip, eye,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fw/pu/q7hl6e1y57f98b4e0d53a110186689_400x400.jpg)

Get on my lips! ABH’s Dusty Rose lippie is the prettiest mauve color and it’s a nice break from predictable reds and nudes. The pigment in this liquid lipstick is intense and you’ll get great coverage with just one swipe. Another bonus is that it’s a long-wearing formula so your lips will be picture perfect for hours. Perfect for ladies who don’t have the time or patience for constant touch-ups!
Available at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for $20

## 3. Colourpop Eyeshadow in Brady

![Colourpop, pink, eye, brown, eye shadow,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mr/au/pdi2r0w157f98b4f79327405330870_600x507.jpg)

Yaaassss!!! I’m a huge Colourpop fan because of their high quality and highly pigmented products along with their great prices. It’s every makeup lover’s dream come true, really! So, they came out with a whole collection of dusty rose products and one of the standouts is this gorg eye shadow, Brady. This matte shadow is the perfect way to warm up your face when it’s chilly out.
Available at [colourpop.com](https://colourpop.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for $5.

## 4. Rose Quartz Semi Permanent Hair Color

![brown, product, cosmetics, lotion, perfume,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ac/e8/f6vpw80457f98b4fb5b78008690652_500x345.jpg)

I’m sure you’ve seen Kylie Jenner’s latest hair color, a pretty rose gold color, and fell in love. Emma Roberts also dyed her hair a subtle desert rose color earlier this year and it was just as striking. Both blondes and brunettes can rock radiant rose-colored hair. For blondes, try Ion’s Color Brilliance in Rose Quartz. For brunettes, check out this article [popsugar.com](https://www.popsugar.com/beauty/How-Color-Dark-Hair-Pastel-37317301?stream_view=1) which shows a before and after of pastel hair. We should be able to show our love for this stunning color in every way possible!
Available at [sallybeauty.com](https://www.sallybeauty.com/hair-color/shop-by-type/semi-permanent-hair-color/brights-semi-permanent-hair-color/ION153.html) for $5.49

## 5. Deborah Lippmann Painted Desert Nail Polish in Earth Angel

![nail polish, nail care, cosmetics, glass bottle, deborah,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yt/d3/l3ydgz5q57f98b4fea619953537955_450x447.jpg)

Inspired by the colors of the desert, Deborah Lippman’s newest nail polish collection contains a dazzling, shimmery, dusty rose color that’s perfect to brighten up those dreary fall days. This formula dries fast, goes on even, and is long-lasting so even though it’s a pricier formula, you’re getting your money’s worth. The colors in this collection can be worn alone or layered together!
Available at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for $18

## 6. Benefit Cosmetics Cheekathon Blush & Bronzer Palette

![picture frame, organ, eye, flooring, furniture,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dr/q3/yuzymjtj57f98b508ad7f929554728_450x256.jpg)

I was practically drooling over the spectacular colors in this palette! Both Rockateur (top row on right) and Dallas (bottom row on left) are in the dusty rose/rose gold family and they are just to die for! Variety is the spice of life and why shouldn’t it apply to our makeup? These blushes and bronzers are buildable, long-lasting, and super lightweight making them great for every day.
Available at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for $58

## 7. Colourpop Get Paid Liner

![cosmetics, ball pen, brush,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rl/sj/an8x6pge57f98b5083146161143576_600x600.jpg)

I saved the best for last! If black or brown eyeliner is your go-to liner, try using Colourpop’s shimmery rose gold liner for a change. It’s beautiful on all on skin tones and it’s a surefire way to get yourself out of a makeup rut! If wearing it on your upper lashline isn’t your thing, try wearing this on your waterline instead of a white or nude liner to really open up those eyes and make ‘em pop!
Available at [colourpop.com](https://colourpop.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for $5

Ready to rock the dusty rose trend? There are so many pretty ways to wear it and you can even pair some of the products together for a monochromatic look.
