# 7 Makeup Tips for a round Face ...

With all of the [makeup tips](https://allwomenstalk.com/make-up-tips-to-look-older/) for a round face out there, it’s hard to find tips that work for your face right? I’ve got a round face and it took me forever to find the right makeup tips for a round face! Don’t worry though ladies, I’ve got the top 7 makeup tips for a round face that’ll make your face look beautiful!

## 1. Contour

The goal for a round face is to make it appear a little slimmer and not quite so round. With that said, the top makeup tip for a round face that I follow is contouring. Trust me on this ladies, contouring makes all of the difference in the world when you have a round face and can make your face appear longer instead of round!

## 2. Apply Blush under Your Cheekbones

When you have a round face, one of the makeup tips for a round face that I avoid is actually applying blush to the apples of my cheeks. That can make your face appear rounder, instead of slimmer – so instead, why not try applying the blush along the underside of your cheeks? This will elongate your face and make it appear not quite so round.

## 3. Brown Tones

Did you know that with a round face, brown tones are typically the best? These tones highlight the best and even make any eye color pop! For me, I use brown eyeshadow and it really highlights my green eyes and makes them pop!

## 4. Berry Tones

If you are looking for a way to make your lips pop when you have a round face, berry tones are a great way to do it! This makeup tip for a round face has worked so many times for me! Just a little berry lip gloss and I’ve got people constantly complimenting me on how nice I look!

## 5. Bronzer

It all depends on your skin tone of course, but bronzer can be a best friend for you if you have a round face! Contouring with bronzer is a great way to [draw](https://diy.allwomenstalk.com/drawing-websites-to-visit-when-youre-bored/) attention to how long your face is instead of how round it is! This is one makeup tip for a round face that I use all of the time!

## 6. Angled Eyebrows

While your eyebrows aren’t exactly makeup, when you give your face an angled eyebrow, it’s a great way to really highlight your face and draw attention to your eyes. Truthfully, I have a higher arch in my brow and it makes my face look way longer!

## 7. Darker Eyeshadow

Finally, if you really want to draw people into your eyes, one surefire makeup tip for a round face that you should follow is darker eyeshadow! Browns, deeper blues and even some blacks are a great way to draw people in!

So ladies, those are all of the makeup tips for a round face that I use. What other makeup tips for a round face do you have? Any that I haven’t listed here?
