# 8 Things to do to Get Amazing Make up Post Gym !

Going to the gym is important for your health and for keeping your weight in check, but it can really mess up your makeup. Use these handy tips to get cleaned up and look fabulous for the rest of the day with these 8 things to do to get amazing makeup post gym sesh!

## 1. Use an Aloe Mask

![face, head, headgear, mask,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vb/x0/vgo1j7ih596d51db2b340075375524_600x600.jpg)

The day before actually going to the gym, sleep with an aloe mask.

## 2. Don't Forget the Primer

![eyebrow, beauty, human hair color, blond, fashion model,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a8/yp/go454c2h596d521d9a865544150146_600x750.jpg)

Travel with an primer to prep your skin after you get done exercising.

## 3. Mix It with Your Foundation

![eyebrow, beauty, human hair color, black hair, chin,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ty/mn/p4qlqh9y596d532518c38881460741_600x463.jpg)

Quickly mix a small portion of primer and foundation, making a paste that does double duty on your face.

## 4. Blend It in

![beauty, eyebrow, lip, cheek, eyelash,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h6/lf/d2shb1dp596d5350cd55b415166659_600x512.jpg)

Quickly blend the mixture on your skin, using a sponge or your fingers.

## 5. Give Yourself Some Color

![hair, eyebrow, face, human hair color, hairstyle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d0/v9/zx749dn1596d52daa2290983911407_600x600.jpg)

Add a bit of blush to complete that natural look.

## 6. Fill in Your Lips

![eyebrow, human hair color, beauty, black hair, chin,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h3/z0/o55v3pf4596d52bd1aa42465501662_600x657.jpg)

Add a bit of nude lipstick, cause well.... lipstick is life.

## 7. Pay Attention to Your Lashes

![eyebrow, face, skin, cheek, beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/up/vd/cp8wkl1i596d52a66bc3c541499595_600x442.jpg)

Apply a couple of coats mascara to open your eyes and define your look.

## 8. Check Yourself out

![eyebrow, human hair color, beauty, hairstyle, chin,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yq/x2/xsi4xao2596d5473a7850744021634_600x600.jpg)

Look at that fabulous person in the mirror and you're ready to get back to the rest of your day.
