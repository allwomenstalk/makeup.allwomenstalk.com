# 8 Makeup Tips for Latina Women ...

The right makeup tips for Latina women aren't easy to find – believe me, I know! If you're a Latina woman, you know that your skin type is absolutely beautiful and finding the right makeup tips for Latina women is so important! So chicas, you ready to explore my top 8 makeup tips for Latina women that will make you look even more beautiful than you are now? Let's do it!

## 1. Red & Yellow Tones in Foundation

![Red & Yellow Tones in Foundation](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/08/15_594x880.jpg)

So every single foundation has some sort of undertones to it and one makeup tip for Latina women is to find the foundation with the red and yellow tones. This will not only match your skin tone a bit better, but it will reduce any redness that you might have in your skin. Give it a try ladies!

## 2. Concealer to Hide Dark Spots

![Concealer to Hide Dark Spots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/08/26_1259x1600.jpg)

Concealer is of course something that we all use, but girls, if you keep to the red and yellow tones, it'll take away a lot of those dark spots that you might have. For me, I use a yellow based concealer and I just dab it a bit under my eyes to take away the dark circles.

## 3. Bright Red Lips

![Bright Red Lips](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/08/36_682x1023.jpg)

Another makeup tip for Latina women is all about the lips. Ladies, you've got some of the best lips out there and why not highlight them a bit by adding some bright red? Bright red lips with a Latina woman's skin tone looks absolutely beautiful – give it a try girls!

## 4. Green Eyeshadow

![Green Eyeshadow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/08/46_512x424.jpg)

Another great makeup tip for Latina women is all eyes. Green eyeshadow not only compliments your skin tone, but it looks beautiful with any eye color! Give it a try ladies and test out different colors of greens!

## 5. Pink Tones for Fair Complexions

![Pink Tones for Fair Complexions](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/08/56_703x800.jpg)

If you have a fair complexion, it is always better for you to go with some pink tones. Whether it is adding in a bit of pink blush or just opting for pink eyeshadow. Pink will look great with your complexion and will compliment it beautifully!

## 6. Darker Red for Darker Complexions

![Darker Red for Darker Complexions](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/08/65_600x480.jpg)

If you have a darker complexion and are looking for a tone that works for you, darker reds such as brick will look beautiful! Start out with a blush ladies and see where this makeup tip for Latina women leads you!

## 7. Use Bronzer

![Use Bronzer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/08/75_500x750.jpg)

The next makeup tip for Latina women is all about the bronzer. You don't have to use very much bronzer if you have a bit of a darker complexion, but use just a little bit. If you have a fairer complexion then you can amp up the bronzer quite a bit!

## 8. Choose Makeup Based on Your Complexion

![Choose Makeup Based on Your Complexion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/08/84_500x733.jpg)

One makeup tip for Latina women that you'll have to keep in mind is your complexion. You always want to choose your makeup shade and foundation on your complexion, just to make sure that everything matches.

There you have it ladies! My top 8 makeup tips for Latina women that really work! So girls, what other makeup tips for Latina women do you have? Share 'em below!
