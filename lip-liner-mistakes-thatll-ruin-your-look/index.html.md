# Lip Liner Mistakes Thatll Ruin Your Look ...

Lip liner is still in style. You can use it to make your lips look larger, or to simply define them. Of course, you need to apply it the right way. Follow these tips from [Health](https://www.health.com/beauty) and you'll always look fabulous:

## 1. Choosing the Wrong Hue

![Choosing the Wrong Hue](https://img.allw.mn/content/pg/bb/d23ub7vo5661b0fe0a2a1406109297_320x320.gif)

Try to find a lip liner that's the same shade as your lip stick. Unless, of course, you want to create the illusion of bigger lips. In that case, "choose a liner that is one shade darker than your lipstick."

## 2. Lining Lips before Lipstick

[https://media.giphy.com/media/BnbU4iJcirkUo/giphy.gif](https://media.giphy.com/media/BnbU4iJcirkUo/giphy.gif)
You should actually apply your lipstick first, and then put on your lip liner, so it's easier for you to change or define your lip line.

## 3. Working Too Fast

![Working Too Fast](https://img.allw.mn/content/wy/lq/h8spm7dd5661b15ea1ad6629481526_500x281.gif)

Don't outline your entire mouth without picking up your pencil. Use small strokes instead.

## 4. Not Blending Enough

![Not Blending Enough](https://img.allw.mn/content/qn/jo/sv9nrrof5661b0fc1fd21725190956_600x400.gif)

You don't want to see a harsh line, which is why you should make sure to blend the color with a q-tip.

## 5. Skipping Shine

![Skipping Shine](https://img.allw.mn/content/du/fp/hrgmh53p5661b0fbdfba7123120798_500x245.gif)

To make your lips look even better, try applying a highlighter on your cupid's bow.
