# 12 Glamorous Glitter Looks from Instagram ...

I always say, "when in doubt, add glitter." I think glitter in all forms, whether it's in eyeshadow pigments, nail polish, etc., always makes everything better. Check out these glamorous glitter looks from Insta!

## 1. GLITTER EYESHADOW

Amp up those eyes with glitter shadow and make heads turn, like whaaat?!

## 2. GLITTER LIPS

Make those luscious lips shine! Add some glitter to your vamped lips!

## 3. GLITTER HAIRLINE

Glitter hairline is the new trend, though not so new for cheer/dance enthusiasts as this would be on for competitions!

## 4. GLITTER NAILS

Glitter nails are an awesome way to make dull nail polish shine!

## 5. GLITTER LASHES

Bat those eyes, flaunt those lashes, add some glitter and make those eyes sparkle!

## 6. GLITTER EYEBAGS

Do you have dark under eye circles?! Look no more, add some glitter to hide those bad boys! What dark circles?

## 7. GLITTER EYELINER

Add some funky flair to your eyeliner by throwing on some glitter!

## 8. GLITTER EYEBROWS

Brows on fleek? What about glitter brows on fleek!? YAAAS!

## 9. GLITTER HIGHLIGHTER

Highlight on fleek? Now it is! Add some glitter to define and highlight that cheekbone!

## 10. BODY GLITTER

Want to channel your inner vampire? Sparkle in the sun? Now you can just use body glitter!

## 11. GLITTER TEMPLES

Make a statement by adding glitter to your temples!

## 12. GLITTER HANDS

Or make a statement by adding glitter designs to your hands!
