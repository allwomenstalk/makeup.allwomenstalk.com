# 5 Most Common Makeup Mistakes to Avoid if You Want the Perfect Look ...

Every girl needs to know the **most common makeup mistakes to avoid**. You might think you have to pay a lot of money to get quality products to give you a natural look. But more money doesn't always equal better quality. Getting a healthy look is related to many other things, like a good skincare routine, knowing your face shape, what colors flatter you, how much to apply, and how to apply it. These are the **most common makeup mistake to avoid** so you can always look your best.

## 1. APPLYING FOUNDATION to BARE SKIN

Using foundation on bare skin is one of the top **most common makeup mistake to avoid**. A moisturizer and primer is a must before you put on your foundation. Moisturizer is the most important part of putting on makeup because it removes the dryness from your skin, while the primer reduces the appearance of pores and acts as a barrier between your skin and makeup. Also, don't forget to apply foundation to your neck. You don't want to seem like your face is one color and your neck is another color.

## 2. Not BLENDing BLUSH below the APPLE of the CHECK

You must apply blush on the apple of cheeks and blend it upward.

## 3. USing a HARSH LIQUID LINER

Using a harsh liquid liner will give you an awful look. Use one that glides on smoothly, which is usually very creamy but melts and smears easily. Apply eyeliner as close to your lash line as possible.

## 4. USing BLACK BROWS PRODUCTS

Use products that are from 1 to 2 shades darker than your hair color.

## 5. USING DIRTY BRUSHES

Brushes are an important key to your makeup. You would be surprised how effective they are! But don't keep using old dirty brushes. Instead, buy a new one! Every brush has its own function. So don't forget that when using them.
