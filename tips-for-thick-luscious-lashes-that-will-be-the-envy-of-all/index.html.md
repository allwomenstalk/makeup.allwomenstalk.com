# 7 Tips for Thick, Luscious Lashes That Will Be the Envy of All ...

I am always collecting tips for thick luscious lashes and have found some of the best. Beautiful eyelashes can make your whole face appear lovelier and more feminine. They are the envy of every woman. They are very attention grabbing with all the men. Use these tips for thick luscious lashes every day.

## 1. Use Eye Makeup Remover

One of the best tips for thick luscious lashes I can give you is to use eye makeup remover when removing your eye makeup. Your eyelashes are fragile so it is best to remove your eye makeup with eye makeup remover rather than using your facial cleanser. Eye makeup remover is formulated to be gentle on your eyes. Cleanser is designed to remove makeup and impurities from your skin rather than your eyelashes. Using the correct product makes all the difference.

## 2. Treat Your Lashes Gently

Eyelashes are not to be treated roughly but we sometimes forget that. We can treat them gently by using the right product and using it gently rather than with rough scrubbing. Another way to treat your lashes gently is to apply your mascara with a gentle hand rather than dragging the mascara bristles through them roughly. If you curl your eyelashes, only do so the minimum number of times. Overusing an eyelash curler can cause breakage.

## 3. Never Sleep with Makeup on

You should never sleep with makeup on and you should especially never sleep with your mascara on. Mascara makes your lashes hard and stiff, which makes them more susceptible to breakage. If you want to have full, luscious lashes then you can’t afford to lose any and especially not because of bad habits that cause breakage. If you are absolutely too exhausted to stumble to the bathroom then do what I do. Keep a package of facial cleansing wipes on your nightstand for those nights you simply cannot wash your makeup off with soap and water.

## 4. Use an Eyelash Primer

Eyelash primer can take your eyelashes from bland to grand. Eyelash primer is a product that you apply to your eyelashes with a wand, like mascara. It coats each lash to make it ‘fatter.’ Then when you apply your mascara, there is more lash for the mascara to grab onto. It is a total game changer and one you cannot afford to pass up.

## 5. Find Your Perfect Mascara

Aren’t we all on the quest for the perfect mascara? I know I have been, all of my life! The key to having a more successful search is to know what you are looking for in mascara. Do you want a lengthening formula, a volumizing formula or one that defines and separates? It is also helpful to read reviews online to give you some inside info on what other women love.

## 6. Stay Away from Waterproof Mascara

Waterproof mascara is a great product for those times that you need it, such as weddings and funerals. And you should really limit wearing it to those times because it is not good for your eyelashes. It is very difficult to remove, which means you have to work harder to remove it. This can cause damage to your eyelashes. If you must wear it be sure to remove it by placing pads soaked with eye makeup remover on your lashes for a few moments before you start applying pressure.

## 7. Add Falsies for Special Occasions

Falsies are a gorgeous way to kick it up a notch. While tedious to deal with everyday, they are certainly something that you can pull out for special occasions. They come in a wide variety so that you can choose how much glam factor that you want to add. It is best to only wear them on occasion because they can be hard on your eyelashes as well. The goal is to get to the point you have such beautiful eyelashes that you don’t need falsies anymore. Following these other steps listed here can help you to do that.

There are a lot of little tricks you can use to get the full, luscious lashes you desire. Now I want to know what tricks you use? I am still on the lookout to improve my lashes, too!
