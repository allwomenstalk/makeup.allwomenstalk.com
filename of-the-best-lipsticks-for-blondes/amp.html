7 of the Best Lipsticks for Blondes ...

Revlon’s Pink in the Afternoon • Revlon’s Sassy Mauve • Rimmel London’s Airy Fairy • Revlon’s Finale • Mary Kay’s Sweet Nectar • More ...

By Alicia

Have you been looking for the best lipsticks for blondes? I can certainly help you here. I am a blonde and a lover of lipsticks. Lipstick is the final step in making you look put together so it is very essential. Let me share some of the best lipsticks for blondes with you.

1. Revlon’s Pink in the Afternoon

Ladies, this color is where it is at. I don’t know if it's the combination of my fair coloring and blue eyes but when I wear this color, I almost always get compliments on my lipstick. It is a warm pink and almost reminds you of the lipstick that Audrey Hepburn wore in “Breakfast At Tiffany’s.” I pair it with a neutral lip liner for all day wear. It is one of the best lipsticks for blondes.

2. Revlon’s Sassy Mauve

I bought Revlon’s Sassy Mauve because I had read that it was a dupe for Mac’s Syrup and I thought that for a third of the price, it was worth a try. In my opinion, it is not a dupe. It is a very pretty lipstick and one I have ended up liking very much but it is very different than the color I love of Mac’s. This lipstick is a plummy pink. I usually pair it with a light pink liner to bring out the pink in it.

3. Rimmel London’s Airy Fairy

This one is not only a great lipstick for blondes but a fairly universal lip color. It is a nude and falls closely in what I think of as the muddy pink category. It is a muted pink with the tiniest hints of brown. I usually wear this when I am doing a smoky eye and want my eyes to take center stage. It reminds me of some of the lipstick colors I have seen Adele wear.

4. Revlon’s Finale

Revlon’s Finale is a red. I am not usually a red lipstick kind of girl but I do like to have one around. I tend to reach for this one when I am wearing red, black or grey. It is great for date night and special occasions that call for a little black dress. Be warned, though, as part of Revlon’s Colorstay Ultimate Suede line, it is not a moisturizing formula.

5. Mary Kay’s Sweet Nectar

Mary Kay really has some wonderful lipsticks. Their formulas are moist and creamy that always leave my lips feeling so smooth. Sweet Nectar is a pale coral pink that is more on the coral side. The good thing about this lipstick is that it really goes with anything and any makeup look. I have been wearing this one for years.

6. Maybelline Baby Lips in Strike a Rose

Ladies, for around three bucks a pop, you cannot go wrong with the Maybelline Baby Lips products. They are moisturizing and provide some color. I don’t love all of the colors but I do like this one. It lasts several hours. The tube also has a lot of product in it for the price.

7. Mac’s Syrup

I do not have this lipstick yet but I am lusting after it! It is so pretty. If you haven’t seen it, you must look for it. It is a perfect neutral pink. It will be my next lipstick purchase, for sure.

Now it’s your turn. I want to know what lipsticks make your favorites list. You have to share this with me!

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best lipstick colors for blondes?
Blondes often look great in lipstick shades like soft pinks, peachy nudes, coral hues, rosy pinks, and classic reds. The best color can depend on your skin tone and the occasion.
Does hair color affect how lipstick looks?
Yes, hair color can influence how lipstick shades look on you. Since blondes have lighter hair, some lipstick colors can make a stronger impact or provide a soft complement to their overall appearance.
How can blondes choose the right lipstick for their skin tone?
Blondes with cool undertones in their skin can opt for lipsticks with blue or pink bases, while those with warm undertones may look better in lipsticks with peach or golden bases. It's important to consider your skin's undertones when selecting a lipstick color.
Can blondes wear bold lipstick colors?
Absolutely! Blondes can wear bold lipstick colors like bright reds, deep plums, or vivid pinks. It's all about balancing the makeup and choosing a color that complements your complexion and confidence.
Is there a difference between day and night lipstick for blondes?
Daytime lipstick shades for blondes might be lighter and more natural, like pale pinks and nudes, while nighttime may allow for more dramatic or darker colors, such as bold reds and berries, to create an evening look.
