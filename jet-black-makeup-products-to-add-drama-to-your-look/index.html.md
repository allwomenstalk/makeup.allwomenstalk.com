# 7 Jet Black Makeup Products to Add Drama to Your Look ...

Black makeup products can add some serious drama to your look when applied well. Black makeup products are perfect accents to show off your eyes and sometimes even your lips! Create some depth and drama in your makeup look with these jet-black makeup products.

## 1. Maybelline Great Lash Mascara in Blackest Black

![Maybelline Great Lash Mascara in Blackest Black](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/07/1_maybelline-great-lash-mascara-in-blackest-black_300x300.jpg)

Of all the black makeup products in existence, Maybelline’s Great Lash Mascara is probably the most well known. For a large majority of us, our first mascara was Great Lash! The Blackest Black version of this fabulous mascara will emphasize your lashes to the extreme. Having jet-black eyelashes will make your eyeshadow and your eye color really pop.

## 2. Lime Crime Lipstick in Styletto

![Lime Crime Lipstick in Styletto](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/07/2_lime-crime-lipstick-in-styletto_350x500.jpg)

Black lipstick isn’t a product most of us wear very often, but if you’re going to rock it you better do it right! Lime Crime Lipstick in Styletto provides 100% coverage with a glossy sheen to give you the blackest lip possible. Black lips can be refreshing and are a great way to mix up your makeup look. Consider wearing black lips in summer to really shake things up!

## 3. Laura Mercier Tightline Cake Eye Liner in Black Ebony

![Laura Mercier Tightline Cake Eye Liner in Black Ebony](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/07/3_laura-mercier-tightline-cake-eye-liner-in-black-ebony_286x298.jpg)

Since cake eyeliner is made to be applied using a damp makeup brush, the color of this product goes on very dark and smooth. This eyeliner from Laura Mercier was created to tightline your lashes, which means you should use a flat eyeliner brush to wiggle the product underneath the lashes at the root of the upper lash line. Tightlining ensures that there is no gap between your eyeliner and your lashes, making your eyes really stand out.

## 4. Sugarpill Bulletproof Pressed Eyeshadow

![Sugarpill Bulletproof Pressed Eyeshadow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/07/4_sugarpill-bulletproof-pressed-eyeshadow_600x600.jpg)

Sugarpill is known for developing products with an amazing amount of pigmentation, which is why their matte black eyeshadow is so great! This is one of the deepest black eyeshadows and it stays on extremely well. Matte black shadows are great for creating a smoky eye, and they can even be used as an alternative to eyeliner.

## 5. The Lipstick Queen Black Tie Optional Gloss

![The Lipstick Queen Black Tie Optional Gloss](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/07/5_the-lipstick-queen-black-tie-optional-gloss_300x398.jpg)

The Lipstick Queen is always introducing innovative lip products! Black Tie Optional is a high-shine gloss in deepest black. This sheer black gloss is sexy and sultry on its own or layered on top of your favorite [lipstick shades](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/gorgeous-lipstick-shades-for-pale-skin/) for a smoky finish.

## 6. Make up for Ever Aqua Cream in Black

![Make up for Ever Aqua Cream in Black](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/07/6_make-up-for-ever-aqua-cream-in-black_600x404.jpg)

Aqua Creams from Make Up For Ever are very versatile, particularly the black one. It can be used as eyeliner by applying it with a small, angled brush or it can be smudged on the eyelid to create a smoky eye. It will not crease or wear off throughout the day. This product is perfect for creating a jet-black cat eye.

## 7. Violent Eyes Jet Black Glitteratti

![Violent Eyes Jet Black Glitteratti](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/07/7_violent-eyes-jet-black-glitteratti_550x550.jpg)

You’ve probably heard of the Violent Lip Tattoos, but did you know the company also makes eye makeup products? Violent Eyes Jet Black Glitteratti gives you the perfect glitter smoky eye without the work. These temporary eye appliques are smudge proof and last up to 16 hours.

Jet-black makeup products add emphasis to your makeup look to totally accent your features. Dark blacks will make the other colors of your face really stand out while providing emphasis to your facial features. Do you wear jet black makeup products on a regular basis?
