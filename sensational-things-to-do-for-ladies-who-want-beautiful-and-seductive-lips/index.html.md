# Sensational Things to do for Ladies Who Want Beautiful and Seductive Lips ...

It doesn't matter if you're a complete pro at makeup or just getting into the game; how you color and line your lips can reveal a lot about your personality. I'm not ashamed to admit that I've walked into the drugstore in search of neutral lipstick on multiple occasions, only to walk out with *another* shade of the perfect red-pink-purple. I may not be on expert level when it comes to my eyebrows, but I know exactly how to accentuate my lips for a fuller and sexier look. If you've been looking for ways to make your lips pop, any one of these fifteen beauty hacks will do the trick.

## 1. Warning: before You Start

![eyebrow, face, hair, nose, lip,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sz/e9/rpdmplis5835e63e9e648763189693_600x600.jpg)

Make sure your lips are moisturized. Not only will it help your lipstick glide on smoothly, it'll make it last longer.

## 2. For Fuller Lips

![eyebrow, face, hair, lip, cheek,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wq/zg/tdxp72v25835e6575bd7b058717552_600x600.jpg)

All you'll need is two lipsticks in a light and dark shade. Apply the darker lipstick first before blending the lighter color in the middle of your lips.

## 3. No Lipstick? No Problem!

![eyebrow, face, color, cheek, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kq/xh/m2del3lr5835e668c23f7234016507_600x600.jpg)

If you're like me, forgetting to pack your power lipstick (or losing it at the bottom of your bag) is a daily routine . That's why I always make sure to carry THIS backup in case of emergencies: An eyeshadow palette and a travel size jar of vaseline. Mix up a tiny amount and voila!

## 4. Lipstick Too Bright? Mix It in with a Drop (or Two) of a Darker Shade

![face, eyebrow, hair, nose, black hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kg/bd/iqxwv8ho5835e699c0dab055303202_600x600.jpg)

Before I tried Colourpop's Creeper matte lipstick, I did a lot of research on how it would look on my skin color. You can imagine how disappointed I was when the bright orange-red hue looked more clownish on me than it did on the makeup vloggers on YouTube. But I was determined to make it work; I dabbed a little of their neutral brown shade "Limbo" over the red lipstick and haven't turned back since!

## 5. Matte Lovers, Rejoice! ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ Here's an Easy Way to Get the Look

![hair, eyebrow, face, blond, black hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dt/jm/zekkq7975835e6af47604578606153_600x600.jpg)

Want to try matte lipstick without the commitment? Dab a small amount of translucent powder over your lipstick before wiping off the rest.

## 6. Speaking of Translucent Powder, It Works for Smudges Too!

![face, eyebrow, hair, person, cheek,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wa/la/zqwda30a5835e6c11d8cd174271274_480x480.jpg)

For lipstick that won't smudge or kiss off, using translucent powder is the way to go. Just make sure to brush off any extra powder.

## 7. Put Your Old Lip Liners to Good Use

![color, face, hair, eyebrow, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ku/hs/y4tz8i905835e6d0270ec673867027_600x600.jpg)

Instead of getting rid of your lip liners, try coming up with a new lipstick shade.

## 8. For a More Precise Outline of Your Lips

![eyebrow, face, black hair, woman, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ld/tj/lruf2exl5835e6de7ea57891039842_600x551.jpg)

Before you line your lips, make sure you rub the tip of the pencil against a cotton pad.

## 9. How to Achieve Fuller Lips Using a More Natural Look

![color, face, hair, blue, eyebrow,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/aw/o4/o2k0emah5835e6f63ad73520237113_480x480.jpg)

You can ditch the lipstick for this one. Instead, apply lipgloss in the middle of your lips for a fuller look.

## 10. Buy a Lipstick + Lip Liner Duo Set for the Perfect Match

![face, eyebrow, black hair, black, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vq/af/v0ojfd9u5835e7203563f184488033_600x600.jpg)

Because you don't deserve to struggle.

## 11. For a Natural Sheen, Use Your Highlighter

![color, hair, face, eyebrow, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e5/k4/gpnb104b5835e706c8a24468891478_600x600.jpg)

This will really make your lips pop with little effort on your part.

## 12. Try the Sexy Ombre Trend

![hair, face, clothing, nose, beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cn/wx/u7nf9qcp5835e7533c685523605647_600x599.jpg)

If you want to go for a bolder look, you can start by creating the ombre effect using dark and light shades in a similar color family.

## 13. The Direction You Apply Your Lipstick in Makes a Huge Difference

![hair, human hair color, black hair, face, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p4/qo/rovm5xqa5835e76e8b7e2513561352_600x600.jpg)

You should always start from the middle and work your way out. This will make your lips look plumper.

## 14. Slightly over-line the Top of Your Lips

![eyebrow, color, face, hair, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cp/sl/xnmrddvi5835e780a3929093129868_480x480.jpg)

Once you gently fill it in, you'll see major results!

## 15. Don't Forget to Brush!

![face, hair, clothing, image, person,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dp/na/eaxmipsg5835e795a0599352062881_600x600.jpg)

When you're applying lipstick (especially if it's matte), it's better to work on a smooth surface. Always brush your lips in the morning.
