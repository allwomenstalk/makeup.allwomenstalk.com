Frequently Asked Questions

What are some easy eye shadow looks for a night out?
Try a smoky eye with neutral shades or a bold shimmer to add some glam quickly.
How can I make my eye shadow last all night?
Use an eye shadow primer before applying your colors to ensure they stay vibrant and crease-free.
Which colors work best for evening eye shadows?
Deep plums, rich browns, metallic golds, and classic black create stunning night-time looks.
Can I use bright colors for a night out look?
Absolutely! Pair bright shades like cobalt blue or emerald green with neutral tones to balance the look.
How do I blend eye shadow for a flawless finish?
Use a clean, fluffy brush in gentle circular motions to seamlessly blend your colors together.
Should I use glitter for a party eye look?
Yes, a touch of glitter on the eyelids or inner corner can add sparkle without looking overdone.
How can I highlight my eyes for a nighttime glow?
Apply a light, shimmery shade on your brow bone and inner corners to brighten your eyes.
What’s a quick eye shadow look if I’m short on time?
A simple wash of a single shimmery shade across the lids paired with eyeliner can be done in minutes.
How do I avoid fall-out when applying dark shadows?
Tap off excess product from your brush before application and use a light hand to minimize fall-out.
Can I use the same eye shadow look for day and night?
Yes, start with a neutral base for day and add deeper shades or shimmer for nighttime sparkle.
