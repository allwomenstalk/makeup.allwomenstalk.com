8 Quirky Cosmetics Cases ...

Anya Hindmarch Lips and Eyes Canvas Cosmetics Case • Urban Decay the Quinceañera Bag • Forever21 Rose Printed Cosmetic Bag • Juicy Couture Eyelash Barrel Cosmetic Bag • Burberry Printed Canvas Cosmetics Case • More ...

By Sophia

Cosmetics cases are great for keeping all your essential makeup needs in the one spot. Simply transport cases between your handbag, gym bag, or travel bag. Cases come in a variety of materials, sizes, and designs. Instead of a plain black case, why not opt for one of the quirky cosmetics cases below?

1. Anya Hindmarch Lips and Eyes Canvas Cosmetics Case

Price: $90.00 at net-a-porter.com
This canvas cosmetics case features a makeup inspired illustration on the front. It has a zip top fastening, tassel zip pull, and designer stamped canvas lining. With ‘lips and eyes’ scrawled on the front and illustrations of lipstick and mascara it’s safe to say that this case in intended to house its namesake items.

2. Urban Decay the Quinceañera Bag

Price: $26.00 at sephora.com
This purple glitter cosmetics case would make a fun addition to any makeup drawer. It has a double zipper that opens on three sides and also has an interior zip pocket. The lining features Urban Decay’s limited-edition anniversary logo. It is PVC coated and can be cleaned with a damp cloth.

3. Forever21 Rose Printed Cosmetic Bag

Price: $6.80 at forever21.com
Quirky cosmetics cases come in a range of designs. This one looks like a vintage clasp purse. It features an all-over floral print, coloured lining, and a kiss clasp. This printed cosmetics case is also available in three different colours.

4. Juicy Couture Eyelash Barrel Cosmetic Bag

Price: $58.16 at asos.com
This barrel cosmetics case features a quirky all-over eyelash print. Each pair of eyelashes also seems to have a different silhouette. This case has a zip top fastening, silver hardware, and logo branded lining. It’s the perfect size for storing your makeup essentials.

5. Burberry Printed Canvas Cosmetics Case

Price: $230.00 at net-a-porter.com
This cosmetics case takes the iconic Burberry check pattern and transforms it into vivid brush strokes. The black, burgundy, and pink print is modern and vibrant. This case has a zip top fastening, gunmetal hardware, and patent leather trim. It also has interior pouches and zip pockets for neater storage.

6. Fossil ‘Key-per’ Wedge Cosmetic Bag

Price: $25.00 at shop.nordstrom.com
Quirky cosmetics cases often feature interesting patterns or prints. This case combines two colourful prints in the one wedge-shaped case. This bag is made from coated cotton and has a zip top fastening with charm-detailed zip pull. It’s available in three different retro-inspired prints.

7. Forever21 Vibrant Leopard Print Cosmetic Bag

Price: $6.80 at forever21.com
Leopard print will always stick around. This brightly coloured cosmetic bag features highlighted neon spots. This bag has a zip top fastening with tassel-detail zip pull. It comes in a medium size so could house a fair few makeup items.

8. Juicy Couture 2011 Barrel Cosmetic Pouch

Price: $83.09 at asos.com
When looking for quirky cosmetics cases, keep an eye out for unexpected materials. This barrel-shaped cosmetics case has a velour outer. It also has a zip top closure with logo bead tassels. It also has a handle at one end which is convenient for carrying.

No matter if you wear a lot of makeup or very little, cosmetics cases are a handy thing to have. Keeping all of your essential items in the one spot makes it easy to transfer them between bags. Which of these quirky cosmetics cases do you like best?
