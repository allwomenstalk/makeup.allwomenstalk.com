# 9 Awesome Makeup Tips for Your 30s ...

Following all of the right makeup tips for your 30's isn't easy! When you are in your 30's, your skin starts to change and you could start to see the early signs of wrinkles! If you're looking to beat that ladies, I've got the top 9 makeup tips for your 30's that you aren't going to want to miss! So girls – let's dive right into the top makeup tips for your 30's!

## 1. Full Coverage Foundation

When you are in your 30's, all sorts of things start to change, especially when it comes to your skin. If you are seeing all sorts of different spots on your face and dark circles, you want to make sure that you get some full coverage foundation! Trust me ladies, this makeup tip for your 30's works wonders on making sure that you stay looking younger!

## 2. Use Bronzer

The next makeup tip for your 30's that we're going to talk about is using bronzer! Now of course, you don't ever want to over-bronze, but a light dusting can give you a glowing complexion and make you look absolutely beautiful – no matter what age you are!

## 3. Contour

When you are in your 30's, you always want to make sure that you are contouring. The reasoning behind this is the fact that it can make your face appear thinner, slimmer and even younger! So girls, contour your blush and your bronzer and you'll see a huge difference in how you look!

## 4. Choose a Concealer That Matches Your Foundation

A lot of women make the huge mistake of getting their concealer actually lighter than their foundation. Ladies, this just isn't true! One makeup tip for your 30's all revolves around getting a concealer that matches your foundation perfectly!

## 5. Highlight Your Brow Bones

When you highlight your brow bone, it actually brightens up your entire eye area. Just add a bit of highlighting cream or even just a lighter color and you'll see just how beautiful and awake your eyes can look! Trust me on this one girls!

## 6. Champagne Hues for Light Skin

One of the oldest tricks in the book for women in their 30's is using champagne hues on light skin. These hues compliment the pale skin so well, it'll make your face lighten up instantly and look so much better! Give it a try!

## 7. Bronze Hues for Darker Skin

If you are on the darker side, bronze hues around your eyes will really make them pop! Trust me on this ladies, I personally love bronze and it will really allow your eyes to stand out beautifully, no matter what color they are!

## 8. Caffeine under-Eye Cream

While this might not exactly be a makeup tip for your 30's, it is a best practice. If you use caffeinated under eye cream, not only will it wake up all of the skin there, but you will appear more awake in no time at all!

## 9. Blush It up

Finally ladies, did you know that a little bit of blush can go a long, long way to making your cheeks appear warmer and your face lightened up? Give it a try! I promise if you add in some blush to your makeup bag, you'll see just how different and younger you look!

Well ladies, there you have it! These are my top makeup tips for your 30's. Do you have anymore makeup tips for your 30's that have worked for you? Sound off!
