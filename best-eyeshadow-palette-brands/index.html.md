# What's the Best Eyeshadow Palette Brand? 7 Top Picks ...

What are the best eyeshadow palette brands? A beautiful eyeshadow palette with loads of colors from mattes to shimmers and neutrals to bolds is a serious asset to your makeup stash. It's even better if it's small enough to pop in your purse. Check out Brit.co's best eyeshadow palettes of 2017 that are super travel friendly and smaller than your passport!

## 1. [Viseart Theory Palette](https://rstyle.me/~9Vnj0) ($45) in Theory II Minx

![eye, brown, eye shadow, organ, human body,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y2/xv/uwewia7a59382ea1e6d59690528126_600x600.jpg)

Professional makeup artists rave about this eyeshadow palette brand - and we can see why. This charming neutral palette offers an equal mix of super wearable matte and shimmery tones in rich hues from a deep, chocolate brown to a champagne gold luster shade. This brand is amazing - check it for more variations.

## 2. [Smashbox Double Exposure Travel Palette](https://rstyle.me/n/cm2pryvi4e) ($24)

![eye, eye shadow, organ, human body,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z3/f2/k71gnpay59382ea200e33379435588_600x600.jpg)

This selection serves up eight slightly moody, neutral shades that are both versatile (see: sand) and trendy (hello, rose gold). Even better? The shadows can be worn wet as liners for added drama.

## 3. [Tarte Tartelette Tease Clay Eyeshadow Palette](https://rstyle.me/n/cm2psfvi4e) ($23)

![pink, eye, organ, food, dessert,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sm/ve/be5p3bx559382ea23deeb097417036_600x600.jpg)

Flattering for any eye color, this mini houses dope shades in warm and cool tones that coordinate with any occasion. Four matte shadows that play for day are paired with two glittery options to bring nighttime vibes to your OOTD.

## 4. [L’Oreal Colour Riche Quads](http://www.kmart.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) ($8) in Good Luck Charm

![brown, eye, organ, face powder, head,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e7/da/lbyd02cn59382ea29b88a241445092_600x600.jpg)

If you love a good shimmery shadow, brace yourself - because this compact comes with four must-have tones of metallic. Mix and match the gold and champagne to create a gorgeous daytime look, then add a dash of the deep eggplant in the crease and outer corners to dress it up for nighttime. The darkest color makes for a seriously stunning eyeliner when used with a wet brush.

## 5. [Stowaway Cosmetics Essential Eye Palette](https://rstyle.me/n/cm2ptwvi4e) ($26)

![eye, eye shadow, organ, hand, human body,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ps/k1/xftvore959382ea2cd88a076767079_600x600.jpg)

Who knew that a palette so tiny could pack such a stylish punch?! Equipped with eight hues and a double-ended brush, you can throw this baby in your bag and know that you’ve got any look covered. Pair the plum hues together for a springy, smokey eye or lighten things up with the shimmery, sandy shades.

## 6. [Maybelline EyeStudio Color Plush Silk Eye Shadow Quad Palette](https://rstyle.me/n/cm2pt8vi4e) ($10) in Coral Oasis

![eye, eye shadow, organ, cosmetics, human body,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k4/vi/oistsjip59382ea2eacfd500174448_600x600.jpg)

Warm-weather months call for daring but doable eye looks in adventurous colors. This Maybelline pick makes it easy to step into the wild side with its four standout hues, including bold orange and deep, bronzey plum.
