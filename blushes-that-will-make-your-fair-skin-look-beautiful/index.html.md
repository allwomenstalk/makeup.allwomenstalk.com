# 9 Blushes That Will Make Your Fair Skin Look Beautiful ...

Fair skin is such a tricky thing to put blush on – but there are blushes for [fair skin](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/makeup-tricks-for-pale-skin/) out there! I have super fair skin, skin that burns in the sun and that is pretty pale. It took me a long time to find the right blush for fair skin, but now I've found 9 of 'em! So ladies, if you have fair skin and are looking for blushes for fair skin, I've got 'em right here!

**Price:** $7.93 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
One of my favorite blushes for fair skin is this fresh plum color. Just a small little brush of it along the arches of your cheeks and it'll bring your fair skin out that much more! Trust me ladies, it might seem dark, but it is beautiful on!

![Maybelline Mineral Power Blush - Gentle Pink](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2012/05/2_maybelline-mineral-power-blush-gentle-pink_300x294.jpg)
