# How to Get an Intense Smokey Eye for Girls Looking to Boost Their Makeup Game ...

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oj/sp/lvn4v0g656a01165137bd232240168_600x335.jpg)

Makeup can make or break your look. Even though you own plenty of lip and eye products, that doesn't mean that you know how to use them correctly. If you've been struggling to do your eye makeup lately, follow these tips from [Fashion Style Mag](https://www.fashionstylemag.com/) that will show you how to get an intense smokey eye with makeup forever:

## 1. Trace a Cat Liner Line along the Base of the Upper Lashes

![Trace a Cat Liner Line along the Base of the Upper Lashes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gs/q7/gyg9u1zc56a0116624cb8986989814_600x335.jpg)

Grab your favorite eyeliner and outline your upper lid. Make sure you create a tiny flick at the end to create the cat eye look.

## 2. Blend Upwards with a Small Flat Brush

![Blend Upwards with a Small Flat Brush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dd/ke/ur0djkqe56a0116576acb551100724_600x335.jpg)

Any brush will work, but you should stick to using a flat one if you want to get the best results possible. Why smudge your makeup at all? Well, it will make your eyes look sexier than ever.

## 3. Trace a Line along the Base of the Lower Lashes with Artist Liner

![Trace a Line along the Base of the Lower Lashes with Artist Liner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b0/ov/ze3kdyiy56a01165c7416011231100_600x335.jpg)

Now it's time to line your lower lashes. Just be careful not to poke yourself in the eye!

## 4. Blend Downwards with a Small Flat Brush

![Blend Downwards with a Small Flat Brush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fl/ro/h7p3v5fe56a011668b23f870666740_600x335.jpg)

Do the same thing you did with your upper lashes, except this time you should be blending downward instead of upward.
