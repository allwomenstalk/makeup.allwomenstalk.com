# 8 Super Benefits of Tinted Moisturizers ...

The benefits of tinted moisturizers are completely endless, especially if you use them everyday! Tinted moisturizers are not only a time saver, but they can leave your skin looking amazing in the end! If you've never tried a tinted moisturizer, but you've been dying to, you've got to read all of my benefits of tinted moisturizers so that you know exactly what to expect. Tinted moisturizers are different than foundation, girls, so remember that!

## 1. Time-Saver

Remember how I mentioned that one of the greatest benefits of tinted moisturizers is the fact that they are a huge time-saver? Truthfully, tinted moisturizers can save you the time it takes to put on moisturizer, wait for it to dry and finally put on your foundation. It is a two-in-one time-saver! That's a huge bonus!

## 2. Sun Protection

Did you know that a lot of foundations don't actually protect you from the sun at all? Instead, they can just dry your skin out a bit. With tinted moisturizers, not only are you getting a moisturizer with a foundation combined, but you are also getting a lot of sun protection! Trust me girls, in the summer or winter, that's a benefit!

## 3. Younger Skin

With tinted moisturizer, it can actually give you healthy, younger looking skin. That is incredible for a makeup, especially for one that you are already using to do that! Because tinted moisturizers are packed with all kinds of things that keep your skin looking beautiful, you can use them more often!

## 4. Moisture Protection

I think that the best benefit (besides it being a time-saver of course!) is the fact that tinted moisturizer is truly a moisturizer. The moisturizing part of this particular makeup will keep your skin hydrated yet still allow it to breathe! It's also great for any type of skin: oily, dry or even sensitive!

## 5. More Natural Look

You know how foundation can actually give you that caked on look sometimes? With tinted moisturizer, it doesn't allow for that. It will give you a way more natural look and will leave your skin uncaked and totally beautiful. Give it a try girls, you won't regret it!

## 6. Packed with Vitamins

When you put on any moisturizers, you know that they are packed with vitamins, right? With tinted moisturizers, most of them are packed with amazing vitamin C, vitamin E and SPF. Of course, it all depends on which brand you decide to use, so read carefully! If you're going to go with any type of coverage for your skin, tinted moisturizers is the way to go!

## 7. Great for the Summer

One of my favorite times of year to use tinted moisturizers is not just in the winter, when my skin is feeling dry, but actually in the summer, when I am looking for something that is not too heavy. A tinted moisturizer is the greatest thing in the world to throw into your makeup bag and go!

## 8. Easy Coverage

Finally girls, the last benefit of tinted moisturizers that you'll experience once you get one is the easy coverage. It's so easy to glide the formula onto your cheeks, your chin, your forehead and really feel like it is completely covered. It's also light and airy!

So girls, if you are looking to lighten up on the foundation and go with someone that is actually good for your skin, tinted moisturizer is the way to go! What's your favorite tinted moisturizer?
