Check out How Your Favorite Celebrity Wears the Cat Eye Look ...

Katy Perry • Ariana Grande • Rachel Bilson • Rosie Huntington-Whiteley • Taylor Swift • More ...

By Lisa

Cat eyes are a classic look and I love that with just a flick of the wrist, you can instantly elevate your look. I'm sure you've seen tons of variations of the cat eye, but I thought it would be interesting to see how our favorite celebs and makeup muses like to wear it. Check out this collection of celebrities rocking ferocious cat eyes!

1. Katy Perry

Source: We Heart It - Inspiring
Katy's cat eye isn't that obvious, but her gorgeous eyes, with a subtle cat eye, are definitely the focal point of this look.

2. Ariana Grande

Source: Ariana Grande's Response to 'Sam
When I think of celebs who rock the heck outta cat eyes, Ariana immediately comes to mind!

3. Rachel Bilson

Source: Jewelry
Meeeooooww! Rachel wears a bold cat eye for a simple yet sexy look.

4. Rosie Huntington-Whiteley

Source: The Barbie Resemblance In This
What could be more sultry than Rosie's super sexy pout? Her eyes of course!

5. Taylor Swift

Source: Quiz Your Taylor Swift Knowledge
Taylor's so good with the cat eye that there are numerous tutorials on how to achieve her look!

6. Naya Rivera

Source: What Colors Suit Me
Naya wears just a subtle cat eye flick to accent her beautiful eyes.

7. Marilyn Monroe

Source: 8 Beauty Lessons We've Learned
Iconic beauty Marilyn Monroe knew exactly how to compliment her famous bedroom eyes.

8. Angelina Jolie

Source: #TheLIST: 12 Cat-Eyed Icons
Angelina Jolie kills it with her seductive cat eye!

9. Emma Stone

Source: Emma
Emma Stone looks ravishing as always.

10. Olivia Wilde

Source: beautyeditor.ca
Olivia wows with her thick, bold cat eye. I can't take my eyes off her eyes!

11. Adele

Source: How to Use Scotch Tape
Adele is another cat eye master.Talk about stunning beauty!

12. Emmy Rossum

Source: Emmy Photostream
Here's a very bold cat eye that was created for a red carpet event, but it's great makeup inspo! Just look how her eyes light up.

13. Coco Rocha

Source: Photos I Like : Photo
This is case where both the eyes and lips take center stage and they're equally stunning.

14. Beyonce

Source: Beyonce Covers 'Vogue' March 2013
This is a softer version of the look. Something I like to call a smoked out cat eye!

15. Gugu Mbatha-Raw

Source: The
Gugu's thick flick is bold, eye-catching, and super chic!

16. Cara Delevingne

Source: Celebrity makeup - Cara Delevingne
Cara looks exquisite with this daring look!

17. Kim Kardashian

Source: comodynes.net
I don't know what I love more, Kim's mile long lashes or her alluring eyes!

18. Shay Mitchell

Source: Pretty Little Liars Argentina: Shay
Shay's smokey cat eyes are so dang pretty! Neutrals have never looked better.

19. Zoe Saldana

Source: Pinterest
Zoe looks stunning! I love everything about this look from the updo to the makeup.

20. Rihanna

Source: 7Newz- Latest Industry News, Updates
I love the blue and black liner combo, but I'm loving it even more paired with the hot pink lips.

21. Eva Mendes

Source: Pinterest
Eva Mendes is smoking hot with her smokey cat eyes and glasses!

22. Dita Von Teese

Source: ibitmes.com
When you think old Hollywood glamour, Miss Von Teese is the queen of the cat eye!

23. Lauren Conrad

Source: timeinc,net
Lauren's makeup is always flawless and she was one of my main inspirations for wearing a cat eye.

24. Selena Gomez

Source: ouloumagazine.com
Selena stuns in her sexy makeup and the cat eyes definitely take the cake.

25. Chrissy Teigen

Source: SheKnows Entertainment, Recipes, Parenting &
Last but certainly not least, Chrissy Teigen pairs her understated cat eye with dark cherry lips. Gorgeous!

What did you think? Is there a certain look you liked best? I honestly can't pick a favorite, everyone looks amazing!
