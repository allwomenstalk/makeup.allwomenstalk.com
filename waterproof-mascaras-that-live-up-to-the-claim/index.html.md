# 13 Waterproof Mascaras That Live up to the Claim ...

If you’re looking for the best waterproof mascaras-this list recommended by elle.com is all you need. These smudge proof mascaras are perfect, if you’re searching for the best waterproof mascaras for swimming, suffer from oily skin or want to avoid raccoon eyes. This list features a mixture of the best drugstore waterproof mascaras and high-end waterproof mascaras. Whatever your budget, I’m sure you’ll find the perfect waterproof mascara formula here, to add to your makeup arsenal.

## 1. Blinc Mascara Amplified

![eyelash, mascara, cosmetics,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vk/ry/jcwuqefj5948fdbb6dc07253004993_480x720.jpg)

Blinc created a formula that creates waterproof "tubes" around your lashes that is both sweat-proof and smudge-proof. It's a wet formula too, meaning it won't dry out and you can reapply throughout the day.

Blinc Mascara Amplified, $26; [sephora.com](https://goo.gl/2acj8D)

## 2. Rimmel ScandalEyes Retroglam Mascara

![product, bottle, sports equipment,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bj/yo/btnmomio5948fdbbcfd43981448185_480x720.jpg)

When it comes to cheap and reliable, Rimmel is it. Use this formula for a daytime look, since it's known for giving natural definition and separation.

Rimmel ScandalEyes Retroglam Mascara, $4; [target.com](http://www.target.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 3. BENEFIT COSMETICS BADGAL WATERPROOF MASCARA

![mascara, cosmetics, eyelash,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l6/hn/bblczbjt5948fdbc04102845568559_480x720.jpg)

This tube has all the benefits of the BADgal formula, but with the added bonus of being waterproof. Its spiral brush will add volume and length while separating your lashes, all in one flick.

Benefit Cosmetics BADgal Waterproof Mascara, $19; [benefitcosmetics.com](https://www.benefitcosmetics.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 4. Clinique Lash Power Mascara Long-wear Formula

![mascara, cosmetics, eyelash,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tc/xc/ybbxit1d5948fdbc493ee768347396_480x720.jpg)

According to Clinique, his mascara will last up to 24 hours. The best part is that you can gently remove it with just soap and water, meaning there's no need to bust out the industrial-strength makeup remover.

Clinique Lash Power Mascara Long-Wearing Formula, $16.50; [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 5. DIOR DIORSHOW WATERPROOF MASCARA

If you love this cult-fave then you might as well carry its waterproof formula, too. Try the Diorshow Waterproof Mascara in Azure blue for an electric, poolside look.

Dior Diorshow Waterproof, $28.50; [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 6. Covergirl Lashblast Volume Waterproof Mascara

![orange, cosmetics, mascara,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a9/ce/ttabbq5k5948fdbccfa16296063922_480x720.jpg)

CoverGirl LashBlast Volume Waterproof Mascara, $6; [amazon.com](https://goo.gl/m7aj6V)

## 8. Lancôme Grandiose Waterproof Mascara

![cosmetics, bottle, glass bottle, wine bottle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/em/ql/dfhzk9c25948fdbe61a57919600903_480x720.jpg)

Lancôme Grandiose Waterproof Mascara, $32; [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 9. M.a.C Cosmetics Extended Play Gigablack Lash Mascara

![product, mascara, cosmetics, eyelash,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k3/m2/hbf78law5948fdbe845ef141023570_480x720.jpg)

M.A.C. Cosmetics's waterproof formula will last you up to 16 hours, but is thermal-sensitive and easily removed with warm water. The thin bristles will lift and lengthen your lashes for a natural finish.

M.A.C. Cosmetics Extended Play Gigablack Lash Mascara, $18; [nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
