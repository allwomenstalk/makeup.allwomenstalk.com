# 7 Luscious Lipsticks You Can Wear Day or Night ...

Luscious lipsticks are always a great find, but I’m about to share with you some great ones you can wear to work during the day then go ahead and refresh them for a night out. Lipstick doesn’t have to cost a ton, but you generally get what you pay for. If you want a slick application and long lasting wear, it’s natural to spend a bit more. That being said, you can still enjoy your most beautiful look on a budget. Check out these luscious lipsticks that will leave you beautiful, but won’t empty out your pocketbook either.

## 1. Luscious Cosmetics Signature Lipstick

![Luscious Cosmetics Signature Lipstick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/0v/y4/ei0g8c8k_600x543.jpg)

True to its name, the Luscious brand of lipstick is truly one of the most luscious lipsticks out there. It’s both lightweight and smooth so you can slick it on easily and quickly, but it won’t feel sticky on your lips. There are 16 shades to choose from so you’re sure to find the perfect one for day and night.

## 2. Lancome L’Absolu Crème De Mat Lip Color

![Lancome L’Absolu Crème De Mat Lip Color](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cg/xr/bm853fub_400x320.jpg)

This revolutionary product goes on like gloss, but covers like your favorite lipstick. It’s easy to apply and leaves you with a look that goes from day to night with ease. There are only three shades in this line, but each and every one is a color you’ll love.

## 3. Motives Rich Formula Lipstick

![Motives Rich Formula Lipstick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uc/tg/0glfmf2g_271x271.jpg)

You can apply this great lipstick sheer or heavy, so you can wear it to the office, then slick it on for a night on the town. This one goes on so smooth, you might get addicted to how it feels. With added vitamin E, this lipstick looks good and leaves you with kissably soft lips you’ll love.

## 4. Khroma Beauty

![Khroma Beauty](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sp/gr/z29274ra_300x97.jpg)

This awesome lipstick from the famous Kardashian sisters, is double sided. You get a matte lipstick and a glossy lipstick in one product. Who could ask for more? The design of this lipstick is so cool, but it also goes on smooth and gives you several options for day and night wear.

## 5. Lancome Rouge in Love

![Lancome Rouge in Love](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l4/cn/yajg6m9b_300x360.jpg)

This fun line of lipsticks comes in several vibrant shades that go on smoothly and easily. It’s also designed for long wear, so it will last all day, then one reapplication leaves you with coverage all night. The shades are perfect for a variety of occasions, so you might decide you need the entire collection.

## 6. Lipstick Queen

![Lipstick Queen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qi/lc/kqfk0r7v_200x200.jpg)

You can only get this lipstick online, but it’ll be totally worth the wait. The line claims to work with casual outfits, but also with your most formal ensemble. That means you can keep just one tube of lipstick in your purse, no matter what the day has in store for you. The various shades are designed to be worn alone or piled on top of one another for your own customized shades.

## 7. Maybelline New York Day to Night Lipstick

![Maybelline New York Day to Night Lipstick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ld/ib/nh6wrqcf_450x450.jpg)

This awesome lipstick even says it goes from day to night on the label. And with a price tag that simply can’t be beat, you’re going to love this product. It goes on smooth and gives you the coverage you crave. A variety of shades allows you to find just the right hue for your day and night looks.

What’s your favorite lipstick? Do you wear it night and day or do you go back and forth among shades?
