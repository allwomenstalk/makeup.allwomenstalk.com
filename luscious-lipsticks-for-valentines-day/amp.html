9 Luscious Lipsticks for Valentine's Day ...

Lime Crime – Velvetines in Red Velvet • Lipstick Queen – Medieval • Bite Beauty - Luminous Crème Lipstick in Tannin • Nars – Semi Matte Lipstick in Schiap • MAC – Divine Choice • More ...

By Madison  · Feb 3, 2026

There are many wonderful lipsticks for Valentine’s Day in many color options. Since the traditional colors for Valentine’s Day are pink, purple, and red, I chose my favorite lipsticks in each of these shades. If you are looking for a classic yet fun pout for the holiday, than you need to check out these luscious lipsticks for Valentine’s Day.

1. Lime Crime – Velvetines in Red Velvet

Lime Crime’s Velvetines tops my list of the best lipsticks for Valentine’s Day. This lipstick goes on as a liquid and dries completely matte. It will almost feel like powder on your lips! This lipstick is kiss-proof making it a great option to wear out on your Valentine’s Day date. This lipstick has developed a cult-like following so order yours as soon as you can! Visit limecrime.com to grab Velvetines ASAP.

2. Lipstick Queen – Medieval

If you have always wanted to wear red lips but aren’t sure if you can pull off a bright red pout, Lipstick Queen’s Medieval is a good place to start. This lipstick is formulated with two ingredients – Vitamin E and pigment. Because of this, the lipstick goes on nearly transparent with just a hint of red color. You can visit lipstickqueen.com to get this shimmering red.

3. Bite Beauty - Luminous Crème Lipstick in Tannin

Bite Beauty’s Tannin is a pretty poppy red that will last all night. Aside from this lipstick looking fabulous, it is full of moisture and fights against signs of aging. Another special aspect of this lipstick is the shape. Bite molds their lipsticks in different designs for different colors for perfect application. You can get this wonder lipstick at sephora.com

4. Nars – Semi Matte Lipstick in Schiap

If you are looking for a velvety lipstick that isn’t completely matte, then Nars makes just the lipstick for you! All of the Semi Matte lipsticks are formulated to be highly pigmented and moisturizing. Schiap is the perfect pink to wear on Valentine’s Day if you are looking for a stand out shade. Go to narscosmetics.com to get Schiap.

5. MAC – Divine Choice

Divine Choice is a gorgeous bright baby pink that is fabulous for Valentine’s Day. Mac guarantees this lipstick to be lightweight and non-sticky for easy wear. If you want a low maintenance lipstick for your date, this creamy pink is a great option. You can get Divine Choice at maccosmetics.com

6. Obsessive Compulsive Cosmetics – Lip Tar in Anime

If you are looking to turn heads on Valentine’s Day, look no further than OCC’s Anime Lip Tar. This neon pink lipstick is so bright, it’ll shock you! Besides the fact that this lip tar is vegan, my favorite part is that a little goes a very long way. Just a dab will get you full opaque coverage. You can get a tube of Anime at occmakeup.com

7. Lime Crime – D’Lilac

Lime Crime’s D’Lilac is a purple unlike any other. This soft, baby purple is an interesting twist on the classic violet Valentine’s Day color. If you are looking for a sweet pastel look for Valentine’s Day, you should consider D’Lilac. This opaque lipstick is loaded with pigment so your lipstick will look amazing all night long. Head on over to limecrime.com to get D’Lilac.

8. Illamasqua – ESP

If you are looking for a more traditional purple for Valentine’s Day, look no further than Illamasqua’s ESP lipstick. This matte violet shade is creamy matte with a beautiful iridescent finish. ESP will help you make a statement with your Valentine’s Day makeup. You can order ESP on illamasqua.com

9. Make up for Ever – Rouge Artist Intense in #15

Make Up For Ever is one of my all time favorite makeup lines and this lipstick is one of the reasons why. I love every shade in their Rouge Artist Intense collection, but #15 is perfect for Valentine’s Day. This pearly violet color is packed full of pigment and is long-lasting. sephora.com sells this stunning shade.

No matter if you choose purple, pink, or red for Valentine’s Day, you can’t go wrong with any of these lipsticks. Wearing a festive lipstick is a fun way to celebrate the occasion. Will you be picking up any of these lipsticks for your Valentine’s Day celebration?
