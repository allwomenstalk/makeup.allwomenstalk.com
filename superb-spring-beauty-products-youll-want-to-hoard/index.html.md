# 9 Superb Spring Beauty Products You'll Want to Hoard ...

Each winter I anxiously await the arrival of new spring beauty products and this season is no different. There are so many stupendous spring beauty products out right now that it would be a shame for any beauty addict to miss out on. So whether you’re looking to stock up on some new beauty products or you just want to see what’s new, don’t miss out on these amazing spring makeup products!

## 1. Yves Saint Laurent Baby Doll Kiss & Blush Lips & Cheeks

![Yves Saint Laurent Baby Doll Kiss & Blush Lips & Cheeks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/do/w1/qu99eqpn_450x448.jpg)

If one of the spring beauty products you have on your list is a new lippie, you’ll be tickled pink with YSL’s new product for lips and cheeks. Not only is the packaging on this baby irresistible, there are so many alluring colors to choose from that are perfect for a spring makeup look. Using colors and shades in the same color family on both your lips and cheeks can give you a nice uniform look. Available at Sephora for $40.

## 2. Sephora Favorites Meet Your Match

![Sephora Favorites Meet Your Match](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wi/px/s1yxtf8j_450x368.jpg)

Sephora’s Meet Your Match box allows you to try out a variety of spring makeup products for your lips and tips. If you’re indecisive about what products to try like I am, this set offers three different nail polishes and lip products in complimentary shades that will help make getting ready for any spring soiree simple and fun! Available at Sephora for $25.

## 3. NYX Macaron Lippies

One of the best things about makeup is having fun experimenting with different hues. NYX has made it easy for us to incorporate some bright, cheery colors into our beauty routine with their new Macaron Lippies. These creamy, long-wearing lipsticks come in 12 neon and pastels colors so you can be a little daring or a little demure with your lip colors this season. Available at NYX.com for $6 each.

## 4. Renpure Advanced Advanced BB Crème Shampoo, Coconut Oil Fragrance

This next beauty product is quite unique and I’m pretty psyched to try it out for myself. I’m sure you’ve noticed a ton of BB products in beauty aisles these days. There is everything from BB cream for your face to BB cream for your hair. Renpure’s BB Crème Shampoo doesn’t contain any sulfates and comes out like mousse. Safe for color treated hair and formulated to lubricate your hair to reduce friction damage while you shampoo, this product is as gentle as is it is effective. Available at Walgreens for $7.99.

## 5. Cetaphil Gentle Skin Cleansing Cloths

Cetaphil fans will be happy to know that they now make makeup wipes. Honestly, I don’t know what took the good folks at Cetaphil so long to create them, but I’m glad to see that the non-drying, gentle formula available in this form. These are perfect for freshening up your skin, removing any eye shadow fall out and of course removing makeup. Available at Ulta for $6.49.

## 6. Physicians Formula Super CC+ Color Correction + Care CC+ Concealer SPF 30

There’s a good reason why this beauty product has such a long name, it’s because it offers plenty of beauty benefits! Physicians Formula’s CC+ Concealer contains color correcting pigments and conceals dark spots, discoloration, redness, blemishes, evens skin tone and decreases the appearance of fines lines and wrinkles. Available at Target for $12.95.

## 7. Shu Uemura the Lightbulb Foundation

Shu Uemura is mainly known for his cleansing oil and eyelash curler, but I was equally impressed with this new foundation. The Lightbulb foundation comes with its own sponge applicator that was created to apply the foundation with just the right amount of control. You’ll achieve great coverage and an enviable glow in one lightweight product. Available at ShuUemura.com for $55.

## 8. REN Rosa Centifolia No.1 Purity Cleansing Balm

![REN Rosa Centifolia No.1 Purity Cleansing Balm](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dx/jd/ejbq7fcy_450x444.jpg)

Finding a decent facial cleanser is tough. If you want it to be gentle, it doesn’t always remove all the oil and makeup from your face. And if you want it to do a thorough job of cleansing your skin, it can often be irritating or drying. Worry no more, with REN’s cleansing balm you get the best of both worlds where it melts away dirt, makeup and even waterproof mascara while calming skin and conditioning your brows and lashes. Available at Sephora for $48.

## 9. Jordana Best Lash Extreme Volumizing Mascara

This might be one of the best yet cheapest spring beauty products on this list. I’m sometimes wary of trying beauty products that are too cheap as I seriously question their efficacy, but this mascara has tons of fans who vouch for its benefits. If you’re looking for buildable volume, lengthening and defining, you’ve got to give this a try. Available at drugstore.com for $2.99.
