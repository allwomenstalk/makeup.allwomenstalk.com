# Steal Kylie Jenner's Mascara Trick ...

Whether you love or hate the Kardashians, you have to admit that they're stylish--especially when it comes to Kylie Jenner. She sure knows how to rock her makeup. That's why [Cosmopolitan](https://www.cosmopolitan.com/style-beauty/beauty/a48237/kylie-jenner-mascara-eyeliner-trick/) has brought us this cute little tip:

Instead of trying to hit your lashes and only your lashes with your mascara wand, you shouldn't be afraid to hit the skin under those lashes. Why? Well, Kylie lets it hit her skin, because it "gives her eyes a more dramatic look, almost like you would get if you applied black eyeliner."

You might as well try this trick! If it gets too messy, you can always wipe it all off.
