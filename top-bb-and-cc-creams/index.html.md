# Top 8 BB and CC Creams ...

BB and CC creams are everywhere these days! If you’re still new to these beauty balms, let me introduce to you some of the best of the bunch! BB and CC creams are must-haves for many makeup mavens, as they provide so many skin benefits while providing coverage, correction and color. These souped-up tinted moisturizers offer us so many skin benefits that they should not be passed up!

## 1. Dior Diorskin Nude BB Crème

![Dior Diorskin Nude BB Crème](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/02/1_dior-diorskin-nude-bb-cr-me_250x250.jpg)

Both drugstores and designer brands are jumping on the wagon when it comes to BB and CC creams! Dior’s Diorskin Nude is known for its luxuriously light and airy formula. This BB cream is enriched with mineralized floral water and antioxidants to provide even coverage and a gorgeous glow to many shades of skin and skin types!

## 2. Maybelline Dream Fresh BB Cream

![Maybelline Dream Fresh BB Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/02/2_maybelline-dream-fresh-bb-cream_410x410.jpg)

This next BB Cream is affordable, comes in multiple shades and offers great coverage. Maybelline’s Dream Fresh BB Cream can offer you a hand in evening out your skin tone, blurs imperfections and gives your skin a dose of nourishing moisture. This is a great tinted moisturizer for everyday use as it contains SPF 30 and is true to color.

## 3. Chanel Complete Correction CC Cream

![Chanel Complete Correction CC Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/02/3_chanel-complete-correction-cc-cream_350x350.jpg)

Chanel was one of the first cosmetics companies to come out with the much anticipated CC cream. CC creams are similar to BB creams, except they typically have a lighter formula and focus on concealing problem areas. Chanel’s CC cream is formulated to smooth, moisturize, protect and even out skin imperfections.

## 4. Rachel K CC Cream

![Rachel K CC Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/02/4_rachel-k-cc-cream_350x302.jpg)

This next CC cream might not come from a familiar brand but it’s making waves nonetheless. This beauty brand based in Singapore was created by Miss Singapore Universe 2009 and creates skin care and beauty products. Their CC Cream is made with pure minerals, vitamins, hyaluronic acid, sea water and white tea extracts, and is said to be suitable for all skin types!

## 5. Urban Decay Naked Skin Beauty Balm Broad Spectrum SPF 20

![Urban Decay Naked Skin Beauty Balm Broad Spectrum SPF 20](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/02/5_urban-decay-naked-skin-beauty-balm-broad-spectrum-spf-20_250x250.jpg)

If you’re looking for a lightweight BB cream that provides some major anti-aging benefits, Urban Decay’s Naked Skin BB cream is a great choice! This BB cream is said to blur imperfections, minimizes pores, lines, wrinkles and redness. As you can see, this multitasking BB cream contains anti-aging benefits and is ideal for ladies looking for light coverage.

## 6. L'Oreal Studio Secrets Magic BB Cream

![L'Oreal Studio Secrets Magic BB Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/02/6_loreal-studio-secrets-magic-bb-cream_410x410.jpg)

Another great drugstore option for BB cream is L’Oreal’s Studio Secrets Magic BB cream. What makes this formula magic? Well, it comes out of the tube white but quickly changes to match your skin tone once you blend it! This baby does a great job evening out your skin, blurring imperfections and it blends like a dream!

## 7. Olay CC Cream Total Effects Daily Moisturizer + Touch of Foundation

![Olay CC Cream Total Effects Daily Moisturizer + Touch of Foundation](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/02/7_olay-cc-cream-total-effects-daily-moisturizer-touch-of-foundation_409x409.jpg)

Olay was the first brand to bring CC creams to the drugstore. Their Total Effects CC cream provides immediate coverage, protection and correction to fight the seven signs of aging. Use this lightweight formula to conceal imperfections, minimize pores and improve the overall condition of your skin!

## 8. Stila Stay All Day 10-in-One HD Illuminating Beauty Balm with Broad Spectrum SPF 30

![Stila Stay All Day 10-in-One HD Illuminating Beauty Balm with Broad Spectrum SPF 30](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/02/8_stila-stay-all-day-10-in-one-hd-illuminating-beauty-balm-with-broad-spectrum-spf-30_250x250.jpg)

Here’s another multi-tasking BB cream with 10 skin benefits! Count on this high definition beauty balm to protect your skin, hide skin imperfections, minimize the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles, improve skin texture, support collagen production and it even contains emollients that increase antioxidant activity!

As you can see, you have lots of choices when it comes to BB and CC creams. Finding the best formulation might not be easy, but it’s comforting to know there are so many marvelous beauty creams out there! Do you use BB or CC creams? Which brand is your favorite?
