# 8 Makeup Brands to Love ...

There are tons of [makeup brands](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/worst-makeup-brands-out-there/) to love that I am obsessed with! You all read my 'Worst Makeup Brands' article, I've gotten so many requests asking about which makeup brands to love and which are my favorites, I thought that I would share! These are brands that I use almost every day, and while some of them might be a little more on the expensive side, you do get what you pay for, girls!

## 1. MAC

Ah, no makeup brands to love list would be complete without MAC! MAC makeup is absolutely flawless all of the time. From the eyeliner all the way to the mascara and primer, MAC makes my life so much easier. The eyeliner that I use is a gel and it doesn't smudge anywhere! The primer is amazing and the foundation? Don't even get me started!

## 2. Maybelline

If you've ever read any of my other posts, then you know that I am actually pretty obsessed with Maybelline. I love everything about this particular brand but my favorite product is their mascara. I think that they make the best formula, the best brushes and the best mascara products out there!

## 3. Urban Decay

If you have never tried anything Urban Decay, you've got to give their eyeliner and their primer a try. The Urban Decay primer is by far one of the best that I've ever used. Not only does it help my makeup stay on for hours, but it actually makes any blemishes or imperfections that I have disappear!

## 4. Smashbox

Smashbox is another brand of makeup that I absolutely love. The reason? It reminds me a little bit of MAC, but a tad cheaper! It's got all kinds of different colors and their line for spring? It's going to be beautiful! If you haven't tried Smashbox, give it a try!

## 5. LUSH

Now, if you girls are looking for makeup brands that are completely all natural and have no chemicals at all in them, this is the brand for you! LUSH makes some of the best bath bombs, but they just came out with makeup too and I love it! It is fresh, it is all natural and it is completely different!

## 6. Lancome

Lancome is a brand that has been around for quite a while. Not only are their skin care products some of the best, but their makeup is awesome! It goes on smooth, feels so great on my skin but it stays put! Their mascara is incredible, their foundation is awesome and their eyeliner is so amazing!

## 7. L'Oreal

Ah, L'Oreal, this is one of my favorite affordable makeup brands to love. I think that L'Oreal makes some of the best foundation, the best eyeliner and it honestly is super affordable! What's your favorite L'Oreal makeup product, girls? Mine is the eyeliner!

## 8. Philosophy

Finally, Philosophy is the last makeup brand that I absolutely love. Their moisturizer is amazing, their lipstick is incredible and their lipgloss smells amazing – and lasts forever! If you want something all-natural and affordable, this is it!

So girls, you asked for all of the makeup brands to love that I am obsessed with and here you go! These are all of the makeup brands that I choose before all others, what are some of yours? Another brand that I love is Chanel -- can't miss that one!
