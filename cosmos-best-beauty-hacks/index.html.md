# The Best Makeup Hacks Cosmo Has to Offer ...

[Cosmopolitan](https://www.cosmopolitan.com/style-beauty/beauty/advice/a46257/beauty-hacks-from-kylie-jenners-video/) isn't old news. They still have hot tips for women when it comes to relationships, sex, and style. In order to prove it, here are some of Cosmopolitan's best beauty hacks that they took from stylist, Hrush Achemyan, during Kylie Jenner's Birthday Beauty Video Tutorial:

## 1. Eye Makeup Comes First

![eyebrow, face, hair, cheek, black,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gl/wp/gt3mapge5803aec604cb8698512787_600x600.jpg)

Before you put on your foundation or concealer, you should do all of your eye makeup. If you make a mistake, then you'd going to have to wipe your face clean, which means you'd end up messing up your concealer. That's why your eyes should always come first.

## 2. Use Tape for Cat Eyes

![eyebrow, face, hair, cheek, black hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vm/mf/ostz2dxt5803aea98fdb2771047481_600x600.jpg)

It's difficult to create the perfect wing with your eyeliner. That's why you should place a piece of tape on the outer corner of your eyes beforehand. Then you can apply your liner and shadow to create a sharp edge.

## 3. Replace Eye Shadows with Contour Powders

![hair, face, eyebrow, black hair, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sz/h4/nngc89xa5803aee47e674562620787_400x400.jpg)

You don't have to use every beauty product the way that it's advertised. You can actually use contour powders as eye shadows, and they do an excellent job!

## 4. Contour Your Nose

![hair, face, black, nose, black hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c3/wg/vowcrmt05803af388fa37860647383_600x600.jpg)

If you're unhappy with the size of your nose, you can use makeup to make it appear smaller. All you have to do is contour the sides of it. It works like magic.

## 5. Use Orange Blush under Pink Blush

![color, face, eye, eyebrow, cheek,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p4/ua/t7u7fpvi5803af6f24eca607676491_600x600.jpg)

Women are always talking about eyeliner and lipstick, but blush doesn't get a lot of attention. If you want yours to look intense, you should layer an orange blush under your pink blush to amplify the color.

## 6. Replace Contour Products with Foundation

![hair, human hair color, face, eyebrow, blond,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/st/j3/uyi4v7xs5803af888ec73017113969_600x600.jpg)

If you want to contour your face, but don't have the right products, you can use foundation instead. Of course, you should make sure that the foundation is a shade darker than your actual foundation in order for the trick to work.

## 7. Blend Your Lines

![eyebrow, face, hair, cheek, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mh/os/l71s2gp35803afa352b2e072227608_600x574.jpg)

When you blend your contour lines, make sure that you use a brush that's been dipped in a cream bronzer. That way, your face will end up looking tanner than it actually is.

If you follow these tips, you'll end up with a flawless face full of makeup. What other beauty hacks do you think other girls should try?
