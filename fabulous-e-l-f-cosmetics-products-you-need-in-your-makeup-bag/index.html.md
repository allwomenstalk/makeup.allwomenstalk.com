# 7 Fabulous E.l.f. Cosmetics Products You Need in Your Makeup Bag ...

If you're looking for ways to save some money on makeup, why not try some e.l.f. cosmetics products? By purchasing these items, you'll be able to save a ton of money, while making some great additions to your makeup bag. Most e.l.f. cosmetics products cost less than $10, which is much less than many other brands. Here are 7 of my favorite products for you to check out.

## 1. E.l.f. Studio Mineral Infused Face Primer

![E.l.f. Studio Mineral Infused Face Primer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/13103828_2680_599x565.jpg)

One of my favorite e.l.f. cosmetics products is the mineral infused face primer. I have been using it for about a month and it does a great job reducing the appearance of my pores. Most good foundation primers will cost at least $20, but this one is a great deal at only $6. When you apply this primer, make sure that your face is clean and rub a small amount all over your face. For me, I have the best results if I allow this primer to dry for 5 to 10 minutes before applying my foundation. I currently have the clear version of this product, but it is available in 4 different shades to target your individual needs.

## 2. E.l.f. Studio Endless Eyes Pro Eye Shadow Palette

![E.l.f. Studio Endless Eyes Pro Eye Shadow Palette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/13104000_3320_599x518.jpg)

If you've ever wanted to try one of Oprah's favorite things, now is your chance. I am an eye shadow lover, so I was very excited to get this product. This eye shadow palette features 100 different shades and can be purchased for only $10. With such a wide array of colors, you can create basically any look that you want. This product is available in both matte and shimmer pallets, so be sure to check them both out.

## 3. E.l.f. Studio Makeup Remover Exfoliating Cleansing Cloths

![E.l.f. Studio Makeup Remover Exfoliating Cleansing Cloths](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/13104051_2330_599x363.jpg)

I purchased this product after it was highly recommended by a friend. I usually stay away from makeup cleansing cloths because they tend to bother my skin. I was very surprised that this product didn't irritate my skin at all. In fact, my skin feels very smooth after using them. My only complaint about this product is that there are only 10 cloths in the package, but they are only $3 per package, so they're still a good deal. I am also able to remove all of my makeup, including heavy eye makeup, with just one cloth.

## 4. E.l.f. Studio Eyebrow Kit

![E.l.f. Studio Eyebrow Kit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/13104156_0035_599x532.jpg)

I decided to purchase this item after seeing it in several different magazines. I was very happy to see that the kit is available in 4 different shades to easily blend with any eyebrow color. I have a scar in one of my eyebrows and this kit does a good job camouflaging it. With this product, you can easily make your eyebrows look fuller and more defined. It also seems to provide me with longer lasting results than an eyebrow pencil. The e.l.f. Studio Eyebrow Kit can be purchased for $3.

## 5. E.l.f. Essential Shimmer Eyeliner Pencil

![E.l.f. Essential Shimmer Eyeliner Pencil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/13104541_8553_499x472.jpg)

I love eyeliner and my makeup bag just wouldn't be complete without it. When ordering from e.l.f. cosmetics, I was shocked to see that they had eyeliner for only $1. Even though this is a pencil eyeliner, it glides on very smoothly. Plus, you can choose from a variety of colors, such as plum passion, grassy green, and my favorite, black bandit. My favorite thing about this eyeliner is that the cap of each eyeliner pencil has a built-in sharpener.

## 6. E.l.f. Studio Glossy Gloss

![E.l.f. Studio Glossy Gloss](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/13104646_1994_599x545.jpg)

I'm really not a lipstick girl. Instead, I use lip gloss to achieve the lip color I want. I currently use several different brands of lip gloss, so I didn't expect much from this product, but I was pleasantly surprised. This item can be used alone or over your favorite lipstick to add a nice glossy shine. Plus, this lip gloss is thicker than most and seems to have a good amount of moisturizer added to it. You can get the e.l.f. Studio Glossy Gloss in 10 different shades for only $3 each.

## 7. E.l.f. Crystal Crystal Travel Brush Set

![E.l.f. Crystal Crystal Travel Brush Set](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/13104738_7180_599x488.jpg)

There are several different e.l.f cosmetics brush sets to choose from. I decided to purchase this one because I wanted a set that would easily fit into my purse. It can also be purchased in 4 different colors and comes with a cute case. Each set comes with a lip brush, an eye shadow brush, an angled eyeliner brush, a powder brush, and a defining eye brush. This limited edition set can be purchased for $10.

There are many other e.l.f. Cosmetics products that I hope to try in the future. What has been your experience with e.l.f. products? Do you have any favorites that you would like to recommend to others?
