# 18 New Melted Matte Lipsticks for Spring ...

[Too Faced](https://goo.gl/K5OlOc) is releasing eighteen new melted matte lipsticks that will look amazing during Spring. They're going to cost $21 each and will be sold at everyone's favorite makeup shop, [Sephora.](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) According to [allure.com](https://www.allure.com/story/too-faced-melted-matte-lipstick-shades?mbid=social_twitter) this is what they look like and what they're called:

## 1. Miso Pretty, Child Star, Cool Girl, Sell out, Queen B, and 1998

![Miso Pretty, Child Star, Cool Girl, Sell out, Queen B, and 1998](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/st/et/tmhgljo256e2e1afa683c938653221_500x336.jpg)

These light shades are perfect for days when you want to look casual.

## 2. Feelin' Myself, Mrs. Roper, Lady Balls, It's Happening, Bend & Snap, and Marcia, Marcia, Marcia

![Feelin' Myself, Mrs. Roper, Lady Balls, It's Happening, Bend & Snap, and Marcia, Marcia, Marcia](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yp/qa/jxrks8g756e2e1b01069c743829949_500x336.jpg)

These bright shades will give your lips a pop of color!

## 3. Unicorn, Drop Dead Red, on Point, Naughty by Nature, Who's Zoomin Who, and Evil Twin

![Unicorn, Drop Dead Red, on Point, Naughty by Nature, Who's Zoomin Who, and Evil Twin](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ai/ka/mw2yef0x56e2e1b01a1d6072521849_500x336.jpg)
