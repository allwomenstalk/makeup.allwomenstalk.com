# How to Keep Your Makeup Looking Fresh All Day for Girls Who Don't Have Time for Touch Ups ...

Ladies, we’ve all been through that same experience, haven’t we? That all too common experience where you apply your very best face of makeup before leaving home in the morning only to have it ruined as the hours go by, succumbing to the wear and tear of your busy day. You're probably wondering **how to keep your makeup looking fresh all day**, aren't you?

It can be kind of demoralising to see your makeup go south so quickly, almost to the point where you feel like you don’t even want to do it in the first place, but don’t fear, we have a handful of great tips for **how to keep your makeup looking fresh all day**! Once you have read through this list, you will feel much more confident that the face you wanted will remain the face you wear from sunrise to sunset!

## 1. Wash Your Face

Washing your face is one of the best answers for **how to keep your makeup looking fresh all day**. Making sure that your face is completely clean before applying new makeup every day is a solid first step in making your look last. The act of laying new makeup on top of old makeup will only result in it melting and fading more quickly, and this is simply down to the fact that the product won’t be adhering to your skin, but rather to a layer of old makeup that won’t help it to stay in place. Warm water, gentle soap, and a good old-fashioned pat dry will do the trick!

## 2. Good Quality Primer

It’s really worth investing in a great quality primer product rather than skimping on a cheaper brand. Applying a great primer to your face and eyes before putting on your makeup can help to extend the freshness of the application by several hours. Sometimes you get away with going for cheaper makeup alternatives, but with primer, it really helps to splash the cash.

## 3. Set Everything!

It is vital that you use a setting spray to lock your makeup into place before you leave the house and open your face up to all of the world’s elements! The spray creates a seal between your makeup and the outside world that will help to keep it looking fresh and new for as long as possible, much longer than if your face was in direct contact with the wind and rain!

## 4. Waterproof Eye Products

To be honest, I can’t believe that in this day and age people are wearing eye makeup that isn’t waterproof! It might be slightly more difficult to remove at the end of the day, but that is only because it's so much better at staying on your face! Waterproof eye makeup is simply more resistant to the elements and therefore is an obvious choice for longer lasting looks.

## 5. Layer Your Makeup

Although many women are wary of layering because they don’t want to end up with that chalky look, if you do it right, it can really help your makeup to stay fresh looking and in place all day long. Moisturise first, then layer on primer, foundation, powder and finally a setting powder. It’s a bit more work, but it’s definitely worth it!
