# Perfect Goth Makeup Products for a Gorgeous Dark Beauty Look ...

These products for a gothic makeup look will help you achieve one of the hottest looks for fall. Makeup looks that have influences of the goth culture are one of the biggest beauty trends this year. Goth beauty is romantic and sparks intrigue. In order to get a true gothic look, apply a foundation that is a few shades lighter than your skin tone to make these products for a gothic makeup look really pop against your pale skin.

## 1. Kevyn Aucoin - the Expert Lip Color in Blood Roses

![Kevyn Aucoin - the Expert Lip Color in Blood Roses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/08/1_kevyn-aucoin-the-expert-lip-color-in-blood-roses_335x382.jpg)

This rich merlot lipstick is one of my favorite products for a gothic makeup look. When it comes to a gothic makeup look, darker is always better. This deep red is preferable to a bright, cherry red for pulling off this beauty trend. Aside from the gorgeous color, my favorite part about this lipstick is that it is enriched with hydrating Wild Mango extract. Not only will it leave your lips feeling luscious, the lipstick will also smell slightly fruity. This long-lasting formula is free of parabens and is guaranteed to elevate your beauty routine to another level.

## 2. Sugarpill - Eyelashes in Supreme

![Sugarpill - Eyelashes in Supreme](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/08/2_sugarpill-eyelashes-in-supreme_399x192.jpg)

To really bring on the drama of a gothic makeup look, false eyelashes are a must. Supreme lashes by Sugarpill are thick and long, making them my favorite lashes for a gothic makeup look. They are made from natural human hair and are reusable. Falsies are one of the best ways to open up your eyes to make them appear larger, enhancing your already gorgeous eyes. With Sugarpill you get quality lashes for a under $10!

## 3. Make up for Ever - Eyeshadow in Deep Plum Shimmer Iridescent

![Make up for Ever - Eyeshadow in Deep Plum Shimmer Iridescent](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/08/3_make-up-for-ever-eyeshadow-in-deep-plum-shimmer-iridescent_249x231.jpg)

Puprle eye shadow is a great way to break up all the black on a gothic makeup look and still get a dramatic effect. The glitter in this eyeshadow is great for achieving a goth glam look. You will fall in love with this Make Up For Ever eyeshadow because of its high pigment density. This eyeshadow is guaranteed to last and last. The iridescent shade of this eyeshadow gives a transparent sheen so you can make a subtle statement by wearing it alone or make a bold statement by swiping it on top of other shadows.

## 4. Kat Von D - Tattoo Liner in Trooper

![Kat Von D - Tattoo Liner in Trooper](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/08/4_kat-von-d-tattoo-liner-in-trooper_449x438.jpg)

The tattoo liner by Kat Von D is a gothic beauty must-have. The innovative brush tip makes applying this liner a breeze. Trooper is water-resistant so your eyeliner is guaranteed to hold up all day long. For achieving a gothic beauty look, don't be shy while applying eyeliner and make sure to put some on your bottom lashes. If you want to go all out, you can use Trooper to create temporary tattoos on your face and body.

## 5. Ardency Inn - PUNKER in Semi-Goth

![Ardency Inn - PUNKER in Semi-Goth](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/08/5_ardency-inn-punker-in-semi-goth_249x248.jpg)

PUNKER is a gothic beauty must-have to elevate your existing lipsticks and glosses to goth status. Simply apply this gloss over your lipsticks for a dark and sexy alternative. PUNKER is also perfect for a more casual approach to black lipstick. Just apply this sheer gloss to your lips for a softer alternative to solid black lips. No matter how you choose to wear this fabulous creation from Ardency Inn, I promise you will love it!

## 6. Eyeko London - Mascara in Black Magic

![Eyeko London - Mascara in Black Magic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/08/6_eyeko-london-mascara-in-black-magic_449x745.jpg)

Eyeko has designed a perfect mascara for ladies who want to pull off the goth look. This black mascara will give you drama, volume, and curl. The name, Black Magic, is so fitting as well, don't you think? The curvaceous helix brush lifts your lashes to a 90 degree curl and coats each lash evenly with a jet-black color for full lash definition. You'll love this mascara because it is enriched with keratin in shea butter for long-term lash care and superior staying-power. Eyeko formulated this mascara to be free of parabens, sulfates, phthalates, and is water resistant, smudge and fade proof, and it won't crumble or fade. There is so much to love about this amazing mascara!

## 7. Tarte - Cheek Stain in Blushing Bride

![Tarte - Cheek Stain in Blushing Bride](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/08/7_tarte-cheek-stain-in-blushing-bride_249x230.jpg)

Blushing Bride is a sheer deep plum cheek stain that smells like its description. What's great about the cheek stain is that it's a natural-looking gel blush that gives you a natural flush. Tarte formulated this product to be alcohol-free so your skin won't dry out. They also made it free from oils so it won't clog your pores. The best part about it, though, is that it contains a T5 Super Fruit Complex, which is a proprietary blend of the five most active super fruits. It contains pure water to help your skin retain moisture and uses mineral-derived pigments which are known for their soothing and softening properties. The purple tint to this gel blush is perfect to really pump up your gothic makeup look.

You can use all of these products at once for a goth makeup look that's bold and daring or you can use just a couple of them to make a subtler gothic statement. There are many variations to the gothic beauty trend, the choice is yours how you wear it.
