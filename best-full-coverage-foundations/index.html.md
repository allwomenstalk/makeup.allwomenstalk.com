# 7 Best Full\-coverage Foundations to Have You Looking Flawless ...

Looking for the **best full coverage foundations**? It's true - not all of us are blessed with perfect skin. Whether you're prone to dark circles, redness, or the odd zit, you need a great foundation that will give you a flawless complexion. So, here are the **best full coverage foundations**.

## 1. Lancôme Teint Idole Ultra Long Wear Foundation

![product, product, cosmetics, liquid, health & beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/et/zl/xxon3frp5a8491256764d373522164_600x600.jpg)

[sephora.com](https://www.sephora.com/product/teint-idole-ultra-24h-long-wear-foundation-P308201)

Lancôme's Teint Idole is a smooth, buildable medium-to-full coverage foundation. It's great for oily and acne-prone ladies, especially when paired with the right primer. It lasts, too - for up to 24 hours. Teint Idole should be applied quickly, as it can dry and get tacky, leaving streaks. This is one of the **best full coverage foundations**.

## 2. Amazonian Clay 12-Hour Full Coverage Foundation SPF 15

![product, product,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l1/vx/rmgsyqox5a84916255d3a040774395_450x450.jpg)

[sephora.com](https://www.sephora.com/product/amazonian-clay-12-hour-full-coverage-foundation-spf-15-P302918)

Tarte's Amazonian Clay foundation is pretty unique. It's got a spongy, almost foam-like consistency, and is best applied with Tarte's own Bamboo Foundation Brush (https://www.sephora.com/product/airbrush-finish-bamboo-foundation-brush-P302919). When buffed or stippled on, this foundation creates a glowing, airbrushed effect that's nevertheless full coverage. It's great for oily and acne-prone skin and gets rave reviews from ladies with warm and olive skin tones. However, it might cling to any rough, dry patches you may have, emphasizing them. As always, exfoliation is key.

## 3. Maybelline Fit Me Matte + Poreless Foundation

![product, product, skin care, cosmetics, cream,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mh/bw/uoyhuz5j5a8491def32d1437319004_600x600.jpg)

[maybelline.com](https://www.maybelline.com/face-makeup/foundation-makeup/fit-me-matte-poreless-foundation/natural-tan)

This foundation provides medium-to-full coverage that's semi-buildable. While it won't cover absolutely everything, it's airy, breathable, and looks natural on the skin. Use a good concealer on any dark or red spots. Maybelline's Fit Me comes in a wide array of colors, which are helpfully labeled warm, neutral, or cool. It may cake up or look greasy on very oily skin, though, so be sure to use primer.

## 4. MAC Pro Longwear Foundation

![product, cosmetics, product, liquid, health & beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bl/g0/cy8rtpm65a84923d51299923384777_587x900.jpg)

[maccosmetics.com](https://www.maccosmetics.com/product/13847/10180/products/makeup/face/foundation/pro-longwear-foundation#/shade/NC20)

When it comes to full coverage, MAC is king. This creamy, blendable foundation will have you looking like an Instagram model for up to 15 hours. You might want to use a light hand, though - some ladies find MAC Pro Longwear to be cakey, or too pigmented. Also, be sure to thoroughly wash and exfoliate your face, as MAC can be a little clingy. Don't forget the sides of your nose!

## 5. Estee Lauder Double Wear Stay-in-Place Makeup

![product, product, liquid, cosmetics, health & beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i5/s0/ixleujwj5a84928b1e7d6458316480_600x600.jpg)

[sephora.com](https://www.sephora.com/product/double-wear-stay-in-place-makeup-P378284?skuId=1465327)

Somehow, this foundation manages to be dewy, fresh, and full coverage. Estee Lauder's Double Wear foundation lasts for up to 24 hours, although oily-skinned ladies may need a touch up now and then. A little goes a long way, too. What more could you ask for?

## 6. Clinique Superbalanced Makeup

![product, product, cosmetics, liquid, health & beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ru/pr/y0jbn7j25a8492db59e03907771073_400x400.jpg)

[sephora.com](https://www.sephora.com/product/superbalanced-makeup-P122740)

Clinique's Superbalanced foundation is also dewy, fresh, and wonderfully moisturizing. It doesn't provide as much coverage, though, so you'll need a good concealer for the tough spots. It's oil-free, allergy tested, and non-comedogenic, which makes it perfect for ladies with acne-prone skin. Clinique's foundations are often runny, so be careful when applying.

## 7. Rimmel Stay Matte Liquid Mousse Foundation

![skin care, product, product, cream, lotion,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yz/r3/mz773wao5a84930145a62177833381_450x450.jpg)

[rimmellondon.com](https://www.rimmellondon.com/en_us/products/face/stay-matte-liquid-mousse-foundation)

Rimmel's Stay Matte foundation is high coverage, long-lasting, and oil-free, with a lovely foamy texture. It's buildable but requires a fair amount of blending to achieve that flawless, crease-free look. Sadly, Rimmel's color selection is limited. You may need to mix shades (or dilute a darker shade with moisturizer) to get your perfect match.
