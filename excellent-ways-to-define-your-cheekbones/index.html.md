# Tricks to Define Your Cheekbones ...

There are tons of ways to define cheekbones out there, but do you know how? Personally, it took me forever to find the right way to define cheekbones for me! So ladies, with all of my experience, I've come up with the top 7 ways to define cheekbones that will work for any type of face!

## 1. Use Light Tones to Highlight

One of the best ways to define cheekbones that I've found is using light tones to highlight your cheeks. You can actually use light blush, light foundation (which we'll explore next!) and even use some lighter colors to really highlight those cheekbones!

## 2. Lighter Shade of Foundation

Using a lighter foundation is a great tool to really ensure that your cheekbones stand out and look beautiful! Truthfully, this is one of my secrets and one of the surefire ways to define cheekbones that I use all of the time! Try it and let me know how it works for you!

## 3. Contour

Contouring is another key thing that you've got to do if you want to really learn how to define cheekbones! Contouring might seem really difficult in the beginning, but it's easy ladies! You just have to make sure that you look up the right tutorial and you should have no problems contouring at all and really highlighting your cheekbones!

## 4. Blush

Oh, how I love blush! Whether you are using pink or coral blush, blush is absolutely another way to define cheekbones that a lot of people don't use! Just a little, tiny bit of color to the apples of your cheeks can go a really, really long way ladies!

## 5. Bronzer

Bronzer is another surefire way that you'll be able to define your cheekbones! Just a little bronzer along your cheeks and also along your nose, forehead and chin and you'll be great! It'll make your entire look appear a little warmer and make your cheekbones stand out!

## 6. Powder

You can use powder too. Powder is something that I've used for years to highlight my cheekbones and it's worked wonders! Just a little powder up and down your cheekbones to really highlight them and you'll have celebrity cheekbones in no time!

## 7. Suck in Your Cheeks

Finally, whenever you are putting blush on or even contouring, suck in your cheeks! That way you can really see the line of where your cheeks are and what you should be highlighting and defining!

There you have it ladies, my top 7 ways to define cheekbones! Do you have any other ways to define cheekbones that may have worked for you?
