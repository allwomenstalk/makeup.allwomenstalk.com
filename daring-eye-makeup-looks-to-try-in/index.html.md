# 21 Daring Eye Makeup Looks to Try in 2015 ...

[giphy.com](https://giphy.com/embed/O9eVfjdr6vFiU)
Another year means another change in styles! You can redecorate your house, revamp your wardrobe, and redye your hair, so why not switch up your eye makeup? Although, I wouldn't recommend wearing most of these to the office, they're fun to try for a girls' night out, or a girls' night in! Check out these daring [eye makeup looks](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/classic-eye-makeup-looks-that-never-fail-to-impress/) and see if you'd be willing to try any of them!

## 1. Upside down Cat Eye

![Upside down Cat Eye](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qk/ba/l63fibpa_422x384.jpg)

Via [FLIP IT!](http://thebeautydepartment.com/2014/04/flip-it/)
We all love and adore a classic cat eye, but who's to say you shouldn't try this upside down cat eye? To me, this daring eye makeup style is exotic and beautiful!

## 2. Chrome Yellow

![Chrome Yellow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zg/jt/qbze2sxl_387x555.jpg)

Via [CHICSTUDIOS NYC](http://www.chicstudiosnyc.com/)
For women who can pull it off, a bold, bright liner might be the way to go! You can even match your lips for an overall high fashion, captivating look!

## 3. Bow

![Bow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mt/cy/dh733i54_600x800.jpg)

Via [Bow Eyeliner](https://goo.gl/gZabZt)
You'll need to have an incredibly steady hand to make this style possible! If you canmake it happen though, it will look incredible!

## 4. Emerald City

![Emerald City](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yg/he/c02xk5w0_600x902.jpg)

Via [New Work](https://www.beautylish.com:443/f/gzsiqr/new-work)
Emerald is one of my favorite colors, but I never thought to use it as an eye shadow! I think this look is especially gorgeous on red heads, but anyone who thinks they can pull it off should give it a go!

## 5. Wild Eyes

![Wild Eyes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gs/ou/f8ggwehk_450x450.jpg)

Via [Project Wedding Photos - Project ...](https://www.weddingwire.com/projectwedding-is-now-weddingwire)
This is another one that, like the bow, not everyone can achieve. However, if you can recreate this look, all eyes will be on yours wherever you go!

## 6. Rainbow Eyeliner

![Rainbow Eyeliner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eb/zh/ef20avw8_400x343.jpg)

Via [Cool Makeup: Eye, Lip, and ...](https://goo.gl/igQ4rf)
This whimsical, fun style is certainly daring and adventurous! This look is sure to make your eyes pop, without looking too crazy!

## 7. Mod

![Mod](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p2/c6/ukrli54l_393x544.jpg)

Via [World of makeup.](https://aqfer.lijit.com/1/d/r?aqet=clk&v0=195e35e893abffd6abfcb21286d52266&ru=http%3A%2F%2Flover-of-makeup.tumblr.com%2Fpost%2F14302403877)
I'm a big fan of mod makeup, so perhaps I'm a little bit biased. But you can't deny that this artful eye makeup is both daring and gorgeous!

## 8. Poison Apple

![Poison Apple](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/up/wk/me2zsgim_426x608.jpg)

Via [Make-up Artist Me!: Daring! Red ...](http://makeupartistme.blogspot.com/2013/10/daring-red-eyeshadow-makeup-tutorial.html)
Red hair, red lips, and red eye shadow! Red is such an intensely beautiful color that you can easily pull it off on your eyes, but few people dare to. It's a new year though, time to break boundaries and try something new!

## 9. Sparkles

_ Via Hairstyles, Makeup and Cosmetics Inspiration ..._
This eye makeup is pretty all on its own, but the stick-on sparkles really make it unique and youthful!

## 10. Steam Punk

![Steam Punk](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qk/ph/guaqg8dt_467x700.jpg)

Via [all things makeup&hearts;](https://all-things-makeup.tumblr.com/page/141)
Steam punk fashion is intriguing and beautiful! This steam punk eye makeup is unlike anything I've ever seen, so if your eyelashes are strong enough to hold feathers and sparkles, I recommend giving it a chance!

## 11. Purple Ombre

![Purple Ombre](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yc/tf/uck06hgb_480x521.jpg)

Via [Find your inspiration. Learn it. ...](https://www.preen.me/looks/)
Ombre is all the rage when it comes to hair, but why not try it for eyes? This purple ombre is feminine and sweet!

## 12. Candy Cane

![Candy Cane](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p5/a1/kwduwnjp_490x470.jpg)

Via [Christmas Eye designs](https://mamaslatinas.com/group/4/forums/read/30338/Christmas_Eye_designs)
Sure the holidays are passed, but candy canes are great all year round! This look is great for when you want to try something new and festive-even if it's not festive for this season.

## 13. Paint with All the Colors of the Wind

![Paint with All the Colors of the Wind](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ni/th/bsek07ps_500x500.jpg)

These deep, gorgeous tones will look fabulous on anyone, but especially women with darker skin! If done wrong, you could look like you're a circus performer. But if you do it well, wear it confidently, and pull it off-absolutely stunning!

## 14. Oh My, Orchid!

_ Via OCC Lip Tar - Matte_
Orchid is a hot color for 2015! But why should you only wear this trending shade? Orchidify your life with some bright, orchid eyeshadow and even lipstick!

## 15. Sunset

![Sunset](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wx/mm/myqvkyg1_420x655.jpg)

Via [The secret of controlling your ...](https://www.dailymail.co.uk/sciencetech/article-2112709/Forget-meditation--using-wrong-hand-stir-tea-helps-train-self-control.html)
This look combines the upside down cat eye with an ombre eye shadow, creating a stunning look that resembles a sunset!

## 16. Into the Sea

![Into the Sea](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zl/yh/aeyi9a5w_415x415.jpg)

Something about this striking look reminds me of The Little Mermaid! Combining blue and [green eye makeup](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/awesome-makeup-tips-for-green-eyes/) can look positively fabulous when you do it right!

## 17. Black as Night

![Black as Night](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ss/mq/htmfiogp_365x256.jpg)

Via [An item from Weheartit.com](http://www.fashiolista.com/item/3880887/)
Not only does the black lipstick make this look intriguing and high fashion, the intricate eyeliner also takes it to another level!

## 18. White Lashes

![White Lashes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mg/ff/rqha0knn_600x376.jpg)

Via [Moon, Stars, Holy Mary, Florals ...](http://thecherryblossomgirl.com/moon-stars-holy-mary-florals-and-mermaids/19937/)
Blonde women often cover up their gorgeous lashes with black mascara, but I've never understood it!, because I find white lashes are gorgeous! To be daring this year, you can wear white mascara and take the world by storm!

## 19. Shimmer

_ Via popularimg.com_
Our lives could always use a little bit more shimmer! Break out the sparkly eyeshadow and add a dab here and there, with an edgy cat eye, and you'll create a gorgeous look!

## 20. Electric Eye

_ Via Top 10 Makeup Looks With ..._
Electric blue looks like it's going to be another big color this year. I love how the electric eye is both subtle and striking at the same time!

## 21. Ice Queen

_ Via 西安彩妆写真 - 白_Bai.com_
This icy cool makeup is fresh and fabulous! You'll freeze everyone in their tracks with this daring look!

These eye makeup looks certainly aren't ones you should wear every day; some are just for fun to experiment with. Which of these looks are you most likely to try?
