# 7 Best Budget\-friendly Makeup Palettes ...

If you love having a variety of beauty products at your fingertips, but hate the high price tag they usually have, you’ll love these budget friendly makeup palettes! Drugstore makeup doesn’t always have to be of sub-par quality, there are tons of beauty products that are highly pigmented, long-lasting and very cost efficient! Take a peek at some of these budget friendly makeup palettes that are cheap and chic!

## 1. E.l.f. Beauty Eye Book in Bright

![E.l.f. Beauty Eye Book in Bright](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/04/1_e-l-f-beauty-eye-book-in-bright_410x410.jpg)

e.l.f. is very well known for their budget friendly makeup palettes and beauty tools. This beauty book filled with gorgeous eye shadows allows you to create a variety of looks without having to carry around multiple containers, as well as an applicator and eye liner. This retails for about $5 and also has a how-to guide on where to apply the eye shadows on the inside cover!

## 2. Wet N Wild Nude Awakening Palette (Limited Edition)

![Wet N Wild Nude Awakening Palette (Limited Edition)](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/04/2_wet-n-wild-nude-awakening-palette-limited-edition_600x400.jpg)

I think you’ll find that the colors in this next makeup palette look quite familiar. The first thing I thought when I saw it was Urban Decay Naked Palette! Wet N Wild’s take on the uber popular eye shadow palette contains only eight colors but they’re bold, beautiful and affordable! If you’re a saver and not a spender, this is a great neutral colored palette priced at $4.99 to own!

## 3. IMAN Adorned Cosmetic Kit

![IMAN Adorned Cosmetic Kit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/04/3_iman-adorned-cosmetic-kit_401x224.jpg)

Who says drugstore makeup comes in cheapie-looking packaging?! IMAN’s Adorned makeup palette has your eyes, cheeks and lips covered in a super cute container! This palette is perfect to throw in your bag for touch-ups or when you’re traveling. It has bronzer, lip gloss and eye shadow so you’ll never be caught without your makeup must-haves! Available online at Target.com for $10.

## 4. Sonia Kashuk Eyeliner Palette in Lay It One the Line

![Sonia Kashuk Eyeliner Palette in Lay It One the Line](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/04/4_sonia-kashuk-eyeliner-palette-in-lay-it-one-the-line_355x505.jpg)

We don’t see a lot of eyeliner palettes so I was really excited to see this makeup palette filled with amazing colors for the eyes! Sonia Kashuk’s website says that these colors are great for layering under shimmery eye shadow for more concentrated color. I just think it’s cool that you have a variety of colors at your fingertips to match your mood! Find this at Target for $13.29.

## 5. NYX Luscious Lip Gloss Pallet in Red Rediscovered

![NYX Luscious Lip Gloss Pallet in Red Rediscovered](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/04/5_nyx-luscious-lip-gloss-pallet-in-red-rediscovered_274x190.jpg)

NYX isn’t exactly a drugstore makeup brand, but luckily they offer some affordable makeup! This lip gloss palette contains five different shades of red lip gloss and it's small enough to throw in your clutch for a night on the town. You can use the glosses as by themselves or blend them together for a custom color! Find this at Ulta or Drugstore.com for around $8.

## 6. Pacifica Radiant Shimmer Coconut Multiples

![Pacifica Radiant Shimmer Coconut Multiples](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/04/6_pacifica-radiant-shimmer-coconut-multiples_401x353.jpg)

I think the first thing that caught my eye about this makeup palette, aside from the incredible colors, was that they’re coconut infused! I’m crazy for anything coconut so I was instantly intrigued! This trio might not look like much but it can be used on the cheeks, eyes and lips. Use it to add shimmer, highlight or contour! You can find this at Target for $11.99.

## 7. Profusion Spotlight Lip/Eye Palette

![Profusion Spotlight Lip/Eye Palette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/makeup/2013/04/7_profusion-spotlight-lip-eye-palette_464x344.jpg)

I’ve never heard of Profusion Cosmetics before but this company has one of the cutest and cheapest lip and eye palettes around! You can find this particular makeup palette at Ulta for $3.99, but the brand is also available at other drugstores like Walgreens. This small palette is packed with six shades of lip color and six shades of eye color so you’ll never be caught with boring makeup!

Drugstore makeup and lower priced makeup in general sometimes get a bad rap but I personally love them. I think you can find good and bad products at every price point but it’s especially nice when you find your perfect shade at a very low price! What’s your favorite drugstore beauty product?
