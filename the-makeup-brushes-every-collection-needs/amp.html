9 Makeup Brushes Every Collection Needs to Go from Humdrum to Hot Damn ...

Lay the Base • Complexion Perfection • Define • The Eye(shadows) Have It • Line It up • More ...

By Kristina

One of the keys to flawless makeup is using the right brush and these are the makeup brushes every collection needs. Having the right brushes makes it easy to put on your various cosmetics for the exact look you want every single day. Whether you're new to makeup or an old pro, these are the makeup brushes every collection needs.

1. Lay the Base

avon.com
A foundation brush is best to use for smoothing liquid foundation onto your skin evenly and is one of the makeup brushes every collection needs.

2. Complexion Perfection

avon.com
A concealer brush is perfect for concealing blemishes and circles under your eyes.

3. Define

avon.com
A contour brush is perfect for contouring.

4. The Eye(shadows) Have It

avon.com
Contour your eyeshadows with a crease brush.
avon.com
Get the perfect application and blend for your powder eyeshadows with an eyeshadow brush.
avon.com
Get a sultry look by smoothing on your cream eyeshadow with a cream eyeshadow brush.

5. Line It up

avon.com
Control the flow and application of your eyeliner with a dual-ended eyeliner brush. This gives you the option of a precision look or a smoky look.

6. Bow to the Brows

avon.com
Get the perfect arch with an eyebrow brush.

7. Get Cheeky

avon.com
A full-sized blush brush is perfect for perfectly applying and blending your blush.

8. Sizzle with a Sun-kissed Look

avon.com
A bronzer brush that is flat and flared is perfect for adding a natural-looking glow to your skin with bronzer.

9. Shine on (without the Shine)

avon.com
A powder brush is perfect for all-over the face application of powder, bronzer, or highlighter – perfect for a natural, light application of loose or pressed powders.
