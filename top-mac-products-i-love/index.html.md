# Top 10 MAC Products I Love ...

There are tons of top MAC products out there that I absolutely love! MAC is one of my favorite brands in the world and it's so hard to choose just the 10 top MAC products out there, but I've narrowed it down! Want to know my 10 top MAC products? Gotta take a look below, ladies!

## 1. MAC Fluidline Eye Liner Gel

I am so, so in love with this eyeliner. Not only does it go on so smooth, but the reason why this is one of my top MAC products is because it lasts! It'll stay on you no matter what!

## 2. MAC Prep + Prime Skin Base

Now, I just recently started to use this incredible prep and prime skin base and I gotta say, it makes all of the difference in the world! Nowadays, I am constantly looking for something that is going to make sure that my skin stays soft and my makeup stays on. That's exactly what this top MAC product does for me! It's seriously awesome ladies!

## 3. MAC Haute & Naughty Lash Mascara

I swear, I've tried almost every single mascara out there and this one? Oh man, it's awesome! Not only does it go on so smooth, but it really makes your eyelashes look really, really long and beautiful! It also stays on!

## 4. MAC Sheertone Shimmer Blush

Another top MAC product that I love is the blush. Every blush that MAC has is not only awesome looking, but the colors are pretty beautiful too. They truly have a color for every skin type!

## 5. MAC Matte Lipstick

Next up ladies is the matte lipstick that MAC has. Personally, I love the 'Please Me' color, but really, any MAC lipstick that you get is going to look beautiful. What's your favorite MAC lipstick?

## 6. MAC Studio Fix Fluid Foundation

If you are looking for a foundation that is not only beautiful on, but a foundation that lasts for a while as well. This foundation ladies is one of my favorite MAC products for a reason! Give it a try, it totally makes a huge difference!

## 7. MAC Viva Glam Gaga Lipglass

I love how MAC is constantly coming up with unique and different collections and the collection inspired by Lady Gaga is no different! This awesome lipglass is beautiful on and it is super shiny! Trust me ladies, you'll want to try this one out!

## 8. MAC Fix+ Skin Refresher / Finishing Mist

Let's say that you've been working all day and now you have a date. Just a little spritz of this skin refresher will have your skin feeling completely rejuvenated! I use this all the time!

## 9. MAC Eye Shadow 6 Color Palette

I think that eyeshadow palettes are some of the best things out there. Not only do you get a ton of different colors, but you also get a lot of different looks that you can create. Take this awesome color palette! This is MAC's Devoted Poppy and it's ideal for spring and summer!

## 10. MAC a Tartan Tale 5 Thrillseekers Pigments/Glitter Collection

Finally, if you truly want the best gift in the world and one of my favorite and top MAC products, you've got to try the Thrillseekers Pigments/Glitter collection! This awesome tin is filled with everything that you need to make all of the ideal looks!

MAC is definitely one of my favorite makeup brands in the world. These are just of a few of my top MAC products out there. I have a ton more! What are some of your top MAC products that you love? Share 'em!
