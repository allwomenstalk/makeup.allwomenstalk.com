# 7 Ways to Wear Statement Lipstick This Season ...

You can wear statement lipstick a number of ways this season. I have outlined a few of my favorite ways in this post. I am a big fan of statement lips and am glad they are a trend this year. 2014 is the year of trying new colors and experimenting with your beauty routine. Even if you aren’t sure about rocking statement lips this season, check out this post on different ways to wear statement lipstick and you may just change your mind!

## 1. With Matching Nails

![With Matching Nails](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/06/so/l4pdo4iq_442x625.jpg)

One of my personal favorite ways to wear statement lipstick is to match my lips to my fingernail polish. It is a simple way to get a polished and fashionable look. Coordinating your look in this way makes a great statement!

## 2. With Minimal Eye Makeup

![With Minimal Eye Makeup](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bu/zg/hrjel35f_500x750.jpg)

If you aren’t used to wearing a lot of makeup, wearing statement lips can be intimidating. Therefore, one of the best ways to wear this trend confidently is by toning down the rest of your makeup. This also works as a huge time saver! You can put on a little eye shadow or mascara depending on personal preference, but you can skip the eye makeup all together as well. Playing down your eyes will really play up your lips.

## 3. With Dramatic Eye Liner

![With Dramatic Eye Liner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bp/ps/d6b82npr_500x750.jpg)

Adding a little definition to your eyes can really make your statement lips look even better. Adding some dramatic black eye liner can really make your statement lips stand out. To channel a classic Hollywood vibe, paint your lips red and rock winged liner. This is one of my favorite ways to wear statement lips.

## 4. The Monochromatic Look

![The Monochromatic Look](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ku/qa/c5bbggkj_524x794.jpg)

Another fun way to wear statement lips this season is by doing a monochromatic look. Simply do your eyes, lips, and cheeks the same color. Although it’s a loud look, it’s a great way to let out your creative side. This season is all about experimenting with color and stepping out of your comfort zone. Would you ever try a monochromatic beauty look?

## 5. Matching Blush

![Matching Blush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nt/m3/xjkr0p0y_600x900.jpg)

If you don’t want to go all out with a monochromatic look, consider just matching your statement lips to your blush. This is a great spring and summer look that is bright and refreshing. This way to wear statement lips looks especially good with pinks and reds.

## 6. With a Smoky Eye

![With a Smoky Eye](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dh/o1/bnxq09cx_500x750.jpg)

A smoky eye goes with everything, including statement lips. This is a great look for a night out with your friends or on a date. It’s the perfect amount of sexiness, class, and glamour. Try this look out with some of the hottest [lipstick shades](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/gorgeous-lipstick-shades-for-pale-skin/) of the season: purple and orange. I have a feeling you will love the results!

## 7. With Matching Accessories

![With Matching Accessories](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/aw/kj/oy4dii6t_600x752.jpg)

Another way to add a little something special to your statement lips is by matching the color of your lipstick to your accessories. You can match your lips to your purse, heels, bracelets, head scarves, belts, and anything else you can think of! Matching your lipstick to your accessories is an easy way to achieve a well-put-together look. This is a great way to wear statement lips this season!

These are a few of my favorite fun ways to wear statement lipstick. Remember, there is no wrong way to wear makeup. Makeup is about self-expression and having fun! Share with us your favorite ways to rock bold lips so we can try out your ideas as well!
