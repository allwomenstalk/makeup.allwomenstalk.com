# 7 Tips to Keep Your Natural Beauty without Makeup ...

Learning how to keep natural beauty without any makeup at all is hard! I know that I wear my makeup all of the time – truthfully, I even wear it to bed because I forget to wash it off. If you are looking to ditch some makeup this summer and learn how to keep natural beauty, I've got the tips for you. So ladies, let's explore the top 7 tips on how to keep natural beauty without any makeup at all!

## 1. Drink Water

You probably already know this, but water is absolutely one of the best ways to keep your skin looking beautiful and hydrated. You should drink at least 8 cups of water a day, that way you won't have to worry about foundation, because your skin will already look beautiful! This is one of the most important tips on how to keep natural beauty without having to turn toward makeup!

## 2. Exfoliate Your Skin

Now that you've got your skin hydrated, you also want to make sure that you scrub out all of that oil too. The next tip on how to keep natural beauty is all about exfoliating your skin. This can be using a scrub that you have or simply using a exfoliating sponge. It's all up to you!

## 3. Apply Coconut Oil to Your Skin

Did you know that coconut oil is a great alternative to a lot of the creams that you are using? Trust me, this oil is great to soften up your skin and really make it stand out and glow. Try it ladies and let me know how it works!

## 4. Moisturize Your Skin Daily

Moisturizing is also super, super important when you are going all natural. After all, you want your skin to look it's best right? For me, I use all kinds of different moisturizers, but my favorite has to be from Lush, only because it is all natural.

## 5. Groom Your Eyebrows

Just because you are looking for ways on how to keep natural beauty without makeup, that doesn't mean you can forget about your brows! Trimming, waxing and making sure that you keep those brows in shape is a great way to keep your natural beauty and you don't need makeup for it!

## 6. Wear Colors to Highlight Your Eye Color

Just because you've decided to ditch the makeup, doesn't mean you can't highlight your eyes another way! Why not wear something that really sparks your eye color? I have green eyes, so something bright green will really bring out my eye color and make them stand out, all without makeup!

## 7. Chapstick is Your Friend

Finally, you want to keep your lips hydrated too, but you don't need lipstick for that! Just a swipe of the best chapstick out there and it will not only gloss up your lips, but it will keep them hydrated and kissably soft too!

See ladies, you don't need makeup to feel beautiful! These are just a few tips on how to keep natural beauty without any and all makeup. What other tips on how to keep natural beauty do you have? Share!
