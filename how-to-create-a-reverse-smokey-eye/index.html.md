# How to Create a Reverse Smokey Eye ...

The next big trend is to create reverse smokey eyes, which means that most of your eye makeup will be on your lower lash line instead of on the upper lash line. All you really have to do is alter your makeup application process a teeny tiny bit to pull off this look. If you want to learn this new technique, [Good Housekeeping](https://www.goodhousekeeping.com/beauty/makeup/g2772/reverse-smokey-eye-tutorial/) has instructions for you. Here they are:
