# Want a Smaller Nose? Bigger Breasts? Contouring Can Help!

Don't underestimate the magic of make-up. Why do you think that all the actresses and models you see on TV always look so gorgeous? It isn't due to their genes. It's due to the amount of make-up that they wear and how they apply it. So if you're unhappy with your looks, you don't have to think about getting surgery. All you have to do is figure out how to contour correctly.

## 1. Smaller Nose

Are you unhappy with the size of your nose? Well, this tutorial will show you the right way to use powder and cream to make your nose look smaller and slimmer. All you have to do is pay attention to the natural shadows on your face and blend, blend, blend! It's not nearly as difficult as it sounds, so pay close attention to this tutorial.

## 2. Bigger Breasts

You don't need a pushup bra to make your breasts look bigger. All you need is a few extra minutes in the morning to contour your breasts. Of course, you probably don't want to apply makeup to your body before you go to the beach in your new bikini. Save the contouring for days you're far away from water.

## 3. Slimmer Cheeks

Are you unhappy with your round face? Well, you don't have to lose weight in order to achieve the look you're hoping for. If you want your cheeks to look slimmer, all it takes is a little make-up to make you look like an entirely different person. So make sure you try to contour before you head to a plastic surgeon for a consultation.

## 4. Hide Hooded Eyes

Most women with hooded eyes aren't that happy about them. However, when you contour your eyes, you can make it look like your eyes aren't even hooded. By the time you apply the rest of your eye make-up, no one will know the difference between your eyes and anyone else's.

## 5. Disguise Bump on Nose

If you have a bump on your nose, have no fear. You can't make it disappear, but with the help of make-up, you can create the illusion that it doesn't exist. Of course, this tutorial shows you a bit more than how to cover up a bump. It also shows you how to make your nose look smaller. So if the previous video didn't help, maybe this one will help you get the idea.

## 6. Longer Neck and Collarbone

Most women don't pay much attention to their neck, but everyone is insecure about something. If your neck is the thing you hate the most about yourself, then you can contour your collarbone in order to make it pop out and to make your neck appear longer. If it makes you happy, go for it.

## 7. Defined Abs

This one sounds absolutely ridiculous, but hey, if you want your abs to really stand out, nothing is stopping you from applying make-up to them. Just don't expect to "create" abs when you have none. If you try to do that, you'll just look silly. You need to have the outline of actual abs in order for the effect to appear realistic.

There's nothing wrong with using make-up to create illusions. You're beautiful just the way you are, but if you need to contour to gain confidence, go for it, girl! Do you typically contour your face?
