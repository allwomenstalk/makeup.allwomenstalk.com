# Elaborate Makeup for when You Have Time on Your Hands ...

If you have a lot of time on your hands, you don't have to put on your makeup in a hurry. You can use an elaborate routine instead. Then you'll feel sexy for the rest of the day! The next time you really feel like making yourself up, you should try following one of these elaborate makeup tutorials for when you have plenty of time on your hands:

## 1. Bombshell Makeup Tutorial

This tutorial will make you look like a superstar. After you prime your eyes, you should apply a dark shadow on the bottom of your lid. Then it's time to add a lighter eyeshadow in your crease. After that, you can use a medium shade over your lid and up into your crease. Then you can take a brownish, reddish shadow and place it on the inner corner of your eye. And that's just the eye shadow! Then it's time to move on to the liner and lips.

## 2. Acne Coverup

If you've been struggling with acne, then you can always use makeup to cover it all up. There's nothing that a little bit of powder can't fix. That means you no longer have to feel self-conscious about your face, because all of those little spots that embarrass you will disappear.

Of course, you can always get creative and use whatever products you already own that are similar in style.
