# From Your Lattes to Your Face: Pumpkin Spice Makeup is Now a Thing!

It seems like the pumpkin spice flavor is synonymous with fall so what better way to celebrate the start of fall than with pumpkin spice themed beauty products?! Even if you’re one of those people who despises PSL and anything having to do with it, we still think you’ll be interested in some of these fall-themed products. I mean, fall makeup shades like browns and burnt oranges look good on just about everyone so there’s no reason you shouldn’t treat yourself to some new beauty booty! Right this way to a pumpkin spiced wonderland!

## 1. Pumpkin Latte Highlighter

![food, produce, dish, dessert, vegetable,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r0/vy/x2508zwq57ce2060c5303473172443_570x404.jpg)

Highlighters are everywhere and honestly, they are so much fun to use that you can’t blame a girl for being a little too enthusiastic about it! Etsy shop FeatherRiverBody sells this pumpkin hued highlighter that will transform your look. If you haven’t tried a colored highlighter yet, don’t let the orange sherbet-y color scare you away, it’s a very soft color that looks fab on lots of skin tones and best of all, it’s five whole dollars!
Available at [etsy.com](https://www.etsy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for $5.

## 2. Pumpkin Velvetine Liquid Lipstick

![lip, energy drink,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ao/lh/bjlpmmom57ce2061156a5233676580_600x741.jpg)

Fans of orange-y red will love, love, love this lip color. Liquid lipsticks are another big beauty trend this year and it doesn’t show any signs of going away. Grab this pumpkin liquid lipstick by Lime Crime and celebrate the fall season without going overboard on orange. Despite the way it looks in the tube, this lip gloss is the perfect combination of orange and red that’s prefect for casual get-togethers to more upscale looks, too.
Available at [dollskill.com](http://www.dollskill.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for $11.

## 3. Game Face Eye Shadow

![Colourpop, brown, powder, medicinal mushroom, produce,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x7/wl/es0xrtgz57ce2060ec23e583824656_600x505.jpg)

Copper and bronze eye shadows are a must-have for fall. They look good on so many skin tones and they look good with just about anything. It doesn’t get any more versatile than that! Colourpop’s Game Face Eye Shadow is incredibly pretty and looks like a liquid copper color on the lids. Shimmery and bold but subtle enough for everyday looks, football games, and fall festivals.
Available at [colourpop.com](https://colourpop.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for $5.

## 4. Sexy Plunge Nail Polish

![orange, nail care, product, peach, cosmetics,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y1/h1/uoj2qjk457ce2060bdf11805147929_500x351.jpg)

There’s no reason for your nails to not get in on the fun! Pumpkin or shimmery orange shades look festive and fun on the nails and there’s no better time to rock this hue than the fall don’t ya think?! Essie’s Sexy Plunge is a bright orange with shimmer so it’ll be a nice pop of color on those dreary fall days. If this color is too summer-y for you, Essie also has a burnt orange polish called Laying Koi which might be more your speed.
Available at [ulta.com](http://www.ulta.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for $4.49.

## 5. I'm Real anti-Aging Sheet Mask

![produce, food, meal, body wash, Anti,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k7/oc/y4cu6okh57ce2060d491d188770978_400x400.jpg)

If you’re not keen on pumpkin-colored cosmetics, try this anti-aging mask on for size. This sheet mask contains natural pumpkin extract that contains nutrients that are ideal for, you guessed it, anti-aging benefits. There are some mixed reviews as far as the fragrance goes, some think it smells nothing like pumpkin while some say it has a vanilla and spice scent. Either way, it’s a fairly inexpensive way to pamper your face.
Available at [ulta.com](http://www.ulta.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for $3.75.

## 6. Pumpkin Enzyme Mask

![skin, body wash, PETER, THOMASROTH, CLINICAL,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fb/ep/o649gyti57ce20628c462449622978_600x431.jpg)

For those looking for a higher end product, Peter Thomas Roth makes a pumpkin enzyme mask that’s said to exfoliate, peel, and polish in one convenient product. This mask is said to smell like pie and gets rid of dead skin cells like a dream. If exfoliation isn’t already part of your skin care routine, it’s time to get started! Exfoliating reveals new skin, can help clear up your skin, even out your skin tone, reduces the appearance of enlarged pores, and brightens skin.
Available at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for $58.

## 7. Pumpkin Cupcake Lotion

![food, body wash, PUMPKIN, CUPCAKE,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vr/h0/yr3lh3mm57ce2062936f5311687435_500x370.jpg)

And finally, for the ladies who love the smell of pumpkin we have Pumpkin Cupcake lotion. I mean, who doesn’t want to smell like a cupcake? Moisturize your parched hands with the heavenly fragrance of orange, apple, toasted pecan, vanilla, and cinnamon swirl. You’ll smell downright edible and you can the coordinating candle to really get in the fall spirit!
Available at [bathandbodyworks.com](http://www.bathandbodyworks.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for $13.
