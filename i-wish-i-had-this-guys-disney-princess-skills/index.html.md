# I Wish I Had This Guys Disney Princess Skills ...

Have you ever wondered how you'd look with Ariel's wide eyes and red hair, or with Snow White's signature coif and red lip? Disney fanatic and [cosplay](https://lifestyle.allwomenstalk.com/fun-reasons-to-cosplay/) king (princess?) Richard Schaefer can make it happen - he's worked magic with makeup and transformed himself into a few of our fave Disney Princesses. Jealous!
