# This is the Best Foundation Routine to Cover Acne Scars ...

My teen years were not pretty to say the least. I'm 30 now and I still get a couple of pimples every now and then. Acne is frustrating and it can totally destroy your self-confidence, right? But, thanks to Cassandra Bankson's amazing foundation routine video, there's hope. This video truly proves the power of makeup!

So, here are the steps once again.

## 1. A Little Something for Your Oily Spots

Cassandra uses the Smashbox Anti-shine to control the oil secretion on her nose and T-zone area.

## 2. Use a Primer

Primers are very important to get a clean makeup look. While she opts for the Hourglass Veil Primer, you can choose any primer that works for you.

## 3. Moving on to Foundation

You'll need something with really good coverage! Cassandra uses the Makeup For Ever HD Foundation in 125 and 120 and uses her fingers to blend it on her face.

## 4. Follow That up with a Concealer

Use a concealer like the Mac Studio Finish Concealer NC 30 mainly on your problem areas.

## 5. Set It with Powder

Cassandra uses a combination of a lighter powder like the Mac NC5 in the centre of her face and follows that up with the Smashbox Halo in Light on the outside of her face and finally tops it with the Makeup Forever Microfinish Setting Powder.

## 6. And Finally...

Spray your face with the Mac Fix Plus Spray and let it dry before moving on to the rest of your makeup.

And done!
