# Halloween Makeup Looks for Girls Who Want to Steal the Show ...

One of the best things about fall is Halloween. You get to dress up, go to parties, and eat tons of candy all in the name of the holiday. I really think Halloween should happen more than once a year, don’t you think? In any case, if you haven’t decided on a costume yet, don’t worry, we’ve gathered up lots of Halloween makeup inspo for you courtesy of the newest and first ever male Cover Girl, James Charles! As you’ll see below James Charles knows a thing or two when it comes to makeup and he’s provided us with 17 unique and breathtaking Halloween makeup looks. Take a look!

## 1. Marvel Pop Art

![face, clothing, nose, head, costume,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z9/jy/cvn3vixn580427eb2ebf5685466309_600x664.jpg)

Talk about pops of color! Pop art makeup is a fun way to dress up this Halloween and this look isn't too complicated for someone who's never tried pop art makeup before. Don't forget to outline all of your makeup with a black eye pencil!

## 2. Mad Hatter

![face, hair, clothing, nose, beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fv/cc/dnvikmcb580422c8a9064018051517_600x600.jpg)

We are loving this softer take on the Johnny Depp's Mad Hatter makeup! This look really focuses on the eyes and brows so make you sure pay close attention to those areas when doing your makeup.

## 3. The Butterfly Effect

![blue, head, art, illustration, fictional character,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uf/w9/wnwj5z71580427f5513c2346695052_600x600.jpg)

That highlight tho! Is this not the most gorgeous look?! Aside from the butterflies, the blue makeup on the face and decolletage is so ethereal and just so dang pretty!

## 4. Galaxy Makeup

![hair, eyebrow, face, nose, eyelash,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x7/mi/ibqj73vg5804280417640584918619_600x600.jpg)

I am really into all the details that went into this look! This is an eye-catching look that will get tons of attention. The purple smoky eye is to die for.

## 5. Ethereal Sin

![face, hair, nose, clothing, beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ow/t2/d0pkllut58042bbadfa05887016230_600x688.jpg)

Lots of makeup artists do skeleton makeup but what I like about this look is the use of purple instead of the traditional black and the use of glitter! It's a really creative and unique take on a classic Halloween look.

## 6. Two-Faced

![face, clothing, nose, cheek, beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m8/iw/vtc9098n58042bc823e8d506765640_600x739.jpg)

What's hiding behind that mask? It's cool but also a little sickening that this "mask" looks so real! This is another look that basically speaks for itself and the rest of your look can be kept pretty simple.

## 7. Trippy Rainbow

![face, clothing, nose, head, mouth,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xf/f3/u99fwbp858042bd23982d006217071_600x726.jpg)

Is this what unicorn tears look like? James went all out with this look and I can't stop staring! From the sparkly rainbow lips to the detailed drips, this is a truly unique look that you won't see on anyone else.

## 8. Queen Bee

![face, nose, beauty, eyelash, lip,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g2/dd/wgbz6qez58042bdc25c2a247497387_600x600.jpg)

You know what they say, you can catch more bees with honey than with vinegar... James kills it again with the honeycomb detail and that ferocious cat eye.

## 9. Zodiac

![eyebrow, hair, face, cheek, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c0/wg/u8qvqon458042be808edc972494564_600x600.jpg)

This look is a little deceiving at first because you see the bold lip and eyes and then bam! He hits you with the night sky and constellations. Imagine wearing a low cut dress with this makeup. Meow!

## 10. Under the Sea

![eyebrow, face, cheek, nose, forehead,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v8/py/vn99sesd58042bf5380fc479545104_600x600.jpg)

Whether you're planning on being a mermaid or maybe a creature from the sea, take a cue from James' amazing eye makeup topped off with silver foil and pearls. Amazing!

## 11. Lightning Strikes

![face, nose, beauty, head, glasses,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g5/h0/mp20ds3m58042c012d1c7978893926_600x600.jpg)

The storm's a brewin', Show everyone that you don't mess with Mother Nature with this crazy cool lightning storm makeup.

## 12. Skeletal

![face, hair, facial expression, nose, eyebrow,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t3/b8/icjz39wt58042c11ae076869488005_600x600.jpg)

For those who like to keep things on the simpler side, try James' take on a skeleton so you can wear your regular makeup and then get into character in a matter of seconds.

## 13. Splatter

![face, hair, nose, eyebrow, blue,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ct/dh/tiy7r84758042c2ceba56740944809_600x600.jpg)

You're a work of art so why not let your Halloween makeup/costume reflect that? The good thing about this makeup is that you really can't mess up. You want a really messy look so don't be afraid to get crazy!

## 14. Pixelated

![eyebrow, face, hair, cheek, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lj/c1/p74gm3ei58042c4fac63f088065985_600x600.jpg)

Simple but impactful pixelated lips FTW! If you want to celebrate Halloween but can't or don't want to go head-to-toe in a costume, opt for a subtle look like this.

## 15. Inspired by Butterflies

![eyebrow, face, nose, eyelash, eyelash extensions,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l1/k8/a5urshzt58042c57ca388363542649_480x480.jpg)

Contrasting eyeshadows add interest to the bold, black lips with pearls. Be sure to wear long, lush lashes that resemble butterfly wings if you try this out!

## 16. Water Goddess

![face, eyebrow, hair, cheek, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qo/q8/q2nc3nio58042c62a0c54897366033_600x600.jpg)

I think this makeup would even work for an ice princess. The white and blue remind me of the snow and looks oh so intense! Put on a white wig and you're ready to go!

## 17. Gore to the Core

![face, hair, black, clothing, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mw/c0/yax6abm458042c6e5bba0028316587_600x702.jpg)

.
If you're feeling a little more on the gory side, why not try experimenting with special fx makeup? With striking makeup like this, you really don't need to do much else costume-wise!
