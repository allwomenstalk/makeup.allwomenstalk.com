# 7 Best\-Ever Bronzers for Summer ...

You already know the dangers of too much sun, but if you want that sweet sun-kissed golden-goddess glow, you can get it, safely, with the right bronzer. Which bronzers can give you the best bang for your buck? Here are my picks.

## 1. Too Faced Sweethearts Bronzer

![Too Faced, heart, turquoise, locket, jewellery,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ho/w6/vru3eknr57683de4b3979918877435_600x414.jpg)

Price: $30 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Get a sweet, romantic, candle-lit glow with this two-color bronzer palette. The color is a perfect descriptor: it's called Sweet Tea.

## 2. BECCA Shimmering Skin Perfector

![face powder, powder, skin, cosmetics, cheek,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rd/lr/u8t6kq8657683de7d6191807208418_600x564.jpg)

Price: $38 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This bronzer comes in six shades, each one designed to enhance your own natural glow with a glorious matte finish.

## 3. KEVYN AUCOIN the Neo Bronzer

![television, lcd tv, television set, led backlit lcd display, flat panel display,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ik/mm/n7ws20ve57683deab3dd1092466508_600x298.jpg)

Price: $58 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Choose from Siena (warm coral) and Capri (cool pink), and bask in a glow that looks like it came straight from the beach. This palette combines blush, bronzer, and highlighter, so it's worth every penny.

## 4. NARS the Multiple

![NARS Cosmetics, lipstick, lip, cosmetics, skin,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kd/co/foadmstm57683de7b9985154544319_600x546.jpg)

Price: $10 at [amazon.com](https://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This handy bronzer is ready for travel, and it comes in 12 natural-looking shades. It's designed to be used as a bronzer, lip color, and even an eye shadow. Wow!

## 5. Benefit Cosmetics Hoola Matte Bronzer

![face powder, lip, skin, petal, cosmetics,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rm/b3/rmklbhlc57683de8138c9310278973_600x400.jpg)

Price: $29 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you've been dying to try the contouring trend, but aren't sure where to start, this matte bronzer is great for beginners (like me, I confess). Brush it across your chin, cheeks, and forehead (anywhere the sun would normally brighten) and bang, you're a contouring pro.

## 6. Urban Decay Naked Flushed

![wood stain, cheek, CAY, SHED,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ft/y1/wg7q6m3457683de79d6c8099963694_600x348.jpg)

Price: $32 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you're a fan of the Naked shadow palettes, you'll love this set of bronzers, blushes, and highlighters. Brush them on together for a sun-kissed look, or use them more carefully, each on their own, to contour.

## 7. Hourglass Ambient Lighting Bronzer

![eyebrow, powder, face powder, brown, leather,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ce/mw/wo6md4mw57683deb1a7fa177562428_600x530.jpg)

Price: $50 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Are you ready for your close-up? Of course you are! You swiped on this bronzer, made to provide golden-goddess color and depth, so you're totes camera-ready.
