# 11 Habits of Women with Phenomenal Eye\-Lashes ...

Your lashes are naturally beautiful. However, that doesn't mean you can't make them look even sexier than usual. If you follow [Cosmopolitan](https://www.cosmopolitan.com/style-beauty/beauty/news/a47654/things-women-with-great-lashes-do/)'s advice, your lashes will look better than they ever have before. So here are some habits of women with great eyelashes:

## 1. They Condition Their Lashes Nightly with Oils

![They Condition Their Lashes Nightly with Oils](https://img.allw.mn/content/xn/v6/ctvr388s561fa77312905832161921_500x281.gif)

Apply marula or castor oil to strengthen your lashes. It'll also hydrate them and make them last longer.

## 2. They Use a Lash Growth Serum

![They Use a Lash Growth Serum](https://img.allw.mn/content/y6/bn/qp1n2kd9561fa7732cf4b967656449_400x300.gif)

This can prevent brittleness and breakage. That means your lashes will last longer.

## 3. They Take Biotin, a Type of B Vitamin

![They Take Biotin, a Type of B Vitamin](https://img.allw.mn/content/ql/h0/gypcwfcy561fa77390aea241149277_500x266.gif)

This will make your hair, skin, and nails healthier. Just make sure you ask your doctor about it before you actually start taking it.

## 4. They Curl Their Lashes before Applying Mascara

![They Curl Their Lashes before Applying Mascara](https://img.allw.mn/content/bi/gr/cow3hr4r561fa82cce1c7673321949_500x281.gif)

If you apply your mascara first, then your wand could get stuck to the product and rip out your lashes. That's the last thing you want.

## 5. They Heat Their Eyelash Curler with a Blow-dryer

![They Heat Their Eyelash Curler with a Blow-dryer](https://img.allw.mn/content/o5/ak/rl8atd5s561fa77445d49860276334_576x324.gif)

This will help your lashes stay curled for a longer period of time. It's a fancy little trick.

## 6. They Only Pump Their Eyelash Curler Twice

![They Only Pump Their Eyelash Curler Twice](https://img.allw.mn/content/fa/q4/zelvw1ge561fa774b5d19891142837_480x273.gif)

If you do it more than that, it could make your eyelashes look crinkled. It could also make them fall out.

## 7. They Use a Moisturizing Mascara

![They Use a Moisturizing Mascara](https://img.allw.mn/content/jv/pl/zdkwqhi8561fa7744a593436475928_500x208.gif)

Keep an eye out for products containing panthenol and beeswax. It'll keep your lashes hydrated.

## 8. They Dust Baby Powder over Their Eyelashes

![They Dust Baby Powder over Their Eyelashes](https://img.allw.mn/content/hx/oq/ye3ug9lq561fa774923ba018151995_500x213.gif)

Do this between your first and second coats of mascara. It'll make your lashes look thicker.

## 9. They Remove Their Mascara Gently Using Light, Downward Motions

![They Remove Their Mascara Gently Using Light, Downward Motions](https://img.allw.mn/content/fr/t0/y22v291m561fa7796088c896663120_500x286.gif)

Don't move your cleansing wipe from side to side. Move it up and down.

## 10. They Change Their Mascara Every Three Months

![They Change Their Mascara Every Three Months](https://img.allw.mn/content/j8/xr/jocetkwy561fa77663052808960182_500x281.gif)

You don't want clumpy mascara. You don't want an eye infection either, so change your mascara four times a year.

## 11. They Don't Play with Their Lashes or Sleep on Their Face

![They Don't Play with Their Lashes or Sleep on Their Face](https://img.allw.mn/content/qy/a6/oahyeep8561fa7759653b195086082_500x271.gif)

This will cause your lashes to fall out faster. That's why you need to keep your hands away from your face.

Follow these tips and you'll always feel amazing! What do you do to your eyelashes to make them pop?
