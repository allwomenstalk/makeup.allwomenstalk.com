# Want a Perfect Pout to Brag about? Use These 7 Lovely Lippies for Spring ...

There’s something about spring that makes you crave a new lipstick… or three. These are some lovely lippies for you to try. You can find pretty lipsticks in many different brands. I have both high-end and drugstore brands and love them all.

## 1. Tarte Amazonian Lip Butter in Tulip

![Tarte Amazonian Lip Butter in Tulip](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vi/qh/vu3x2vnf552d4be38867d711206303_550x450.jpg)

You can’t get anymore spring-like than tulip, right? This lip product feels as wonderful on your lips as it looks. It’s hydrating and has a lovely little added pleasure by having a hint of mint. The mauvey-pink color of this lip butter definitely says spring. And I can’t neglect to mention that it’s packaged beautifully in a tube with sweet floral designs.

## 2. Cover Girl Colorlicious in Sweetheart Blush

![Cover Girl Colorlicious in Sweetheart Blush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qs/gc/ubh8gj3c552d4c203cfc7437069122_500x496.jpg)

What is it about spring that makes us want to change up our lipsticks? I’m not sure but this one is perfect for the season. The color in the tube looks like it has a hint of lilac but it goes on as a shimmery pink. This lipstick feels wonderful to apply which is probably because it contains shea butter. It promises to give you lasting color for all day wear.

## 3. NYX round Case Lipstick in Margarita

![NYX round Case Lipstick in Margarita](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ia/oz/kw9s8ma3552d4c9b48a89804902183_600x483.jpg)

If you can’t pass up a lipstick that’s a beautiful mixture of both pink and coral then you’ll have to have this one. It leans more toward the pink side but it definitely has some coral tones. What’s great about a lipstick like this one is that it’s versatile. You can wear it with practically anything and it looks lovely. NYX is a brand you can always count on to give you quality products without a scary price tag.

## 4. Revlon Lip Butter in Tutti Frutti

![Revlon Lip Butter in Tutti Frutti](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a9/yx/yuzrn6oe552d4d627eff9595090736_250x206.jpg)

I must confess that I have a slight obsession with lip butters so it’s not entirely surprising that this list includes two. Revlon lip butters are amazing, including this shade. Tutti Frutti is a coral with some strong hints of orange. It’s bold and bright and completely perfect for spring. And the combination of mango, shea and coconut butter delivers a smooth feel to your lips that you may become obsessed with, too.

## 5. Maybelline Rebel Bloom in Orchid Ecstasy

![Maybelline Rebel Bloom in Orchid Ecstasy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w3/sd/wiz1dgdu552d4f00761be051720467_550x505.jpg)

If you’re a girl that likes to live on the edge and tends toward vivid color then this one’s for you. When most people think of spring, they think of all the colors that seem to be everywhere. You can carry that out in this lipstick. It’s bold but still wearable. The reviews on Ulta give it a 4.6, showing that it’s loved by many.

## 6. Maybelline Baby Lips in Cherry Me

![Maybelline Baby Lips in Cherry Me](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oz/eh/dwte9hpi552d4f7e0cfcf297983907_308x254.jpg)

I actually just bought this one a few days ago and I love it. I worried that it would be too red on me but it actually tends more toward a bright pink. It’s a sheer lip product but you can layer it to give you a more solid color if you want. It feels lovely on and for $2.98 at my local Walmart, I’m a happy girl. It’s also the perfect product to toss in your bag for those busy days when you want just a hint of color.

## 7. Urban Decay Sheer Revolution Lipstick in Walk of Shame

![Urban Decay Sheer Revolution Lipstick in Walk of Shame](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gn/x1/obd9qnvh552d501267c31548910547_550x497.jpg)

Nudes are so very in right now. In fact, it seems that many of the celebrities I see in magazines are tending toward nude lips. This is a sheer, barely there color that’s perfect for spring. It’s a sweet neutral that has a high shine finish. It’s one that’s practically perfect for everyone.

These are 7 lovely lippies to try this spring. Now, I have to hear from you! What’s your favorite springtime lip product?
