Frequently Asked Questions

How can I make my makeup last all day?
Use a good primer before applying foundation and set your makeup with a setting spray to keep it in place longer.
What’s the best way to choose foundation shade?
Test foundation on your jawline in natural light to find a shade that blends seamlessly with your skin tone.
How do I prevent my makeup from looking cakey?
Apply thin layers of product and blend well; avoid using too much powder to keep your makeup fresh and natural.
What’s an easy tip for flawless skin makeup?
Moisturize well before makeup and use a lightweight foundation or tinted moisturizer for a smooth finish.
How can I make my eyeshadow pop?
Apply a primer or a light concealer on your lids before eyeshadow to enhance color vibrancy and longevity.
What’s the secret to perfect winged eyeliner?
Use small strokes and a steady hand, starting thin at the inner corner and gradually thickening towards the outer edge.
How do I get natural-looking false lashes?
Choose lashes that match your eye shape and apply them as close to your lash line as possible, blending with mascara.
Can I use makeup products after they expire?
It’s best not to; expired makeup can irritate your skin or cause infections, so check expiration dates regularly.
What’s a quick way to fix makeup mistakes?
Use a clean makeup sponge or a cotton swab dipped in micellar water for precise corrections without disturbing the rest.
How can I keep my lipstick from smudging?
Line your lips with a lip liner before applying lipstick and blot with tissue to set the color for longer wear.
