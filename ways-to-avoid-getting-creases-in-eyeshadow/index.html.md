# 7 Ways to Avoid Getting Creases in Eyeshadow ...

Learning how to avoid creases in eyeshadow makeup isn't easy! In fact, it took me years of dealing with creases in my eyeshadow to learn how to avoid it! So ladies, with that being said, let's take a look at the top 7 ways on how to avoid creases in eyeshadow, so that you can keep your makeup looking beautiful! So ladies, with that in mind, let's talk about how to avoid creases in eyeshadow!

## 1. Primer

Eyeshadow primer is honestly, one of the best things in the world. Not only does it help get rid of the eyeshadow creases that you can get, but it also helps keep your eyeshadow looking beautiful! My favorite eyeshadow primer? MAC! It works great, stays on and really makes a huge difference in how long my makeup lasts!

## 2. Use Powder Shadow

One of the biggest tips that I've learned on how to avoid creases in eyeshadow is to use powder eyeshadow. Not only does it go on super smooth, but it also looks incredible and stops the clumping up! Try this ladies, you'll see a huge difference in how your eyeshadow looks on your eyes!

## 3. Skip the Cream Shadow

No matter how much you want to try the latest cream shadow, let it rest and stick with the powder. Trust me, it'll completely get rid of the creases and look beautiful on. If you do want to wear cream shadow though, I've got some tips below to help you with that.

## 4. Use Loose Powder

How to avoid creases in eyeshadow isn't always easy, but using a bit of loose powder on your eyelids absolutely works! This can actually work on cream shadow or powder, but just use a little on your eyelids. This will keep your eyeshadow in tact and will absolutely make sure that the creases in your shadow don't happen.

## 5. Use a Shadow Brush

Using a shadow brush to put on your eyeshadow is actually a great way to ensure that you don't get creases in your eyeshadow. Just a quick brush of the color on and you'll see a huge difference than if you put the shadow on with your finger. It'll go on smoother and more even.

## 6. Concealer before Eyeshadow

Concealer is actually similar to using eyeshadow primer on your lids. Not only does it work to keep the creases away, but it'll also help ensure that the color and powder stays on your eyes. I use this trick all of the time!

## 7. Foundation before Eyeshadow

Similar to the concealer trick, foundation also one of the things that you can use to keep your eyeshadow in place. Just dab a little bit on your lid before you put on the rest of your makeup and you'll see just how long your eyeshadow lasts and just how it doesn't crease up!

Learning all of the ins and outs of how to avoid creases in eyeshadow isn't always easy, but it can be done! Personally, I hate creases, but there are tons of ways on how to avoid creases in eyeshadow in addition to the ones that I have listed here! So ladies, what other tips and tricks do you have for avoiding creases?
