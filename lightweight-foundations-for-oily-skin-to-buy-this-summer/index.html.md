# 10 Effective and Lightweight Foundations for Summer for Girls with Oily Skin ...

Summer time is right around the corner and us oily skin girls know it is a struggle to keep your face looking matte and oil-free throughout the day without slapping tons of product on!

Here are some great lightweight foundations for you oily skin gals that are a must have this summer!

## 1. L'oreal True Match Super Blendable Makeup - $9.99

[go.magik.ly](https://go.magik.ly:443/ml/rgm/)
This foundation is tried and true one for me. You cannot go wrong with the affordability. This foundation is an oldie but a goodie as it has been a must have drugstore foundation for years. It is very lightweight, but buildable, and great for oily skin. This combo is perfect for summertime!

## 2. Mac Studio Fix Powder plus Foundation - $27.00

[go.magik.ly](https://go.magik.ly:443/ml/rgo/)
This foundation is a powder formula, which may be off-putting to some. However, if you apply some concealer and a little bit of this powder with a large powder brush all over your face, you are good to go. It gives you the buildable coverage you may need to cover up redness or blemishes while keeping you matte all day!

## 3. Estee Lauder Double Wear Foundation - $38.00

[go.magik.ly](https://go.magik.ly:443/ml/rh5/)
This foundation is a fan favorite for oily skin girls. It is a great everyday foundation that does not look oily throughout the day and is lightweight!

## 4. Garnier BB Cream Skin Renew Miracle Skin Perfector - $12.99

[go.magik.ly](https://go.magik.ly:443/ml/rgp/)
A BB cream is a beauty balm so it is not meant to give you too much coverage. It protects your skin and this specific one is created for oily skin gals. This kind of formula is perfect for summer time and you really cannot beat this price!

## 5. IT Cosmetics Your Skin but Better CC Cream with SPF 50+ - $38.00

[go.magik.ly](https://go.magik.ly:443/ml/rgq/)
This CC cream may be on the pricier side but for great reason. A CC cream is a step up from a BB cream. It gives you skincare like benefits while the high SPF protects your skin from the sun and still creates a smooth canvas. This is absolutely perfection for oily skin. My mom is super oily and hates a cakey foundation. She swears by this product!

## 6. Revlon ColorStay Makeup for Combo/Oily Skin - $12.99

[go.magik.ly](https://go.magik.ly:443/ml/rgr/)
This foundation is another oldie but goodie. The packaging, however, is new as it now comes with a pump! The formula is created specifically for combo to oily skin. They have one that is intended for dry skin, so make sure to read the label! This is one I personally have been using for years and it is one of my drugstore favorites!

## 7. L'oreal Infallible Pro-Matte 24HR Foundation - $12.99

[go.magik.ly](https://go.magik.ly:443/ml/rgs/)
L'oreal recently came out with a matte foundation that has quickly become a sensation by beauty bloggers. This is personally my ultimate favorite drugstore matte foundation for oily skin and is just a perfect every day or even night time foundation to beat the summer heat. With a fairly affordable drugstore price tag, you just cannot go wrong with this one!

Not only does it even out your complexion, but it will also help soak up the oil so you don't even have to deal with it in the first place. You can use this foundation on a daily basis and you are going to love the results.

## 9. Maybelline New York Fit Me Matte plus Poreless Foundation Makeup - $5.69

![nail polish, skin, cosmetics, hand, eye,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nq/no/s3ojx7f558ff69dfa9e0a708797473_500x679.jpg)

Here's a totally affordable liquid foundation that doesn't skimp on benefits. You can find this one any drugstore and it is ideal for someone who suffers with oily skin. It's super lightweight so it won't clog your pores and evens out your complexion at the same time. If you don't have a ton of money to spend, but you want something that works, this is the foundation for you.
