# 7 Very Best Organic Makeup Brands for Girls Who Want to Go Natural ...

You're already buying organic veggies and milk, but are you ready to go all eco-chic and try organic makeup? There are so many fab [organic makeup brands](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/best-organic-makeup-brands/), with so many marvelous products, chances are, you're already using at least one of them. Curious? Let's have a peek at a few of the best organic makeup brands and see if your fave line is on the list.

## 1. Josie Maran

![hair,face,nose,black hair,blond,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pq/zk/tiqge_hair_face_nose_black-hair_blond_500x495.jpg)

If you're using an Argan oil product, you might already be a fan of Josie Maran. The entire line of lux products is organic and fair trade.

## 2. Alima Pure

![eyebrow,face,person,black hair,nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ww/w7/v8r96_eyebrow_face_person_black-hair_nose_500x500.jpg)

I love the backstory of this line: founder Kate O'Brien developed these makeup products (including an amazing set of shadows) so her teenage daughter would have an all-natural makeup bag so gorg, she'd never want to use anything else.

## 3. Bite Beauty

![eyebrow,hair,face,cheek,nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/le/xi/twsom_eyebrow_hair_face_cheek_nose_500x510.jpg)

I adore their lippies - seriously, half my hues are from Bite - but I feel no guilt buying another one, because they're organic.

## 4. Origins

![hair,human hair color,color,face,pink,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dl/m0/zwfa3_hair_human-hair-color_color_face_pink_600x567.jpg)

Their tagline is "Powered by Nature, Proven by science" and I'd say it's spot on. If you're not already using the GinZing line, give it a go... you'll love it!

## 5. Tarte

![eyebrow,face,hair,nose,cheek,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xa/pc/og9pk_eyebrow_face_hair_nose_cheek_600x600.jpg)

Not only is Tarte organic, it's also cruelty-free... bonus! Their shadows and bronzers are long-lasting and so rich. The secret? Amazonian clay!

## 6. Drunk Elephant

![eyebrow,hair,face,cheek,nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ji/dq/t6a2o_eyebrow_hair_face_cheek_nose_400x396.jpg)

Okay, they don't have a makeup line, but their entire skin care line (especially their sunscreen and night serum) is fantastic! Besides, can you think of a better company name?

## 7. RMS

![hair,clothing,face,bridal accessory,fashion accessory,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a1/lo/dg0i1_hair_clothing_face_bridal-accessory_fashion-accessory_600x600.jpg)

Two words: Living Luminizer. Seriously, if you've never tried this, do. As the name implies, it gives your skin a serious glow, and it's actually good for your skin!

Is the maker of your best red lippie on this list? Which other organic brands should I add to my list?
