# 8 Smooth Cream Blushes ...

Easy to apply, easy to blend and oh-so-natural-looking – smooth cream blushes probably aren’t the invention you’ll get to read about in history books so mentioning them now seems like a fair thing to do. Powder and cake blushes then to wear out but their creamy, long lasting versions won’t let you down! So the only thing left to do now is find the shade that fits you perfectly and, in order to help you do that, I’ve compiled a list of 8 fab smooth cream blushes that are going to help you look fresh all day and every day!

## 1. NARS Cream Blush

**Price:** $27.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Non-greasy and impossible to overdo- Well, that just seems like a dream come true although I know a few girls that are very capable of overdoing EVERY blush. LOL! I absolutely adore this sexy shade and oh, just think about how good it must look on bronzed skin!

## 2. Josie Maran Cream Blush

![Josie Maran Cream Blush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jq/gd/is8tv3vf54c92c8d4f11d927812904_250x250.jpg)

**Price:** $22.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Formulated without parabens, sulfates, synthetic fragrances and a whole bunch of other, harmful junk the makeup today is full of – this soft, creamy blush will not only wash your cheeks with that breezy, natural color but make sure they keep their healthy glow for years to come!

## 3. YSL Cream to Powder Blush

![YSL Cream to Powder Blush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rf/jw/x5hg9hnu54c92c8e0d3b7673105289_250x248.jpg)

**Price:** $38.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Meow! This elegantly packed cream to powder blush is available in this sexy, bright, kind of reddish coral shade only and I must say I’m now torn between NARS and YSL! I’ll definitely try it on too as I’m really anxious to find out which one of them will look better on me once I start working on my beach babe tan.

## 4. La Prairie Cellular Radiance Cream Blush

![La Prairie Cellular Radiance Cream Blush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vl/ht/lp2xwvme54c92c8ebfb81710382446_213x250.jpg)

**Price:** $70.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Next one on my list of smooth cream blushes is this gorgeous and quite pricy La Prairie find, so if you’re on a mission to find a perfectly natural shade to wear all year round, do take a look at these five right here. Ranging from sexy berry to neutral, soft rose- these smooth cream blushes might prove to be a good investment.

## 5. MAC Cremeblend Blush

![MAC Cremeblend Blush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q3/t2/ca70zr0154c92c8f8fa62747357029_230x249.jpg)

**Price:** $19.50 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
You don’t have to be a makeup artist to apply and get the most of this cool blush as it’s not only easy to blend but very light and long lasting too! Gorgeous choice of colors and they are not only extremely easy to work with but can be built up in case you want to go from subtle day makeup look to more dramatic evening look.

## 6. Giorgio Armani Blending Blush Duo

![Giorgio Armani Blending Blush Duo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gv/qy/lhfgdo3554c92c9054066011564615_192x238.jpg)

**Price:** $60.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you thought La Prairie smooth cream blushes aren’t worth all that cash, here’s one you’ll definitely love and use often. Why? Because this duo gives you a chance to whip up your perfect shade! Pure natural glow, sexy flushed cheeks or something in-between- the choice is all yours!

## 7. Stila Convertible Color Blush/Lipstick

**Price:** $25.00 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Awww… well this reminds me of childhood! Come on, admit you used to steal your mom’s red lipsticks and used them as a blush, too! Yes, I know - his multifunctional piece of makeup is more than just a toy but it sure brings back nice memories. I also know that finding a shade that looks good on both cheeks and lips might be a challenge but, with nine colors to choose from, it’s definitely worth the shot.

## 8. Laura Mercier Face & Cheeks Crème Color Palette

![Laura Mercier Face & Cheeks Crème Color Palette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bd/u8/qu3iqk4a54c92c91e0506267776156_250x248.jpg)

**Price:** $48.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I’m ending my list of 8 smooth cream blushes with this cool Laura Mercier palette that’s definitely going to wake up your inner makeup artist. It’s packed with everything you’ll ever need to give your cheeks and face a fabulous makeover and you’ll definitely have a lot of fun blending and mixing too. Isn’t that just perfect?

Eeny, meeny, miny, moe – which one of these smooth cream blushes would you go for?
