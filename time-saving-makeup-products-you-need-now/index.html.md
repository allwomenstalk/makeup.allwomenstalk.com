# 7 Time Saving Makeup Products You Need Now ...

Applying makeup takes time and that’s something a lot of us are short on. These are 7 products that can help you save time. Some of them offer the benefits of multiple products. Some of them are for multiple but separate purposes which is helpful, too. I hope that at least one of these becomes a time-saver for you.

## 1. Get Coverage Faster when You Use Cover Girl OUTLAST Stay Fabulous All-in-one Foundation

![Get Coverage Faster when You Use Cover Girl OUTLAST Stay Fabulous All-in-one Foundation](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rm/zi/yajq47nn55400c78318ea304403759_550x282.jpg)

Cover Girl’s Stay Fabulous Foundation is a product that offers you many benefits. In addition to being a foundation, it also has sunscreen, primer and concealer built in. So you’re essentially getting four products in one. The sunscreen is SPF 20 so it’s a decent coverage. Using Cover Girl Stay Fabulous Foundation not only saves you time but also money, since you have to buy far less products.

## 2. L’Oreal Color Lift Blush Can Give You Some Shimmer, Too

![L’Oreal Color Lift Blush Can Give You Some Shimmer, Too](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kt/l6/i7n81os355400d5aa432d213331627_236x225.jpg)

Girls, this is my new favorite blush. I purchased it about a month ago and I’m in love! It’s a cream to powder blush that goes on very easily. It has good staying power, too. And best of all, there’s no need to add a highlighter because of the light shimmer this product has.

## 3. Maybelline Lash Sensational Can save You from Having to Curl Your Lashes

![Maybelline Lash Sensational Can save You from Having to Curl Your Lashes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xs/bn/xixxd29055400d93f377e395166459_600x616.jpg)

This is my current favorite mascara. It has a curved brush and applies slowly but can really build up to a beautiful finish. The curved brush can be used to push your lashes up into a curl which can save you the time you usually spend curling them. Curved brushes always seem to assist in giving your lashes curl. If it isn’t enough curl, you can always go back to curling them before applying mascara. Either way, this mascara is one you don’t want to miss.

## 4. Nars the Multiple Stick Can Be Used in Multiple Ways

![Nars the Multiple Stick Can Be Used in Multiple Ways](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i8/qu/gvz341yj55400de1c4e4b911405333_525x484.jpg)

I haven’t used this product but it looks very interesting. It’s for your eyes, cheeks and lips. It’s said to be very easy to apply and has great reviews. Nars knows their stuff when it comes to makeup so I can’t imagine this being a disappointing product. Anything that offers you this many benefits is difficult to pass up.

## 5. There’s No Need for Eyelash Primer when You Use L’Oreal Butterfly Mascara

![There’s No Need for Eyelash Primer when You Use L’Oreal Butterfly Mascara](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fh/fb/o0tiu32055400e349a4f4235500534_400x402.jpg)

This is the other mascara I passionately love. I tend to go back and forth between this one and the Lash Sensational. If you’re a girl that’s hooked on eyelash primer, this mascara may break you of that. It makes your lashes look so long and full that there’s hardly a need for primer. Primer isn’t built in but it truly seems as if it is from the results that it gives you.

## 6. OLay CC Cream is a Great Makeup and Age Fighting Product

![OLay CC Cream is a Great Makeup and Age Fighting Product](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b7/a1/k5s1fd8n55400e9e933b0521904580_600x514.jpg)

This’s a good product when you want a little coverage for your face but you don’t want the full coverage of a foundation. It offers you the benefits of a moisturizer, sunscreen and sheer tint in one. It’s perfect for those days you don’t want to wear full makeup. It’s an age fighting product, too. It fights against dull skin tones, lines, wrinkles and more.

## 7. Mac’s Eyeshadow Brun Can Also Be Used to Fill in Your Brows

![Mac’s Eyeshadow Brun Can Also Be Used to Fill in Your Brows](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zk/la/t2xes1gf554010a4193a4478250944_600x518.jpg)

Are you looking for the perfect eyebrow product? Look no further. You may already have this product in your makeup bag for use as an eyeshadow. It’s also a perfect choice for filling in your brows. If Brun isn’t quite your shade, there’re many others you can choose from that can double as an eyeshadow and an eyebrow product.

These are some makeup products that can save you time and money, too. What’re your favorite time saving products? I’d love to hear about them.
