# 7 Best Liquid Eyeliners for a Fierce Cat Eye ...

If you’re one who loves to rock a fierce cat eye, then you know that only the best liquid eyeliners will do. Although I’m quite fond of gel and pencil liners, nothing says classic and chic quite like a liquid liner. If you’ve yet to find a good liquid eyeliner that is both easy to use and gives you the look you desire, I picked out seven of the most loved and highly rated liquid eyeliners that I think you’re going to adore. Don’t settle for just any old product, check out some of these best liquid eyeliners and make eye makeup a breeze!

## 1. Marc Jacobs Beauty Magic Marc’er Precision Pen

![Marc Jacobs Beauty Magic Marc’er Precision Pen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uj/0o/tn/hjqg7vv3_591x886.jpg)

I’m a firm believer that the best liquid eyeliners don’t have to cost an arm and a leg, but in the case of Marc Jacobs’ Magic Marc’er, this baby is everything! I typically don’t like eyeliners that have a felt tip. I usually have better luck with the flexible brushes however, this is very easy to use and is very pigmented so you get a very dark line with one stroke. You can also flawlessly draw thin or thick lines and the color lasts all day.

## 2. L'Oreal Paris Lineur Intense Brush Tip Liquid Eyeliner

![L'Oreal Paris Lineur Intense Brush Tip Liquid Eyeliner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eu/s9/rb/x34abvcf_300x400.jpg)

L’Oreal’s Lineur Intense liquid eyeliner is another favorite for budget and high-end beauty enthusiasts alike. This has a flexible brush that makes drawing lines very easy and precise. It’s also fairly long lasting although I’ve had instances where I laughed so much I cried and the end of my cat eye disappeared. But for regular days, this eyeliner is a fab budget buy.

## 3. Wet N Wild MegaLiner Liquid Eyeliner

![Wet N Wild MegaLiner Liquid Eyeliner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ob/jx/hp/qddw3lx3_499x429.jpg)

If you prefer your liquid eyeliner to have a thinner brush, you’ll love WNW’s MegaLiner. This eyeliner is a really sweet buy; you can usually find this for less than $5 and it lasts a long time. I like to keep this as a backup in my makeup bag in case I lose my main liquid liner. While I appreciate this super cheap eye liner that works just as good as some of the liners that are double the price, my only gripe is that the brush tends to fray.

## 4. KAT VON D Tattoo Liner

![KAT VON D Tattoo Liner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kk/mp/qe/yixj8860_449x438.jpg)

I’ve always heard nothing but fantastic things about Kat Von D’s makeup line so I wasn’t surprised to see that her Tattoo Liner is one of the best liquid eyeliners in stores today. The Tattoo Liner is said to have an innovative, flexible brush that makes lining eyes effortless. The formula is also resistant to running, fading, cracking and melting so you can feel confident knowing your eye makeup is on point.

## 5. LANCÔME ARTLINER Precision Point EyeLiner

![LANCÔME ARTLINER Precision Point EyeLiner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rs/l7/m4/lzhx2cnb_449x446.jpg)

Artliner is a super popular liquid liner so I wouldn’t be surprised if you were already using this product. Artliner was made to be easy-to-handle with rich color pigments and a flexible foam tip to seamlessly create beautiful lines. The formula is long-lasting, waterproof, smudgable if desired and easy-to-apply. This is another one of the pricier liquid eyeliners, but from all the rave reviews, it appears worth the price.

## 6. Milani Eye Tech Extreme Liquid Eye Liner

![Milani Eye Tech Extreme Liquid Eye Liner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lj/kz/ec/shkvx6e5_499x490.jpg)

Tons of users agree that Milani’s Eye Tech Extreme Liquid Eye Liner is the best eyeliner they’ve ever tried. Some of the features that make this eye liner stand out from the rest is that it’s waterproof, resists feathering, you get full color in one stroke without any tugging, is long-wearing and very easy to use. With an extremely fine tip, defining your eyes has never been easier.

## 7. STILA Stay All Day Waterproof Liquid Eye Liner

![STILA Stay All Day Waterproof Liquid Eye Liner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l9/ne/my/pgqjr5ad_449x447.jpg)

I’ve tried another one of Stila’s waterproof liquid eyeliners in the past and loved it, so this baby is definitely on my list to try next. Although some of the other eyeliners on this list offer a few other colors, Stila’s comes in 15 waterproof colors. The felt tip marker glides on easy and is recommended for even the liquid liner novice. Long-wearing, dramatic and precise, this eyeliner is a must-try!

What do you think of this list of the best liquid liners? Have you tried any of them before? We might all look for different things when it comes to our eye makeup, but all of these products are best-sellers that deliver. What’s your favorite liquid eye liner?
