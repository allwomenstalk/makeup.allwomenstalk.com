# Girl's Guide on How to Wear Makeup when You Have Acne Prone Skin ...

Are you trying to figure out **how to wear makeup when you have acne prone skin**? When you have acne, after the initial attempts to clear it up, next come the attempts to cover it up. In a cruel twist of irony, it can often be the case that the more makeup you put on your face, the more risk you run of causing breakouts if you are susceptible to them. Don’t worry though. Just because you are suffering from acne, it doesn’t mean that you have to sacrifice wearing makeup for it to clear up. If you know what to use and how to use it, then you can have a nice face of makeup whilst also giving your skin a good chance of recovering from breakouts. Here is **how to wear makeup when you have acne prone skin**.

## 1. Read Labels

The first thing to make sure you do when buying makeup is to thoroughly read all of the product labels. Look for words like non-comedogenic, which means that the product is less likely to clog your pores, and non-acnegenic, which, as you might be able to guess, means that it is less likely to cause breakouts associated with acne! You should always opt for oil-free formulas, even if they cost that little bit extra. And that's one of the best answers for **how to wear makeup when you have acne prone skin**.

## 2. Stick to Your Skin Type

If you have oily skin, then stick to products that are labelled as such. For example, if you pick up something that is designed for naturally dry skin, then the ingredients are going to send your face into overdrive and cause an unnecessary breakout. Products specifically targeted for oily or acne-prone complexions are your best friend.

## 3. Multi-Task

Treat your makeup look like a multi-tasking operation. Find products that you like the end result of, but also try to find ones that contain something called salicylic acid. This is an ingredient that helps to fight bacteria and clear the dirt and debris out of your pores. You can look good and do good for your skin at the same time!

## 4. Mineral Makeup

If you are greeted with a wide choice of products, then it is recommended to go for mineral makeup because it is purer and free from harmful preservatives that could cause a reaction on your skin. It won’t clog and will still provide great coverage.

## 5. Art of Distraction

Makeup is an amazing thing; it can really perform miracles and optical illusions when you know how to apply it properly! Look at the areas on your face where the acne is most present, and work to achieve the art of distraction by highlighting different areas. If your cheeks are bad, accentuate your lips and eyes!

## 6. Wash Your Face before Working out

Please, please, please don’t work out with a full face of makeup! The sweat combined with the makeup will only serve to clog your pores even more than they already are. Wash you face thoroughly before getting down to business, and your skin will most definitely thank you later!
