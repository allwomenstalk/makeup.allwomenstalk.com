# Awesome Places to Buy Makeup That Arent Sephora ...

When it comes to buying makeup, there has long been a name that has soared above the rest in terms of popularity, availability and general notoriety in pop culture, and that is Sephora. It’s a place where you can get pretty much whatever you are looking for, with a variety of choice along the way, but what if you want to go against the grain and try out something different? It might come as a shock, but Sephora isn’t the only place in the world for this kind of thing! Here are some great **places to buy makeup that aren’t Sephora**.

## 1. Target

Don’t be a makeup snob, Target have got some great products! Alongside most of the big name brands, they also have some more obscure ranges that give you a lot of choice. It’s the perfect one-stop shop for all of your basic beauty needs.

## 2. Amazon

Amazon maybe isn’t the first place you think of when considering where to buy makeup online, but if you know where to look they have some great deals and offers. Another plus is that you can sometimes get items that might not even be available in stores in your country, so it basically opens up the whole world of makeup to you wherever you are.

## 3. Nordstrom Rack

Don’t forget about Nordstrom! Their products are highly regarded for their superior quality, with high end brands like MAC and Clinique being heavily present. Nordstrom Rack, however, is an offset that gives you the big brands for better prices!

## 4. Urban Outfitters

It might be a stereotypical hipster hangout at this point, but Urban Outfitters can actually be really good for makeup. Recently, they have been particularly good at selling a huge range of products that are cruelty free and vegan, including the brushes, so that’s a great thing to know for people who are environmentally conscious.

## 5. Madewell

Madewell is another company where you know you are going to be getting the absolute best, but not always for a huge price. It might not be a place where you buy all of your essentials, but if you ever looking for a few special pieces to add to your collection, this is the place to look.

## 6. Hot Topic

I know, I know, you haven’t been to Hot Topic since your early teen Nightmare Before Christmas phase, but trust me, they do some great makeup products! Everything is super affordable, and most of it comes in really bold, stand out colours, so perfect for party looks!
