# Awesome Tips for How to Apply Matte Lipstick for a Perfect Finish ...

Looking for some **tips on how to apply matte lipstick**? When it comes to makeup, it would be fair to say that if you are going to get one thing absolutely on point and correct, it should be your lipstick! A great lip application can make a huge difference to an overall look and can give you a lot of confidence with regards to the rest of your aesthetic. Within the realm of lipstick, there are very different types to consider, and one of the absolute hardest lipsticks to apply with a professional touch is definitely matte! Here are some **tips on how to apply matte lipstick**.

## 1. Even Base

This is one of the most important **tips on how to apply matte lipstick**. You need to have a well primed, even base if you want to achieve any good lip look. This involves getting rid of any dryness or flakiness before applying any product because matte lipstick will only serve to dry your lips out even further. A good tip is to use a toothbrush to gently scrub away dead skin and debris, and then using a hydrating lip balm before getting to the lipstick.

## 2. Prime

If you have naturally dark lips but you want to rock a more candy coloured shade of matte, then you need to use a slightly creamy foundation or concealer to prime your lips before applying the colour. This will help it to really pop and retain its high pigmentation.

## 3. Blotting

You should aim to apply one initial layer of the matte lipstick and then blot straight away afterward. This will help to soak up any excess moisture and lock in that first layer, which will give you a strong and colourful base to add further layers to, depending on how vivid you want the final result to look.

## 4. Right Formula

You need to make sure that you are buying products that possess the right formula for the look you are trying to achieve. To achieve a matte look, you need to be avoiding any lipstick that has a high castor oil content, because that is used to uphold as much moisture and produce as much shine as possible.

## 5. Line Your Lips

You should always line your lips with a similar colour to the stick that you are using, just to give them that extra edge of definition and pop that any good matte lip needs to really stand out. Lining your lip can also make your pout look much fuller!

## 6. Clean up

Trying to achieve the perfect matte lip look can often lead to a few more smudges and smears than usual, so make sure that you take a cotton bud and some makeup remover and really work out all of the edges and surrounding lip areas until your mouth is looking fresh and fierce.
