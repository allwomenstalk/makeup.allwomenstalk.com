# 7 Awesome Make\-up Artists to the Stars ...

Wouldn’t it be cool to be one of the make-up artists to the stars? Not only would you get to rub shoulders with the most famous celebrities around, but you’d also get to try all the best products on the market. Not all makeup artists start out famous and well-known, and they certainly don’t all make it in the industry. But the ones who do often become very well-known and are sought after by all the big name Hollywood elite. Here are some of the biggest and baddest make-up artists to the stars.

## 1. Bobbi Brown

![Bobbi Brown](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ss/rk/l7ncmzlo_600x833.jpg)

Bobbi Brown got her start by working at a cosmetics store, but has become one of the most well known make-up artists to the stars around. She majored in theatrical make-up in college and now has her own line of make-up. She’s applied make-up to many big name celebrities and shares her advice in magazines all the time.

## 2. Sam Fine

![Sam Fine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ll/zq/ycxoz7rw_600x696.jpg)

This guy makes a boatload of money – to the tune of $3500 per day plus all expenses. He’s worked his magic with many celebrities, including Queen Latifah and Tyra Banks. As an African American man, he has a lot of experience and understanding of the cosmetic needs of women with dark skin. In addition to making the top names in the business look great, he’s penned a book about his work and is a spokesperson for Revlon.

## 3. Gucci Westman

![Gucci Westman](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nf/ob/mfd12klv_600x901.jpg)

In addition to training in Paris, Gucci Westman has plenty of real world experience in the realm of movie make-up. She’s made up the face of Natalie Portman, and many other famous stars. Her passion for cosmetics and her wealth of experience and knowledge makes Westman one of the most popular make-up artists to the stars.

## 4. Carmindy

![Carmindy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pi/yj/heitdqg9_332x500.jpg)

Anyone who has ever watched “What Not to Wear” on television knows who Carmindy is. She has made over hundreds of women on the show by giving them quick and easy tips for everyday make-up looks. She has also worked with tons of celebrities and has written books about her make-up expertise.

## 5. Tom Pecheux

![Tom Pecheux](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pp/nu/rmwysmtf_600x814.jpg)

Tom Pecheux started out wanting to be a famous chef, but changed careers while living in Paris. He’s the genius behind the 1997 Vanity Fair cover that featured the lovely Diana, Princess of Wales. He’s worked with L’Oreal, Shiseido and Estee Lauder. His work is often featured by Prada and Gucci and his work can be seen on celebrities all over the place.

## 6. Luis Casco

![Luis Casco](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w4/s5/yro9pp5l_600x974.jpg)

You’ve probably seen Luis Casco’s work on the faces of Heidi Klum and Charlize Theron. They both look stunning nearly all the time so you know he’s got to be good. In addition, Casco has spent plenty of time at photo shoots, catwalk shows and red carpet events.

## 7. Charlie Green

![Charlie Green](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k8/jt/vesq6oou_350x500.jpg)

For celebrities who want something new or unique, Charlie Green has the answer. She is known for bending the rules and not doing the same thing all the time. She has worked with loads of celebrities and companies, including Estee Lauder, Guess, Lancome, Maybelline, Katie Holmes, Paris Hilton and Shania Twain. That esteemed list speaks for itself. She’s got to be good!

Wouldn’t it be awesome to have a make-up artist to glam you up every day? I’d snatch some extra sleep while someone applied my cosmetics. Not only would I look great, but I wouldn’t have to do any of the work. Do you know of any other great make-up artists to the stars?
