# 25 Unexpected Shades of Lipstick ...

I love coming across really bold, unexpected shades of lipstick. I can't pull off all the ones I love, and they aren't all practical to try, but every once in a while I'll stumble upon a new style or idea that I know would be perfect for me. I always have to try it! If you're looking for some truly unexpected shades of lipstick, I've got you covered!

## 1. Jawbreaker Inspired

![Jawbreaker Inspired](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rp/on/k1at5tp7_600x600.jpg)

Via [Plethera Of Paints.](https://www.pinterest.com/pin/28429041369554828/)

I'm starting out with some unexpected shades of lipstick that really aren't practical for day to day life – or, rather, the design isn't really practical. But check out the green, the yellow, the violet – would you be willing to try one of those?

## 2. Pastel Perfection

![Pastel Perfection](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tz/k3/g3lkjglq_600x636.jpg)

Via [17 DIFFERENT LIPSTICK LIPS](https://www.fashiondivadesign.com/17-different-lipstick-lips/)

There are plenty of pastel shades around these days, but they're still new enough that they're a bit unexpected on the daily. Wouldn't you love to rock a mint, or a creamy light lilac?

## 3. Ombre Style

![Ombre Style](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yv/qq/q59d2z8f_400x796.jpg)

Via [ladypandacat.tumblr.com](https://thewolfjumpedoverthemoon.co.uk/post/3737227699)

Done subtly, this actually could be a day to day look, and there are even lipsticks to help you do it. For special occasions and fun parties, however, go bold – I love the pink and the galactic black, especially.

## 4. Silver and Gold

![Silver and Gold](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fw/js/pav38mm4_500x353.jpg)

Girl, your kisses have teeth! But seriously, this is ideal as club or concert makeup.

## 6. Gorgeous Gradient

![Gorgeous Gradient](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zo/nz/yeq1z78x_422x500.jpg)

Via [Lips](https://www.flickr.com/photos/florentijn/3287572946/in/photostream/)

## 7. Sunset

![Sunset](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t8/uf/va4w22fb_500x744.jpg)

Via [Makeup](https://www.pinterest.com/pin/427630927087287073/)

## 8. Black Lipstick

![Black Lipstick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nv/r8/t7p2qrw3_450x703.jpg)

Via [Fall 2013 Trend Alert: Black ...](https://goo.gl/he3hDK)

Maybe it's a relic from my goth days, but I still adore black lipstick. It looks gorgeous against so many skin tones, and it's so dramatic!
