# 7 Gorgeous Lipstick Colors for Fall 2014 ...

There are many gorgeous lipstick colors for fall 2014 to incorporate into your beauty routine. Branch out from the orange hues of the season to try something new and unexpected, yet still seasonal. The colors on this list are great choices for transitioning from summer into winter. These are my favorite lipstick colors for fall 2014.

## 1. Chocolate Brown

![Chocolate Brown](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pd/nt/uhkx0c5n_600x744.jpg)

One of my favorite lipstick colors for fall 2014 is chocolate brown. This dark shade is surprisingly versatile and looks good on almost everyone. It’s a great way to sport the colors of the season without going for a typical shade such as orange. Chocolate brown is my go-to shade this fall. My favorite brown lipstick is from Lime Crime’s Velvetines collection and the shade is called Salem. You can get it here: [limecrime.com](http://limecrime.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 2. Eggplant

![Eggplant](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q6/x1/x6hltyk1_585x880.jpg)

I personally believe purple is the most underrated lipstick shade. There is no better time to start wearing [purple lipstick](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/tips-on-how-to-wear-purple-lipstick-like-a-pro/) than the fall. For the cooler months, I prefer a darker hue, such as eggplant. If you’re worried about the intensity of the color but like the idea of wearing purple, lightly dab on the lipstick instead or opt for a stain to give your lips a wine-stained appearance. To try this look, I suggest using Bite Beauty’s Matte Crème Lip Crayon in Aubergine – Eggplant available at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 3. Nude

![Nude](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kd/v6/fbhtvmll_500x719.jpg)

Let’s be honest, a nude lip is always in season. Sometimes the best look is a natural one. I love to pair a nude lip with a smoky eye, but a nude color will obviously go with any look. Finding the right nude to match your skin tone can be quite the challenge, but it’s worth the hunt! I like the nude options Chanel and Clinique have but it’s important to find out what brands carry a nude to best fit your skin tone.

## 4. Peach

![Peach](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qa/tv/yzr6lhzj_466x595.jpg)

If you’re still obsessed with the peach and coral shades you bought this summer, don’t think you need to stop wearing them this fall. Peach is one of my favorite colors of the season. You can use a soft peach to mimic the color of your lips or go for a more bold appearance. One of my favorite peach toned lipsticks is Nars Audacious Lipstick in Julie. You can get it here: [narscosmetics.com](http://www.narscosmetics.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 5. Berry

![Berry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pp/nw/uwnbqzr4_400x598.jpg)

Berry lips aren’t just for winter. Dark lips look great in the fall as well. Just like with an eggplant shade, you can opt for a stain if you want a subtle look. A berry lip is the perfect choice for going from day to evening makeup. If you’re looking for a product recommendation, give Marc Jacobs Beauty Kiss Pop Lip Color Stick in Headliner 608 – Berry Sangria a try. You can purchase it by visiting: [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 6. Crimson

![Crimson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bh/zu/qnfxdfxl_456x684.jpg)

Channel your inner vamp this autumn with a crimson lip. A red lip always makes a statement and is appropriate any time of the year, day or night. You can find a crimson lipstick in any of your favorite makeup lines, but my current obsession is Kat Von D’s cult favorite Studded Kiss Lipstick in Hellbent – matte crimson red. Her Sephora-exclusive line also has crimson colors in a sparkly formula. To shop the collection, visit: [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 7. Lilac

_ Lilac lipstick is another great color option for fall. Although I love the bright and pastel hues of lavender, for fall, a muted lilac can provide an almost neutral look. One of the perfect lipsticks to try to pull off a subtle lilac shade is Lipstick Queen’s Let Them Eat Cake. You can buy it by visiting: lipstickqueen.com_

Will you be trying out any of these colors this fall? Tell us what shades you plan on wearing in the comments section.
