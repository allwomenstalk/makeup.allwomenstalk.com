# 7 Coral Makeup Products You Need This Summer ...

Coral makeup is a great way to celebrate summer! Coral is made to be worn under the sun and next to the waves. Anyone can rock this summer beauty trend and these coral makeup products will help you look fabulous doing so!

## 1. Obsessive Compulsive Cosmetics – Dune Generation Summer 2014 Stained Gloss in Aurora

![Obsessive Compulsive Cosmetics – Dune Generation Summer 2014 Stained Gloss in Aurora](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k7/cr/iddq6ybr_450x447.jpg)

One of my favorite coral makeup products is this vegan lip gloss by Obsessive Compulsive Cosmetics. Each of the shades in the Dune Generation collection are translucent but also highly-pigmented. In other words, it is a must-have lip color for summer because it won’t leave your lips feeling heavy! Aurora is the perfect lip shade to throw on before you head off to the beach, lake, or pool. Shop the shade at [occmakeup.com](https://occmakeup.com/).

## 2. Tarte – Amazonian Clay 12-Hour Blush in Fearless

![Tarte – Amazonian Clay 12-Hour Blush in Fearless](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vb/wi/zsd9mf5x_450x408.jpg)

Coral blush should be a staple in your summer makeup bag. The color looks flattering on nearly every skin tone and will give you a sunkissed appearance. Personally, I can’t get over how amazing Tarte’s Amazonian Clay Blush is! Tarte has formulated this blush to be free of chemicals and uses natural products. You can get it at [tartecosmetics.com](http://www.tartecosmetics.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 3. Make up for Ever – Aqua Lip Waterproof Lipliner Pencil in Vintage Coral

![Make up for Ever – Aqua Lip Waterproof Lipliner Pencil in Vintage Coral](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cv/hm/gx3t84yr_450x432.jpg)

If you don’t already own a coral lip liner, summer is the perfect time to add one to your makeup collection. A coral liner will boost the appearance of coral lipstick you already own and it looks beautiful under pinks, oranges, and reds. Make Up For Ever’s Aqua Lip Waterproof Lipliner Pencil is one of my favorites because of how smooth it glides on, making shaping a breeze! To get this liner, visit [makeupforever.com](https://www.makeupforever.com/us/en?utm_source=pepperjam&utm_medium=affiliate&utm_campaign=43737&clickId=4035668798).

## 4. NARS – the Multiple in Portofino

![NARS – the Multiple in Portofino](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x9/pz/f4jnasw3_450x416.jpg)

If you want the summer coral beauty trend but don’t want to shell out the money on several different products, than NARS The Multiple is a must-have! This all-in-one product can be used all over your face and body. Make your eyes pop, add color to your cheeks, and give yourself a summery pout with this one product. Portofino is a gorgeous, shimmering coral shade you’re sure to love! You can shop this time saving product at [narscosmetics.com](https://www.narscosmetics.com/?utm_content=generic&utm_medium=affiliate_display&utm_source=43737&utm_campaign=8-10989&clickId=4042352493).

## 5. MAC – Eyeshadow in Free to Be

![MAC – Eyeshadow in Free to Be](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nm/ad/l45kpz0m_300x399.jpg)

Free to Be is MAC’s true coral matte eyeshadow. Coral is a flattering eyeshadow color and looks great paired with golds and browns. I think you’ll be surprised how much you’ll love wearing coral on your eyes. Shop Free to Be at [maccosmetics.com](https://goo.gl/hv5vlM).

## 6. Lime Crime – Opaque Lipstick in Babette

![Lime Crime – Opaque Lipstick in Babette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tq/09/iqxnzs00_475x379.jpg)

Lime Crime’s Babette combines two of my favorite trends – pastels and the color coral. Babette goes on smooth and provides an even, opaque finish. This color is subtle and loud all at once! You can get this flattering shade that’s loaded with pigment by checking out [limecrime.com](http://www.limecrime.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 7. Obsessive Compulsive Cosmetics – Cosmetic Colour Pencils in Grandma

![Obsessive Compulsive Cosmetics – Cosmetic Colour Pencils in Grandma](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/un/iq/t67l0uzp_450x448.jpg)

OCC’s Cosmetic Colour Pencils can be used on the eyes or on the lips, and just like all their products, they are vegan and cruelty-free. If you want to give your eyes or your pout a pop of coral, consider the color Grandma. It is a classic coral color that will transform your summer beauty routine! Head over to [occmakeup.com](https://occmakeup.com/) to get this pencil.

A touch of coral in your daily makeup will give you a bright and sunkissed-appearance. Do you plan on incorporating coral into your makeup this summer?
