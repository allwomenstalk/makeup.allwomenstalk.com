13 Gorgeous Lipstick Shades for Pale Skin ...

By Jennifer • 10 Comments

While half the women I know are all in a flutter about 50 Shades of Grey, I’m a lot more worried about Shades of Lipstick… I have pale skin by design after a recent skin cancer scare, but I’m loathe to give up my hot shades. What’s a lily-white girl to do? Pick up a steamy novel and scour the internet for lipstick shades for pale skin! I’ve done the scouring and put this list together, so you can read the book and scan my list between steamy love scenes. Ready? Here are 13 gorgeous lipstick shades for pale skin.

1 NARS Lipstick in Honolulu Honey

Price: $24.00 at sephora.com
This is my new favorite lipstick shade for pale skin. It’s creamy coral, with beige undertones, perfect for my pale, but olive-hued, complexion. I love that I can still wear the coral trend, without looking washed-out.

***

This creamy coral beauty is a dream for those with fair skin. I adore how it applies smoothly and doesn't appear too harsh against lighter complexions. The beige undertones are truly a blessing, offering just enough warmth to enhance natural lip color subtly. With NARS Lipstick in Honolulu Honey, you get to join in on vibrant trends while maintaining an elegant, understated look. The quality is outstanding, providing lasting wear without the need for constant touch-ups. It's become a staple in my makeup bag for its versatility and the way it effortlessly complements a range of makeup looks.

2 Yves St. Laurent Volupté Sheer Candy Glossy Balm Crystal Color in Succulent Pomegranate

Price: $30.00 at sephora.com
Regardless of what hue this lippie came in, I’d love it! It’s lush, with a blend of antioxidant-rich fruit extracts to hydrate your lips for an irresistibly kissable pucker. Which is the best lipstick shade for pale skin? Try Succulent Pomegranate, as exotic and gorgeous as the name implies, with undertones of red that will flatter even the whitest complexion.

***

The Succulent Pomegranate isn't just a feast for the eyes; it's a treat for your lips too. At $30.00, it could be seen as a splurge, but this balm is worth every penny with its luxurious texture and nourishing ingredients. It glides on with the ease of a balm, yet has the pigment payoff of a lipstick, giving just the right amount of color without overwhelming your delicate complexion. The glossy finish adds a fullness and brightness to your smile, making it a staple for any beauty aficionado seeking that perfect pop of color.

3 Make up for Ever Rouge Artist Intense in Matte Flesh

Price: $19.00 at sephora.com
This lipstick lasts forever, as bright and vibrant at the end of the day as it was in the morning. I love “Matte Flesh,” which sounds a little dull, but that looks gorgeous with a pale complexion. If you like pearlescent hues, try Pearly Rust, too.

4 Illamasqua Lipstick in Obey

Price: $22.00 at sephora.com
If you’ve read the book, you know why this shade, Obey, reminds me of 50 Shades. It’s the ideal lipstick shade for pale skin, though, which is why I love it! It’s a lush, coral-infused pink, the color of a blush.

***

The Illamasqua Lipstick in Obey truly captures the essence of a submissive but powerful femininity that resonates with the daring spirit of the iconic novel. Not only does it complement fair skin tones, but it also offers a sophisticated pop of color that works just as well for everyday wear as it does for more special occasions. The formula is long-lasting and glides on effortlessly, ensuring that your statement lips remain the center of attention. Plus, it's infused with Vitamin E, so it moisturizes while providing vibrant color. For those with a porcelain complexion, this shade is a must-have in your makeup bag!

5 Shisheido Perfect Rouge in Serenity

Price: $25.00 at sephora.com
This lipstick shade for pale skin is just as delicate and sweet as the name, a light orchid-pink with violet undertones that looks especially pretty with both olive and peaches-and-cream complexions. On some, it’s almost a nude, too… it’s so versatile!

***

The Shiseido Perfect Rouge in Serenity slides effortlessly over your lips, infusing them with long-lasting moisture and vibrant color. At such a reasonable price point, it’s a steal for those looking to pamper their pout without breaking the bank. And, it doesn't just look gorgeous straight out of the tube, it also boasts a non-drying formula and full-bodied texture that can withstand a busy day without constant touch-ups. Whether you're in the boardroom or at brunch, this shade has just the right hint of sophistication to elevate any look.

6 Korres Mango Butter Lipstick SPF 10 in Warm Pink

Price: $18.00 at sephora.com
Here’s a lipstick shade for pale skin that’s also designed to make your lips smoother while protecting them from the sun’s harmful rays. Mango butter hydrates, and the color is amazing, a warm, rich pink.

***

The Korres Mango Butter Lipstick SPF 10 not only nurtures with its creamy mango butter base but also adds a hint of tropical luxury to your beauty routine. Ideal for those with lighter complexions, the Warm Pink shade provides a natural-looking pop of color that doesn't overwhelm. Plus, the built-in SPF 10 means you're getting a touch of UV protection, an often overlooked necessity for maintaining healthy lips. At an affordable price, this lipstick is a staple that offers both aesthetic and practical benefits.

7 LORAC Cream Lipstick in Babydoll

Price: $19.00 at sephora.com
At first glance, this wouldn’t seem like a lipstick shade for pale skin, but you’d be amazed how flattering it is! The bright coral goes on a lot lighter than it looks in the tube, and it lasts a long time, too! Gorgeous!

8 Maybelline Color Sensational Creamy Mattes in Siren in Scarlet

Price: 7.99 at ulta.com
There's no reason you shouldn't be absolutely bold with your lipstick! This red lipstick looks incredible on women with pale skin, makes your lips pop, and draws attention to your face. Plus, it can't help but remind me of Taylor Swift and her signature red lip! Now who doesn't want a little T Swift in their makeup collection?

***

It's an absolute must-have for when you're aiming to turn heads and exude confidence. The sensational matte finish means long-lasting wear without the worry of constant touch-ups. It's a versatile shade that can take you from day to night effortlessly, and it's the perfect pop of color for any outfit. Plus, the formula is infused with nourishing oils, ensuring that your lips stay moisturized and comfortable. So, channel your inner pop star and let your lips make the statement with this stunning shade!

9 Mac a Novel Romance

Price: $16.00 at macys.com
This dusty rose shade of lipstick is absolutely to die for! On fair skin, it will stand out, but not too much, so that it's just a little bit extra. You're sure to love this youthful, flirtacious shade on your lips!

***

The MAC a Novel Romance lipstick is the perfect balance of subtle and statement for those with a paler complexion. Its dusty rose hue exudes a romantic vibe that's hard to resist, especially when you want that flawless, not-overdone look. The quality of MAC lipsticks is renowned; they're known for their long-lasting, rich pigmentation and smooth application. If you want to add a touch of sophistication to your everyday makeup or need a reliable go-to for your evening look, this shade is a versatile choice. Plus, its creamy texture ensures your lips stay hydrated and looking plump throughout the day.

10 Revlon Super Lustrous Lipstick in Black Cherry

Price: $4.99 at target.com
Feeling especially daring? This is the lip color for you! Lorde has pulled it off, so why shouldn’t you? This shade makes you seem mysterious, glamorous, and overall stunning!

11 Clinique Long Last Soft Shine Lipstick in Merlot

Price: $16.00 at sephora.com
I'm absolutely in love with this shade! It has mild undertones, but still is a striking shade of red! It especially compliments ladies with fair skin, plus it's long-lasting! Who could ask for more?

***

This Clinique lipstick glides on effortlessly, leaving behind a luxurious feel without the heavy weight of some long-wearing formulas. The soft shine finish offers just the right amount of luster, perfect for both a day at the office or a night out. With its staying power, you won't need constant touch-ups, which is a total win for busy beauties. It's also formulated without any fragrances, making it a great pick for those with sensitive skin or noses. Dress up your lips with this sophisticated Merlot hue that promises to stay put through coffee sips and sweet kisses.

12 Mac Lipstick in Sushi Kiss

Price: $16.00 at shop.nordstrom.com
I love the name all on it's own, but this shade really looks stunning on fair-skinned beauties! It's nude and barely noticable, but adds a soft flare of beauty to your face.

***

Mac Lipstick in Sushi Kiss is a beautiful nude shade with a hint of pink that is perfect for pale skin tones. It is part of MAC's Retro Matte Lipstick line, which features a long-lasting, matte finish. This shade is enriched with emollients and conditioners to keep lips soft and hydrated. It also contains antioxidant-rich vitamin E to help protect lips from environmental stressors. The creamy, lightweight formula glides on easily and provides full coverage with a single swipe. This lipstick is also vegan and cruelty-free.

13 Nars Semi Matte Lipstick in Schiap

Price: $26.00 at narscosmetics.com
This shade isn't for everyone, but if anyone can pull it off, it's a woman with pale skin! This one reminds me of Cher from Clueless—any fans? It's fun, flirty, and adventurous!
