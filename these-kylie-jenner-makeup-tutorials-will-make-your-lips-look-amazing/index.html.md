# Kylie Jenner Makeup Tutorials to Give You Kissable Looking Lips ...

You don't have to buy Kylie's lip kit in order to look like her. There are plenty of drugstore products that you can use in order to get her big lips and sultry eyes. If you're interested in copying the young star, here are a few Kylie Jenner inspired makeup tutorials that will make your lips look absolutely amazing:

## 1. Look for Less: Kylie Jenner

This is an affordable makeup look that includes contouring, bronzer, and blush. Of course, it also involves a lot of lip products. Try using a dusty lipliner and outlining your lips much further out than they actually reach. To do this, you should bite down on your own lips and extend the line where it would normally be and fill it all in. You can also apply a light brown shadow under your lower lip. Then your [small lips](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/awesome-makeup-tips-for-thin-lips/) will look as big as Kylie's.

## 2. Kylie Jenner Inspired Makeup Tutorial

Skip ahead to 2:30 if you want to see how to create makeup that looks just as flawless as Kylie's makeup. The woman in the video actually created her own lipstick that was inspired by the Kylie Jenner's lip color. However, she also gives you an important piece of info, which is that Kylie's makeup artist uses MAC World pencil on her. So if you want her exact look, try buying that brand!

## 3. Kylie Jenner Bronze Glam

This tutorial involves earthy tones, bronzer, and a smokey eye with wings. Of course, when it comes to the lips, you should avoid trying the Kylie Jenner challenge by sucking on something to puff up your lips, because it will only end up damaging your lips instead! Instead, stick to using a lip pencil around the top of your cupid's bow and in a small section under your lip.

## 4. Kylie Jenner Inspired Makeup

If you want to rock the mauve lip color that Kylie is always seen wearing, then you should check out this tutorial for the right product to buy. According to the woman in the video, you should start by putting foundation on your lips, then add a cream stick liner from MAC, and fill your lips in with that same liner. That means you don't actually need lipstick. The liner does all the work!

## 5. Kylie Jenner Inspired Makeup Look

By the end of this tutorial, you'll end up with colorful lips and sultry eyes. When it comes to the lips, the woman in the tutorial doesn't actually overline her lips like Kylie does, and you don't have to either! Some women look best with their natural shape. Your lips don't have to be big in order to be beautiful.

## 6. Kylie Jenner Makeup Tutorial

For this tutorial, you should use two liquid lipsticks in order to create a look that's similar to Kylie's. You'll start by outlining your lips with the one color and filling it in on the sides. Then you'll use the second color on the inside of your lips. Blend it a bit and you're all finished!

## 7. Kylie Makeup Transformation

If you're going to overdraw your lips like Kylie does, then you should round your lip out on the bottom. You should also round your cupid's bow on your upper lip.

## 8. How to Fake Big Lips

Fake big Kylie Jenner lips with this flawless tutorial! Over line lips by focusing on the center of your lips above your cupids bow and below the middle of your lower lip. Then, follow the natural line and shape of your lips on the corners.

## 9. Kylie Jenner Lipstick Tutorial

This tutorial takes minutes and lasts all day long! Add definition with a lip pencil, overdrawing slightly, starting on the bottom lip and working your way up to the cupids bow. Fill in lips with the same color, and take another slightly darker lip pencil to create a 3D effect, concentrating on the outer corners, center of the bottom lip, and cupids bow to add dimension. Finish by applying lipstick all over.

## 10. Natural Smoky Eye and Classic Kylie Lip

In this tutorial, you'll learn how to create a natural smoky eye and classic Kylie lip. Start with all over foundation, define and highlight your brows, and use an all matte palette for your eyes. Do a dramatic smoked out wing with dark shadow, add fake lashes, and finish with a dark lip and add extra plump with gloss, keeping it in the middle.

Now you'll be able to create lips that look just like Kylie's! Which one of these makeup tutorials do you find the cutest?
