# 8 Gorgeous Eye Dusts by Lime Crime ...

I adore eye make-up, especially fun eye shadows and tints. I recently discovered a rainbow of vegan-friendly eye dusts that I just adore by a brand called Lime Crime. All of their colors are vivid and lush, and they go on, and stay on, so nicely! Brush on a little, and the eye dust is subtle, modest… or go bold and layer it for a brighter, darker look… either way, you’ll love the way they look and feel! Here are 8 gorgeous eye dusts by Lime Crime.

## 1. Twilight Magic Dust Eye Shadow

_ **Price:** $14.00 at limecrimemakeup.com_
Whether you’re Team Edward or Team Jacob or don’t even know what I’m talking about, you’re sure to adore this amethyst-meets-aqua eye dust. It makes brown eyes pop, and blue eyes shine, and looks just as pretty brushed on lightly as it does layered to be bolder. So pretty!

## 2. Nymph Magic Dust Eye Shadow

_ **Price:** $14.00 at limecrimemakeup.com_
Add a dimension of cool silver (in some light) or pale gold (in other light) to your lids with this sheer-ish powder, the color of fairy dust and dew. Use it to highlight your brow-bone and make your eyes look wider, brighter, and perhaps even a little magical.

## 3. Cupcake Thief Magic Dust Eye Shadow

_ **Price:** $14.00 at limecrimemakeup.com_
The exact hue coveted by centuries of princesses and ballerinas (and ballerina princesses) the world over, this gloriously pale pink eye dust looks marvelous with every complexion and eye color, and co-ordinates with most tutus… and birthday cupcakes.

## 4. Dragon Scales Magic Dust Eye Shadow

_ **Price:** $14.00 at limecrimemakeup.com_
Is that a rich terracotta brown, or a glimmering turquoise green? It’s actually a magical combination of the two, with a little shine thrown in for good measure, the color that Lime Crime’s designers imagine dragons’ scales to be. And why not? Add a little to your lids, especially if you have blue eyes… gorgeous!

## 5. Medusa Magic Dust Eye Shadow

![Medusa Magic Dust Eye Shadow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o9/y5/p4qlji6s54c8f21911c04701350970_220x169.jpg)

**Price:** $14.00 at [limecrimemakeup.com](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.limecrimemakeup.com/products/MEDUSA-magic-dust-eyeshadow.html)
Apply it like an eyeliner with a thin, wet brush, or with a dry, wide brush as a shadow, either way this color’s perfect for the smoky eye look, all sooty black and glimmering blue. It’s gloriously dramatic, and is sure to become an instant favorite!

## 6. Mirror Mirror Magic Dust Eye Shadow

![Mirror Mirror Magic Dust Eye Shadow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qo/cw/jro7lrjy54c8f219b4b04125965273_220x169.jpg)

**Price:** $14.00 at [limecrimemakeup.com](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.limecrimemakeup.com/products/MIRROR-MIRROR-magic-dust-eyeshadow.html)
Add a layer of shine over any of the other eye dusts, or just wear it alone, this glittering silver dust is sure to attract compliments and perhaps even make you more fair that The Fairest of Them All. It’s icy-cool, like dew-covered spider webs, and gives a fresh-faced, wide-eyed look.

## 7. Diva Magic Dust Eye Shadow

_ **Price:** $14.00 at limecrimemakeup.com_
Aren’t we all divas, at least a little, maybe just on the inside? Celebrate that sassy inclination with this brilliant, warm bronze dust, perfect for green or blue eyes. Use it to make a warmer version of a smoky eye, or just to highlight another pink-ish color.

## 8. Troubadour Magic Dust Eye Shadow

_ **Price:** $14.00 at limecrimemakeup.com_
This bright, brilliant turquoise is available exclusively online, so you won’t find it anywhere else… snap it up now, sweetie! It’s made for the deepest of velvety brown eyes, and looks oh-so-pretty on! Wear it for fun all alone, or layer it with other colors (like the cool silvers) for a night out with the girls.

Those are just a few of the high-intensity, long-lasting eye dusts by Lime Crime. I can’t wait to try a few more! Which of these colors do you like best, or is there another one in their line-up you like more? Please share!
