# 11 Best Blushes to Have in Your Makeup Bag ...

If you’re in search for a new blush on the market, try some of these best blushes out there! These best blushes are highly rated for their top quality and best-selling properties. Most have won multiple awards for long-staying power, beautiful color and are easy to use on any skin type. When choosing a good blush, it’s always best to go with one that matches your own complexion. If your overall makeup is in cooler shades (cool gray, cool brown, cool wine and berry colors of lip products), than your blush should be in a cooler shade as well. If you wear warm-colored lip products and eye-shadows, then you’ll definitely look fabulous with a warmer shade of blush. Try some of our favorite best blushes and let us know which one you loved best!

## 1. NARS Blush

![NARS Blush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/22095921_4521_521x343.jpg)

I’ve always loved Nars products, which makes one of the best blushes on the market. NARS blush earns five stars among users rating this product and it is always a bestseller wherever it is sold. By the way, the shade ‘Orgasm’ is said to be so popular it has its own cult! Also, it’s said to match all skin tones, so if you are not sure which shade of blush to get - go with “Orgasm” and it’ll definitely be a great match.

**Price:** $29.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 2. BareMinerals Blush

![BareMinerals Blush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/22095921_5983_521x344.jpg)

Oh, how I love this brand! It is an all natural formula that gives skin a beautiful glow for an affordable price. The product also seems small but lasts along time. It comes in many different varieties as well. It is also safe for acne-prone skin and dry complexions.

**Price:** $19.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 3. Illamasqua Powder Blusher

![Illamasqua Powder Blusher](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/22095920_5632_521x405.jpg)

A new brand to me was Illamasqua, which I was recently introduced to through their powder blush product. It provides an intense, bold color for those in search of a more dramatic look and is really great for obtaining that glamorous look you might be trying to achieve.

**Price:** $28.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 4. Lancôme Blush Subtil

![Lancôme Blush Subtil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/22095920_7211_521x459.jpg)

**Price:** $30.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 5. Cargo Blush

![Cargo Blush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/22095920_9915_521x446.jpg)

Cargo is a great brand to try if you’re looking for a new blush to love. Made of a moist, sheer powder, it blends well and fits any skin type and need. It’s also pretty affordable and lasts a long time. This blush is also one of the best selling products by the Cargo brand, so you know you can feel pretty safe trying it for the first time.

**Price:** $29.00 at [macys.com](http://www1.macys.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

![DiorBlush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/22095921_9036_521x375.jpg)

## 7. Tarte Amazonian Clay 12-Hour Blush

![Tarte Amazonian Clay 12-Hour Blush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/22095922_5030_521x343.jpg)

Tarte's Amazonian Blush can be used with all skin types too, and is especially wonderful to hydrate the skin while adding color. It also helps hide large pores and nourish the skin without making it oily.

## 8. Stila Convertible Color

![Stila Convertible Color](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/22095922_6991_521x395.jpg)

Need an all in one product? Try Stila’s popular Convertible Color. It’s ideal for ladies who need a creamier product, or who have dry skin. It seems translucent at first glance but actually delivers a beautiful shine and glowing results.

**Price:** $25.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 9. Benefit Benetint

![Benefit Benetint](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/22095922_1040_521x487.jpg)

I love benefit products and benefit's Benetint is a personal favorite of mine. It is basically a liquid stain that can be used on your cheeks or your lips for a subtle rose-tinted effect. I personally love keeping it in my purse for touching up my face and adding a bit of color whenever needed.

**Price:** $30.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 10. Laura Mercier Second Skin Cheek Colour

![Laura Mercier Second Skin Cheek Colour](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/22095923_4221_521x374.jpg)

This cheek color also helps even out your skin tone and enhance your natural glow, or give you one! It also contains natural botanicals that nourish the skin and provide hydration without oiliness.

## 11. Josie Maran Lip and Cheek Color Stain

![Josie Maran Lip and Cheek Color Stain](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/22095922_6741_249x135.jpg)
